Artichoke, pepper and broad bean salad with lemon dressing

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • ¼ onion, very finely sliced
  • ¼ red pepper, finely sliced
  • 4 artichoke hearts in oil, drained and cut into wedges
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh chives
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
  • 50g/1¾oz broad beans, skins removed
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Combine the onion, pepper, artichoke hearts, chives, coriander and broad beans in a bowl.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil and salt and freshly ground black pepper. Pour over the salad, stirring to coat everything. Serve.

Spicy fifty

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Salvatore Calabrese
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 50ml/2fl oz vanilla vodka
  • 1 tbsp elderflower cordial
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp honey syrup
  • 2 slices red chilli
  • red bird's-eye chilli, to garnish

Method

  1. Place the vanilla vodka, elderflower cordial, lime juice, honey syrup and chilli slices into a cocktail shaker and shake well to combine.

  2. To serve, strain into a Martini glass and garnish with a red bird's-eye chilli.

Lemon oregano potatoes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

1 to 2 hours

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By David Laris
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 2.5kg/5½lb potatoes
  • 80ml/5tbsp olive oil
  • 80ml/5tbsp water
  • handful fresh oregano (or ½ tsp dried oregano)
  • 1 lemon, juiced.

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. Peel and slice the potatoes lengthways. Place in roasting tray.

  3. Cover with olive oil, water, lemon juice and oregano.

  4. Season to taste and mix.

  5. Place the baking tray in the oven for 1½ hours. If cooking with Thyme, Sage and Garlic Roast Chicken, put the tray under the chicken to catch all the juices.

Stuffed nectarines

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

A simple baked fruit dessert with tangy mascarpone and spicy ginger nuts.

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 4 ginger nut biscuits
  • 1 free-range egg yolk
  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 nectarine, halved, stone removed

To serve

  • 2 tbsp mascarpone cheese
  • 2 tbsp double cream
  • 1 sprig fresh mint

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. Place the biscuits, egg yolk, butter and sugar into a food processor and pulse until well combined.

  3. Place the nectarine halves on a baking tray, cut side up, and spoon the biscuit mixture inside. Bake in the oven for 6-8 minutes, or until the nectarines are tender and the stuffing is golden-brown and crisp.

  4. Whip the mascarpone cheese together with the cream until smooth and creamy. Using two spoons, form the mixture into quenelles and place on top of the nectarine halves. Garnish with a sprig of mint.

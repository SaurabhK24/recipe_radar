Spinach and onion salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the salad

  • 100g/3½oz spinach
  • ¼ onion, sliced thinly
  • ¼ red pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped

For the dressing

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Method

  1. For the salad, mix together the salad ingredients in a bowl and set aside.

  2. To make the dressing whisk together the ingredients. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and serve.

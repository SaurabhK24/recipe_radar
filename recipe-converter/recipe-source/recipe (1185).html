Brownie and peach knickerbocker glory

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 3 ready-made brownies, broken into pieces
  • 100g/3½oz mascarpone
  • ½ vanilla pod, seeds removed
  • 15g/½oz icing sugar
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream, whipped
  • 1 peach, skinned and cut into cubes
  • sprig of fresh mint, to garnish

Method

  1. Place half of the brownies in a tall ice cream glass.

  2. Place the mascarpone, vanilla seeds and sugar in a bowl, mix well and fold through the cream.

  3. Place half of the mixture onto the brownies and top with half of the peaches.

  4. Repeat with a layers of brownie, mascarpone mixture and peaches.

  5. Garnish with mint and serve.

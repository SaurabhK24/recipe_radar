Shaved fennel, herb and tomato salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • ½ fennel bulb, finely sliced
  • ¼ ripe tomato, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ lemon, zest and juice
  • handful mixed fresh herbs, such as chives, chervil, parsley and basil, chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Place all of the ingredients into a large salad bowl and stir.

  2. To serve, place onto a serving dish.

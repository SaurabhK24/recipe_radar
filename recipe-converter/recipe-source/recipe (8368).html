Salt and pepper buttered samphire

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Alex Mackay
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 200g/7oz samphire
  • 30g/1oz butter, at room temperature
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Cook the samphire in a pan of boiling salted water for 1-2 minutes, or until tender. Drain well.

  2. Transfer the samphire to a bowl and toss with the butter, season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Pile the samphire onto a plate and serve.

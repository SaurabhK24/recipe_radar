Cauliflower mash

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 25g/1oz butter
  • ½ onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 100g/3½oz mature cheddar, grated
  • 75ml/2½fl oz double cream
  • salt and freshly ground white pepper
  • pinch nutmeg

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan. Add the onion and sweat for 1-2 minutes until softened.

  2. Add the cauliflower and enough water to just cover the ingredients. Stir to combine, bring to the boil and reduce to a simmer for 8-10 minutes or until tender.

  3. Drain through a colander and place the cauliflower mixture in a food processor.

  4. Add the cheese and cream and blend until you have a thick puree.

  5. Season with salt, freshly ground white pepper and pinch of nutmeg.

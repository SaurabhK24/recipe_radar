Pork escalope

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 escalope of pork
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 50g/1¾oz oats
  • 50g/1¾oz breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ apple, cored and sliced
  • 50g/1¾oz butternut squash, chopped
  • 3 tbsp double cream
  • 3 tbsp white wine

Method

  1. Mix the egg, oats and breadcrumbs together in a bowl. Dip the pork in the mixture to coat.

  2. Heat one tablespoon of the olive oil in a pan and fry the pork for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.

  3. Meanwhile, gently heat the remaining tablespoon of olive oil in a pan and sauté the apple and butternut for 8-10 minutes or until soft.

  4. Add the cream and wine and reduce to make a creamy sauce.

  5. Serve the pork on top of the creamy vegetables.

Sautéed sweet potato with coriander

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 sweet potato, diced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ red onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 3 tbsp fresh coriander, chopped

Method

  1. Cook the sweet potato in boiling water until just tender. Drain.

  2. In a pan heat the olive oil and add the red onions and ground coriander. Cook for 2-3 minutes.

  3. Add the sweet potatoes to the pan with the red onion, stir in the fresh coriander and cook until golden.

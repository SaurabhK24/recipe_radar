Stir-fried broccoli with chilli and garlic

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Garrey Dawson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • ½ head broccoli, cut into florets
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • soy sauce, to serve (optional)

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a wok over a high heat. Add the garlic, broccoli and chilli flakes and stir fry for 5-6 minutes.

  2. Add the soy sauce, and season to taste with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. To serve, place the stir-fried broccoli onto a serving plate with a dipping dish of soy sauce on the side if desired.

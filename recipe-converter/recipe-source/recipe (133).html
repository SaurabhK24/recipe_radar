Apple and carrot sesame crumble

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the filling

  • 25g/1oz unsalted butter
  • 2 apples, peeled, cored and thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, peeled and grated
  • 50g/1½oz brown sugar
  • 50ml/2fl oz white wine

For the crumble topping

  • 100g/3½oz plain flour
  • 100g/3½oz unsalted butter, cut into cubes, plus extra for greasing
  • 100g/3½oz brown sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 100g/3½oz Greek yoghurt, to serve
  • mint sprig, to garnish

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. For the filling, melt the butter in a frying pan and cook the apple and carrot until soft.

  3. Add the brown sugar and cook until caramelised.

  4. Add the white wine and simmer until the mixture is thickened.

  5. Lightly grease a small ovenproof dish and pour the mixture into the dish.

  6. For the topping, place the flour and butter into a large bowl and rub together until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs. Add the brown sugar, cinnamon and sesame seeds and mix well.

  7. Sprinkle the crumble topping over the filling and bake in the oven for 12-14 minutes, until golden-brown.

  8. To serve, spoon over the yoghurt and garnish with mint.

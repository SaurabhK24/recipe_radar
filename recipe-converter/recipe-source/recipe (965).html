Blue cheese Welsh rarebit

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 free-range egg, yolk only
  • 85g/3oz Danish blue cheese, crumbled
  • 3 tbsp double cream
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • dash Worcestershire sauce
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 slices crusty white bread, griddled

Method

  1. Preheat the grill to medium.

  2. Place the egg yolk, cheese, cream, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, salt and freshly ground black pepper into a mini food processor and blend until well combined. Top the griddled bread with the egg and cheese mixture and place onto a baking sheet. Grill for 3-4 minutes, or until golden-brown and bubbling on top.

  3. Transfer to a serving plate and serve immediately.

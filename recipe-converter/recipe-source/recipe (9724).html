Stir-fried egg noodles

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 100g/3½oz egg noodles, cooked according to packet instructions
  • 1 handful fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 handful fresh coriander, chopped
  • squeeze lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds, toasted
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • ¼ red chilli, chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • lime wedges, to garnish

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a wok, add the rest of the ingredients and stir-fry over a high heat for 2-3 minutes, or until warmed through.

  2. To serve, spoon the noodles into a bowl and garnish with the lime wedges.

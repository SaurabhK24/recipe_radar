Coconut and syrup sponge with glazed oranges

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 85g/3oz butter, melted
  • 85g/3oz caster sugar
  • 3 tbsp grated coconut
  • 75ml/2½fl oz golden syrup
  • 2cm/1in piece root ginger, peeled and grated
  • 1 large free-range egg, beaten
  • butter, for greasing
  • 85g/3oz self-raising flour, plus extra for dusting

To serve

  • 1 orange, peeled and sliced
  • 3 sprigs fresh mint to garnish
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • double cream, for drizzling

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a medium saucepan over a medium heat. Add the sugar, coconut, golden syrup and ginger and heat, stirring continuously, until the sugar has melted.

  2. Add the beaten egg and flour and stir well to combine.

  3. Grease a microwave-safe bowl with butter and dust with flour. Pour in the sponge batter and cook in a microwave on its highest setting for 4-5 minutes, or until cooked through.

  4. To serve, turn out the sponge onto a serving plate. Decorate with slices of orange and mint sprigs, pour the honey over and finish with a drizzle of cream.

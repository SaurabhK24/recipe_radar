Beetroot matchsticks with garlic, herbs and balsamic vinegar

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Garrey Dawson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 beetroot, peeled and chopped into thin matchsticks
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • small handful fresh parsley, chervil and coriander, chopped

Method

  1. Heat the butter in a frying pan over a medium heat and cook the beetroot matchsticks for 4-5 minutes.

  2. Add the garlic and cook for a further minute.

  3. Add the vinegar, turn up the heat and simmer until reduced. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Remove from the heat, add the herbs and stir well.

  4. To serve, place the beetroot onto a serving plate.

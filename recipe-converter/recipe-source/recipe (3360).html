Fajitas with guacamole

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Richard Phillips
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz fillet steak, cut into strips
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the guacamole

  • 1 ripe avocado, skinned and chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed to a paste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ lime, zest and juice
  • ½ beef tomato, chopped
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander

To serve

  • 2 tortilla wraps
  • 2 tbsp Greek-style yoghurt

Method

  1. Season the steak strips with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Heat the olive oil in a pan, then fry the steak for 1-2 minutes on both sides (for medium), or until cooked to your liking. Remove from the pan and allow to rest on a warm plate.

  2. For the guacamole, mash the avocado and garlic together with a fork, and then mix in the remaining guacamole ingredients.

  3. To serve, wrap the steak, guacamole and yoghurt up in two tortilla wrap.

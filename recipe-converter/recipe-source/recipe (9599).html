Broccoli with chilli and garlic oil

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 small head broccoli, cut into florets
  • ½ red chilli, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Place the broccoli florets into a pan of boiling salted water and boil for four minutes. Drain well.

  2. Heat the olive oil in a wok, add the chilli and garlic and stir fry for one minute. Add the broccoli and stir.

  3. Season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper, then spoon into a serving dish. Serve immediately.

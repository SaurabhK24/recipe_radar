Coleslaw cucumber sticks

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Make these simple, healthy snacks to take to the office, or for your kids to take to school. They are ready in seconds.

By Neil Strawson and Richard Hunt
From I Can Cook on the Go

Ingredients

  • ½ cucumber, sliced in half lengthways, seeds scraped out using a teaspoon
  • 20g/¾oz apple, grated
  • 20g/¾oz carrot, grated
  • 20g/¾oz cooked beetroot, grated
  • 30g/1¼oz cream cheese
  • 15g/½oz raisins

Method

  1. Cut the cucumber into four pieces.

  2. Mix the apple, carrot and beetroot together in a mixing bowl. Stir in the cream cheese until well combined.

  3. Fill each piece of cucumber with a little of the cream cheese mixture.

  4. Sprinkle each filled piece of cucumber with raisins. Wrap separately in cling film and chill in the fridge until needed.

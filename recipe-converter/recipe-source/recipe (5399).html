Mangetout, chilli and rice noodle salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 250g/9 oz thin rice noodles
  • 100g/3½oz mangetout, sliced into thin strips
  • ½ red chilli, seeds removed, finely chopped
  • 2 limes, juice only
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 2 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • ¼ mango, peeled and chopped
  • 1 tsp olive oil

Method

  1. Cook the rice noodles according to the packet instructions and drain.

  2. Add the strips of mangetout and chilli to the noodles.

  3. Whisk together the lime juice, sesame oil, soy sauce, caster sugar and balsamic vinegar and stir through the noodles, then mix in the mango.

  4. To serve, pile onto a plate and drizzle with the olive oil.

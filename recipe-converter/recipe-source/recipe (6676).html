Pea soup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ¼ onion, finely chopped
  • 75g/2½oz fresh peas
  • 150ml/5fl oz hot vegetable stock
  • 50ml double cream, plus 1 tsp extra, to serve
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • fresh mint sprig, to garnish

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a small saucepan over a medium heat. Add the onions and gently fry for three minutes, until softened.

  2. Add the peas and stock, bring to the boil, then reduce the heat to simmer for 10 minutes.

  3. Add the double cream, salt and pepper and heat through for one minute. Pour into a food processor and blend until smooth.

  4. To serve, pour into a bowl and garnish with a swirl of double cream and a sprig of mint.

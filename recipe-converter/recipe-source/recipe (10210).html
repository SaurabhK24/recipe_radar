Tequila sunrise

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Andy Pearson
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 50ml/2fl oz tequila
  • fresh orange juice, to top up
  • 1 tbsp grenadine

To serve

  • orange slice
  • 1 maraschino cherry

Method

  1. Pour the tequila into a highball glass filled with ice and top up with fresh orange juice. Pour the grenadine in down the side of the glass so that it sinks to the bottom.

  2. To serve, garnish with an orange slice and a maraschino cherry.

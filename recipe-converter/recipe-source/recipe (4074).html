Griddled lamb with succotash

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the griddled lamb

  • 1 lamb chop, taken off the bone
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the succotash

  • 1 corn on the cob, corn removed
  • 100g/3½oz frozen peas
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 100ml/3 ½fl oz double cream

For the scones

  • 200g/7oz self-raising flour
  • 100g/3½ butter
  • 2 free-range eggs
  • milk to bind
  • 100g/3½oz cheddar, grated
  • 1 large handful fresh basil

Method

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Season the lamb with salt and freshly ground black pepper, then rub with oil.

  3. Heat a griddle pan to smoking. Add the lamb and cook for 4-5 minutes, turning once.

  4. For the succotash, place the corn, frozen peas and garlic in a saucepan with the double cream. Bring to the boil and reduce to a simmer. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and cook for two minutes

  5. For the scones, mix together the flour, butter eggs and add a little milk in a food processor to form a smooth, firm dough.

  6. Roll out some scone dough onto a flat, floured surface. Fold in the cheese (reserving a little for later) and basil and fold the dough over.

  7. Using a medium-sized chefs' ring or cutter, cut out rounds and place onto a baking sheet. Brush with milk and top with the reserved cheese.

  8. Place into the preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until risen and golden.

  9. Serve the scones with the lamb and the succotash.

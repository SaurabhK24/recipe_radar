Wilted spinach and poached egg on toast

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Start the day right with vibrant green veg and an oozing poached egg.

By Rebecca Sargent

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large handful spinach
  • 1 whole nutmeg, for grating
  • 1 free-range egg
  • white wine vinegar
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 slice sourdough, toasted

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a pan over a medium heat, add the spinach, a small grating of nutmeg and season with salt and pepper. Pop the lid on and cook on low for 1-2 minutes, until just wilted.

  2. Meanwhile, bring a pan of water to a boil and reduce the heat to a simmer. Add a splash of white wine vinegar and gently break the egg in as close to the surface as possible. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until the egg is cooked to your liking.

  3. To serve, place the spinach on the toast, top with the egg and season with salt and pepper.

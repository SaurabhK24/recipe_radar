Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, peeled and sliced
  • 1 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 tin butter beans, drained
  • 1 cooking apple, cored and chopped
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 240ml/8fl oz cider
  • 340ml/12fl oz vegetable stock
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • salt and pepper

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a saucepan and add the onion and apple. Cook for 3-4 minutes until starting to soften.

  2. Add the thyme and the beans and season well. Pour in the cider and the stock, bring to the boil and simmer until the onions are tender.

  3. Take out the thyme sprigs, add the parsley and liquidise until smooth.

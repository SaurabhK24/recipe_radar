Rhubarb ice cream sundae

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 25g/1oz caster sugar
  • 1 lime, zest and juice only
  • 75g/2¾oz rhubarb, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh mint
  • 3 large scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 50g/1¾oz sultanas
  • 50g/1¾oz almonds, chopped
  • mint sprig, to garnish

Method

  1. Melt the butter and sugar together with the lime juice and zest. Tip in the rhubarb and cook for three minutes, until softened.

  2. Add the chopped mint and set aside to cool.

  3. Spoon a scoop of the ice cream into the base of a tall glass.

  4. Sprinkle over a third of the sultanas and nuts, and a third of the cooled rhubarb.

  5. Repeat the layering until all ingredients are used up.

  6. Garnish the sundae with a mint sprig and serve straightaway.

Quick tomato sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 4 Servings

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Saturday Brunch

Ingredients

  • 300g/10½oz cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tsp caster sugar
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 25ml/1fl oz olive oil

Method

  1. Whizz together the cherry tomatoes, caster sugar and seasoning in a food processor.

  2. Slowly add the olive oil until all the ingredients are combined. Check the seasoning, and serve.

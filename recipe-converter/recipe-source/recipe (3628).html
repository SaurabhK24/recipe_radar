Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 125g/4oz cream cheese
  • 120ml/4fl oz natural yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 small cucumber, deseeded and very finely diced
  • 2 tbsp freshly chopped mixed herbs such as basil, coriander, tarragon, chives, parsley

Method

  1. Beat together the cream cheese, yoghurt and white wine vinegar and season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  2. Fold in the cucumber and herbs.

  3. Serve with hot flatbreads, crisps or crudities.

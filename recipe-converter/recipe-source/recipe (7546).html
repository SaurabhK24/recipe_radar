Raspberry and redcurrant sorbet

Preparation time

30 mins to 1 hour

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes about 1l/1>pint

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Hugh Fearnley-Whittingstall

Ingredients

  • 500g/1lb2oz raspberries
  • 500g/1lb2oz redcurrants
  • 250g/9oz icing sugar

Method

  1. Crush and sieve the fruit, then stir in the icing sugar.

  2. Pour into an ice cream machine or make granita style if you don t.

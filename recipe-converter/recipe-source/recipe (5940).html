Mustard grilled chicken fillet with chilli and black-eyed bean salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the chicken

  • 1 chicken fillet
  • 1 tbsp grain mustard

For the salad

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 red chilli, chopped
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh dill
  • 55g/2oz cooked canned black-eyed beans
  • ¼ banana, peeled, chopped

Method

  1. Soak a wooden skewer in cold water for ten minutes.

  2. For the chicken, thread the meat on to the skewer and coat in the mustard. Heat a chargrill pan until smoking and cook the chicken for 3-4 minutes on both sides, or until golden-brown and cooked through.

  3. For the salad, mix all of the salad ingredients in a bowl placed onto a plate. Serve with the chicken on top.

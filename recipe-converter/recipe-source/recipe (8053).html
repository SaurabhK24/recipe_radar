Roast figs with honey and Marsala

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

7 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nigel Slater
From Nigel Slater's Simple Suppers

Ingredients

  • 8 large fresh figs
  • 2 tbsp or so runny honey
  • splash or two Marsala wine (port or Madeira work equally as well)
  • ready-made vanilla ice cream, to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Cut a deep cross down into each fig, nearly to the bottom, then squeeze the sides of each fig to expose the juicy flesh. Place the figs into a lidded baking dish.

  3. Drizzle over the honey and Marsala wine, ensuring some runs down inside the figs. Cover with the lid and bake in the oven for about 20 minutes.

  4. Serve the figs immediately with ice cream and the boozy syrup from the baking dish. Alternatively, serve with cream, custard or mascarpone.

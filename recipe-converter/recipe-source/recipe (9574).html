Steak tartare with toast

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz rump steak, trimmed and very finely chopped
  • ¼ red pepper, finely chopped
  • 50g/2oz green beans, finely sliced
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • few drops Tabasco sauce
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

To serve

  • 1 free-range egg yolk
  • 1 slice crusty bread, toasted and drizzled with oil

Method

  1. Place the chopped steak into a mixing bowl. Add all the remaining ingredients except the egg yolk and toast and mix well.

  2. Place a chefs' ring onto a serving plate and fill with the steak mixture.

  3. To serve, remove the chefs' ring, top with the egg yolk and serve with toast.

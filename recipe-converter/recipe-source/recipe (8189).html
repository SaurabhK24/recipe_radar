Rose petal sandwiches

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

By Jan Greenland

Ingredients

  • fragrant rose petals, white bases removed
  • 250g/8¾oz pat of organic butter
  • white organic bread, thinly sliced
  • organic caster sugar, to taste

Method

  1. The night before you want to make the sandwiches, cover the pat of butter in rose petals, wrap in foil and leave in a cool place.

  2. To make the sandwiches, spread the slices of bread with the rose butter, top with a layer of rose petals and sprinkle with caster sugar. Top with another slice of bread and cut into four.

  3. Serve with a pot of Earl Grey tea.

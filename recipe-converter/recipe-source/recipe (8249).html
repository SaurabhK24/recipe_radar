Saffron couscous with roasted tomatoes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the couscous

  • 200g/7oz couscous
  • pinch saffron
  • 300ml/½ pint chicken or vegetable stock
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh flatleaf parsley
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh chervil
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh chives
  • 1 plum tomato, chopped

For the roast tomatoes

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 fresh plum tomatoes, halved
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 2 garlic cloves, roughly chopped

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. For the couscous, pour the couscous into a large saucepan and add the saffron, stock and olive oil. Heat gently for about 5-6 minutes and then remove from the heat.

  3. Combine the lemon juice and zest and the chopped herbs in a bowl and add to the couscous. Finally fold in the chopped tomato.

  4. For the roasted tomatoes, heat the oil in an ovenproof frying pan and fry the tomatoes, turning once, until they have taken on some colour. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and add the thyme sprigs and garlic to the pan. Place the pan into the oven to roast the tomatoes for 10-12 minutes.

  5. To serve, pile the couscous onto a serving plate and top with the roasted tomatoes.

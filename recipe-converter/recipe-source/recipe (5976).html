Nectarines poached in honey and wine

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 nectarine cut in half, stone removed
  • 50ml/2fl oz white wine
  • 1 tbsp clear honey
  • 1 tbsp double cream

Method

  1. Heat the brown sugar in a frying pan over a high heat, until melted.

  2. Place the nectarines cut-side down into the sugar and cook for one minute.

  3. Add the wine and honey and boil, unitl the liquid has reduced in volume by half.

  4. Remove the nectarines and keep warm, then add the cream to the sauce, bring to the boil and stir until thickened.

  5. To serve, place the nectarines cut side down onto a plate and drizzle over the sauce.

'Spritz coppa' cocktail

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Herbal and citrus flavours combine in style in master mixologist Wayne Collins' delicious cocktail.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 15ml/½fl oz freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 15ml/½fl oz limoncello
  • 25ml/¾fl oz Galliano
  • 25ml/¾fl oz Aperol
  • prosseco, to fill glass

To garnish

  • summer berries
  • orange slice
  • lemon slice

Method

  1. Place the first four ingredients into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and stir.

  2. Strain the cocktail into a large wine glass, top with prosecco and garnish with fresh summer berries, and slices of orange and lemon.

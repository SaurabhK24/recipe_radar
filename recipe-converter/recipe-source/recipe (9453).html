Spinach, paneer cheese and sesame seed salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • large handful fresh spinach, washed and chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 85g/3oz paneer cheese, cut into cubes
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds

Method

  1. Mix all the ingredients together in a large bowl and then spoon into a chefs' ring on a serving plate. Carefully remove the chefs' ring and serve.

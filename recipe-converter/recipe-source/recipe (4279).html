Ham, cheese and fig skewers

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4

By Maria Elia
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of ham, cut in half
  • 1 ball buffalo mozzarella, torn into 12 pieces
  • 12 basil leaves
  • 4 char-grilled figs, quartered
  • olive oil
  • sea salt

Method

  1. On the end of each ham slice, place a piece of mozzarella, basil leaf and a fig quarter.

  2. Drizzle with olive oil and season with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Roll up and secure with cocktail stick.

  4. Refrigerate until required.

Steamed chilli soy tofu

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This low-fat, high-protein recipe is very filling and ready in less than 30 minutes.

By Jill Dupleix
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 250g/9oz silken tofu
  • 1 small red chilli, sliced finely
  • 2 spring onions, trimmed and sliced finely
  • 1 tbsp reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp oyster sauce (or vegetarian oyster sauce, if preferred)
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander
  • steamed rice, to serve

Method

  1. Fill the base of your steamer with water and bring to the boil. Drain the tofu and place it on a heatproof plate that will fit inside the steamer. Steam the tofu for 15 minutes, then drain off the excess water and carefully transfer to a serving plate.

  2. Heat the soy sauce, sesame oil and oyster sauce together in a small pan until hot. Pour the mixture over the tofu, scatter with the red chilli, spring onion and coriander leaves and serve with steamed rice alongside.

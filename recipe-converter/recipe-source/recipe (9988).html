Sweet and sour cabbage soup with heather honey

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 small potato, peeled, chopped
  • 250ml/9fl oz hot vegetable stock
  • ½ green cabbage, shredded
  • 1 glass white wine
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • white wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp heather honey

Method

  1. Place the chopped potato and the vegetable stock into a large saucepan, bring to the boil and simmer for 3-5 minutes.

  2. Add the cabbage and white wine to the pan and cook for a further 3-5 minutes, then add the cumin seeds and white wine vinegar and cook for another 6-7 minutes, or until the cabbage is tender but still retains some bite.

  3. To serve, pour the soup into a serving bowl and drizzle with the heather honey.

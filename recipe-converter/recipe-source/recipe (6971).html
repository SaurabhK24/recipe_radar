Poached pear in red wine

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 150ml/5fl oz red wine
  • 110g/4oz caster sugar
  • 1 pear, peeled
  • Greek-style yoghurt, to serve

Method

  1. Heat the red wine and sugar in a pan until just simmering.

  2. Add the pear and poach gently for up to 20 minutes or longer if time allows, until the pear is soft. Remove pear.

  3. To serve, reduce the poaching liquor until slightly thickened to form a sauce. Spoon the sauce into a serving bowl and stand the pear upright on it.

  4. Spoon the Greek-style yoghurt sauce alongside to serve.

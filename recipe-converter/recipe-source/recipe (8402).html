Sangria

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Recommended by

16 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Mix an ice-cold jug of this traditional Spanish sangria recipe: a perfect cocktail for hot summer days.

By Jill Dupleix

Ingredients

  • 3 parts red wine
  • 1 part orange juice
  • 2 parts lemonade
  • ice
  • fresh mint
  • sliced fruit such as oranges and lemons

Method

  1. Mix all the ingredients together in a large jug and add more red wine, orange juice or lemonade according to taste and desired strength.

  2. Pour into glasses, garnish with mint sprigs and fruit.

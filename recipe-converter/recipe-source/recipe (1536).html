Chargrilled apple and cheese

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 apple, cut in half and core removed
  • 50g/1¾oz cheddar, cut into 2 chunks
  • 2 tsp brown sugar
  • 25g/1oz finely chopped apple
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Heat an ovenproof griddle pan until smoking. Add the apple, flesh-side down, and chargrill for two minutes.

  3. Turn the apples over and place a piece of the cheese into the hollowed core of each apple half.

  4. Sprinkle the sugar evenly over each apple half and transfer to the oven to bake for ten minutes, or until golden.

  5. To serve, place the apples into the centre of a warm plate, sprinkle over the finely chopped apple and drizzle with the cream.

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 6

Recommended by

5 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp French mustard
  • pinch of caster sugar
  • salt and pepper

Method

  1. Put all the ingredients into a clean jar with a secure lid. Shake well to emulsify the dressing and serve. If the dressing separates, just give it another shake.

  2. Add a crushed clove of garlic or 1 tbsp fresh herbs for variety.

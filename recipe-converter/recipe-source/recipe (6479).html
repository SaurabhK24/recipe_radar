Parmesan cups with tomato and tapenade

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Makes 4

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 55g/2oz parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ready-made tapenade
  • handful fresh basil leaves, chopped

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Place four chefs' rings on a baking sheet and fill with a handful of parmesan.

  3. Bake in the oven for five minutes or until golden. Remove from the oven and while warm and pliable, shape around the top of a rolling pin to form a cup.

  4. Mix the tomato, tapenade and basil together and spoon the mixture into each parmesan crisp cup.

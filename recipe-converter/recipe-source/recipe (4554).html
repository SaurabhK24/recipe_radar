Hot cherries in Kirsch

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 miniature Kirsch
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1 x 400g/14oz can of cherries

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.

  2. Heat the Kirsch and sugar in an ovenproof frying pan until the sugar has dissolved.

  3. Add the cherries and then transfer to the oven and bake for 5-6 minutes.

  4. Pour into a bowl and serve.

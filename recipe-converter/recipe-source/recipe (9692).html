Stilton beef medallions with red wine sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • ½ packet ready-rolled puff pastry
  • 1 free-range egg, beaten

For the beef

  • 2 75g/2¾oz beef fillet medallions
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the red wine sauce

  • 55ml/2fl oz red wine
  • 1 banana shallot, finely chopped
  • 1 Romero pepper, finely diced
  • 1 tbsp chives, finely chopped
  • 55ml/2fl oz beef stock
  • 25g/1oz unsalted butter

For the Stilton sauce

  • 100g/3½oz Stilton
  • 100ml/3½fl oz double cream

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Using a 7½cm/3in chefs' ring, cut two rounds from the ready-rolled puff pastry.

  3. Place the puff pastry rounds onto a baking sheet. Brush the pastry with the beaten egg and bake in the preheated oven for ten minutes, or until risen and golden. Remove from the oven and set aside.

  4. For the beef, place a medium frying pan over a high heat. Rub both beef medallions with olive oil and season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Place in the hot frying pan and fry for one minute on each side. Remove from the heat and transfer to a warm plate to rest.

  5. For the red wine sauce, deglaze the steak pan by placing over a medium heat and adding the red wine, stirring well and scraping the bottom to release the sediment on the pan for one minute.

  6. Add the shallot, red pepper, chives and beef stock, bring to the boil and simmer for two minutes.

  7. Add the butter and whisk to melt into the sauce. Season to taste with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  8. To make the Stilton sauce, place the cream and cheese into a small saucepan over a medium heat. Stir until the cheese has melted and the mixture has thickened. Remove from the heat and spoon a small amount onto the steaks.

  9. To serve, remove the pastry rounds from the oven and place the steaks on top of them. Spoon the red wine sauce around.

Citrus cheesecake with sweet pastry top and warm honeyed mandarins

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the cheesecake

  • flour, for dusting
  • ¼ x 425g/15oz packet ready-rolled puff pastry
  • 1 free-range egg, beaten
  • 100g/3½oz cream cheese
  • 100ml/3½fl oz double cream, whipped
  • 2 mandarins, zest only
  • 2 tbsp icing sugar

For the honeyed mandarins

  • 2 mandarins, segmented
  • 3 tbsp honey

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. For the cheesecake, roll the pastry out thinly on a lightly floured surface and cut out a circle of pastry using a 7cm/2¾in round chefs' ring.

  3. Place the pastry circle onto a baking sheet and brush with the beaten egg. Transfer to the oven and bake for 8-10 minutes, or until lightly golden-brown.

  4. In a bowl, mix together the cream cheese, double cream, mandarin zest and icing sugar.

  5. Place a 7cm/2¾in chefs' ring onto a plate and spoon the cream cheese mixture into the ring. Place into the fridge to chill for 30 minutes.

  6. For the honeyed mandarins, place the mandarin segments into a pan with the honey and gently simmer until softened and glazed.

  7. To serve, remove the chefs' ring from the cheesecake and top with the puff pastry circle. Spoon the honeyed mandarins around the plate.

Caramelised pastry straws with sweet rhubarb ketchup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the pastry straws

  • 150g/5½oz ready-rolled puff pastry
  • 1 free-range egg, beaten
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar
  • icing sugar, to dust

For the ketchup

  • 100g/3½oz rhubarb, chopped
  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar
  • 2 tsp red wine vinegar

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. For the pastry straws, place the pastry onto a baking sheet, prick all over with a fork and bake in the oven for five minutes.

  3. Remove from the oven, brush with beaten egg and sprinkle over the caster sugar. Return to the oven and bake for 6-7 minutes, or until golden-brown.

  4. Remove from the oven, dust with icing sugar and cut into straws or long triangles.

  5. For the rhubarb ketchup, place all the ketchup ingredients into a small pan and cook over a medium heat for 6-8 minutes, or until softened. Pour into a small bowl and leave to cool.

  6. To serve, place the pastry straws onto a plate with the rhubarb ketchup alongside.

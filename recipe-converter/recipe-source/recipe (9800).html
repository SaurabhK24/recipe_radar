Strawberry mousse

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100ml/½fl oz double cream, whipped until soft peaks appear when the whisk is removed
  • 100gl/3½oz cream cheese
  • 1 tbsp icing sugar
  • 50g/1¾oz strawberries, cleaned, stalk removed, chopped
  • 25g/1oz dark chocolate, melted
  • icing sugar, for dusting

Method

  1. Place all of the ingredients, except the icing sugar, into a bowl and mix together well.

  2. Place a 3in/7½cm chefs' ring onto a serving plate and spoon in the mixture.

  3. To serve, remove the ring and sprinkle over the icing sugar.

Venison kebabs

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 3 venison sausages, skin removed
  • 6 prunes
  • 3 wooden skewers
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Start by rolling the venison meat into nine small balls.

  3. Take a skewer and alternately place a venison ball then a prune; each skewer should have three venison balls and two prunes.

  4. Heat a frying pan add the oil and gently brown the kebabs.

  5. Place into the preheated oven for six minutes and serve.

Strawberries with quick vanilla sugar

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 225g vanilla sugar

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Saturday Brunch

Ingredients

  • 1 vanilla pod
  • 225g/8oz sugar
  • strawberries, to serve

Method

  1. Place the vanilla pod and sugar into a blender and blend until smooth.

  2. Sieve, and blend again.

  3. Repeat the process until the mixture is very fine.

  4. Transfer to a sealable container and it will keep as long as you like.

  5. To serve with strawberries, sprinkle the vanilla sugar over the strawberries and allow to rest for 15 minutes before serving.

Raspberry and almond slice

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 8

Recommended by

4 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Saturday Brunch

Ingredients

  • 500g/1lb 1½oz shortcrust pastry
  • 50g/1¾oz butter
  • 50g/1¾oz caster sugar
  • 1 small egg
  • 125g/4½oz ground almonds
  • ¼ tsp vanilla extract
  • 200g/7¼oz raspberries
  • 1 small orange, zest only
  • 25g/1oz whole blanched almonds, crushed

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Roll out the pastry to a 12in/30cm x 8in/20cm rectangle and transfer to a greased baking sheet. Prick with a fork and bake for five minutes.

  3. Beat the butter and caster sugar until pale and fluffy. Stir in the egg, ground almonds and vanilla extract to make a stiff paste.

  4. Spread the almond paste on top of the pastry leaving a border of pastry on the outside.

  5. Combine the raspberries and orange zest. Scatter over the paste and push down slightly.

  6. Scatter over the crushed almonds.

  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes until puffed and golden.

  8. Remove from the oven and gently transfer to a wire rack to cool. Slice and serve.

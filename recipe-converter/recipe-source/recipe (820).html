Between the sheets

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Seduce the taste buds with a 1940 twist on a classic sidecar recipe.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 25ml/1fl oz cognac
  • 25ml/1fl oz white rum
  • 25ml/1fl oz orange liqueur
  • 25ml/1fl oz fresh lemon
  • 1 tsp sugar syrup

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.

  2. Strain into a martini glass and serve.

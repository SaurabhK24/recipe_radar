Stilton with raisin purée

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the raisin purée

  • 100g/3½ oz raisins
  • 100ml/3½fl oz white wine
  • 4 tbsp brown sugar

To serve

  • 50g/ 1¾ oz Stilton, at room temperature
  • 1 loaf of crusty bread

Method

  1. For the raisin purée, place the raisins, white wine and sugar in a medium saucepan over a medium heat and let it simmer for eight minutes, until thickened.

  2. Blend the raisin mixture in a blender and let it cool.

  3. To serve, place the purée on a plate with the cheese and serve with crusty bread.

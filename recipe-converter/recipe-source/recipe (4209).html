Guacamole with griddled pitta bread

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2-3

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the guacamole

  • ½ avocado, peeled, chopped
  • 2 tbsp plain yoghurt
  • squeeze lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

To serve

  • 6 ready-made mini pittas, split
  • 3 sun-blushed tomatoes
  • ½ avocado, sliced, to garnish

Method

  1. For the guacamole, place all of the guacamole ingredients into a blender and pulse until combined but still slightly chunky.

  2. Heat a griddle pan and cook the pittas for 1-2 minutes on each side, until warmed through.

  3. To serve, place the guacamole into a serving bowl. Top with the sun-blushed tomatoes and sliced avocado and place the pitta breads alongside.

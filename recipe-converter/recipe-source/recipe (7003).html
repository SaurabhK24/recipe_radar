Pomegranate Christmas cocktail

You need JavaScript to view this clip.

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Add a touch of sparkle to your party with a refreshing pomegranate and ginger cocktail using that very Italian ingredient, vermouth.

By Nigella Lawson
From Nigellissima

Ingredients

  • 60ml/2fl oz pink vermouth
  • 30ml/1fl oz pomegranate juice
  • 30ml/1fl oz ginger ale
  • ice, to serve

Method

  1. Stir together the vermouth and pomegranate juice. Pour into martini glasses, adding an ice cube if you wish.

  2. Top up with ginger ale and serve.

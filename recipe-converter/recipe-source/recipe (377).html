Baked blackcurrant rice

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Great for cooking with kids under five, this fruity purple rice pudding is lovely served with a shortbread biscuit.

Ingredients

  • 250ml/8fl oz apple juice
  • 50g/2oz arborio rice
  • 40g/1½oz fresh blackcurrants
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract

Method

  1. Before cooking, wash your hands and put an apron on. Weigh or measure all the ingredients. Ask a grown-up to preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.

  2. Pour the juice into the ovenproof dish, then add the rice.

  3. Prepare the blackcurrants by pinching out any woody stalks before adding them to the ovenproof dish.

  4. Add the sugar and the vanilla extract. Give the mixture a stir and put on the lid. Place on a baking tray.

  5. You’ll need to ask a grown-up to help with this part. Cook for 20 minutes then leave to stand for at least 10 minutes with the lid on (the rice will carry on cooking). When the rice has cooled a bit, you can eat it!

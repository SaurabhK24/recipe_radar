Spiced oat cookies

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 12

Recommended by

15 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

At last! A sugar-free cookie! Tender, oaty and gently spiced, you'll never miss the sugar.

Each cookie provides 82 kcal, 1g protein, 7g carbohydrate (of which 0.5g sugars), 5.5g fat (of which 1g saturates), 1g fibre and 0.4g salt.

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 50g/2oz porridge oats
  • 75g/3oz wholemeal flour, plus a little extra
  • pinch fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • ½ tsp ground mixed spice
  • 75g/3oz baking spread, plus extra for greasing
  • 50g/2oz pear or apple spread/purée

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 190C/375F/Gas 5 and grease and flour two baking sheets.

  2. Mix together the oats, flour, salt, bicarbonate of soda and mixed spice.

  3. Cream the margarine in a bowl and beat in the pear and apple spread/purée, a little at a time. Beat in the oat mixture.

  4. Place walnut sized portions of the mixture on a baking sheet, leaving a space of about 5cm/2in round each one. Flatten them slightly, with a fork.

  5. Bake the biscuits for 15 minutes or until they are beginning to turn golden. Leave the biscuits on the baking trays until they are cool and firm.

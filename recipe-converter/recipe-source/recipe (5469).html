Barbecued marinated chicken breast with lime, coriander, ginger and chilli

Preparation time

over 2 hours

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

2 people

By Michael Caines
From Countryfile Summer Diaries

Ingredients

For the marinade

  • small bunch fresh coriander, chopped
  • 100ml/3½fl oz olive oil
  • 2 limes, zest of one only and juice of both
  • 20g/¾oz grated ginger
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 fresh chilli, chopped
  • smoked cayenne pepper, to taste (if unavailable, substitute smoked hot paprika)
  • 4 chicken breasts

Method

  1. For the marinade, place the chopped coriander, olive oil, lime zest and juice, ginger, garlic and chili into a bowl.

  2. Season, to taste, with cayenne pepper and whisk thoroughly.

  3. Place the chicken breasts into a deep dish. Pour the marinade over the chicken, covering completely. Cover the dish and leave the chicken to marinate for three hours.

  4. Preheat the barbecue until the coals are white hot.

  5. Place the chicken breasts on the barbecue and cook for 15-25 minutes, or until cooked all the way through, turning occasionally.

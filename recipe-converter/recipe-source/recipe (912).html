Blinis with poached egg and hollandaise

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 4 ready-made blinis

For the hollandaise

  • 2 free-range egg yolks
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • ½ tsp white wine vinegar
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 250g/9oz butter, melted in a pan
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream
  • 50ml/2fl oz hot water

For the poached eggs

  • 500ml/18fl oz boiling water
  • 50ml/2fl oz white wine vinegar
  • 2 free-range eggs at room temperature
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped radish, to garnish

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Place the blinis on a baking tray and bake for eight minutes in the oven.

  3. To make the hollandaise, place the egg yolks, lemon juice and vinegar in a food processor. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and blend together.

  4. Very slowly add the melted butter while blending.

  5. Slowly add the cream then the hot water in the same way.

  6. To make the poached eggs, simmer the water and vinegar in a saucepan. Swirl the water to create a whirlpool, then gently add the eggs and poach for three minutes. Remove with a slotted spoon.

  7. Serve two piles of blinis with a poached egg on each and some of the hollandaise spooned over. Garnish with chopped radish.

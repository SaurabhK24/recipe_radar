Griddled mango with chilli and rum and brazil nut praline

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the griddled mango

  • ½ mango, stone removed, skin left on
  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp soft brown sugar
  • ½ red chilli, seeds removed, chopped
  • dash rum

For the brazil nut praline

  • 100g/3½oz caster sugar
  • 50g/1¾oz brazil nuts, chopped
  • 1 tbsp rum
  • 2 tsp vegetable oil

Method

  1. Preheat the grill to its highest setting.

  2. For the griddled mango, score the mango flesh with a sharp knife to make diamond patterns on the surface.

  3. Heat an ovenproof griddle pan until hot, rub the flesh of the mango with the vegetable oil and griddle flesh-side down for two minutes.

  4. Turn the mango over, sprinkle with brown sugar and chilli and griddle for a further 2-3 minutes. Add the rum to the griddle pan and flambé for one minute.

  5. Place the pan under the grill for 2-3 minutes, until the mango is golden-brown and caramelised.

  6. For the praline, gently warm the sugar in a frying pan until the sugar crystals have melted and caramelised.

  7. Add the brazil nuts, stir well to coat the nuts and cook for 1-2 minutes. Add the rum and mix well.

  8. Pour the mixture onto a non-stick baking sheet greased with a little vegetable oil. Leave to cool for five minutes, then break into pieces.

  9. To serve, place the mango onto a plate and sprinkle with the brazil nut praline.

Tomato mozzarella salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 8 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 thick slice mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

To serve

  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh basil

Method

  1. Place the cherry tomatoes onto a plate.

  2. Heat the oil in frying pan and when hot, add the mozzarella and fry for one minute on each side, until golden.

  3. To serve, place the mozzarella on top of the tomatoes and sprinkle with basil.

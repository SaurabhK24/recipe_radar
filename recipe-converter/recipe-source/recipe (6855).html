Pineapple traybake

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 110g/4oz self-raising flour
  • 110g/4oz brown sugar
  • 110g/4oz butter
  • 4 free-range eggs
  • 1 baby pineapple, peeled and sliced
  • drizzle of honey

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/428F/Gas 7.

  2. Line a rimmed baking tray with baking parchment.

  3. Mix the flour, sugar and butter in a food processor until combined. Add the eggs, one at a time, until the batter is smooth.

  4. Lay the pineapple slices on the baking tray and drizzle with honey.

  5. Pour the batter over the top and level out.

  6. Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes or until cooked through and springy to the touch.

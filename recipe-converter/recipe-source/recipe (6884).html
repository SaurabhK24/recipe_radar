Pistachio syllabub

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 75ml/3fl oz whipped cream
  • 75ml/3fl oz mascarpone
  • 75g/3oz pistachio nuts, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp icing sugar
  • 1 orange, juice and zest only
  • 3 ready-made sponge fingers, to serve

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients except the sponge fingers into a bowl and mix together.

  2. Spoon the mixture into a tall glass on a plate with the sponge fingers served alongside.

Spaghetti all'arrabbiata

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • dash olive oil
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • ¼ leek, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 beef tomato, core removed, roughly chopped
  • pinch fried chilli flakes
  • 1 courgette, cut into ribbons
  • handful fresh basil, chopped, plus extra for garnish
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 200g/7oz fresh spaghetti, cooked
  • parmesan cheese, or other vegetarian grating cheese, grated, to serve

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan, add the onion and leek and cook over a gentle heat for 4-5 minutes, or until softened. Add the garlic and cook for one minute.

  2. Add the tomato and dried chilli flakes and cook for two minutes. Add the courgette and basil and season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. To serve, stir the sauce into the cooked, drained pasta and spoon onto a serving plate or bowl. Scatter over the grated parmesan cheese and basil leaves and serve immediately.

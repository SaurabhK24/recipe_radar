Chicken with marsala cream and mushrooms

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Gino D'Acampo
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 small handful of button mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 5 tbsp of marsala wine
  • 3 tbsp double cream
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

To serve

  • rice, cooked

Method

  1. Flatten the chicken breast with a rolling pin or meat mallet between two sheets of cling film. Place the chicken and mushrooms in the hot oil and cook for three minutes each side.

  2. Add the marsala wine and allow to flame. As the sauce flames, remove the chicken from the pan and blow out the flame.

  3. Add the cream and salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Serve with rice.

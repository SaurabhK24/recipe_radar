Shirley Temple

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Andy Pearson
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 200ml/7fl oz ginger ale
  • splash grenadine
  • 1 maraschino cherry

Method

  1. Pour the ginger ale into a highball glass and pour in the grenadine.

  2. Add a maraschino cherry and serve.

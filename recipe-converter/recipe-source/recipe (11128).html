Yorkshire oatmeal parkin

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Makes 1 large cake

Recommended by

3 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Make this authentic Yorkshire parkin a day or two ahead for a sticky teatime treat that can’t be beaten.

By Stephen Taylor
From Country Show Cook Off

Ingredients

  • 250g/9oz butter or margarine
  • 250g/9oz golden syrup
  • 125g/4½oz black treacle
  • 500g/1lb 2oz muscavado sugar
  • 375g/13oz plain flour
  • 1½ tsp ground ginger
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 500g/1lb 2oz oatmeal
  • ½ pint milk

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4. Line a 15x20cm/6x8in baking tin with greaseproof paper.

  2. In a pan, gently melt the butter (or margarine), golden syrup, black treacle and sugar until combined.

  3. Mix the remaining dry ingredients together in a large bowl.

  4. Add the hot syrup mixture and stir to combine. Add the milk to help loosen the mixture.

  5. Pour into the prepared tin and bake for 35-45 minutes, or until springy to the touch.

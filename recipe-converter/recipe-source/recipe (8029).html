Roasted sea bass with pepper, chilli and basil cream

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • ¼ red pepper, chopped
  • ¼ onion, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed to a paste
  • pinch chilli flakes
  • 100ml/3½fl oz white wine
  • 100ml/3½fl oz double cream
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 5 fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 1 sea bass fillet

Method

  1. Heat half of the oil in a pan and gently fry the pepper, onion and garlic for 3-4 minutes, or until softened, then stir in the chilli flakes.

  2. Pour in the white wine and simmer for 8-10 minutes, or until the liquid has reduced by half, then stir in the cream and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Simmer gently for 1-2 minutes, then stir in the basil.

  3. Heat the remaining oil in a pan, season the fish with salt and freshly ground black pepper and fry, skin-side down, for 2-3 minutes, or until golden-brown and crisp. Turn the fish over and fry for a further 1-2 minutes, or until the fish is golden-brown all over and cooked through.

  4. To serve, spoon the pepper, chilli and basil cream onto a serving plate and place the fish on top.

Steak, egg and chips

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the chips

  • vegetable oil, for deep-frying
  • 1 potato, peeled and cut into thick chips

For the steak and egg

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 rump steak
  • 1 free-range egg

Method

  1. Heat a heavy bottomed saucepan, one-third full of vegetable oil, until a breadcrumb sizzles in it. (CAUTION: hot oil can be dangerous! Do not leave unattended.)

  2. Add the potato chips and deep-fry for 8-10 minutes, until crisp and golden. Carefully remove using a slotted spoon and drain on kitchen towels.

  3. For the steak, place the steak in between two sheets of cling film. Using a meat mallet or rolling pin, bash to flatten.

  4. Heat the oil in a non stick-frying pan. Add the steak and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, for medium, or longer, according to personal taste. Remove the steak from the pan and allow to rest for a few minutes.

  5. Using the same pan the steak was cooked in, crack the egg in and fry for 2 minutes, until the yolk is set.

  6. To serve, pile the chips up onto a serving plate and serve the steak and fried egg alongside.

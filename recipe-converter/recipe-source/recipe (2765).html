Creamed leeks with pork and new potatoes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 75g/2¾oz pork loin
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the creamed leeks

  • 50g/1¾oz butter
  • 4 leeks, chopped and blanched
  • 3 chard stalks, cut from the leaves, finely chopped
  • 250ml/9fl oz double cream
  • 1 tbsp fresh sage, chopped

To serve

  • new potatoes, boiled until soft

Method

  1. For the pork, heat the oil in a frying pan. Add the pork and cook for five minutes on each side, until cooked through.

  2. For the creamed leeks, melt the butter in a hot pan. Add the leeks and chard and sauté for three minutes.

  3. Add the double cream and sage, warm through and season to taste with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  4. To serve, place the creamed leeks onto the centre of a warmed plate and top with the pork. Arrange the new potatoes around the plate.

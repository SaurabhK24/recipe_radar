Red Leicester soufflé

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 200g/7oz Red Leicester, grated
  • 4 free-range eggs, separated
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika, for dusting

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Mix the grated cheese and egg yolks in a mixing bowl.

  3. In a clean mixing bowl, whisk the egg whites until stiff peaks form when the whisk is removed.

  4. Fold the egg whites into the cheese mixture.

  5. Heat the olive oil in a small non-stick frying pan and add the egg mixture.

  6. Cook in the oven for 10-12 minutes until risen and golden.

  7. To serve the soufflé, place it on a white plate and dust with paprika.

Blue cheese and honey bruschetta

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1-2

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 slices white bread
  • 85g/3oz Lanark Blue cheese, or other blue cheese
  • 3 tsp clear honey

To serve

  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh chives

Method

  1. Heat a griddle pan until hot. Drizzle the oil over the slices of bread and griddle on both sides until golden-brown. Remove from the pan and place onto a serving plate.

  2. Crumble the cheese over each slice of bread and drizzle over the honey. Serve garnished with chopped parsley and chives.

Crisp pancetta and herb salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 rashers pancetta, chopped
  • large handful fresh herbs, such as parsley, basil, mint, coriander
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Heat half the olive oil in a frying pan and fry the pancetta pieces until crisp and golden-brown. Remove from the pan and drain on kitchen paper.

  2. Place the herbs into a bowl and drizzle over the vinegar and the remaining olive oil. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and stir to coat the herbs.

  3. To serve, sprinkle the crisp pancetta over the salad.

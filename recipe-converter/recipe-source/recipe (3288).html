Eggnog

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 6

Recommended by

4 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This can be made up to 4 days in advance of drinking, and stored in the fridge. It can be served hot or cold.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 4 free-range eggs, beaten
  • 4 tbsp caster sugar
  • 3 bruised bay leaves
  • 1 tsp ground allspice
  • 50ml/2fl oz vanilla syrup
  • 100ml/3½oz golden rum
  • 100ml/3½oz cognac
  • 300ml10½ fl oz full-fat milk
  • freshly grated nutmeg, to garnish

Method

  1. Beat the eggs together in a bowl with the caster sugar.

  2. Add the remaining ingredients and whisk until combined. Cover with clingfilm and leave to rest for half an hour.

  3. Gently whisk again before straining into a bottle.

  4. Chill in the fridge for up to four days.

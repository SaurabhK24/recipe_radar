Vodka Martini

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Channel James Bond with a suave and debonair vodka Martini – the killer cocktail for parties, it’s licensed to thrill.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 15ml/½fl oz vermouth
  • 60ml/2fl oz Tyrrells potato vodka, or other potato vodka
  • twist lemon peel, to garnish

Method

  1. Pour the vermouth and vodka into a mixing glass and fill with ice cubes. Stir well and then strain into a small Martini glass.

  2. Garnish with a twist of lemon peel and serve.

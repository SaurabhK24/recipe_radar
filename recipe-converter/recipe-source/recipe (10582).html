Tuna melt

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 50g/1¾oz tinned tuna, drained
  • 50g/1¾oz cherry tomatoes, chopped
  • 15g/½oz fresh parsley, chopped
  • 25g/1oz jalapeno peppers
  • 20cm/8in slice baguette, cut in half lengthways
  • 25g/1oz cheddar cheese, grated
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Place the tuna, cherry tomatoes, parsley and jalapeno peppers into a bowl and mix well.

  3. Spoon the tuna mixture onto the baguette then sprinkle over with grated cheddar and salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste.

  4. Place onto a baking sheet then into the oven to bake for 5-6 minutes, or until the cheese has melted.

  5. To serve, slice in half and place on a warm plate.

Sweet plums in red wine

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Ross Burden
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 150g/5oz butter
  • 4 plums
  • 100ml/3½fl oz red wine
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 cardamom pods, crushed

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a small frying pan over a medium heat.

  2. Add the remaining ingredients and cook over a high heat for six minutes, stirring frequently.

  3. Serve in warm bowls.

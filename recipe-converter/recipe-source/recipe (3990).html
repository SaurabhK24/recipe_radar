Gratin of blackberries with zabaglione

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 free-range egg yolks
  • 2 tbsp white wine
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • 85g/3oz blackberries

Method

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-hot.

  2. Stir together the egg yolks, wine and sugar in a heatproof bowl set over a pan of simmering water (do not allow the bottom of the bowl to touch the water).

  3. Whisk the mixture in the bowl using an electric whisk until thick and doubled in volume.

  4. Place the blackberries in an ovenproof gratin dish and pour the zabaglione mixture over the top. Grill for 4-5 minutes, or until the top of the mixture bubbles and turns golden-brown. Serve immediately.

Kumquat caipirinha

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 6 kumquats, halved
  • 2 tsp sugar syrup
  • 35ml/1fl oz cachaça
  • 15ml/½fl oz dry orange curaçao
  • 2 tsp fresh lime juice

To garnish

  • ½ kumquat

Method

  1. Place the ingredients in a long glass and crush with the end of a rolling pin.

  2. Add some crushed ice and swizzle with a long spoon to mix

  3. Garnish with half a kumquat and serve

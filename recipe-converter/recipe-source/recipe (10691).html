Vanilla and golden syrup rice pudding with glazed nectarines

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1-2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the rice pudding

  • 300ml/10fl oz whole milk
  • 100ml/3½fl oz double cream
  • 1 tbsp golden syrup
  • 1 vanilla pod, seeds only
  • 1 orange, zest only
  • 200g/7oz long grain rice

For the glazed nectarines

  • ½ nectarine, sliced
  • 1 tbsp icing sugar
  • 1 sprig fresh mint, to garnish

Method

  1. For the rice pudding, heat the milk, cream, golden syrup, vanilla seeds and orange zest in a saucepan and bring to simmering point. Add the rice and simmer until tender, about ten minutes.

  2. For the glazed nectarines, place the nectarine slices onto a baking sheet and dredge with the icing sugar. Using a mini-blowtorch, caramelise the sugar until golden-brown.

  3. Serve the rice pudding in a serving dish with the glazed nectarines on top. Garnish with a sprig of fresh mint.

Stilton and cider fondue

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the fondue

  • 200ml/7fl oz cider
  • 150g/5½oz Stilton cheese, chopped
  • 2 tsp cornflour
  • 1 tsp cold water
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh chives

For the croûtes

  • 1 slice thick white bread, cut into large chunks
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. For the fondue, pour the cider into a pan and cook over a high heat until reduced by a third. Stir in the Stilton, whisking until completely melted.

  2. In a small bowl, mix the cornflour with the cold water into a paste and stir into the cider and Stilton mixture. Stir in the fresh chives just before serving.

  3. For the croûtes, drizzle the bread chunks with the oil and fry in a pan for 4-5 minutes, or until golden-brown and crisp.

  4. To serve, transfer the fondue to a large serving bowl set on a large plate, arranging the croûtes around the edges of the plate.

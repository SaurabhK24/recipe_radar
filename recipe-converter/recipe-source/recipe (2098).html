Chocolate and cherry slice

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 8-12

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This decadent chocolate slice makes a great treat for a picnic box: serve with clotted cream and fresh raspberries.

By Ed Baines
From Country Show Cook Off

Ingredients

  • 300g/10½oz dark chocolate
  • 225g/8oz unsalted butter
  • 3 free-range eggs
  • 225g/8oz light brown sugar
  • 75g/2½oz self-raising flour, sifted
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 175g/6oz shelled and skinned chopped walnuts
  • pinch salt
  • about 100g/3½oz glacé cherries, chopped
  • 200g/7oz milk chocolate, chopped

To serve

  • clotted cream
  • raspberries
  • few sprigs fresh mint
  • icing sugar

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 190C/375F/Gas 5 and grease and line a 20cm/8in square baking tin with baking parchment.

  2. Melt the dark chocolate and butter together in a heatproof bowl set over a pan of simmering water (don't let the bottom of the bowl touch the water). Remove from the heat and set aside to cool slightly.

  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk the eggs until light, then beat in the sugar until fluffy.

  4. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, baking powder and walnuts.

  5. Stir the melted chocolate mixture into the eggs with a pinch of salt. Stir in the flour mixture, milk chocolate and cherries.

  6. Pour into the prepared tin and bake in the oven for 40 minutes.

  7. Remove from the oven and leave to cool in the tin.

  8. Cut into squares and serve with clotted cream, raspberries, fresh mint leaves and a dusting of icing sugar.

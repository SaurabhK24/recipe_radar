Deep-fried tortilla with lemon dip

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • vegetable oil, for deep frying
  • 2 flour tortillas, cut into small triangles
  • 200g/7¼oz Greek yoghurt
  • handful of parsley, chopped
  • squeeze of lemon juice

Method

  1. Fill a pan one-third-full with vegetable oil and heat to 180C/350F. The oil is ready when you drop a breadcrumb in and it sizzles. (CAUTION: hot oil is dangerous. Do not leave unattended.)

  2. Deep fry the tortilla triangles in the hot oil for 1-2 minutes, until crisp and golden. Remove using a slotted spoon and drain on kitchen paper.

  3. For the dip, mix the yoghurt, parsley and lemon juice in a bowl to combine.

  4. To serve, arrange the tortillas on a serving plate and serve the lemon dip on the side.

Roast mutton on pear, Lancashire cheese and lamb's lettuce salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 100g/3½oz mutton fillet
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ pear, sliced
  • handful lamb's lettuce
  • 50g/2oz Lancashire cheese, crumbled

For the dressing

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • 1 tsp honey

Method

  1. Heat one tablespoon of olive oil in a frying pan. Season the mutton fillet with salt and freshly ground black pepper and fry for 5-6 minutes, turning frequently, until cooked through. Remove from the pan and set aside.

  2. Heat the remaining tablespoon of oil in the pan and gently fry the pear slices until they start to caramelise.

  3. For the dressing, whisk all the dressing ingredients together in a bowl. Pour over the lamb's lettuce in a bowl and stir well to dress all the leaves.

  4. To serve, place the dressed leaves onto a serving plate and arrange the pear slices on top. Slice the mutton fillet and arrange on top. Scatter over the cheese.

Beetroot relish

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1-2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 4 cooked beetroot in vinegar, drained and chopped
  • small handful fresh chervil, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients into a bowl and mix well.

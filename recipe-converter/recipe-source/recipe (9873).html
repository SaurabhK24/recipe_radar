Stuffed prunes with Stilton

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 8 prunes, stones removed, split through the centre
  • 50g/2oz Stilton, crumbled

Method

  1. Stuff equal amounts of Stilton into each prune.

  2. To serve, place the stuffed prunes onto a serving plate.

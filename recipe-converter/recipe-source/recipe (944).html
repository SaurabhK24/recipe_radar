Blueberry muffin Martini

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Salvatore Calabrese
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 30ml/2fl oz vodka
  • 1 tbsp white creme de cacao
  • 1 tbsp blueberry liqueur
  • 20ml/½fl oz vanilla vodka
  • 10 fresh blueberries, plus extra to garnish

Method

  1. Place all of the ingredients into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice. Shake well, then strain into a Martini glass. Garnish with a few extra blueberries.

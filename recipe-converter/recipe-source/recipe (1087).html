Brandy snap baskets with orange cream

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 8

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Mary Berry
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 8 brandy snap baskets
  • 450ml/16fl oz double cream
  • zest of 1 orange
  • 2 tbsp of orange liqueur, such as Grand Marnier

Method

  1. Whip the double cream, add the orange zest and Grand Marnier and spoon into the baskets.

Vinaigrette potatoes with herbs

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Garrey Dawson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • dash Tabasco sauce
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh chives
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh flatleaf parsley
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh chervil
  • 1 potato, peeled, cut into cubes and boiled until tender

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients except the potato into a large bowl and mix well.

  2. Add the potato cubes to the bowl while still warm and stir well to coat with the dressing. Leave to marinate for ten minutes.

  3. To serve, transfer to a serving dish.

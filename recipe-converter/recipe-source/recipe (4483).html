Honeycomb fool

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz double cream
  • 100g/3½oz cream cheese
  • drop of vanilla essence
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar
  • 55g/2oz cinder toffee (also called honeycomb), crushed
  • ¼ mango, peeled and diced

To serve

  • 30g/1oz cinder toffee (also called honeycomb), crushed and toasted

Method

  1. Lightly whip the cream in a large bowl, to thicken. Fold in the cream cheese with the vanilla essence and sugar, to combine.

  2. Carefully fold in the honeycomb and mango.

  3. Spoon the mixture into two serving bowls or glasses.

  4. Sprinkle the toasted honeycomb over, to serve.

Carrot and watercress salad with tomatoes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots, peeled and grated
  • 1 handful watercress, roughly chopped
  • 3 mi-cuit (semi-dried) tomatoes, in oil, cut in half
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Mix the carrots, watercress and tomatoes together in a bowl.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the red wine vinegar and olive oil and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Drizzle over the salad and toss well to coat.

  3. Serve immediately.

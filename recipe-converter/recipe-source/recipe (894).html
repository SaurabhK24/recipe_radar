Black-eyed bean dip with garlic toasts

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Ross Burden
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the dip

  • 2 tsp sesame seeds, toasted
  • ¼ red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • ½ tin black-eyed beans, rinsed and drained
  • ½ lime, juice only
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
  • 100g/3½oz plain yoghurt

For the garlic toasts

  • 2 slices bread
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 3 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 35g/1½oz butter

Method

  1. For the dip, whiz the sesame seeds in a blender until finely ground. Mix with the remaining dip ingredients and set aside.

  2. For the garlic toasts, toast the bread in a pan with the garlic, butter and parsley for 2 minutes until golden. Serve with the dip.

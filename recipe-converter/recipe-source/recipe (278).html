Asparagus 'cappuccino' soup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 100g/3½oz asparagus, chopped
  • 250ml/9fl oz hot vegetable stock
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp double cream
  • 2 thin slices ciabatta, toasted, to serve

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a medium saucepan, add the onion and fry gently for five minutes, or until soft.

  2. Add the garlic and cook for one minute.

  3. Add the asparagus and cook for three minutes, or until softened.

  4. Add the stock, season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and bring to the boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for five minutes, then stir in the cream.

  5. Use a hand blender to blend the soup until smooth.

  6. To serve, pour the soup into a cappuccino cup and serve with toasted ciabatta.

Poached egg salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • large handful friseé lettuce
  • 4 slices ciabatta, toasted
  • 2 free-range eggs, out of their shells
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp white wine vinegar
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Bring a pan full of salted water to the boil.

  2. Give the water a stir and add one egg. Let it poach for 2-3 minutes or until the white is cooked and the middle is runny. Remove with a slotted spoon.

  3. Repeat with the other egg and set them both aside.

  4. Dress the lettuce with the oil and vinegar.

  5. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  6. To serve, put the slices of toast on the side of the plate and the eggs on top of the salad in the middle of the plate.

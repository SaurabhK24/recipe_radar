Stilton and herb-crusted haddock

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 175g/6oz fillet haddock
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 50g/1¾oz fresh breadcrumbs
  • 25g/1oz Stilton
  • handful fresh parsley, chopped
  • 75g/2½oz butter
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh dill
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • lemon wedges, to garnish

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Place the haddock fillet onto a baking sheet and spread the mustard over the fish.

  3. In a mini food processor, blend together the breadcrumbs, Stilton, parsley and 15g/½oz of butter and press onto the fish.

  4. Transfer the baking sheet to the oven and cook for 8-10 minutes, or until golden-brown and cooked through.

  5. Place the rest of the butter, along with the dill and lemon juice into a small saucepan and heat over a low heat until the butter has melted, then season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  6. To serve, place the fish onto a serving plate, with lemon wedges alongside, and pour the sauce over the fish.

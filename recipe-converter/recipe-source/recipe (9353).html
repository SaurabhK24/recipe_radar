Spicy clams

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Recommended by

1 person

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • ½ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 75ml/3fl oz white wine
  • 50ml/2fl oz hot water
  • 250g/9oz fresh clams, washed
  • 2 slices ciabatta, drizzled with olive oil and griddled, to serve
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley, to garnish

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a medium saucepan, add the garlic and shallot and sauté gently for two minutes or until soft but not browned.

  2. Add the chilli flakes, tomato purée, white wine and water, then stir well and bring to the boil.

  3. Add the clams, cover with a lid and cook for three minutes, shaking the pan frequently. Check all the clams have opened and discard any that are still closed.

  4. Pour into a bowl and serve with the griddled ciabatta on the side. Garnish with parsley.

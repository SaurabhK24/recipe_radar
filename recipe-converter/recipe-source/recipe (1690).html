Cheesy mash and spinach pudding

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 potato, cut into small cubes
  • handful spinach
  • 55g/2oz cheddar cheese, grated
  • knob of butter
  • 1 tbsp fresh horseradish, grated
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 225C/450F/Gas 7.

  2. Boil the potato in salted water for 5-10 minutes until soft, then drain.

  3. Steam the spinach for two minutes, then drain on kitchen paper.

  4. Grease the inside of a dariole mould with oil.

  5. Layer the spinach around the inside of the mould, so that the leaves hang over the edge.

  6. Mash the potato with the cheese, butter and horseradish and season to taste, with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  7. Fill the mould with the potato mixture and fold over the excess hanging spinach leaves and place in the oven to bake for ten minutes.

  8. To serve, carefully turn out the pudding onto a warm plate and remove the dariole mould.

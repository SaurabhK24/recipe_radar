Spiced berry whip

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100ml/3½oz spiced berry cordial
  • 250ml/9fl oz Greek yoghurt
  • ½ orange, zest only
  • 150ml/¼pt double cream
  • 2 free-range egg whites, whisked until soft peaks form
  • 1 ready-made meringue nest, crushed
  • sprig mint, to decorate
  • icing sugar, to dust

Method

  1. Pour the cordial into a tall glass.

  2. Place the yoghurt, orange zest, double cream, whisked egg whites and meringue into a bowl and mix together.

  3. Spoon the mixture into a piping bag. Dip the piping bag into the cordial at the bottom of the glass and squeeze out the cream mixture all the way up to the top.

  4. Garnish with mint and dust with the icing sugar.

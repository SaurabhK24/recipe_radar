Warm potato, mustard and dill salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 250g/9oz new potatoes
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • drizzle of olive oil
  • 2 tbsp dill, chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Place the new potatoes into a large saucepan of boiling salted water and boil for 15 minutes, or until just cooked through.

  2. Drain the potatoes and place into a large bowl with all of the other ingredients.

  3. Mix well and serve warm.

Carrot, horseradish and cheese salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 2 tbsp grated, fresh horseradish
  • 55g/2oz spinach, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 25g/1oz cheddar cheese, grated

Method

  1. In a bowl, mix together the carrot, horseradish and spinach.

  2. In a new bowl mix together the oil, mustard and vinegar and season, to taste, with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Pour the dressing over the salad mixture and toss well to coat.

  4. Sprinkle over the cheese and serve.

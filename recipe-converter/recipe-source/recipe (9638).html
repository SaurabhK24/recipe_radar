Stewed gooseberries with custard

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the stewed gooseberries

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice only
  • 75g/2¾oz caster sugar
  • 275g/9¾oz gooseberries

For the custard

  • 600ml/1 pint double cream
  • 50 ml/1¾fl oz milk
  • 1 vanilla pod, split
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • 3 free-range egg yolks
  • 1 free-range egg

Method

  1. For the stewed gooseberries, in a pan melt the butter then add the lemon zest and juice, sugar and gooseberries. Leave to simmer for 3-4 minutes.

  2. In a separate pan heat the cream and milk with the vanilla pod to boiling point.

  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and extra yolk with the caster sugar. Pour the hot cream into the eggs, stirring well.

  4. Return this to the pan and heat gently, stirring, until the custard coats the back of the spoon.

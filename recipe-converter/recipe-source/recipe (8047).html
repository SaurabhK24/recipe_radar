Roasted vegetables with mozzarella

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • ½ red onion, sliced into rings
  • ½ courgette, sliced thickly
  • 3 thick slices of tomato
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 2 slices of toast
  • 2 thick slices of mozzarella

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6 and preheat the grill to high.

  2. Place the red onion, courgette and tomato in a roasting tray. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and drizzle over the olive oil.

  3. Place into the oven for 8 minutes until the vegetables are soft and golden.

  4. Remove from the oven, then place the vegetables on the slices of toast. Top with the mozzarella slices, then place under the grill for 2 minutes until golden. Serve.

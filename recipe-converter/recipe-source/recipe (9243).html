Spiced cashews

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 75g/2½oz cashews
  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 1½ tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp soft brown sugar
  • pinch ground ginger
  • pinch ground allspice
  • pinch paprika

Method

  1. Put the cashews into a dry frying pan and fry over a low heat until slightly browned on all sides.

  2. Add the butter, Worcestershire sauce, sugar and spices and fry for 2-3 minutes.

  3. Pour into a small bowl and leave to cool slightly before serving.

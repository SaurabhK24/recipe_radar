Roasted cod fillet with mint and capers

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

By Mitch Tonks
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • vegetable oil, for frying
  • 2 thick pieces cod fillet weighing about 200g/7oz each
  • ½ tsp mustard
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • pinch sugar
  • 100ml/3½fl oz olive oil
  • squeeze lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp capers
  • 1 tbsp chopped gherkins
  • small handful mint, chopped
  • 1 tbsp chopped tarragon

Method

  1. Preheat an oven to 240C/475F/Gas 8.

  2. Heat some vegetable oil in a frying pan until hot, fry the cod flesh side down until lightly coloured.

  3. Transfer to a roasting dish and roast in the oven for a further five minutes.

  4. To make the dressing mix together the mustard, sugar and vinegar then slowly add the olive oil, when all mixed together add the remaining ingredients and spoon over the fish.

Pisellini al prosciutto (spring peas with ham)

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 6

Recommended by

1 person

By Anna Venturi
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 500g/1lb 2oz spring peas (petit pois)
  • 1½oz of butter or oil
  • ½ medium onion, chopped
  • 200g/7oz of prosciutto ham or pancetta, diced small
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan and add the chopped onion and prosciutto or pancetta.

  2. sauté for a few minutes until the onion turns golden and the ham is brown but not crisp.

  3. Add the peas, still frozen, and a little water. Stir.

  4. Cover and simmer gently until the peas are tender, about 15 minutes. Season to taste and serve.

Corn dogs

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Makes 15

Recommended by

10 people

Fly the flag for the all-American snack with this recipe for corn dogs (deep-fried frankfurters).

Equipment and preparation: You'll need 15 wooden skewers.

By Simon Rimmer
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • vegetable oil, for deep frying
  • 150g/5oz cornmeal or polenta
  • 125g/4oz plain flour
  • 50g/2oz sugar
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 free-range egg
  • 225ml/8fl oz full-fat milk
  • 12-15 frankfurter sausages
  • 12-15 wooden skewers, soaked

To serve

  • 6 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard

Method

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a deep-fat fryer to 160C/320F. (CAUTION: Hot oil can be dangerous. Do not leave unattended.)

  2. Mix together all of the dry ingredients in a bowl. In a separate bowl, beat the egg and milk together and gradually stir the mixture into to the dry ingredients. Whisk together to make a heavy batter.

  3. Put a skewer through each frankfurter sausage and dip it into the batter until well coated.

  4. Place the frankfurters into the deep-fat fryer, a few at a time, and cook for three minutes, or until golden-brown. Drain on a plate lined with kitchen paper.

  5. To serve, mix the mustard and mayonnaise together in a bowl and serve with the frankfurters.

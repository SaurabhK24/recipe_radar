Sausage and red wine casserole

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

14 people

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 Toulouse sausages, cut into 3 pieces
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 75ml/2½fl oz red wine
  • 1 tbsp tomato purée
  • 2 tsp tomato ketchup
  • 150ml/¼ pint hot beef stock
  • handful each fresh chives and parsley, chopped, to serve

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a casserole dish or large saucepan. Add the sausages and cook until browned on all sides.

  2. Add the onion and fry for 3-4 minutes. Add the garlic and fry for a further minute.

  3. Add the red wine, tomato purée, ketchup and beef stock and simmer for 8-10 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened and the sausages are completely cooked through.

  4. To serve, pour into a serving bowl and scatter with the chopped herbs.

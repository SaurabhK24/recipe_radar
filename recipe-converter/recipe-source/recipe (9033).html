Smoked salmon, scrambled egg and potato nibbles

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Makes 10

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 5 cooked new potatoes
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 free-range eggs, beaten
  • 2 tbsp double cream
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 spring onions, finely sliced
  • 50g/2oz smoked salmon slices, finely chopped
  • 30g/1oz lumpfish roe

Method

  1. Slice the cooked new potatoes in half and place on a serving platter cut-side up.

  2. Melt the butter in a pan.

  3. In a bowl, mix the eggs and cream together and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  4. Add the egg mixture to the pan and stir over a medium heat until the eggs are just cooked.

  5. Spoon teaspoonfuls of scrambled egg on top of the potatoes.

  6. Sprinkle the potatoes with spring onion slices and top with the smoked salmon pieces. Place a little lumpfish roe on top of each potato and serve.

Breakfast Martini

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Salvatore Calabrese
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 50ml/2fl oz gin
  • 1 tbsp Cointreau, or similar orange liqueur
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1½ tsp orange marmalade
  • orange peel spiral, to garnish

Method

  1. Place the gin, Cointreau, lemon juice and marmalade into a cocktail shaker with some ice and shake well to combine.

  2. To serve, strain into a Martini glass and garnish with a spiral of orange peel.

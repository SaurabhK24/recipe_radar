Pear, cheese and mint salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Ross Burden
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 pear, peeled, cored and sliced
  • ¼ red onion, finely sliced
  • 1 tbsp fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 140g/5oz brie, sliced

Method

  1. Mix the ingredients in a bowl and serve.

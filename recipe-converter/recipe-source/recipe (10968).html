Watermelon ice lollies

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Makes 8

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Simon Rimmer
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 125g/4oz caster sugar
  • 125ml/4oz water
  • 1 vanilla pod, seeds only
  • 600ml/1 pint watermelon juice (made from the flesh of 1 watermelon, seeds removed, blended and sieved)
  • 2 limes, juice only

Method

  1. Place the sugar, water and vanilla seeds into a pan over a medium heat. Heat until the sugar dissolves, then remove from the heat and allow to cool.

  2. Transfer to the fridge and chill until very cold.

  3. Add the watermelon juice and lime juice and mix well.

  4. Pour the fruit mixture into eight ice lolly moulds and place into the freezer until frozen.

Lemon and orange tuna slices

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz good-quality tuna steak, sliced thinly
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • ½ orange, juice only
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1 spring onion, chopped

Method

  1. Lay the tuna slices onto a serving plate.

  2. Mix the orange juice and lemon juice together in a bowl and then pour over the tuna.

  3. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Drizzle with olive oil. Scatter over the sliced kalamata olives and spring onion and serve.

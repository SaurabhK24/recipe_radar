Oriental tuna and radish salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 85g/3oz fresh tuna, sliced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 lime, juice only
  • 1 tbsp clear honey
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 5 radishes, grated
  • 2 tbsp radish leaves, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Place the fresh tuna in a bowl with the soy sauce, lime juice and clear honey and leave to marinate for three minutes.

  2. Drain the tuna. Fry the tuna slices in olive oil in a small non-stick frying pan for 1-2 minutes on both sides. Add the marinade for the last minute to warm through.

  3. Place all the remaining ingredients in a serving bowl and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  4. Serve the tuna on top of the salad and drizzle over the juices from the pan.

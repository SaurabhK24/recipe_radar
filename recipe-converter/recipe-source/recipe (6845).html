Pineapple carpaccio with mint

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1/3 pineapple, sliced into thin rounds on a mandolin

For the topping

  • 50g/1¾oz caster sugar
  • 1 small bunch of mint leaves only
  • 1 orange, juice only

Method

  1. In a pestle and mortar grind the mint and sugar together until they are well crushed.

  2. Add the orange juice.

  3. To serve place the mint topping all over the pineapple.

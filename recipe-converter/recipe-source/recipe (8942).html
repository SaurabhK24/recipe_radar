Smoked chicken bloomer

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Recommended by

1 person

Perk up pre-bought smoked chicken breasts with avocado and spring onions in this simple sandwich.

By Ed Baines
From Country Show Cook Off

Ingredients

  • 1 bloomer
  • knob of spreadable butter
  • mayonnaise
  • 1 smoked chicken breast, chopped
  • 1 bunch spring onion, finely chopped
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • handful crisp salad leaves

Method

  1. Cut four slices from the bread and spread with butter.

  2. Spread mayonnaise across two of the slices and top with the chicken breast, spring onion, avocado and salad.

  3. Top the chicken and salad with the other slices of bread and serve.

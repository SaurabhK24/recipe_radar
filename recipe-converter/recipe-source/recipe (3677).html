Fried egg with pan-fried haggis and mash

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 potato, peeled and diced
  • ½ haggis, sliced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 free-range egg

Method

  1. Bring a pan of water to the boil and cook the potato for 8-10 minutes until tender. Drain the potato and mash it.

  2. Heat half of the oil in a non-stick pan, spoon the potato mixture in and spread evenly. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and fry for 4-5 minutes on each side until golden brown.

  3. Fry the haggis slices in a separate pan for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden and cooked through.

  4. Heat the rest of the oil in a small frying pan, crack the egg in and fry for a few minutes, or until set.

  5. To serve, arrange the potato cake on a serving plate, serve the cooked haggis on top and top with the fried egg.

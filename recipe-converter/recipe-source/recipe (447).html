Baked feta

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Ross Burden
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz feta cheese
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 lemon, zest only
  • squeeze lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed

Method

  1. Preheat the oven 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Place the feta onto a baking sheet. Drizzle the oil over and sprinkle over the remaining ingredients.

  3. Place into the oven to bake for five minutes, or until beginning to colour.

  4. Remove from the oven, transfer to a dish and serve.

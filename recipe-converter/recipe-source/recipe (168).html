Apple onion salad with blue cheese dressing

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oi
  • l½ red onion, diced
  • 1 apple, cored and sliced

For the blue cheese dressing

  • 25g/1oz Dolcelatte cheese
  • 3 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp wholegrain mustard
  • 50ml olive oil

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a frying pan, add the onion and cook for 3-4 minutes over a low heat until softened. Transfer to a bowl with the apple.

  2. In a separate bowl, mix the blue cheese with the vinegar and the wholegrain mustard.

  3. Gradually add the oil, whisking constantly. Pour the mix onto the apple and red onion and serve.

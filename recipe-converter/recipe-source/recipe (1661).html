Cheese baked with thyme

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 cheese in a box (such as Pont l'Évêque)
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
  • ½ tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ red romero pepper, seeds removed and chopped
  • rustic white bread, broken up, to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.

  2. Place the cheese in a box on a baking sheet and sprinkle with the thyme leaves.

  3. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  4. Place in the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until just beginning to melt.

  5. Remove from the oven and serve on a plate with the chopped pepper pieces and rustic bread pieces beside.

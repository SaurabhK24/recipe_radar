Pasta bows with olives, mozzarella and peppers

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 200g/7oz farfelle
  • 1 garlic clove
  • small handful fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, plus more for drizzling
  • 50g/2oz olives, stones removed, roughly chopped
  • 1 ball mozzarella, torn into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 baby red peppers from a jar, sliced

Method

  1. Bring to the boil a large pan of salted water. Cook the pasta bows in the water, following the instructions on the packet for timing.

  2. Blend the garlic, basil and olive oil in a bowl with a hand blender to make a pesto.

  3. When the pasta is cooked, drain and allow to cool slightly.

  4. To serve, put the pasta in a bowl, add all the other ingredients and mix. Drizzle with olive oil and serve.

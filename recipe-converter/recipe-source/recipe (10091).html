Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp butter and a splash of olive oil
  • 200-300g/7-10oz shallots
  • 1 tsp honey or a heaped tsp of sugar
  • water

Method

  1. Blanch the shallots in boiling water for a couple of minutes. This makes removing the skins easier.

  2. Melt the butter with the oil in a frying pan and add the peeled shallots. Fry for a minute and then sprinkle with sugar or add the honey and coat the shallots. Take care that the shallots don t start to burn.

  3. Season well and then pour over enough cold water to cover. Bring to the boil and cook on a medium heat until all the liquid is reduced and the shallots are softened and caramelised.

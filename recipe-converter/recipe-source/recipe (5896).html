Mushroom soup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

71 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

A really simple mushroom soup recipe - make it extra special by using wild mushrooms.

By James Tanner

Ingredients

  • 50g/2oz butter
  • 2 leeks, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 300g/9 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 -3 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 600ml/1 pint chicken or vegetable stock

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a large pot and gently cook the leeks and garlic for 4-5 minutes or until softened. Add the mushrooms and thyme and cook on high for 2-3 minutes.

  2. Pour over the stock and cook for 5 minutes.

  3. Remove from the heat, carefully pour into a food processor and blend until smooth. Alternatively, keep in the pot and use a stick blender to blitz until smooth.

  4. Divide between four bowls and serve.

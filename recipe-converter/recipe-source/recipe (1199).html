Bruschetta with halloumi

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the halloumi

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 100g/3½oz halloumi, cut into 4 slices

For the tomato sauce

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 plum tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

To serve

  • 1 slice ciabatta, toasted
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • sprig fresh chervil

Method

  1. For the halloumi, heat the oil in a frying pan and fry the halloumi slices for 2-3 minutes on each side, until golden-brown.

  2. For the tomato sauce, heat the oil in a saucepan and cook the tomatoes, sugar and ketchup until thickened. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. To serve, place the toasted ciabatta onto a plate, drizzle with olive oil and top with the tomato sauce and halloumi. Garnish with chervil.

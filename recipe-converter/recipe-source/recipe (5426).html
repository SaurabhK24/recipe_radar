Mango fool with spun sugar

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the fool

  • ½ mango,stone removed, peeled and sliced
  • 50ml/2fl oz whipping cream
  • 100ml/4fl oz Greek yoghurt
  • 25g/1oz icing sugar
  • sprig fresh mint

For the spun sugar

  • 125g/4½oz caster sugar
  • cold water, to cover

Method

  1. For the fool, line the bottom of a tall glass with half of the sliced mango.

  2. Place the cream, yoghurt and icing sugar into a bowl and mix together well.

  3. Spoon the cream mixture into the glass and top with the remaining mango.

  4. For the spun sugar, place the sugar and enough water to cover into a saucepan and place over a high heat. Leave until the sugar has dissolved and the liquid has turned a light caramel colour. Remove from the heat and place the saucepan into a frying pan containing cold water.

  5. Using a fork, carefully pull out sugar threads from the pan and shape around an oiled clean steel or other metal implement in a twisted pattern.

  6. To serve, place the sugar threads on top of the pudding glass along with a sprig of mint.

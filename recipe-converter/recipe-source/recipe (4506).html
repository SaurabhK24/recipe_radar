Honey orange curd pudding with toffee cream sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 115g/4oz plain flour
  • 115g/4oz caster sugar
  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 115g/4oz butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 2 tbsp freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 2 tbsp clear honey

For the toffee cream sauce

  • 125ml/4fl oz double cream
  • 55g/2oz light muscovado sugar
  • 60g/2oz butter

Method

  1. Place the flour, caster sugar, eggs, baking powder and butter into a food processor and blend until smooth.

  2. Grease a 600ml/1 pint microwave-safe pudding basin with butter.

  3. Mix the orange juice with the honey and pour into the bottom of the pudding basin. Spoon the sponge mixture on top.

  4. Cover with cling film and microwave on high for five minutes, or until risen and cooked through.

  5. Meanwhile, for the toffee cream sauce, place the cream into a small pan with the light muscovado sugar and the butter. Place over a low heat and simmer gently for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce is smooth and glossy.

  6. To serve, invert the honey orange curd pudding onto a warm serving plate. Pour over the toffee cream sauce and serve.

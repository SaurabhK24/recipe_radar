Tomato and sweetcorn soup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 6

Recommended by

10 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Housecall

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • olive oil
  • 1 tsp powdered cumin
  • 2 tins(400g/14oz) chopped tomatoes
  • 340g/12oz sweetcorn kernels, tinned or frozen
  • 1L/1 ¾pt vegetable or chicken stock
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • fresh basil or coriander leaves
  • salt and black pepper

Method

  1. Sweat the garlic and onion in olive oil until soft and translucent. Add the cumin and cook for a minute or so before adding the tomatoes, sweetcorn, stock and tomato purée. Bring to the boil, then turn down the heat and allow to simmer for 15-20 minutes.

  2. The soup is good just as it is, rustic and chunky. If you prefer a smoother soup, just blend half of the mixture. Scatter with fresh herbs and serve with a drizzle of olive oil. Season well.

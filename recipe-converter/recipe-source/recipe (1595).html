Chargrilled t-bone steak

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Brian Turner
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 200g/7oz T-bone steak
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 15g/½oz butter
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh chervil, to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Season the steak, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Heat the oil and butter in an ovenproof frying pan. When the butter is foaming, fry the steak for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until browned on both sides.

  4. Transfer to the oven and cook for a further 5-10 minutes (for medium), or until cooked to your liking.

  5. Serve garnished with chopped chervil.

Pineapple, chilli and lime 'carpaccio'

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • ½ fresh pineapple, peeled, hulled and sliced thinly
  • 1 lime, juice and zest only
  • ½ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 6 whole raspberries
  • 1 lime, cut in to wedges, to garnish

Method

  1. Arrange the pineapple slices onto a serving plate and sprinkle over the lime zest. Drizzle over the lime juice and sprinkle over the dried chilli flakes and raspberries.

  2. Serve garnished with the lime wedges.

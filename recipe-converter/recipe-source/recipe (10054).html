Sweet polenta cake

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 110g/4oz butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 55g/2oz self-raising flour
  • 55g/2oz polenta
  • 110g/4oz caster sugar
  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • 15ml clear honey
  • 1 orange, peeled and sliced

Method

  1. Grease the inside of a small microwave-safe bowl with butter.

  2. Place the flour, polenta, sugar, butter, eggs and milk into a food processor and blend until the mixture is smooth.

  3. Pour the honey into the base of the microwave-safe bowl and arrange orange slices on top of the honey at the base of the bowl.

  4. Pour over the polenta mixture and cover the bowl with cling film. Microwave at highest setting for five minutes or until cooked through.

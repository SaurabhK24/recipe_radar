Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 8-10

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1kg/2¼lb sweetcorn kernels
  • 2 red peppers, deseeded and cubed
  • 1 red onion, peeled and sliced finely
  • 4 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan and add the onion and peppers. sauté for 2-3 minutes until starting to soften.

  2. Add the sweetcorn, season well and mix together. Turn down the heat, cover and cook gently until the vegetables are tender.

  3. Stir in the chopped parsley and serve at once.

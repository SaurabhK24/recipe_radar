Wrapped figs with camembert

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 4 figs
  • 55g/2oz Camembert, cut into chunks
  • 8 slices pancetta
  • splash port
  • drizzle balsamic vinegar
  • few fresh thyme or basil leaves

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.

  2. Split open the figs and stuff each one with a piece of Camembert.

  3. Wrap each fig with two slices of pancetta. Place on a baking tray and cook in the oven for 5-6 minute, or until the pancetta is golden and the cheese melted.

  4. Remove the figs from the oven and drizzle with a little port and balsamic vinegar.

  5. Sprinkle with thyme or basil leaves, to serve.

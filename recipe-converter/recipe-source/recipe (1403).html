Caramelised spiced pineapple with sabayon

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the pineapple

  • 100g/3½oz caster sugar
  • 1 vanilla pod, split and seeds scraped out
  • 1 cardamom pod, crushed
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ pineapple, peeled, cored and sliced
  • ½ lime, juice only

For the sabayon

  • 3 free-range egg yolks
  • 4 tbsp white wine
  • 55g/2oz caster sugar

Method

  1. For the caramelised spiced pineapple, heat the sugar, the vanilla and cardamom seeds and the cinnamon in a non-stick frying pan until the sugar is melted and caramelised.

  2. Add the pineapple and lime juice and stir. Remove from the heat and place onto a serving plate along with the pan juices.

  3. For the sabayon, place the egg yolks, wine and sugar into a heatproof bowl set over a pan of barely simmering water and whisk continuously until the mixture is thick and frothy.

  4. To serve, drizzle the sabayon over the pineapple.

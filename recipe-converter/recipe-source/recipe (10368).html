Tiramisu with pears and stem ginger

Preparation time

over 2 hours

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4-6

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Mike Robinson
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1 can of pears, sliced
  • 2 bits of stem ginger
  • 1 tbsp ginger syrup
  • 250ml/9fl oz mascarpone
  • 250ml/9fl oz crème frâiche
  • 200g/7oz ladies fingers biscuits
  • cocoa powder
  • large glass of extra strong coffee
  • Marsala or sweet sherry

Method

  1. Lay the biscuits on the bottom of a large dish. Pour over the coffee and the Marsala. Make sure all the biscuits are soaked.

  2. Mix together the crème fraîche and mascarpone and add the chopped stem ginger and some of the syrup. How much depends on the strength you like.

  3. Lay the pears over the biscuits. Spoon the cream mix over the top and sprinkle liberally with chocolate. Chill for 2 hours before serving.

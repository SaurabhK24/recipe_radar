Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 8

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 225g/8oz tomatoes, deseeded
  • 1 red onion, peeled and finely chopped or 4 spring onions, trimmed and sliced
  • finely
  • 1 jalapeno chilli, de-veined, de-seeded and finely chopped
  • 225g/8oz fresh ripe fruit such as melon, mango, papaya, pineapple, peach
  • large bunch of fresh coriander, chopped roughly
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice (or 2 limes)

Method

  1. Chop the tomatoes and fruit into the same sized chunks - about ½cm/¼in.

  2. Mix in a large bowl with the rest of the ingredients.

  3. Leave to stand for 30 minutes to let all the flavours merge.

Orient martini

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Sweet orange and exotic lychee give this martini a taste of the Orient.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 4 purple basil leaves
  • 10ml/2 tsp mandarin liqueur
  • 15ml/1 tbsp lychee liqueur
  • 50ml/2fl oz gin
  • 15ml/1 tbsp dry vermouth
  • grapefruit twist, to garnish

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.

  2. Strain into a martini glass over ice. Garnish with a grapefruit twist and serve.

Bacon butty with mustard mayo

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the bacon butty

  • 4 rashers streaky bacon
  • 2 slices ciabatta
  • drizzle olive oil

For the mustard mayo

  • 150ml/¼ pint Greek-style yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp double cream
  • 2 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • squeeze lemon juice
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. For the bacon butty, heat a griddle pan, add the streaky bacon and cook until crisp. Set aside.

  2. Drizzle two slices of ciabatta with olive oil and then chargrill on both sides in the same pan in which you cooked the bacon.

  3. For the mustard mayo, mix together the yoghurt, cream, mustard, lemon juice, salt and freshly ground black pepper in a bowl.

  4. To serve, pile the bacon onto the griddled ciabatta. Spoon over a generous dollop of mayo and place the other slice of griddled bread on top.

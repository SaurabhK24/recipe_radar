Peach soufflé

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • ½ peach, peeled and finely chopped
  • ½ orange, juice only
  • 2 free-range egg whites
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1 tbsp icing sugar

Method

  1. Preheat the oven 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Place the peach and orange juice into a food processor and blend until smooth.

  3. In a large bowl whisk the egg whites until stiff peaks form when the whisk is removed. Add the caster sugar and whisk again.

  4. Fold the peach mixture into the egg white mixture and spoon into a ramekin.

  5. Transfer to the oven and bake for 7-8 minutes, or until risen.

  6. Serve on a plate with a dusting of icing sugar.

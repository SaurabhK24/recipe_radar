Ginger sponge pudding

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Ainsley Harriott
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 55g/2oz self-raising flour
  • 55g/2oz butter, softened, plus extra for greasing
  • 55g/2oz caster sugar
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • 1 tbsp clear honey
  • 2.5cm/1in piece root ginger, peeled and finely grated
  • 1 free-range egg
  • Greek-style yoghurt, to serve

Method

  1. Lightly grease a small microwave-proof bowl.

  2. Place all the ingredients except the egg and yoghurt into a food processor and blend to combine.

  3. Crack in the egg and pulse briefly to mix.

  4. Pour the mixture into the prepared bowl and microwave on high for 4-5 minutes, or until the pudding is set.

  5. To serve, turn the pudding out onto a plate. Serve in bowls with a dollop of yoghurt.

Sweet and sour peppers

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

By Gennaro Contaldo
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 6 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 large yellow pepper, deseeded and cut into thick strips
  • 1 large red pepper, deseeded and cut into thick strips
  • 3 anchovy fillets
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled but left whole
  • 6 black olives
  • 1 tbsp capers
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan, add the peppers and cook until the skins are golden brown. Then add the anchovy fillets, garlic, olives and capers. Stir in the sugar, then add the vinegar and allow to evaporate.

  2. Cook on a medium heat for about 5 minutes, or until the peppers are tender. Season to taste and serve hot or cold.

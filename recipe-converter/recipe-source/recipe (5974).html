Nectarine fizz

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Ross Burden
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 nectarine
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1 glass white wine
  • 2 tbsp double cream
  • 2 ice cubes

Method

  1. Blend the nectarine with the caster sugar in a blender.

  2. Add the white wine, cream and ice cubes and blend until smooth.

  3. Serve.

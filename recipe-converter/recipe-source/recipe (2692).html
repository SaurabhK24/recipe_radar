Crab tartare with kiwi

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

5 people

A simple tasty starter of crab on toast with a tangy mix of kiwi, shallot, lime and cucumber.

By Rachel Khoo
From Spring Kitchen with Tom Kerridge

Ingredients

  • ½ lime, zest and juice
  • 80g/3oz chilled crabmeat (brown and white meat)
  • 2 ripe kiwi fruit, peeled and cut into small cubes
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 10cm/4in piece of cucumber, seeds removed, cut into small cubes
  • salt
  • 4 slices of bread
  • 1 tbsp unsalted soft butter

Method

  1. Add half a teaspoon of lime zest to a bowl then flake in the crabmeat. Add the kiwi, shallot and cucumber then squeeze over the juice from the lime and toss to coat the ingredients. Taste and season with salt.

  2. Toast the bread and cut into triangles. Spread with the butter and arrange on a plate next to a bowl of the crab tartare.

  3. For an especially pretty starter, use an 8cm/3in biscuit cutter to cut out four circles from the toast. Spread with the butter then place the biscuit cutter on top and fill with the crab tartare to create neat mounds.

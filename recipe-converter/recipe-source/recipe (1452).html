Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4-6

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 450g/1lb carrots, peeled and finely grated
  • 125g/4oz sultanas
  • 25g/1oz sesame seeds, toasted
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
  • Juice and zest 1 lemon

Method

  1. Mix together all the ingredients and stand for 20 minutes before serving.

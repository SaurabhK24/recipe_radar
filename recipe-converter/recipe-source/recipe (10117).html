Tagliatelle with garlic clams

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 75ml/2½fl oz white wine
  • 125g/4½oz clams, rinsed in clean cold water
  • 50ml/2fl oz boiling water
  • 100g/4oz tagliatelle, cooked
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Method

  1. Put the garlic, white wine, clams and boiling water into a medium saucepan, cover with a lid and cook for five minutes, or until all the clams have opened. Drain and discard any that haven't opened.

  2. Toss the clams with the cooked tagliatelle, place in a bowl and sprinkle with parsley, to serve.

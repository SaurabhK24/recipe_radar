Bruschetta with tomato and red onion

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 thick slices of bread
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the topping

  • 25g/1oz chopped red onion
  • 75g/2¾oz tinned tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Heat a griddle pan and rub the bread with olive oil. Place the bread onto the griddle and cook for two minutes on each side, or until toasted.

  2. For the topping, place the red onion, tomatoes and olive oil into a bowl and mix well.

  3. Add the parsley and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  4. To serve, place the toasted bread on the plate and spoon over the topping.

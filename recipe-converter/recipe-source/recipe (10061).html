Sweet potato and leek bubble and squeak

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 small sweet potato, peeled and cubed
  • 2 baby leeks, finely chopped
  • olive oil

Method

  1. Bring a saucepan of water to the boil. Add the sweet potato and boil for eight to ten minutes, or until soft, then drain.

  2. Add the baby leeks to the potatoes and mash together.

  3. Heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a very small frying pan and add the potato mixture. Fry for four to five minutes over a medium heat and then turn out onto a plate to serve.

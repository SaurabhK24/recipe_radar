Spinach salad with dolcelatte

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the dressing

  • 1 tsp white wine vinegar
  • 2 tsp olive oil

For the salad

  • 140g/5oz baby spinach leaves
  • 1 roasted red pepper from a jar, drained and sliced
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 55g/2oz dolcelatte

Method

  1. Place the vinegar and olive oil together in a bowl and whisk together.

  2. Pour the dressing over the spinach leaves and red pepper and mix together to coat well.

  3. To serve, place the salad into a clean bowl and sprinkle over the sesame seeds. Crumble the dolcelatte over the top and serve.

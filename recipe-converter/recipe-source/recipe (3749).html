Game chips

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 300ml/½ pint vegetable oil, for deep-frying
  • 4 Shetland black potatoes, thinly sliced

Method

  1. Heat a heavy bottomed saucepan, a third full of vegetable oil, until a breadcrumb sizzles in it. (CAUTION: hot oil can be dangerous! Do not leave unattended.)

  2. Add the potato slices and cook for 1-2 minutes, until golden. Remove and drain on kitchen towels.

  3. Serve in a large bowl.

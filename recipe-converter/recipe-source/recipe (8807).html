Silver gin fizz

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This is a simple classic gin fizz from mid-19th century America. The egg white sounds odd, but it adds a lovely frothiness.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 50ml/2fl oz gin
  • ½ lemon, juice only, retaining rind
  • 2 tsp caster sugar
  • 1 small fresh egg white, preferably organic
  • soda water to fill

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.

  2. Strain into a small highball glass, top with soda water and serve with the lemon rind.

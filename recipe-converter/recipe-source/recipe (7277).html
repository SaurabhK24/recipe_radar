Prawn lollipops

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Makes 4

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the marinade

  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the prawns

  • 4 king prawns, shells removed, de-veined
  • 4 small wooden skewers
  • 1 orange, cut in half
  • handful chervil, to serve

Method

  1. For the marinade, place the honey, lemon juice, sesame seeds and olive oil in a small bowl and whisk until combined.

  2. For the prawns, put the prepared prawns in the bowl, coat with the marinade and leave for five minutes. Remove and thread one prawn onto each of the four skewers.

  3. Heat a griddle pan and place the prawn skewers in the pan for 2-3 minutes each side, or until they are pink and cooked through.

  4. Place one half of the orange in the centre of a plate and arrange the chervil around the edge.

  5. To serve, stick the ends of the skewers into the orange half.

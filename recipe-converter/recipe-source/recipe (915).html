Bloody Mary

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

4 people

The perfect Bloody Mary is a hotly debated topic. Try this one for size: the key ingredients are all present and correct; retro celery garnish optional.

By Andy Pearson
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 2 ice cubes
  • vodka, double shot
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • 6 dashes Worcestershire sauce
  • 3 dashes Tabasco sauce
  • 150ml/5fl oz tomato juice
  • pinch salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Place the ice into a tall glass and add the vodka.

  2. Add the lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, Tabasco sauce and tomato juice. Stir well.

  3. Adjust the seasoning, to taste, with salt and pepper and serve straightaway.

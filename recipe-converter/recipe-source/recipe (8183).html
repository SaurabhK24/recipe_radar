Rosemary new potatoes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 170g/6oz new potatoes, diced
  • 1 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • 1 tbsp rosemary, blended in mini food processor
  • 25g/1oz Stilton cheese

Method

  1. Preheat grill to highest setting.

  2. Heat a saucepan of water until boiling and then par-boil the diced potato for 4-5 minutes.

  3. Drain and stir through wholegrain mustard and rosemary with a fork.

  4. Place a ring mould on a baking sheet and fill with the potato mixture, pressing down.

  5. Top with crumbled Stilton cheese and place under the grill for 2-3 minutes, until bubbly. Serve.

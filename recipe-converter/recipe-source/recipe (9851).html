Stuffed mushroom

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

3 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 large flat mushroom
  • ¼ mushroom, finely chopped
  • 85g/3oz white breadcrumbs
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 50g/1¾oz grated Edam cheese
  • handful flatleaf parsley
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Mix the ¼ mushroom, breadcrumbs, garlic, Edam, parsley and oil together and season with salt and pepper.

  2. Fill the whole mushroom with this stuffing and bake in the oven for ten minutes.

  3. Serve.

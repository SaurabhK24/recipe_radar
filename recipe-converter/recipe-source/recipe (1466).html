Carrots with chive and chervil dressing

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Richard Phillips
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 4 baby carrots, peeled but tops left on
  • 2 slices Serrano ham, cut into strips
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh chives
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh chervil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Blanch the carrots in boiling water for 4-5 minutes. Drain.

  2. Arrange the carrots on a serving plate and scatter over the strips of ham.

  3. Whisk the mustard, vinegar, oil, herbs, salt and freshly ground black pepper together in a bowl to make the dressing.

  4. Pour the dressing over the carrots and ham and serve.

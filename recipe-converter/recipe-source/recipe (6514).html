Passion fruit cheesecakes with shortbread

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 200g/7oz mascarpone
  • 100g/3½oz ready-made shortbread biscuits, crushed
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream, lightly whipped
  • 2 passion fruit

Method

  1. Place the mascarpone, crushed shortbread, cream and the pulp from one of the passion fruit into a bowl and mix well.

  2. Line two dariole moulds with cling film and spoon the mixture into the moulds. Cover with cling film and place into the freezer for 10-15 minutes.

  3. Remove from the freezer, remove the cling film and turn out onto two serving plates.

  4. To serve, drizzle over the pulp of the remaining passion fruit.

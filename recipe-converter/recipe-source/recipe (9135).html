Spaghetti alla carbonara

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • dash olive oil
  • 1 courgette, cut into cubes
  • 1 garlic clove, peeled and chopped
  • 2 slices ham, chopped
  • 200ml/7fl oz double cream
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 200g/7oz fresh spaghetti, cooked
  • 1 free-range egg yolk
  • parmesan cheese, grated, to serve

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan, add the courgette and fry for 1-2 minutes.

  2. Add the garlic and cook for one minute. Stir in the ham and double cream and season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Bring to the boil.

  3. Drain the cooked fresh pasta and return to the pan. Add the egg yolk to the pasta and stir well. Pour over the cream sauce and stir again.

  4. Spoon into a serving bowl and sprinkle over the parmesan. Serve immediately.

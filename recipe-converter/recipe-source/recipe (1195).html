Bruschetta of roasted pepper and Dolcelatte

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 slice ciabatta
  • dash olive oil
  • 1 roasted red pepper from a jar, drained, sliced
  • 55g/2oz Dolcelatte cheese, crumbled

Method

  1. Preheat the grill to medium.

  2. Drizzle a slice of ciabatta with olive oil and then griddle for 1-2 minutes on each side.

  3. Warm the sliced roasted red peppers in a small pan and then spoon onto the griddled bread. Place the crumbled Dolcelatte cheese on top of the peppers and place under the grill for 1-2 minutes or until bubbling.

  4. Slide onto a plate and serve.

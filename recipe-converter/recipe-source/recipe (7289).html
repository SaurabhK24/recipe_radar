Prawn skewers with mango salsa

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 8-10 tiger prawns
  • ½ lime, juiced and cut into wedges

For the salsa

  • ½ mango, peeled, stone removed and cut into cubes
  • 1 chilli, finely chopped
  • 1 lime, juice only
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • handful fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 2 tbsp honey

Method

  1. Skewer the tiger prawns onto two wooden skewers that have been soaked in water for half an hour.

  2. Heat a griddle pan, brushed with oil, until smoking. Add the prawn skewers and chargrill for 2-3 minutes on each side, until pink and cooked through. Add a squeeze of lime juice during cooking.

  3. Add the lime wedges to the griddle pan and cook until slightly browned.

  4. To make the salsa, place the mango, chilli, lime juice, soy sauce, coriander and honey into a bowl and mix well.

  5. To serve, place the prawn skewers onto a plate and spoon the salsa alongside. Garnish with the griddled lime wedges.

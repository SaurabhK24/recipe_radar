Rocket and goats' cheese salad with warm redcurrant dressing

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the salad

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 100g/3½oz firm goats' cheese
  • ½ avocado, sliced
  • handful fresh rocket

For the dressing

  • 75g/2¾oz redcurrants
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar

Method

  1. For the salad, heat the oil in a frying pan and fry the goats' cheese for two minutes on each side, or until golden-brown.

  2. For the dressing, place all of the dressing ingredients into a saucepan and cook over a medium heat for 3-4 minutes.

  3. To serve, place the avocado and rocket onto a plate with the cheese on top and the warm dressing spooned over.

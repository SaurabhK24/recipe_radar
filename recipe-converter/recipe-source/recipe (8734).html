Sesame-crusted tuna with broccoli and sweet potato

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the blueberry sauce

  • 50g/1½oz blueberries
  • 2 tsp white wine

For the tuna

  • 100g/3½oz tuna steak
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 free-range egg yolk
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the broccoli and sweet potato

  • 1 sweet potato, peeled, sliced into 2cm/¾in cubes and cooked until tender
  • ½ broccoli head, blanched

Method

  1. For the sauce, place the blueberries and white wine into the bowl of a food processor and blend the ingredients until smooth.

  2. For the tuna, place the tuna, sesame seeds and egg yolk into a clean bowl. Season the ingredients with salt and freshly ground black pepper and mix well.

  3. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan and cook the tuna for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.

  4. To serve, place the broccoli and sweet potato onto a clean plate. Place the tuna on top of the broccoli and sweet potato and pour the sauce over the top.

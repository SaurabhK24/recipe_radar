Stilton dauphinoise potatoes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Richard Phillips
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 150ml/5½fl oz double cream
  • 150ml/5½fl oz milk
  • ½ large potato, peeled, very thinly sliced
  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 100g/3½oz Stilton, crumbled
  • 2 garlic cloves, lightly crushed
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Pour the double cream and milk into a large saucepan and bring to a simmer.

  3. Add the potato slices, butter and Stilton (reserving some to sprinkle over the top), garlic, salt and freshly ground black pepper and simmer gently for five minutes.

  4. Spoon into an ovenproof dish and sprinkle with the reserved Stilton. Bake in the oven for 15 minutes, or until bubbling and golden-brown on top. Serve in the dish.

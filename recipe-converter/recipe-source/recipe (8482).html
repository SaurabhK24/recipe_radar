Steak with melting Brie rosti and green beans

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Alex Mackay
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the melting Brie rösti

  • 1 potato, peeled and grated
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 55g/2oz Brie, sliced

For the sautéed steak

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 150g/5½oz beef steak
  • 100g/3½oz green beans, blanched, to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. For the melting Brie rösti, place the grated potato into a clean tea towel and squeeze the liquid out. Remove the potato from the tea towel and season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. In a small ovenproof, non-stick frying pan, heat the butter and oil together and press in half of the grated potato to cover the base of the pan.

  4. Place the sliced Brie on top of the potato and top with the remaining half of grated potato.

  5. Fry the potato for two minutes on each side until golden-brown.

  6. Transfer the pan to the oven for a further six minutes, or until the potato is tender.

  7. For the sautéed steak, heat the olive oil in a non-stick frying pan and fry the steak on both sides until cooked to your liking. Leave to rest for five minutes.

  8. To serve, place the rösti and green beans onto a serving plate with the steak.

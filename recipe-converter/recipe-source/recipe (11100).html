Preparation time

30 mins to 1 hour

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 135ml marinade

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 6tbsp/90ml virgin olive oil
  • 3 tbsp/45ml wine vinegar or lemon or lime juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • small bunch each of 3-4 herbs e.g. parsley, coriander, thyme, sage or savory, chopped
  • 1 tsp/5ml coarsely ground black pepper

Method

  1. Combine all ingredients and cover the meat, fish or vegetables. Leave in a cool place.

  2. Add salt just before starting to cook and use the marinade to baste whilst cooking.

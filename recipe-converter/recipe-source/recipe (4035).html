Green coconut chutney

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes approx. 250g/9oz

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This beautiful fresh green chutney is fragrant and cooling with hot curries - and can be made in seconds!

By Cyrus Todiwala
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 250g/9oz fresh grated coconut or frozen coconut, thawed
  • or 150g/5oz desiccated coconut
  • 1 large bunch of fresh coriander
  • 1 large bunch of fresh mint
  • 4-5 green chillies
  • 6-8 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp (heaped) cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 large lime, juiced (or to taste)
  • salt

Method

  1. Put all the ingredients into a blender and whiz until you have got a thick paste, adding a little water if necessary.

  2. Taste and adjust the seasoning, adding more lime juice if you like.

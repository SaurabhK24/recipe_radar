Potato cakes with sausage and bacon patties

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the potato cakes

  • 1 medium potato, peeled and grated
  • 1 free-range egg, lightly beaten
  • 3 tbsp plain flour
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

For the sausage and bacon patties

  • 3 sausages, skins removed
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh chives
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 rashers smoked bacon
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • tomato ketchup, to serve

Method

  1. For the potato cakes, place the grated potato into a bowl lined with a clean tea towel. Squeeze the tea towel to remove the excess liquid from the potatoes.

  2. Place the grated potato into a separate bowl with the egg and flour. Season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper and mix. Divide into two patties.

  3. Heat the oil in a large frying pan, add the potato cakes and fry for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden-brown all over. Remove from the pan, set aside and keep warm.

  4. For the sausage and bacon patties, mix the sausage meat with the chives and black pepper in a bowl. Shape into two patties and wrap each pattie in bacon.

  5. Heat the olive oil in the frying pan and fry the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until golden-brown and completely cooked through.

  6. To serve, place the potato cakes onto two serving plates and top with the sausage and bacon patties. Serve with ketchup.

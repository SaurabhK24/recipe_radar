Cream of broccoli soup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • dash olive oil
  • ¼ onion, finely chopped
  • ½ head broccoli, cut into florets
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 300ml/½ pint hot chicken or vegetable stock
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream, plus extra to serve

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a small saucepan, add the onion and fry gently until softened. Add the broccoli florets, salt and freshly ground black pepper and stock and simmer for five minutes, or until the broccoli is tender.

  2. Stir in the cream. Blend with a hand blender until smooth. Pour into a bowl and finish with a swirl of cream.

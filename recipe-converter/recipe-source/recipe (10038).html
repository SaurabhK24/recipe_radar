Sweetcorn fritters with bacon and honey

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the fritters

  • 100g/3½oz self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 5 tbsp milk
  • 100g/3½oz canned sweetcorn, rinsed and drained
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

To serve

  • 4 slices bacon, fried until crisp
  • 2 tbsp honey, for drizzling

Method

  1. Place the flour, salt and milk into a large bowl and whisk together to make a smooth batter. Stir in the sweetcorn.

  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a frying pan and pour spoonfuls of the batter into the pan. Fry the fritters until golden-brown underneath, then turn over with a fish slice and fry until golden-brown on the other side. Remove from the pan and set aside to keep warm. Repeat the process with the remaining batter.

  3. To serve, place the fritters onto serving plates, top with the bacon and drizzle over the honey.

Three cheese and cress muffins

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Use up leftover cheese in this tasty muffin. recipe. They're great for lunchboxes and picnics.

By Kate Morris and Sally Brown
From I Can Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 free-range egg white
  • 4 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3-4 tbsp milk
  • 125g/4½oz plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • pinch English mustard powder
  • small bunch salad cress
  • 15g/½ oz cheddar, grated
  • 15g/½ oz feta, crumbled
  • 15g/½ oz parmesan, grated

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6. Line a mini muffin tin with paper cases.

  2. Whisk the egg white in a bowl until frothy, then whisk in the oil and milk. Season with freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Stir the flour, baking powder and mustard powder together in a bowl.

  4. Stir in the egg mixture, cress, cheddar, feta and parmesan until just combined.

  5. Spoon the mixture into the muffin tin and bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the muffins are risen and golden-brown.

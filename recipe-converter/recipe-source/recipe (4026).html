Green bean salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Ross Burden
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 free-range egg yolk
  • ½ shallot, finely chopped
  • ½tsp white wine vinegar
  • 50ml/2fl oz olive oil
  • 100g/3½oz green beans, blanched, refreshed
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. In a large bowl whisk together the egg yolk, shallot and vinegar. Gradually add the oil while whisking to create a thick emulsion, and then season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  2. Add the beans to the dressing and mix together.

  3. To serve, pile the beans high on a plate.

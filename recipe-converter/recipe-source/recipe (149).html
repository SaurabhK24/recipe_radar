Apple, bacon and black pudding salad with gribiche dressing

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

By Paul Merrett
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1 apple, de-cored and cut into rounds
  • 8 rashers of streaky bacon
  • handful mixed leaves
  • 8 slices black pudding
  • 4 quails' eggs

For the gribiche dressing

  • 2 tsp of capers
  • 2 tsp of gherkins, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp of balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp of red onion, finely chopped

Method

  1. Pan-fry the apple, black pudding and bacon rashers.

  2. Then place the apple on a plate and layer it using leaves, black pudding, bacon and then more black pudding.

  3. Finally fry the quails' eggs and place on top of the layered salad.

  4. To make the dressing mix the capers, gherkins, balsamic vinegar, olive oil and red onion and drizzle over the apple, bacon and black pudding salad.

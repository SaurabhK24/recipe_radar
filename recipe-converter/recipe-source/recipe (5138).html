Lemon and thyme sweet potato fondant

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 sweet potato, peeled and sliced into 1cm/½in thick rounds
  • 3 tbsp water
  • sprig fresh thyme, leaves only
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • lemon wedge, to serve

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a small frying pan and gently fry the sweet potato slices, turning frequently, until lightly golden on both sides.

  2. Add the water and thyme leaves and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Cover and simmer for 6-8 minutes, or until tender. Squeeze in the lemon juice and stir.

  3. To serve, place the sweet potato fondant onto a plate and garnish with a lemon wedge.

Garlic and chilli olives

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 1 jar

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By David Laris
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle olives in brine, drained
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, peeled, sliced
  • 1 chilli, sliced
  • handful fresh basil leaves

Method

  1. Pour the olives into a bowl. Add enough olive oil to coat the olives.

  2. Add the remaining ingredients and mix well.

  3. Either serve immediately or transfer the olives back to the jar and store in the fridge for 2-3 days.

  4. Experiment by trying alternatives, such as lemon rind, oregano stalks, celery, carrots, peppercorns, bay leaves, or any of your favourite ingredients.

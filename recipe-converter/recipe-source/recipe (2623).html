Corn skewers with chilli herb drizzle

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the corn skewers

  • 1 corn-on-the-cob
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the chilli herb drizzle

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
  • ¼ fresh red chilli, chopped
  • 1 lime, juice only
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges, for garnish

Method

  1. For the corn skewers, place the corn-on-the-cob into a small bowl, sprinkle with water and microwave on high for six minutes. Cut into 2cm/1in pieces using a large, sharp knife.

  2. Thread the corn pieces onto skewers, drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Heat a griddle pan until hot and griddle the corn skewers until golden-brown on all sides.

  3. For the chilli herb drizzle, mix all the ingredients together in a small bowl.

  4. To serve, place the corn skewers onto a serving plate and pour over the chilli herb drizzle. Garnish with lime wedges.

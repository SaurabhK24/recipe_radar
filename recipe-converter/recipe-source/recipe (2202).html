Chocolate bread and butter pudding

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • butter, for greasing
  • 6 tbsp milk
  • 6 tbsp double cream
  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 2 free-range egg yolks
  • 55g/2oz caster sugar
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 100g/3½oz bread, cubed
  • 85g/3oz dark chocolate, chopped
  • icing sugar, to dust

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4. Grease a 20cm x 12cm/8in x 5in ovenproof dish with butter.

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the milk, cream, eggs, egg yolks, sugar and vanilla extract until smooth.

  3. Place the bread and chocolate pieces into the base of the dish and pour over the custard mixture.

  4. Transfer to the oven and bake for 15 minutes, or until the custard has set and the pudding is golden-brown on top.

  5. Serve warm, dusted with icing sugar.

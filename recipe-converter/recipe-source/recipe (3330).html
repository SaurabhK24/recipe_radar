Espresso prunes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Jill Dupleix
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp ground espresso coffee
  • 300g/10½oz sugar
  • 2 tbsp brandy
  • 450g/1lb large prunes, dried and pitted

Method

  1. Dissolve the coffee in 125ml/4fl oz boiling water, then strain.

  2. Add the sugar, brandy and 500ml/17fl oz water and bring to the boil, stirring.

  3. Add the prunes, bring to the boil and cook at quite a fast simmer for 30-45 minutes until the liquid is thick and syrupy. Serve.

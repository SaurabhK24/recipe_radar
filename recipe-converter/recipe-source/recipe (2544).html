Coffee meringue with passion fruit cream and summer fruits

Preparation time

30 mins to 1 hour

Cooking time

1 to 2 hours

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Galton Blackiston
From Put Your Money Where Your Mouth Is

Ingredients

For the meringue

  • 4 free-range egg whites
  • 75g/2½oz icing sugar
  • 2 tsp instant coffee
  • 110g/4oz caster sugar

For the passion fruit cream

  • 4 passion fruit, seeds and pulp only, sieved (seeds discarded)
  • 450ml/16fl oz double cream, whisked until soft peaks form when the whisk is removed from the bowl
  • 1 heaped tbsp icing sugar
  • 500g/1lb 1½oz mixed summer berries, such as strawberries, raspberries and blueberries

Method

  1. For the meringue, preheat the oven to 140C/280F/Gas 1. Line a large baking sheet with baking parchment.

  2. Place the egg whites in a large, clean bowl. Sift the icing sugar and the instant coffee into the egg whites.

  3. Whisk the egg whites with an electric hand whisk until soft peaks form when the whisk is removed. Add the caster sugar, a little at a time, while continuing to whisk. Whisk until all the sugar is added and the mixture is glossy and thick and stands in peaks when pulled up by the whisk.

  4. Spread the meringue mixture out into a circle on the lined baking tray (alternatively you can make four smaller merinuges) and smooth the top with a palette knife.

  5. Transfer the tray to the oven and bake for 45-60 minutes until crisp on the outside, then turn off the oven and leave to cool with the door ajar for at least 30 minutes. Remove from the oven and peel off the paper.

  6. For the passion fruit cream, gently fold the passion fruit into the whisked cream. Stir in the icing sugar.

  7. To assemble the meringue, cover the meringue base (or bases) with the passion fruit cream. Top with the summer berries and serve.

Gnocchi with red chard sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the red chard sauce

  • 50ml/2fl oz olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 100g/3½oz red chard, chopped
  • 3 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 25g/1oz fresh breadcrumbs
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • ½ lemon, juice and zest only
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the gnocchi

  • 150g/5½oz gnocchi, cooked according to packet instructions
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. For the red chard sauce, place all of the sauce ingredients into a bowl and mix well.

  2. For the gnocchi, place the gnocchi into a serving bowl and drizzle with oil. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper and stir.

  3. To serve, pour the sauce over the gnocchi.

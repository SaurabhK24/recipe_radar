Rum butter

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 110g butter

By Lotte Duncan

Ingredients

  • 4oz (110g) unsalted butter
  • 3oz (85g) dark brown muscovado sugar
  • 4 tbsp dark rum

Method

  1. Mix the ingredients well until completely combined, store covered in the fridge.

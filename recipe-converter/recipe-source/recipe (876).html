Blackberry fool

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz blackberries
  • 50ml/2fl oz red wine
  • 25g/1oz caster sugar
  • 100g/3½oz Greek yoghurt
  • 50ml/2fl oz whipped cream

Method

  1. Place the blackberries, wine, sugar and yoghurt into a food processor and blend until smooth.

  2. To serve, pour the berry mixture into a tall glass and top with the whipped cream.

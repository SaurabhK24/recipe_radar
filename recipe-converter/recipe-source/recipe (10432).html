Tomato and goats' cheese galette with spinach

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 6

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This recipe looks spectacular but is very cheap and easy to prepare and cook. Like a pizza, any toppings to the pastry can be added depending on what you happen to have in your fridge.

By Ed Baines
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1 roll of pre-rolled puff pastry
  • 1 egg, yolk only
  • 2 large handfuls spinach
  • 4 tomatoes, sliced thinly
  • log soft goats' cheese rolled in herbs
  • drizzle of olive oil
  • 1 tsp of dried thyme
  • salt and pepper

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 190C/375F/Gas 5.

  2. Add the spinach to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes, then dry with kitchen roll.

  3. Take a pre-rolled sheet of puff pastry (rolled out 2cm/¾in thick), take a side plate and use it as a template by placing it on the pastry and cutting around it. Brush the pastry circle lightly with beaten egg yolk.

  4. Place the spinach on the pastry.

  5. Add the slices of tomato in a crescent spiral and season

  6. Sli the goats' cheese log and arrange in a spiral on top of the tomatoes.

  7. Drizzle over olive oil, salt, pepper and dried thyme (optional).

  8. Bake in oven for 15 to 20 minutes. Serve with a rocket salad.

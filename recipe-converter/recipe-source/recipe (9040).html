Smoky bacon and tomato soup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 4 slices bacon, chopped
  • ½ pint chicken or vegetable stock
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and gently cook the onion and garlic for 3-4 minutes or until softened.

  2. Add the bacon and cook for a further three minutes.

  3. Pour over the chicken or vegetable stock and add the tomatoes and purée. Cook for five minutes.

  4. With a hand blender, blend the soup until it's smooth.

  5. Serve warm.

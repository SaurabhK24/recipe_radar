Chilli chicken with stir-fried noodles

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

12 people

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the chilli chicken

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 chicken breast, cut in half lengthways
  • ½ orange, chopped (skin on)
  • 1 tbsp clear honey
  • ¼ red chilli, chopped

For the stir-fried noodles

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • ¼ onion, chopped
  • 2 spring onions, chopped
  • 100g/3½oz ready-made egg noodles, cooked according to packet instructions
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • ¼ orange, juice only
  • pinch dried chilli flakes
  • handful fresh coriander leaves, chopped

Method

  1. For the chilli chicken, heat the olive oil in a frying pan over a medium heat. Add the chicken and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and completely cooked through.

  2. Add the orange pieces, honey and chilli and cook for two minutes, turning the breast to coat in the sauce.

  3. For the stir-fried noodles, heat the oil in a wok over a high heat. Add the onion and spring onion and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.

  4. Add the noodles, soy sauce, orange juice and chilli flakes and stir-fry for one minute.

  5. Add the coriander, mix well and place onto a warmed plate. Place the chilli chicken on top and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

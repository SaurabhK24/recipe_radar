Celery and apple soup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the soup

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ stalk celery, chopped
  • ½ apple, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 150ml/5¼ fl oz hot vegetable stock
  • 50ml/1¾ fl oz milk
  • 75ml/2½ fl oz double cream
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

To garnish

  • 1 tbsp walnuts, chopped
  • drizzle olive oil

Method

  1. For the soup, heat the olive oil in a small pan and add the celery, apple and garlic and cook for three minutes.

  2. Add the hot vegetable stock, milk, cream and salt and freshly ground black pepper to the pan, cover with a lid and cook for three minutes.

  3. Pour the mixture into a blender and blitz until smooth.

  4. Pour the soup into a serving bowl and garnish with chopped walnuts and a drizzle of olive oil.

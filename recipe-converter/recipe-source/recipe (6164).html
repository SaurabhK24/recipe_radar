Oriental-style citrus mackerel with rocket salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

4 people

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 orange, juice only
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • pinch dried chilli flakes
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 1 mackerel fillet, cut in half
  • ½ lime, juice only
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander leaves
  • handful fresh rocket
  • 1 orange, cut into wedges, to serve

Method

  1. Place the orange juice, soy sauce, chilli flakes and sesame seeds into a frying pan and simmer for 3-4 minutes.

  2. Add the mackerel and cook for two minutes on each side, or until cooked through. Remove from the heat, squeeze in the lime juice and scatter with coriander.

  3. Place the rocket leaves into a bowl, pour over the juices from the mackerel pan and stir.

  4. To serve, pile the rocket salad onto a serving plate. Top with the mackerel and garnish with orange wedges.

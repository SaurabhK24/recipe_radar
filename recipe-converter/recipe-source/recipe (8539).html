Scallops with truffle mash

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

By Angela Griffin
From A Taste of My Life

Ingredients

For the truffle mash

  • 550g/1lb 4oz potatoes, peeled and boiled
  • knob butter
  • 3-4 tbsp milk
  • salt and white pepper
  • truffle oil, to serve

For the scallops

  • 12 large scallops (with or without roes, as preferred)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. For the truffle mash, place the cooked potatoes into a bowl. Add the butter and milk and mash until smooth. Season, to taste, with salt and white pepper. Set aside and keep warm until ready to serve.

  2. For the scallops, drizzle the scallops with olive oil.

  3. Heat a griddle pan or frying pan until very hot, then add the scallops. Cook for about 90 seconds on each side, or until just cooked through, then remove from the pan.

  4. To serve, place a portion of the warm mashed potato onto each serving plate and drizzle over a few drops of truffle oil. Top with the seared scallops.

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 8-10 blinis

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 280g/10oz strong white flour
  • 180ml/6fl oz milk
  • 1 tbsp dried yeast
  • 2 eggs, separated
  • 300ml/10fl oz double cream
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 30g/1oz butter for frying

Method

  1. Heat the milk in a saucepan until lukewarm. Pour on to the yeast and leave for 10 minutes until the mixture is frothy.

  2. Sift the flour and salt into a large bowl. Mix the eggs yolks, cream and yeast mixture into the flour. Set the batter aside for approximately 1 hour, until it has doubled in size.

  3. Whisk the egg whites until they form soft peaks and fold into the batter. Add a little more milk, if necessary, so that the batter is of a thick pouring consistency.

  4. Heat the butter in a frying pan over a medium heat. When it is hot, spoon small amounts of batter into the pan.

  5. Fry the blinis until they are golden brown on both sides and serve at once.

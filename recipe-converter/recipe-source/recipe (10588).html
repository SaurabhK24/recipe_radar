Tuna tartare

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 110g/4oz fresh tuna steak, finely chopped
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • ¼ onion, finely chopped
  • ¼ red pepper, finely chopped
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 thick slice ciabatta, toasted
  • olive oil, to drizzle

Method

  1. Place all of the ingredients except the ciabatta and oil into a bowl and mix together gently.

  2. To serve, spoon the tuna mixture onto the ciabatta and drizzle with the olive oil.

Mango hedgehog with chilli

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • ½ mango, skin left on
  • ½tsp chilli powder
  • 25g/1oz icing sugar
  • 5 tbsp Greek-style yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp honey

Method

  1. Preheat grill to high.

  2. Cut the mango flesh in a crisscross pattern on top. Fold the mango inside out, pressing the skin inwards. This should look like a hedgehog.

  3. Place the mango hedgehog onto an oven-proof sheet and sprinkle with the chilli and dust with the icing sugar.

  4. Place under the hot grill for four minutes until the mango is slightly golden.

  5. Mix the yoghurt and honey together.

  6. Transfer the hedgehog from the grill to a dish and serve with the honey yoghurt mix.

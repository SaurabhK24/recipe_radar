'Tex-Mex' chilli beans

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 400g/14oz tin of baked beans
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp balsamic vinegar

Method

  1. Strain the beans and place in a saucepan over a medium heat.

  2. Add the sugar, spices, vinegar and water. Heat through for five minutes and pour into a bowl to serve.

Butterscotch oranges

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 25g/1oz unsalted butter
  • 1 orange, peeled, sliced
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • ½ orange, juice only
  • 100ml/3½fl oz double cream
  • fresh mint leaves, to garnish

Method

  1. Heat the butter in a frying pan, add the orange slices and fry for two minutes.

  2. Add the honey and fry for a further minute on each side. Remove the oranges from the pan and place on a serving plate.

  3. Add the orange juice to the frying pan, along with the double cream. Simmer for 2-3 minutes, or until slightly thickened.

  4. Pour the sauce over the oranges and serve garnished with mint leaves.

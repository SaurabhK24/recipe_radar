Honey and sultana-glazed croissants

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 ready-made croissants, sliced in half lengthways
  • 4 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp sultanas
  • icing sugar, to dust

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Place the croissants cut-side up on a non-stick baking sheet. Drizzle with honey and sprinkle over the sultanas.

  3. Transfer to the oven and bake for five minutes, or until warmed through.

  4. To serve, place the warm croissants onto serving plates and dust with icing sugar.

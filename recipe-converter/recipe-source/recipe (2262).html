Chocolate mousse with chocolate orange drizzle

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz dark chocolate
  • 250ml/9fl oz double cream
  • 2 oranges, peeled, sliced

Method

  1. Melt half the chocolate in a bowl set over a pan of gently simmering water, then leave to cool slightly.

  2. In a bowl, whip the cream to soft peaks and fold in the melted chocolate to make a marbled effect.

  3. To serve, place the sliced oranges into the base of a glass serving dish, spoon over the chocolate mousse and pour over the rest of the melted chocolate.

Savoury hotpot

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

This simple recipe for a comforting sausage and bean hotpot makes a tasty weeknight supper to make with the kids.

By Karen Mulholland
From I Can Cook

Ingredients

  • 1  spring onion, sliced
  • 30g/1oz cooked cannellini beans
  • 1 cabbage leaf, chopped
  • 2 tbsp tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 cooked sausages, sliced
  • 75ml/3fl oz stock
  • ½ tsp wholegrain mustard
  • few stalks fresh thyme, leaves only

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 160C/325F/Gas 3.

  2. Mix all of the ingredients together in a lidded ovenproof dish and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Cook in the oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the sausages are cooked through. Serve with mashed potato or crusty bread.

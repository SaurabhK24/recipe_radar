Raspberry cinnamon meringue

You need JavaScript to view this clip.

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Makes 8

Recommended by

25 people

Light, crisp balls of meringue topped with raspberries and dark chocolate.

By Nigel Slater
From Nigel Slater's Dish of the Day

Ingredients

  • 280g/10oz golden caster sugar
  • 6 free-range eggs, whites only
  • pinch of cinnamon
  • 350g/12oz raspberries
  • 100g/3½oz dark chocolate

Method

  1. Preheat the oven on to 140C/275F/Gas 1.

  2. Pour the sugar into an oven tray and bake for 30 minutes.

  3. Whisk the egg whites while adding the warm sugar. Whisk well until the whites form a hard peak shape when the whisk is removed.

  4. Line an oven tray with baking paper.

  5. Spoon mounds of the egg white onto the baking tray in rough shapes approximately the size of a tennis ball. Sprinkle with cinnamon and bake in the oven for 30-35 minutes.

  6. Melt the chocolate in a bowl over a simmering pan of water.

  7. When the meringues are cooked, flatten the tops down. Evenly distribute the raspberries on each one.

  8. With a spoon, trickle the melted chocolate over the meringues and leave to cool until the chocolate is solid before serving.

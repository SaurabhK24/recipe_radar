Quesadilla

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made flour tortilla
  • ¼ avocado, peeled, stone removed, sliced
  • 100g/3½oz can mixed beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
  • 200g/7oz can cherry tomatoes, drained of juice
  • ¼ onion, finely chopped
  • 30g/1oz gruyere, grated

Method

  1. Place the tortilla in a wide frying pan.

  2. Mix all of the remaining ingredients together in a bowl, then place the mixture onto one half of the tortilla. Fold over the tortilla half to cover the filling and place the pan over a medium heat and cook until base of tortilla is golden-brown. Carefully turn over the tortilla and brown other side.

  3. To serve, place onto a warm plate and cut in half to allow the filling to ooze out slightly.

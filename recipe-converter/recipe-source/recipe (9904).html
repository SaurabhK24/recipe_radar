Summer fruit smoothie

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2-3

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Smoothies are not as sweet as milkshakes and are a little healthier too. If you like yours sweeter, just add more icing sugar. To enjoy this smoothie all year round try it with frozen berries.

By Mary Berry
From Mary Berry Cooks

Ingredients

  • 225g/8oz fresh blackberries
  • 225g/8oz fresh raspberries
  • 225g/8oz fresh blueberries
  • 1 just-ripe medium banana, broken into pieces
  • 150ml/¼ pint natural plain yoghurt
  • 150ml/¼ pint milk
  • 1 tbsp icing sugar, to taste

Method

  1. Measure all the ingredients into a food processor and whiz until smooth. Alternatively use a hand-held blender.

  2. Pour into a tall cool glass and enjoy.

Melon with parma ham

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • ½ cantaloupe melon, seeds and skin removed, quartered
  • 8 fresh basil leaves, plus an extra sprig, to garnish
  • 4 slices parma ham

Method

  1. Place two basil leaves on top of each piece of melon.

  2. Wrap a slice of parma ham around each piece of melon.

  3. To serve, place the parma ham-wrapped melon onto a serving plate and garnish with a sprig of basil.

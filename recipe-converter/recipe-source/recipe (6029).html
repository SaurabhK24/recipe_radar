Oat fig stuffing

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 6

Recommended by

2 people

Here’s a delicious and unusual stuffing that makes a great accompaniment to any roast dinner.

By Nigel Slater
From Nigel and Adam's Farm Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 140g/5oz butter
  • 100g/3½oz jumbo oats
  • 100g/3½oz rolled oats
  • 2 red onions, roughly chopped
  • 200g/7oz pistachios
  • 4 figs, roughly chopped, skin on
  • 8 sprigs thyme
  • 600g/1lb 5oz sausage meat

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Melt 100g/3½oz of butter in a pan, add both types of oats and cook for three minutes. Transfer to a large mixing bowl.

  3. Melt 40g/1½oz of butter in the pan and cook the chopped red onions until softened.

  4. Add the pistachios and figs and tear in the thyme. Warm through for two minutes. Transfer to the mixing bowl with the oats.

  5. Finally add the sausage meat to the bowl and mix well with your hands.

  6. Spread the stuffing mixture evenly across an oven dish and bake for 45 minutes.

Cheese soufflé

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 85g/3oz butter
  • 55g/2oz flour
  • ¼ pint milk
  • 55g/2oz red leicester
  • 2 free-range eggs, separated
  • splash double cream

Method

  1. Heat oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.

  2. In a small pan melt the butter and flour together to make a roux and cook for one minute.

  3. Stir in the milk, cheese, egg yolks and double cream. Cook until smooth, stirring continuously. Leave to cool slightly.

  4. Whisk the egg whites to stiff peaks and gently fold into the cheese mixture.

  5. Pour into a small frying pan and bake in the oven for 8-10 minutes.

  6. Serve in the frying pan.

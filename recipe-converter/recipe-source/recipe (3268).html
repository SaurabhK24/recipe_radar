Easy garlic bread

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Makes about 250g

By Michael Moore
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 250g/9oz butter
  • 30g/1oz tomato paste
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • ½ tsp thyme, chopped
  • ½ tsp rosemary, chopped
  • seasoning
  • toast or hot baguette for serving

Method

  1. Beat butter with wooden spoon until soft, and then add the remaining ingredients and beat until smooth.

  2. Alternatively add all ingredients to a food processor and blend until smooth.

  3. Scrape onto cling film or foil and roll to reform. Refrigerate until ready to use.

  4. Serve on the toast or hot baguette.

Pan-fried pork with oriental salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the oriental salad

  • 50g/2oz caster sugar
  • pinch chilli flakes
  • 75ml/2½fl oz water
  • half a bunch watercress

For the pork

  • 150g/5½oz pork fillet, flattened out using a meat mallet
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. For the salad, put the sugar, chilli flakes and water into a small frying pan and cook slowly for 5-6 minutes or until it has a syrup-like consistency. Leave to cool.

  2. To make the pan-fried pork, roll the pork fillet in olive oil, salt and freshly ground black pepper and sear in a hot non-stick frying pan for two minutes on each side.

  3. Remove from the heat and leave to rest for two minutes. Slice into five pieces and arrange in the middle of a plate.

  4. To serve, scatter the watercress round the pork and drizzle with the oriental dressing.

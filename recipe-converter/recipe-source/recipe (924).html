Blueberries with brown sugar cream

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 50g/1¾oz blueberries
  • 2 tbsp Greek-style yoghurt
  • 4 tbsp double cream, softly whipped
  • 1 tbsp soft brown sugar

Method

  1. Divide the blueberries between two glass dessert dishes.

  2. In a bowl, mix together the Greek-style yoghurt and whipped cream and spoon over the top of the blueberries.

  3. Sprinkle the soft brown sugar over the top of the cream mixture and set aside for ten minutes so the sugar can soak into the cream.

  4. Serve.

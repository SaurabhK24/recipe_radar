Simple cocoa frosting

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes enough for a 20cm/8in round cake

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Dan Lepard makes a stunning, yet simple icing with a strong chocolate flavour and dark colour.

By Dan Lepard

Ingredients

  • 25g/1oz cocoa powder
  • 300g/10½oz icing sugar
  • 50g/1¾oz butter
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream

Method

  1. Mix the cocoa and icing sugar in a bowl. Melt the butter for a few seconds in the microwave, or in a small pan over a low heat. Mix the melted butter and the cream into the sugar and cocoa, and stir until smooth.

  2. Use while smooth enough to spread, or thin with a little boiling water for a runnier icing.

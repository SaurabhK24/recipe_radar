Irish stout punch

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Ainsley Harriott
From Take on the Takeaway

Ingredients

  • 500ml/17fl oz Irish stout
  • 200ml/7fl oz condensed milk
  • 10 ice cubes
  • ground cinnamon or freshly grated nutmeg, for sprinkling

Method

  1. Pour the Irish stout and the condensed milk into a blender and add the ice. Pulse for one minute until light and frothy.

  2. To serve, pour into glasses and sprinkle with a little ground cinnamon or freshly grated nutmeg.

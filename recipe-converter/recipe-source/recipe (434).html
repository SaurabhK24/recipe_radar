Baked chocolate and orange mousse with chocolate sauce and orange cream

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 2

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the mousse

  • 75g/5¼oz dark chocolate, melted
  • 30g/1 oz unsalted butter
  • 1 free-range egg
  • 110g/4oz self-raising flour
  • 1-2 tbsp milk
  • ½ orange, zested

For the chocolate sauce

  • 75g/5¼oz dark chocolate, melted
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • boiled water

For the orange cream

  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream, softly whipped
  • ½ orange, zested

To garnish

  • cocoa powder for dusting
  • fresh mint leaves

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Melt 150g chocolate (for the mousse and the sauce) in a glass bowl over a pan of gently simmering water. The water should not touch the bowl.

  3. Grease two chefs' rings and place on a baking sheet.

  4. In a food processor, blend together the butter, egg and flour. Add the milk and blend again to make a smooth batter.

  5. Add half the melted chocolate and gently fold in.

  6. Fill the chefs' rings with the chocolate mixture. Sprinkle over the orange zest and bake in the oven for 8-10 minutes. They stay soft in the centre.

  7. Once cooked, remove from the oven. Carefully remove the ring moulds, dust with cocoa and place onto plates.

  8. For the sauce, add the sugar and a splash of boiling water to the remaining melted chocolate. Stir well.

  9. For the orange cream, gently whip the cream and fold through the orange zest.

  10. To serve, circle the mousse plates with chocolate sauce. Top the mousses with the orange cream and mint garnish.

Roasted rhubarb with vanilla yoghurt

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the rhubarb

  • 50g/1¾oz unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 sticks of rhubarb, cut into 5cm/2in lengths
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar

For the yoghurt

  • 75g/2¾oz Greek yoghurt
  • 1 vanilla pod, split and seeds scraped out

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. To make the rhubarb, melt the butter in a small ovenproof frying pan over a medium heat. Add the rhubarb and the caster sugar, stir well and transfer to the oven to roast for 10-15 minutes, until the rhubarb has cooked through.

  3. To make the vanilla yoghurt, place the yoghurt together with the vanilla seeds into a small bowl and stir to combine.

  4. To serve, place the roasted rhubarb onto a plate with a dollop of the yoghurt alongside.

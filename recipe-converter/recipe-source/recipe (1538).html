Chargrilled asparagus with olive oil and balsamic vinegar

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1-2

Recommended by

4 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 8-10 asparagus spears
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp balsamic vinegar, to serve

Method

  1. Drizzle the asparagus with the olive oil and season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  2. Heat a griddle pan until smoking hot and griddle the asparagus for about five minutes, or until charred on all sides.

  3. To serve, place the asparagus onto a plate and drizzle with balsamic vinegar.

Catalan potato and bean stew

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 6

Recommended by

5 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Simon Rimmer
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

For the garlic dip

  • 6 garlic cloves, peeled
  • 250ml/9fl oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sherry vinegar

For the potato and bean stew

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 50g/2oz breadcrumbs
  • 3 red onions, peeled and finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 750g/1lb 10oz potatoes, unpeeled, cut into large cubes
  • 250ml/9fl oz vegetable stock
  • 4 tomatoes, peeled, chopped
  • 1 can cannellini beans, drained
  • 1 handful fresh mint leaves
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 75g/2½oz butter
  • ½ lemon, juice only

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/gas 4.

  2. For the garlic dip, place the garlic into a baking dish and drizzle over the olive oil. Toss well to coat, then roast in the oven for 25 minutes, or until the garlic is soft and golden.

  3. Transfer the garlic and oil into a food processor, add the salt and sherry vinegar and blend to a smooth purée. Set aside.

  4. For the potato and bean stew, heat the olive oil in a pan and gently fry the breadcrumbs for 3-4 minutes, or until softened. Add the onions and garlic and cook for 8-10 minutes, or until the onions are soft and translucent.

  5. Add the potatoes and stock to the pan, then reduce the heat and allow to simmer for 10-12 minutes.

  6. Add the tomatoes and beans and simmer for another 4-5 minutes, then stir in the herbs, butter and lemon juice.

  7. To serve, spoon the stew into serving bowls. Serve the garlic dip alongside, or spoon over the stew.

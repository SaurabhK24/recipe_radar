Smoked trout, artichoke and Jersey Royal salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

By Simon Rimmer
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

For the dressing

  • 75ml/2½fl oz lemon juice
  • 225ml/8fl oz olive oil
  • 30ml/1fl oz sherry vinegar
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the salad

  • ½ red onion, very finely sliced
  • 200g/7oz hot-smoked trout, flaked
  • 6-8 Jersey Royals, cooked
  • 4 griddled artichokes, stored in olive oil, drained
  • few sprigs fresh dill
  • 30g/1oz cottage cheese, to serve

Method

  1. For the dressing, mix together the lemon juice, olive oil, sherry vinegar and salt and freshly ground black pepper in a bowl until well combined.

  2. For the salad, place all of the salad ingredients, except the cottage cheese, into a large bowl. Pour over the dressing and stir.

  3. To serve, divide the salad between two serving plates and top with a spoonful of cottage cheese.

Yellow jacket

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Lemon curd gives this whisky cocktail a sherbety kick

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp lemon curd
  • 2 dashes angostura bitters
  • 1 tbsp fresh lime
  • 1 tsp vanilla syrup
  • 1 tbsp white cacao
  • 35ml/1fl oz whisky

To serve

  • Freshly ground nutmeg

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.

  2. Strain the cocktail into a martini glass and garnish with nutmeg.

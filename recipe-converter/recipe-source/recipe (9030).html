Smoked salmon pâté

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 150g/5oz Greek yoghurt
  • squeeze of lime juice
  • 110g/4oz smoked salmon, chopped
  • freshly ground black pepper

To serve

  • rye bread
  • lemon wedge
  • fresh dill

Method

  1. Place the yoghurt, lime juice, salmon and freshly ground black pepper in a food processor and blend until smooth. Transfer to a serving bowl.

  2. Serve the rye bread alongside with the lemon wedges. Garnish with dill to serve.

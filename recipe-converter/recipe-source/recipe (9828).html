Stuffed apricots

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By The Vegetarian Society

Ingredients

  • 110g/4oz dried apricots, soaked overnight
  • 30g/1oz whole almonds
  • 30g/1oz pitted moist prunes
  • 40g/1½oz desiccated coconut
  • 1 heaped tsp ground cinnamon
  • 55ml/2fl oz maple syrup
  • 30g/1oz pistachios, chopped

Method

  1. Drain the apricots and allow to dry.

  2. Dry-fry the almonds and set aside until cool.

  3. Finely chop the prunes and mix with the coconut, cinnamon and maple syrup.

  4. Carefully spoon the mixture into the opening of each apricot and then gently stuff with an almond.

  5. Dip the open end of the apricot into the chopped pistachios and continue until all the apricots are filled and serve.

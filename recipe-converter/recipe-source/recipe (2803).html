Creamy leek and cheddar bake

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • ½ leek, finely sliced
  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 5 tbsp double cream
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 100g/3½oz cheddar, grated

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Heat the butter in a small saucepan and sauté the leeks for 3-4 minutes, or until soft.

  3. Add the cayenne pepper and double cream and simmer for 3-4 minutes, or until slightly thickened. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper and spoon into a small ovenproof dish.

  4. Sprinkle over the grated cheese, then transfer to the oven and bake for five minutes, or until the cheese is melted and golden. Serve immediately.

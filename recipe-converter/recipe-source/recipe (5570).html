Meatballs in a tomato and lentil sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the sauce

  • slice pancetta, chopped into cubes
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 55g/2oz tinned lentils, drained
  • 100g/3½oz tinned cherry tomatoes, drained
  • small handful fresh basil leaves, torn

For the meatballs

  • 1 slice of bread, crusts removed
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 2 sausages, skins removed
  • 1 free-range egg yolk
  • 2 tsp olive oil

Method

  1. Place the pancetta into a saucepan over a high heat and fry until crisp and golden.

  2. Lower the heat to medium and add the onion and soften.

  3. Add the sugar and vinegar and cook for two minutes.

  4. Add the lentils and tomatoes and simmer for five minutes.

  5. For the meatballs, place all the meatball ingredients into a food processor and blend until combined.

  6. Transfer the meatball mixture onto a clean work surface and using your hands, shape into meatballs.

  7. Heat the olive oil in saucepan over a high heat and fry the meatballs until golden and cooked through.

  8. Add the basil to the sauce and stir through, then transfer into a shallow serving bowl.

  9. To serve, place the meatballs on top of the sauce.

Radish with homemade mayonnaise

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the mayonnaise

  • 3 free-range egg yolks
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • pinch of salt
  • 100ml/3½fl oz vegetable oil
  • 50ml/2fl oz olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

To serve

  • 100g/3½oz radishes
  • 50g1¾oz rock salt

Method

  1. Place the egg, mustard and a pinch of salt into a food processor and blend together.

  2. Mix the two oils together and add half the oil to the egg and mixture, very slowly, while still blending. The mixture will thicken.

  3. With the blender still running, add the lemon juice and resume pouring in the remaining oil.

  4. Add the vinegar and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  5. To serve, place the radishes on a plate, with the salt and mayonnaise beside in separate ramekins.

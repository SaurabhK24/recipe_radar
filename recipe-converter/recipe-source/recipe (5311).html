Long Island iced tea

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This classic cocktail certainly packs a punch! It includes vodka, gin, rum and orange liqueur.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 15ml/½fl oz vodka
  • 15ml/½fl oz gin
  • 15ml/½fl oz light rum
  • 15ml/½fl oz orange liqueur
  • 15ml/½fl oz fresh lemon juice
  • 15ml/½fl oz freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1 tsp sugar syrup
  • cola, to top up
  • lemon wedge, to garnish

Method

  1. Place all of the ingredients into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.

  2. Strain the cocktail into a highball glass, top up with cola and garnish with a lemon wedge.

Greek-style salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz feta, crumbled
  • 1 beef tomato, diced
  • 2 tbsp raisins
  • handful fresh parsley, leaves only
  • handful fresh mint, leaves only
  • handful fresh basil, leaves only
  • handful fresh chives, chopped
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp red wine vinegar
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Place the feta, tomato, raisins and herbs together in a small bowl and gently stir to combine.

  2. Whisk the olive oil with the red wine vinegar in a separate bowl and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. To serve, pour the dressing over the salad and stir to coat the salad well.

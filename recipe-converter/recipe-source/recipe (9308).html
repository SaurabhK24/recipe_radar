Spicy baked wraps

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dining alone doesn't have to be boring. Try this quick and easy recipe for a cheese and bean wrap.

By Kate Morris and Sally Brown
From I Can Cook

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp cooked broad beans
  • 1 spring onion, sliced
  • 1 snack-sized salami sausage, sliced (about 25g/1oz)
  • pinch paprika
  • 1 tortilla flatbread
  • 50g/2oz chopped tomatoes
  • 25g/1oz cheddar, grated

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4. Line a baking tray with aluminium foil.

  2. Mash the broad beans in a bowl, then stir in the spring onion, salami, paprika and some freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Put the tortilla flatbread on the baking tray and spread over the chopped tomatoes. Spoon the bean mixture onto one half of the wrap and sprinkle over the grated cheese.

  4. Fold the wrap in half and cover with the aluminium foil, scrunching the edges into a parcel.

  5. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese in melted.

Glazed sweet and sour chilli carrots

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 15g/½oz butter
  • 55g/2oz caster sugar
  • 150g/5½oz baby carrots, tops trimmed
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • sprig fresh parsley, to garnish

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a frying pan over a medium heat and add the sugar.

  2. Add the carrots and the chilli powder and cook for one minute.

  3. Add the red wine vinegar and simmer for two minutes.

  4. Transfer the carrots to a serving dish and garnish with a sprig of parsley.

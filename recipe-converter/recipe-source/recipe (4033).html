Green chilli salsa

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 350g/12oz green chillies
  • 350g/12oz plum tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 medium red onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, chopped
  • ¼ tsp ground cumin
  • salt
  • freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Place chillies in a roasting tray and roast at 220C/425F/Gas 7 for 10-15 minutes, depending on the size of chillies.

  2. They should be blackened on the outside and soft to the touch.

  3. Remove any black skin from the chillies and pass through a fine sieve.

  4. Fold together with the tomatoes, garlic, onion, and cumin.

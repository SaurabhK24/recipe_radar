Steamed salmon with courgette ribbons

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

4 people

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the steamed salmon

  • 140g/5oz salmon fillet, with skin
  • 250ml/10fl oz chicken stock
  • 1 courgette, cut into ribbons with a vegetable peeler
  • drizzle soy sauce

For the sauce

  • 110ml/4fl oz white wine
  • 110g/4oz cream cheese
  • 2 tsp chopped fresh dill

Method

  1. Heat the stock in a medium-sized saucepan and place a bamboo steamer on top. Add the salmon skin-side up and the courgette ribbons. Steam for 5-10 minutes, until the salmon is cooked through, then drizzle with soy sauce.

  2. Meanwhile, for the sauce, place the wine into a pan over a high heat. Boil for 4-5 minutes, or until the liquid volume has reduced by half.

  3. Add the cream cheese and whisk to melt and combine, then stir through the dill.

  4. To serve, place the salmon and courgette onto a plate and drizzle with the sauce.

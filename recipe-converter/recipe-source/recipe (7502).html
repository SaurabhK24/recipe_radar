Ramos gin fizz (New Orleans fizz)

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 35ml/1¼fl oz gin
  • 1 tbsp triple sec
  • 2 tsp orange flower water
  • 25ml/1fl oz freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 tsp sugar syrup
  • 25ml/1fl oz egg white
  • 25ml/1fl oz double cream
  • soda water, to serve

Method

  1. Pour all of the ingredients except for the soda water into a cocktail shaker and shake well.

  2. To serve, strain the mixture into a highball glass and top up with the soda water.

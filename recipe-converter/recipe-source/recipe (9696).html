Stilton crostini with spring onion and chilli jam

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1-2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1 spring onion, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • ½ red chilli, chopped
  • 2 tsp tomato paste
  • pinch ground ginger
  • 50g/1¾oz soft brown sugar
  • 50ml/2fl oz red wine

For the crostini

  • 75g/2½oz Stilton
  • 2 slices ciabatta, griddled on both sides

Method

  1. To make the chilli jam, heat the vegetable oil in a small frying pan, add the spring onion and sauté until soft. Add the garlic and chilli and cook gently for one minute. Preheat the grill to high.

  2. Add the tomato paste, ground ginger, soft brown sugar and wine and simmer gently for six minutes, or until thick and sticky.

  3. For the crostini, place the Stilton on the griddled ciabatta and put under the grill for two minutes.

  4. To serve, slide the ciabatta slices onto a plate and drizzle over the chilli jam.

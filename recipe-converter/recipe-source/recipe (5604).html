Melon with Serrano ham

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • ½ cantalope melon, peeled and sliced
  • 2 slices Serrano ham
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • flatleaf parsley leaf, to garnish

Method

  1. Place the melon slices on a plate and lay over the slices of Serrano ham.

  2. Grind over some black pepper and garnish with the parsley leaf.

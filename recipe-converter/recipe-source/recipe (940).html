Blueberry and mascarpone tart

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4-6

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Housecall

Ingredients

  • packet of shop bought puff pastry
  • 2 tubs of mascarpone
  • 1 tub of clear honey
  • fresh blueberries
  • icing sugar to dust

Method

  1. Roll out the puff pastry into a large square and cook according to the instructions on the packet (approximately 7 minutes). Once cooked, leave the pastry to cool.

  2. Mix the mascarpone and honey together and spread over the cooled pastry.

  3. Decorate with blueberries and a light dusting of icing sugar.

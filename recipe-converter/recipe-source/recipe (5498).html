Marshmallow and cream scones

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 4 scones

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the scones

  • 150g/5½oz self-raising flour, plus extra for flouring
  • 50g/1½oz unsalted butter
  • 50g/1½oz caster sugar
  • 50ml/2fl oz milk

For the topping

  • 12 marshmallows
  • 150ml/4½oz double cream, whipped
  • cocoa powder, to dust

Method

  1. Preheat a baking sheet in the oven at 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. For the scones, place the flour and butter into a food processor and pulse until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs. Add the sugar and pulse again, then gradually add the milk until the mixture comes together to form a dough (you may not need all the milk).

  3. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface, knead gently and cut into four 5cm/2in rounds using a pastry cutter.

  4. Place the scones onto the preheated baking sheet and bake in the oven for 6-8 minutes, until golden-brown. Remove from the oven and allow to cool slightly.

  5. For the topping, place three marshmallows on top of each scone. Using a mini-blowtorch, colour the marshmallows all over. Top each scone with a dollop of whipped cream.

  6. To serve, place the scones onto a plate and dust with cocoa powder.

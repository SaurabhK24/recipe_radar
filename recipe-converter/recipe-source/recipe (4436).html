Honey, feta and thyme pancakes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Add a Greek twist to your pancakes for Shrove Tuesday. This topping sits on the fence between sweet and savoury and is so moreish.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 tbsp clear honey
  • 100g/3½oz feta
  • 2-3 sprigs thyme, leaves picked
  • 4 pancakes, to serve

Method

  1. Put the pancakes on serving plates and crumble over the feta.

  2. Drizzle with honey and scatter the thyme leaves over the top.

  3. Serve hot or cold.

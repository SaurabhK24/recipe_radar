Spicy beef koftas

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Makes 5

Recommended by

2 people

By Richard Phillips
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the koftas

  • 175g/6oz minced beef
  • ¼ tsp ground turmeric powder
  • 1 garlic clove, peeled
  • ¼ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • pinch salt
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp ground coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp olive oil, for frying

For the herb salad

  • small bunch fresh parsley leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. For the koftas, soak five wooden skewers in a bowl of water for ten minutes.

  2. Place all of the kofta ingredients except the oil into a food processor and blend together until smooth. Divide the mixture into five equal portions and shape the mixture onto the top half of the skewers.

  3. Heat the oil in a frying pan over a high heat. Fry the koftas on both sides for about 5-6 minutes until cooked through.

  4. For the herb salad, mix the parsley leaves with the oil and seasoning in a bowl.

  5. To serve, place the herb salad in the middle of a plate and arrange the koftas on top.

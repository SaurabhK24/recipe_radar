Tomato soup with basil oil

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • 200g/7oz tinned cherry tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 courgette, diced
  • ¼ pint vegetable stock

For the basil oil

  • large handful basil
  • 4-6 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and gently sauté the garlic and onion for 2-3 minutes or until soft.

  2. Add the tomatoes and courgette and simmer for 8-10 minutes.

  3. To make the basil oil, place the basil, oil and seasoning in a blender and blend until smooth.

  4. Serve the soup in a bowl topped with a drizzle of basil oil.

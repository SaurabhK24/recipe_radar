Watercress soup with crisp bacon

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 25g/1oz butter
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 300ml/10fl oz vegetable stock
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream
  • 75g/2¾oz watercress

To serve

  • 1 rasher smoked bacon, chopped
  • 1 tbsp Greek-style yoghurt

Method

  1. Heat the oil and butter in a pan and gently fry the onion and garlic until softened, about 3-4 minutes.

  2. Pour in the vegetable stock and bring to a gentle simmer. Add the cream and the watercress, stir well and bring to a simmer. Allow to cool slightly, then blend with a hand-blender until smooth.

  3. To serve, place the chopped bacon into a pan and fry until crisp.

  4. Pour the soup into a shallow serving bowl. Garnish with a dollop of yoghurt and sprinkle over the bacon.

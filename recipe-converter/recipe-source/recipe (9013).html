Smoked mackerel and wild sorrel pâté with apple relish

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the mackerel and sorrel pâté

  • 55g/2oz butter, melted
  • 300g/10½oz smoked mackerel, skin and bones removed
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • small handful wild sorrel

For the apple relish

  • 2 apples, cored, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh dill
  • 2 tsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ¼ onion, finely chopped

To serve

  • 4 slices baguette, chargrilled

Method

  1. For the mackerel pâté, place all of the pâté ingredients into a food processor and pulse until smooth.

  2. For the apple relish, mix all of the relish ingredients together in a small bowl. Transfer to a small serving bowl.

  3. To serve, spread the mackerel pâté onto the chargrilled toast and serve with the apple relish on the side.

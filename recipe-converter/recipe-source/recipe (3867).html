Glazed mango with a white wine sabayon

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Richard Phillips
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the sabayon

  • 4 free-range egg yolks
  • 55g/2oz caster sugar
  • 150ml/¼ pint white wine
  • 150ml/¼ pint double cream, whipped

To serve

  • 1 mango, peeled and sliced
  • ½ lime, zest only
  • ½ orange, zest only
  • fresh mint sprigs

Method

  1. For the sabayon, place the egg yolks, sugar and wine into a large bowl over a pan of gently simmering water. Whisk with an electric whisk for 15 minutes, or until the mixture is thick. Remove from the heat and continue to whisk until cooled slightly.

  2. Fold the sabayon into the whipped cream in a large bowl.

  3. To serve, fan the mango slices out on a serving plate and pour over the sabayon. Use a mini-blowtorch to glaze the top and garnish with lime and orange zest and mint sprigs.

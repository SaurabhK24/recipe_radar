Spiced rice frittata

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ¼ onion, finely chopped
  • ½ red chilli, finely chopped
  • large handful baby spinach, chopped
  • 150g/5½oz jasmine rice, cooked
  • 4 free-range eggs, beaten
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a medium-sized frying pan, add the onion and chilli and fry until softened but not coloured.

  2. Add the spinach and stir to wilt.

  3. Add the cooked rice and warm through for 2-3 minutes.

  4. Add the beaten egg, season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and fry over a medium heat for three minutes, or until golden-brown underneath.

  5. Turn over the frittata and fry on the other side for 2-3 minutes, or until cooked through. Cut into wedges and serve.

Beef taquitos

Preparation time

30 mins to 1 hour

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

By Gino D'Acampo
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

For the taquitos

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled and finely chopped
  • 500g/1lb 2oz sirloin steak, cut into 1cm/½in cubes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 3 tbsp tomato purée
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 100ml/3½fl oz beef stock
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
  • 8 ready-made flour tortillas

For dressing

  • 250g/9fl oz soured cream
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tbsp warm water
  • 3 tbsp chopped fresh chives

To serve

  • salad leaves of your choice
  • squeeze lemon juice

Method

  1. For the taquitos, heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over a medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and fry until softened.

  2. Add in the steak and fry for two minutes, until the meat starts to brown.

  3. Add the oregano, cumin, sugar and tomato purée. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper, stir well and leave to cook for a further minute.

  4. Add the beef stock, bring to the boil and reduce the heat to simmer for a further 5-6 minutes, until the sauce thickens and the meat is cooked through.

  5. Add the coriander and stir in.

  6. Fill the centre of each flour tortilla with the beef mixture and roll each up like a big cigar. Place two onto each of four plates.

  7. For the dressing, place the soured cream, cayenne pepper, warm water and chives into a bowl and mix well.

  8. To serve, drizzle the dressing over the taquitos and serve with a lemon juice-dressed salad of your choice.

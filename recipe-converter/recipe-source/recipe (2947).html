Crushed new potatoes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Bonnie and Peter Woods
From Recipe for Success

Ingredients

  • 600g/1 lb 5oz new potatoes
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp crème fraîche
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Bring a large pan of water to the boil and add the new potatoes. Boil until tender

  2. Drain and crush them with the back of a fork.

  3. Stir in the butter and crème fraîche and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  4. Serve.

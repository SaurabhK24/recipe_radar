Honey and mint oranges with vanilla cream

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Alex Mackay
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 orange, peeled and sliced
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh mint
  • 85ml/3fl oz double cream
  • ½ vanilla pod, seeds only
  • sprig fresh mint, to garnish

Method

  1. Arrange the orange slices in the bottom of a serving glass. Drizzle over the honey and scatter over the mint.

  2. In a bowl, whip the cream and vanilla seeds together until soft peaks form when the whisk is removed.

  3. To serve, spoon the cream mixture into the glass on top of the oranges. Garnish with a sprig of mint.

Herb butters

Preparation time

30 mins to 1 hour

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 2x200g/7oz rolls

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin

Ingredients

For the sage and apple butter

  • 200g/7oz butter, softened
  • 6 leaves of sage, finely chopped
  • 1 eating apple, grated
  • seasoning

For the three thyme butter

  • 200g/7oz butter
  • handful each of common, lemon, and orange thyme leaves stripped from the stalks and finely chopped
  • seasoning

Method

  1. Make the sage and apple butter: mash all the ingredients together (you need to taste the sage before including it in the butter as its strength varies dramatically).

  2. Pack into an ice cube tray or lay on cling film and roll to make a neat sausage shape and chill or freeze until needed.

  3. Make the three thyme butter: mash all the ingredients together.

  4. Pack into an ice cube tray or lay on cling film and roll to make a neat sausage shape and chill or freeze until needed.

Recipe Tips

Make up the butters and store in the fridge (for up to a week) or the freezer, to use as needed.

Beef satay with crisp noodles

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the beef satay

  • 200g/7oz rib-eye beef, cut into strips
  • ½ tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 lime, zest and juice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the noodles

  • 300ml/½ pint vegetable oil, for deep frying
  • 50g/2oz rice noodles
  • 1 tbsp chopped peanuts, to garnish

Method

  1. For the beef satay, place the beef into a small bowl with the soy sauce, honey, chilli flakes, sesame oil, lime juice and zest and leave to marinate for ten minutes.

  2. Thread the beef onto skewers that have been soaked in water. Heat the olive oil in a pan, add the skewers and brown them on all sides for 1-2 minutes.

  3. For the noodles, place the vegetable oil into a deep, heavy-based saucepan and heat until a small cube of bread sizzles and turns golden brown when dropped into it. (CAUTION: hot oil can be dangerous. Do not leave unattended.)

  4. Carefully drop in the noodles and cook for two minutes, or until puffed up and crisp. Carefully remove with a slotted spoon and drain on kitchen paper.

  5. To serve, pile the noodles onto a serving plate, top with the beef satay kebabs and sprinkle with chopped peanuts.

Pyramid tower of venison tartare

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz venison loin, chopped
  • ¼ red onion, chopped
  • pinch chilli flakes
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • Tabasco sauce, to taste
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh herbs
  • fresh chives, to garnish

Method

  1. Place the venison into a food processor and pulse until finely minced. Add the chopped red onion, chilli flakes, mustard and a few drops of Tabasco sauce, to taste, and pulse again to combine. Transfer to a bowl and chill for 10-15 minutes in the fridge.

  2. Lightly oil the inside of a Martini glass. In a bowl, mix together the red wine vinegar, olive oil and herbs and pour into the glass. Press the chilled venison mixture on top, and invert onto a serving plate. Carefully remove the glass and garnish the tartar pyramid with fresh chives.

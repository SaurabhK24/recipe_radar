Ricotta with honey and figs

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1-2

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 200g/7oz tub ricotta
  • runny honey, to drizzle
  • 1 fig, sliced

To serve

  • sprig of fresh mint
  • 1½ tsp flaked almonds, toasted

Method

  1. Turn the tub of ricotta out onto a plate.

  2. Surround with slices of figs.

  3. Drizzle with honey and top with mint and flaked almonds.

Toad in the hole

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 110g/4oz self-raising flour
  • pinch baking powder
  • splash milk
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 100g cooked cocktail sausages

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.

  2. Beat the eggs in a large bowl. Add the flour and baking powder and whisk in enough milk to make a thick batter, the consistency of double cream.

  3. Add the thyme leaves and stir again.

  4. Heat a the oil in a small, ovenproof, non-stick frying pan. Pour the batter in and place the cocktail sausages on top. Transfer to the oven and cook for 10-12 minutes, until the batter is golden and the sausages are cooked.

  5. Remove from the oven and serve.

Spaghetti 'pizza'

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 free-range egg
  • 100ml/3½fl oz whole milk
  • 3 tbsp plain flour
  • 125g/4½oz fresh spaghetti, cooked for 2 minutes, drained and chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 50g/2oz parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 slices prosciutto, diced
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 5 fresh basil leaves, torn

Method

  1. Ina clean bowl, combine the egg, milk, flour and spaghetti together until it forms a ball. Squash flat onto an oiled baking tray. Heat the grill to hot.

  2. Cover with the sliced tomatoes, parmesan, prosciutto, salt and freshly ground black pepper and put under the grill for 4 minutes.

  3. To serve, cut into six triangles and sprinkle with basil leaves.

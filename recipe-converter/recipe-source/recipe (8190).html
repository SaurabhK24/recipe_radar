Rose prosecco cocktail

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4-6

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Try Sophie Dahl’s delicate and fragrant take on a champagne cocktail – perfect for special occasions or summer parties.

By Sophie Dahl
From The Delicious Miss Dahl

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle prosecco
  • 4-6 tbsp rose syrup (available from specialist suppliers)

Method

  1. Pour the prosecco into the serving glasses and then drizzle the rose syrup gently on top, allowing it to trickle down to the bottom.

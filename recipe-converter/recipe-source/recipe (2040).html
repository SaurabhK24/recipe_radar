Chilli and basil pasta with chargrilled fennel

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 110g/4oz fresh spaghetti
  • 1 fennel bulb, sliced into 1¼cm/½in slices
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • ½ tsp harissa
  • 6 tbsp red wine
  • handful basil leaves, torn

Method

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to packet instructions. Drain and keep warm.

  2. Drizzle the fennel with one tablespoon of the olive oil and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Heat a griddle pan and fry the fennel for 4-6 minutes, or until softened.

  4. Heat the remaining one tablespoon of oil in a separate pan and fry the onion until soft but not coloured. Add the garlic and cook for one minute.

  5. Add the harissa and red wine and cook over a medium heat, until reduced to three tablespoons of liquid.

  6. Stir the cooked spaghetti through the sauce and add the basil leaves.

  7. Serve the spaghetti placed on a bed of the griddled fennel.

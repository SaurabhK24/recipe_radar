Avocado salsa

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1-2

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • ¼ red onion, peeled and finely chopped
  • ½ avocado, peeled and diced
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • squeeze lime juice
  • pinch chilli flakes
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Mix the onion, avocado and caster sugar in a large bowl. Stir in the lime juice, chilli flakes and seasoning.

  2. Spoon the mixture onto a serving plate and serve.

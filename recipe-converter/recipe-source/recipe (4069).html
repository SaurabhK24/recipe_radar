Griddled honey and lime mango wedges

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 mango
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp clear honey, plus 1 tbsp extra, to serve
  • 100ml/3½fl oz double cream
  • 1 tbsp icing sugar
  • 1 lime, zest only
  • 2 tbsp Greek-style yoghurt

Method

  1. Slice the mango in half, leaving the skin on and discarding the stone in the centre.

  2. Slice each mango half into three wedges.

  3. Heat the olive oil in a small non-stick frying pan and fry the mango slices, cut-side down for two minutes on each side.

  4. Drizzle over two tablespoons of honey for the last minute of frying the mango.

  5. Arrange the mango wedges on a serving plate.

  6. In a small bowl, whisk the cream and icing sugar together until soft peaks form when the whisk is removed.

  7. Add the lime zest and Greek-style yoghurt to the cream and briefly whisk again to combine.

  8. To serve, spoon the cream mixture into a small bowl and drizzle with the extra tablespoon of honey. Serve alongside the mango wedges.

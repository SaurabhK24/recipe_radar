Espresso and ice cream (affogato)

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves as desired

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Saturday Brunch

Ingredients

  • vanilla or chocolate ice cream
  • espresso or strong-brewed coffee

Method

  1. Place the ice cream in serving bowls.

  2. Pour the coffee over the ice cream and serve.

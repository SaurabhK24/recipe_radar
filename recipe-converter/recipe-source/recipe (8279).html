Salami tarts

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 10

By Jill Dupleix
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 500g/18oz ready-rolled frozen puff pastry
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 12 medium thick slices of salami
  • 2 tbsp pesto

Method

  1. Heat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Thaw the pastry, and roll out thinly.

  3. Cut into rounds slightly larger than the salami, brush with beaten egg, and top each round with salami (two slices if very thin).

  4. Bake for 10 minutes or until the pastry borders are puffy and golden. Transfer to a wire rack - they will crisp as they cool.

  5. Serve the tarts at room temperature topped with a little dollop of pesto.

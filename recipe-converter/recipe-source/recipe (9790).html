Strawberry ice lollies

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 8

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Simon Rimmer
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 300ml/10½fl oz yoghurt
  • 125g/4oz strawberries, hulled
  • 50g/2oz caster sugar
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Place the yoghurt, strawberries and sugar into a food processor and blend until smooth.

  2. Add a little freshly ground black pepper and pour into ice lolly moulds. Place into the freezer and serve when frozen.

Lemon mascarpone with cherry syrup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the lemon mascarpone

  • 85g/3oz mascarpone
  • ½ lemon, zest and juice

For the cherry syrup

  • 4 tbsp caster sugar
  • knob of butter
  • handful tinned cherries, juice reserved
  • 1 orange, zest only
  • splash Kirsch

Method

  1. For the lemon mascarpone, place the mascarpone, lemon zest and juice into a bowl and mix well to combine.

  2. For the syrup, heat the sugar in a pan until melted and caramelised. Add the butter, cherries and cherry juice and simmer for 6-8 minutes, until reduced.

  3. Stir in the orange zest and Kirsch and cook for a further minute.

  4. To serve, place the mascarpone mixture between two spoons to make a quenelle and transfer to a serving plate. Pour over the cherry syrup.

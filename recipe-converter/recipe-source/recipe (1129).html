Brie en papillote with spicy pear and chilli dressing

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the Brie en papillote

  • butter, for greasing
  • 75g/2½oz Brie
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme leaves
  • ¼ pear, cored, sliced
  • dash olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the spicy pear and chilli dressing

  • 15g/½oz butter
  • ½ pear, cored, chopped
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • 2 tsp white wine vinegar
  • pinch chilli flakes
  • dash olive oil
  • bread, to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Grease a 20cm/8in x 20cm/8in square of aluminium foil or baking parchment. Place the Brie into the middle of the foil or parchment, add the thyme, pear and a little olive oil and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Seal the foil or parchment at the edges to make a bag enclosing the ingredients. Place onto a baking sheet, transfer to the oven and bake for 6-7 minutes.

  4. For the spicy pear and chilli dressing, melt the butter in a small saucepan, add the chopped pear and cook for 2-3 minutes. Add the sugar, vinegar, chilli flakes and olive oil and cook for a further 1-2 minutes.

  5. To serve, place the Brie parcel onto a serving plate, open it and pour over the dressing. Serve with bread to dip into the hot cheese.

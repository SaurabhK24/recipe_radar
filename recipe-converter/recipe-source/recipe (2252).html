Chocolate mealie meal pudding

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 8

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Peter Gottgens
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 5 egg yolks
  • 125g/4oz caster sugar
  • 250g/9oz dark cooking chocolate, chopped
  • 250g/9oz unsalted butter, cut into cubes
  • 40g/1½oz mealie meal or maize meal
  • 20g/¾oz flour
  • cocoa powder, for dusting
  • softened butter, for greasing

Method

  1. Grease dariole moulds liberally with softened butter and dust with cocoa powder. Place in the fridge to chill.

  2. Melt the chocolate and butter over a bain-marie.

  3. Allow to mix thoroughly.

  4. Whisk the eggs, egg yolks and caster sugar together.

  5. When the chocolate and butter have melted, mix slowly in the egg mixture.

  6. Fold the mealie meal or maize meal and the flour in the chocolate mixture gradually.

  7. Fill the moulds 3/4 full with the chocolate mixture and bake at 200C/400F/Gas 6 until just firm on top approx. 7 min.

  8. The center of the pudding should be liquid. Serve with a flavoured ice creams see separate recipes).

Honeyed figs with Greek-style yoghurt

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream
  • 1 fresh fig, halved
  • Greek-style yoghurt, to serve

Method

  1. Melt the butter and honey together in a small frying pan over a medium heat. When the mixture starts to turn brown and caramelise, stir in the cream and reduce the heat.

  2. Place the fig halves in the pan, spooning over the sauce, for about 3-4 minutes, or until the fig is tender.

  3. Place the honeyed fig onto a serving plate and serve with a dollop of Greek-style yoghurt.

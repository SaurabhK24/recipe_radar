Spicy tagliatelle with parsley

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 spring onions, chopped
  • 3 rashers vegetarian bacon, sliced
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 x 400g/14oz can cherry tomatoes, drained
  • 150g/5oz fresh tagliatelle, cooked according to packet instructions and drained
  • 4 tbsp chopped fresh parsley, to serve

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a frying pan and fry the spring onions, vegetarian bacon and chilli flakes for 2-3 minutes.

  2. Add the cherry tomatoes and simmer for 2-3 minutes.

  3. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and stir well to coat in the sauce.

  4. To serve, stir the parsley into the pasta and place into a serving dish.

Waldorf salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

1

Recommended by

3 people

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the salad

  • ½ red apple, sliced
  • ½ stalk celery, chopped
  • 100g/3½oz cooked chicken, chopped
  • 50g/1¾oz walnuts
  • handful fresh rocket

For the mayonnaise dressing

  • 2 free-range egg yolks
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 290ml/10¼ fl oz vegetable oil
  • 1 lemon, juice only
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. For the salad, put all the salad ingredients into a large bowl and mix well.

  2. For the mayonnaise dressing, put the egg yolks and mustard into a mixing bowl and whisk together.

  3. Gradually add the vegetable oil, whisking continuously until all the oil is mixed in.

  4. Whisk the lemon juice into the mixture and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  5. To serve, mix the mayonnaise with the salad and spoon into a serving bowl.

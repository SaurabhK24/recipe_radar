French toast with caramelised apples and sultanas

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the French toast

  • 2 free-range eggs yolks
  • 150ml/6fl oz double cream
  • ½ vanilla pod,split, seeds scraped out
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • ½ lemon, zest only
  • 2 thick slices of ready-made brioche, cut into 10cm/4in rounds
  • knob of butter

For the sauce

  • 15g/½oz butter
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1 apple, cored and sliced into 8
  • pinch cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp sultanas

Method

  1. For the French toast, place the egg yolks, cream, vanilla seeds, sugar and lemon zest into a large bowl and mix thoroughly. Dip the brioche into the egg mixture.

  2. Heat the butter in a pan until foaming. Add the brioche and fry for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown. Set brioche aside and keep warm.

  3. For the sauce, melt the butter and sugar together in a pan until golden brown. Add the apple, cinnamon and sultanas to the pan and fry for 3-5 minutes, stirring occasionally to coat the apples.

  4. To serve, place the french toast onto a warmed plate and drizzle with the sauce.

Lime and coconut rice pudding

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100ml/3½fl oz coconut milk
  • 2 limes, zest only
  • 1 vanilla pod, seeds scraped out
  • 50g/2oz caster sugar
  • 100g/3½oz cooked basmati rice
  • sprig fresh mint, to garnish

Method

  1. Place the coconut milk into a pan with the lime zest, vanilla pod and seeds and the sugar. Bring to the boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for five minutes. Discard the vanilla pod.

  2. Add the rice to the pan and stir well.

  3. To serve, place the rice pudding into a serving bowl and garnish with the mint.

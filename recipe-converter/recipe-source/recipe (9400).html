Spicy prawn linguine with garlic, tomato and herbs

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

9 people

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 pinch chilli flakes
  • 4 tiger prawns
  • 6 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 50ml/2fl oz white wine
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh basil
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • 150g/5½ oz linguine, cooked according to packet instructions, drained

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a pan and fry the onion and garlic for 3-4 minutes, or until softened, then add the chilli flakes and prawns. Cook until the prawns have turned pink and are just cooked through.

  2. Add the tomatoes and wine and cook for 2-3 minutes, then stir in the herbs and lemon juice. Add the cooked linguine and stir well to coat.

  3. Pile the linguine onto a serving plate and serve.

Dolcelatte risotto with watercress oil

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the Dolcelatte risotto

  • 25g/1oz unsalted butter
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 100g/3½oz risotto rice
  • 50ml/2fl oz white wine
  • 300ml/10fl oz hot chicken stock (vegetarians can substitute vegetable stock)
  • 75g/2¾oz Dolcelatte cheese
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh tarragon
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh chives, to serve

For the watercress oil

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp watercress, chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. For the risotto, melt the butter in a pan over a low heat and cook the onion and garlic for 3-4 minutes, until softened.

  2. Add the rice and wine, bring to the boil and simmer until the wine is absorbed.

  3. Gradually add the stock, a ladleful at a time, stirring well between each addition until the stock is absorbed.

  4. When the rice is al dente and all the stock has been absorbed, stir in the cheese, cream and tarragon. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper and cook for a further 2-3 minutes.

  5. For the watercress oil, place the oil and watercress into a mini food processor. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and blend to combine.

  6. To serve, place the risotto into bowls, sprinkle over the chives and drizzle over the watercress oil.

Rum butter

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 300g/10½oz unsalted butter, softened
  • 250g/9oz icing sugar
  • 250g/9oz soft dark brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp warm water
  • 125ml/4fl oz rum

Method

  1. Beat the unsalted butter, icing sugar, brown sugar, water and brandy together until creamy, then refrigerate.

Baked garlic

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 6

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By baking the garlic it softens the sharpness of the flavour and brings out it's sweetness.

By Rick Stein
From Fresh Food

Ingredients

  • 6 plump bulbs of garlic, tops taken off
  • olive oil, to drizzle
  • rock salt
  • warmed rustic bread, to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Place the garlic onto a baking sheet and drizzle with a little olive oil.

  3. Sprinkle over a little rock salt and place in a hot oven for 20-30 minutes or until the cloves have softened.

  4. Squeeze out the garlic purée from each clove and spread onto warmed bread.

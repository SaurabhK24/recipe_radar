Chocolate soufflé with dark chocolate sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Richard Phillips
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the soufflé

  • 55g/2oz butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 55g/2oz plain flour
  • 3 free-range egg whites
  • 55g/2oz dark chocolate
  • icing sugar, for dusting
  • 25g/1oz dark chocolate, melted, to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4. Grease the insides of two ramekin dishes.

  2. For the soufflé, melt the butter in a small pan and stir in the flour to make a roux.

  3. Place the chocolate into a bowl set over a pan of simmering water (make sure the bottom of the bowl doesn't touch the water surface) and leave to melt, about 4-5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from the heat and allow to cool slightly.

  4. Whip the egg whites in a bowl until soft peaks form when the whisk is removed. Gently fold the chocolate into the egg whites, followed by the roux.

  5. Spoon the soufflé mixture into the buttered ramekins and place onto a baking sheet. Bake in the oven for 6-8 minutes, or until well-risen.

  6. Dust the soufflé with icing sugar and drizzle over the melted chocolate. Serve immediately.

Brandy butter

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 6

Recommended by

8 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Try this easy recipe for traditional brandy butter; perfect for slathering over mince pies or Christmas pudding.

Ingredients

  • 125g/4oz unsalted butter, softened
  • 125g/4oz icing sugar
  • 2 tbsp boiling water
  • 3 tbsp brandy

Method

  1. Cream together the butter and the icing sugar.

  2. Beat in the boiling water and brandy until smooth.

  3. Chill until needed and serve with mince pies or Christmas pudding.

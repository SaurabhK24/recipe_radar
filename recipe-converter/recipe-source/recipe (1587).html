Chargrilled steak with spiced cabbage and herb oil

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the cabbage

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • ¼ savoy cabbage, finely sliced
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the steak

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 200g/7oz rump steak
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds

For the herb oil

  • 1 tbsp finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped fresh basil
  • ½ tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. For the cabbage, heat the oil in a frying pan and fry the chilli and cabbage for 4-5 minutes, or until softened. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  2. For the steak, heat the oil in a non-stick frying pan. Season the steak on both sides with salt and freshly ground black pepper and fry for 3-4 minutes on each side.

  3. Sprinkle over the chilli flakes, garlic and sesame seeds and fry for a further two minutes on each side, or until cooked to your liking. Remove from the heat and set aside to rest for five minutes, then slice into three pieces.

  4. For the herb oil, mix the herbs with the oil in a bowl and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  5. To serve, pile the cabbage onto a serving plate, top with the steak and drizzle over the herb oil.

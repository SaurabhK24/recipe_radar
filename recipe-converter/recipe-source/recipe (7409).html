Quesadillas with cheese, tomatoes and chives

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 8 cherry tomatoes, cut into quarters
  • ½ tsp caster sugar
  • ¼ onion, finely chopped
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ orange, zest only
  • ½ lime, zest and juice only
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
  • 3 ready-made flour tortillas
  • 100g/3½oz mature cheddar, grated

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. Put the tomatoes, sugar, onion, 2 tbsp of the olive oil, the orange and lime zest and juice and the coriander into a bowl and mix thoroughly to combine.

  3. Heat the remaining oil in an ovenproof frying pan over a medium heat and gently fry one of the tortillas.

  4. While it's still in the pan, add a third of the grated cheese to the fried tortilla, then put a third of the tomato mixture on top. Put another tortilla on top and repeat the process with a third of the cheese and a third of the tomato mixture. Finish with the third tortilla.

  5. Transfer the pan and its contents to the oven for 2-3 minutes or until the cheese has melted.

  6. To serve, remove the tortilla stack from the pan, cut into wedges and put onto a plate. Spoon the remaining third of the tomato salsa around the outside of the stack and sprinkle the remaining cheese over the top.

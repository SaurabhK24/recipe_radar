Snowbound

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Stuck indoors in the snow? Might as well warm up from the inside out. Cinnamon syrup is available in coffee shops and suppliers.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 25ml/1fl oz vanilla vodka
  • 25ml/1fl oz Galliano
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 50ml/2fl oz freshly brewed coffee
  • 1 dessertspoon cinnamon cream (double cream whipped with cinnamon syrup)
  • grated nutmeg, to garnish

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients, apart from the cinnamon cream, into a latté glass and stir to mix.

  2. Spoon the cinnamon cream on top, garnish with nutmeg and serve.

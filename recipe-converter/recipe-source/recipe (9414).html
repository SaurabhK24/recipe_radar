Spigola barcoletta (sea bass boat)

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the sea bass

  • 1 sea bass, back-bone removed, belly left sealed
  • 50m/2fl oz olive oil
  • ½ lemon, juice and zest only
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the rice salad

  • 50g/2oz basmati rice, cooked according to packet instructions
  • 1 asparagus spear, blanched, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp flaked almonds
  • 1 tbsp pomegranate seeds
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

To garnish

  • 2 lime wedges
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh chives

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. For the sea bass, place the sea bass onto a baking sheet, and open it up like a boat shape.

  3. In a bowl mix, together the oil, lemon juice and zest, then season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  4. Pour the lemon oil into the sea bass and place into the oven and roast for 15 minutes, or until completely cooked through.

  5. For the rice salad, in a separate bowl, mix together the rice, asparagus, almonds and pomegranate seeds and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  6. To serve, place the sea bass on a large plate and fill the cavity with the rice salad. Garnish with the lime wedges and sprinkle with the chives.

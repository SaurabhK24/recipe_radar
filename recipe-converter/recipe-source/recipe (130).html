Apple and blackberry crumble with vanilla crème anglaise

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Ed Baines
From Put Your Money Where Your Mouth Is

Ingredients

For the crumble

  • 30g/1¼oz unsalted butter
  • 3 large Bramley apples, or similar tart cooking apples, peeled, cores removed, each apple cut into 0.5cm/¼in slices
  • 150g/5¼oz caster sugar
  • 80g/3oz fresh blackberries

For the crumble topping

  • 110g/4oz plain flour
  • 50g/1¾oz unsalted butter, diced
  • 55g/2oz caster sugar

For the vanilla crème anglaise

  • 290ml/10¼fl oz full-fat milk
  • 1 vanilla pod, split, seeds scraped out with a knife
  • 2 free-range egg yolks
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. For the crumble filling, heat the butter in pan over a medium heat. Add the apple slices and fry for 3-4 minutes, or until softened.

  3. Add the sugar and stir well to coat the apples. Continue to cook until the apples are tender and golden-brown and the sugar has melted.

  4. Add the blackberries and stir gently to combine.

  5. For the crumble topping, sift the flour into a large mixing bowl. Add the butter and sugar and rub together, using your fingertips, until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.

  6. Spoon the apple and blackberry filling into a shallow, oval, 23cm/9in ovenproof dish. Sprinkle over the topping mixture to just cover the filling. Transfer the dish to the oven and bake for 20 minutes, or until the topping is pale golden brown and the filling is bubbling.

  7. Meanwhile, for the vanilla crème anglaise, heat the milk, vanilla pod and seeds in a pan until almost boiling.

  8. Beat the egg yolks and sugar together in a bowl until pale and fluffy and well combined.

  9. Slowly pour the hot milk mixture over the egg and sugar mixture, whisking continuously, until all of the milk has been incorporated into the egg and sugar mixture.

  10. Return the mixture to a clean pan and heat over a low to medium heat, stirring continuously, until the mixture is thick enough to coat the back of a spoon.

  11. To serve, spoon a portion of the apple and blackberry crumble into the centre of each of four serving plates. Drizzle over the vanilla crème anglaise.

'Bitter that' cocktail

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Bitter citrus flavours shine in this sophisticated yet simple party cocktail.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 25ml/¾fl oz orange vodka
  • 35ml/1fl oz Kammerling's
  • 10ml/½fl oz apricot syrup
  • 25ml/¾fl oz golden grapefruit juice
  • slice of fresh apricot, to decorate

Method

  1. Put the vodka, Kammerling's, apricot syrup and golden grapefruit juice in a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.

  2. Strain into a coupette glass and garnish with a slice of fresh apricot.

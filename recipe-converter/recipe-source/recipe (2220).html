Chocolate chilli soufflé

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 3 free-range eggs, separated
  • 150g/5½oz caster sugar
  • 70g/2½oz dark chocolate, melted
  • pinch dried chilli flakes
  • dash vegetable oil

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Place the egg whites into a large, clean bowl and whisk until stiff peaks form when the whisk is removed. Gradually add the sugar one tablespoon at a time until all the sugar is incorporated and the mixture is glossy.

  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, melted chocolate and chilli flakes, then gently fold in the egg white mixture.

  4. Heat the oil in a small ovenproof frying pan and add the soufflé mixture to the pan.

  5. Transfer to the oven and bake for eight minutes, or until risen. Serve in the pan.

Rhubarb compôte with Greek-style yoghurt and shortbread

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 30g/1oz butter
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 stalks rhubarb, chopped
  • 2 oranges, juice only

To serve

  • 2 tbsp Greek-style yoghurt
  • sprig fresh mint, to garnish
  • 2 ready-made shortbread biscuits

Method

  1. Melt the butter and sugar together in a saucepan.

  2. Add the rhubarb and orange juice to the saucepan and simmer for ten minutes, or until rhubarb is soft.

  3. Pour the rhubarb into a dessert bowl.

  4. Serve warm, garnished with a dollop of yoghurt and sprig of mint, with the shortbread biscuits on the side.

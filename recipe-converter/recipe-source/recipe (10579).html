Tuna burger

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 55g/2oz tuna steak, finely chopped
  • ¼ onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 2 tsp olive oil

Method

  1. Place the tuna, onion, mustard and sesame seeds into a bowl and mix well. Using your hands, mould the mixture into a patty.

  2. Heat the oil in a frying pan over a medium-high heat and fry the patty for 1-2 minutes on each side, or until cooked to your liking.

  3. To serve, place the tuna burger onto a serving plate.

Roasted baby onions with savoy cabbage

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the roasted baby onions

  • 4 baby onions, peeled
  • 200ml/7fl oz hot water
  • 3 slices pancetta, cubed
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar
  • 25g/1oz butter

For the savoy cabbage

  • 50ml/2fl oz water
  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 2 slices pancetta, cubed
  • ½ savoy cabbage, thickly sliced

Method

  1. For the baby onions, place the peeled onions in a pan of hot, but not boiling, water. Poach for six minutes, or until soft.

  2. In a frying pan, dry fry the cubed pancetta until golden.

  3. Add the oil and baby onions and cook until the onions are golden.

  4. Add the caster sugar and butter and cook for a further two minutes.

  5. For the savoy cabbage, place the water, butter, pancetta and cabbage in a sauce pan, bring to the boil and cook for eight minutes.

  6. To serve place the braised cabbage in a bowl and top with the roasted onions.

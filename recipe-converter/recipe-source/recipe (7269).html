Prawn crackers with dipping sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 200g/7oz ready-made prawn crackers

For the dipping sauce

  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp runny honey
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh coriander

Method

  1. Place all of the dipping sauce ingredients into a small bowl and mix well.

  2. To serve, place the prawn crackers onto a plate with the dipping sauce in a bowl alongside.

Quick 'pizza' with goats' cheese

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 thin slices bread, cut from a round loaf
  • 50g/1¾oz tinned cherry tomatoes
  • 50g/1¾oz goats' cheese, crumbled
  • 2 handful rocket salad
  • 1 handful basil
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/390F/Gas 6.

  2. Place the slices of bread on a baking tray and top with the cherry tomatoes.

  3. Scatter over the goats' cheese, rocket and basil leaves. Drizzle over the olive oil.

  4. Cook in the oven for five minutes or until golden.

  5. Serve.

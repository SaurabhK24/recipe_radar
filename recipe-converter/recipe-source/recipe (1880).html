Chicken and courgette skewers

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken mini-fillets
  • ½ courgette
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce

To serve

  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • ½ orange, cut into wedges
  • small handful mixed red and yellow cherry tomatoes, halved
  • sprig fresh parsley

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. Cut each chicken mini-fillet in half and cut the courgette half into six pieces. Thread the chicken and courgette pieces alternately onto two skewers.

  3. Heat the oil in an ovenproof frying pan and fry the chicken and courgette skewers for 4-5 minutes, turning frequently, until they begin to turn golden-brown.

  4. Sprinkle over the soy sauce and transfer to the oven to cook for five minutes, or until the chicken is completely cooked through.

  5. Serve with a garnish of orange and lime wedges, halved cherry tomatoes and a sprig of fresh parsley.

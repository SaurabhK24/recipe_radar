Spicy roast cashews

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 5 tbsp cashew nuts
  • 2 tbsp soft brown sugar
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp cumin seed

Method

  1. Place the nuts into a frying pan over a medium heat and fry until golden.

  2. Place the sugar and spices into a mortal and pestle or spice grinder and grind together.

  3. To serve, transfer the hot nuts into a serving bowl and sprinkle over the sugar and spice mixture.

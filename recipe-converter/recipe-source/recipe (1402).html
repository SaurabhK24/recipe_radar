Caramelised satsumas with mint yoghurt

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the caramelised satsumas

  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 2 satsumas, peeled
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar

For the mint yoghurt

  • 2 tbsp Greek-style yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh mint
  • 1 tsp grated lemon zest, to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. Melt the butter in an ovenproof frying pan over a medium heat, then add the whole satsumas and sprinkle over the sugar. Gently fry for 2-3 minutes, until the sugar has dissolved, then transfer to the oven to roast for 4-5 minutes, or until the satsumas have caramelised.

  3. In a serving bowl, combine the yoghurt, honey and chopped mint.

  4. To serve, place the caramelised satsumas on top of the yoghurt, drizzle over the pan juices and sprinkle over the grated lemon zest.

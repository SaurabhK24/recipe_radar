Honey-baked figs with Manchego cheese

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 3 fresh figs, stalks removed
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tbsp clear honey
  • 2 tsp chopped fresh thyme leaves
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 30g/1oz Manchego cheese, grated

Method

  1. Using a sharp knife, cut a cross into the top of each fig. Push the fig down on a chopping board to open it slightly, then place in a 25 x 25cm/10 x 10in square of foil. Spoon over the vinegar, honey and thyme and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Fold the foil over to make a parcel and seal the edges.

  2. Place the parcel in a small frying pan over a medium heat and cook for 8-10 or until the figs have softened. Open the parcel and sprinkle with the cheese to serve.

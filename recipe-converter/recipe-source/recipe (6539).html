Pasta with almond pesto

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 55g whole blanched almonds, toasted
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 2 large handfuls fresh basil leaves
  • 1 large handful fresh parsley leaves
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 150g/5½oz shell pasta, cooked according to packet instructions
  • 1 sprig fresh basil, to serve

Method

  1. Place the almonds, garlic, basil, and parsley into a food processor and blend to a smooth paste. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  2. Stir the pesto into the cooked pasta in a serving bowl and serve garnished with the fresh basil sprig.

Ginger cheesecake

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 85g/3oz digestive biscuits, crushed
  • 2-3 tbsp honey
  • 200g/7oz mascarpone
  • 1-2 tsp stem ginger, chopped
  • 2 tbsp icing sugar

Method

  1. Mix the crushed digestives with the honey in a bowl.

  2. Spoon the mixture into a chefs' ring on a serving plate and press down.

  3. Meanwhile, mix the mascarpone, ginger and sugar together in a bowl to combine.

  4. Spoon the mixture into the ring and smooth off the top using a palette knife.

  5. Carefully heat the ring using a blowtorch and remove the ring.

  6. Serve.

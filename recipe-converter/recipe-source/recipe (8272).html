Salad of chicory and orange segments

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 chicory, shredded
  • 1 orange, peeled, segmented
  • 1 tbsp fresh chives, chopped
  • handful each fresh chervil and parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients into a bowl and mix well.

  2. To serve, pile high on a large plate.

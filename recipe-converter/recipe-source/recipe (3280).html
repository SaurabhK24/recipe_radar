Egg fried ham and stuffed mushrooms

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Gino D'Acampo

Ingredients

  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 55g/2oz plain flour
  • a little milk to taste
  • 2 slices ham, 1cm/0.5in thick
  • 2-3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 large mushrooms
  • 2-3 tbsp parmesan, grated
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • handful of rocket
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Make a batter by beating the egg with the flour. Add a little milk if it is too thick.

  2. Dip the ham in the batter.

  3. Pan-fry the ham in the oil for one to two minutes either side until crisp.

  4. Remove the stems from the mushrooms.

  5. Stuff the mushrooms with the parmesan and drizzle with honey.

  6. Bake in a hot oven for five to six minutes.

  7. Season and serve the mushroom and ham on a bed of rocket.

Thai salmon and lemongrass broth

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 200ml/7fl oz coconut milk
  • 1 spring onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 stick lemongrass, roughly chopped
  • 125g/4½oz salmon fillet, sliced
  • small handful fresh coriander
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Place the coconut milk, spring onion and lemongrass into a small saucepan and bring to a gentle simmer.

  2. Add the salmon and cook for 1-2 minutes. Stir in the coriander, salt and freshly ground black pepper

  3. Serve straightaway poured into a soup bowl.

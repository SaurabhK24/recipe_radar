Dirty martini

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

6 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Butter-wouldn’t-melt Sophie Dahl adds olive brine to this classic combo of vodka and vermouth to dirty up her Martini.

By Sophie Dahl
From The Delicious Miss Dahl

Ingredients

  • 70ml/2fl oz vodka or gin
  • 1 tbsp dry vermouth
  • 2 tbsp olive brine
  • 1 wedge of lemon
  • 1 green olive

Method

  1. Pour the vodka, dry vermouth and olive brine into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake well.

  2. Rub the rim of a martini glass with the wedge of lemon.

  3. Strain the contents of the cocktail shaker into the glass and add the olive.

Caramelised red onion and Stilton crostini

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ¼ red onion, finely sliced
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 1 tsp soft brown sugar
  • 2 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • 25g/1oz Stilton, crumbled
  • 2 slices baguette, toasted

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a pan, add the red onion and fry over a gentle heat for five minutes until softened.

  2. Add the garlic and sugar and cook for 1-2 minutes. Add the balsamic vinegar and cook for three minutes.

  3. To serve, place the toasted baguette slices onto a serving plate. Top with the caramelised onions and scatter with crumbled Stilton.

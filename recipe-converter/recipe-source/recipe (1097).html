Bread and butter pudding

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the custard

  • 150ml/4½fl oz whole milk
  • 150ml/4½fl oz double cream
  • 2 free-range egg yolks
  • 50g/1½oz caster sugar
  • ½ tsp vanilla essence

For the pudding

  • 50g/1¾oz unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 thick slices Madeira cake
  • 50g/1¾oz raisins
  • icing sugar to dust

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Grease a small ovenproof dish.

  3. For the custard, place the milk and cream into a heavy-bottomed saucepan and bring to the simmer.

  4. Place the egg yolks and sugar into a bowl and whisk until pale and smooth.

  5. Pour the milk and cream mixture over the sugar and egg yolk mixture and whisk until smooth.

  6. Add the vanilla essence.

  7. For the pudding, spread the butter onto the Madeira cake slices on both sides.

  8. Place the Madeira slices into the greased ovenproof dish and sprinkle over the raisins.

  9. Pour over the custard mixture, and transfer into the oven to cook for 15 minutes.

  10. To serve, remove from the oven and dust with icing sugar.

Grilled peaches with honey and yoghurt

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 peaches
  • 1 tbsp runny honey
  • 1 tbsp Greek-style yoghurt

Method

  1. Heat a griddle pan and grill the peaches for 2-3 minutes on both sides.

  2. Serve with a drizzle of honey and a dollop of Greek-style yoghurt.

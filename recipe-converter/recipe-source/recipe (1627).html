Cheddar soufflé

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1-2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 110g/4oz broccoli, cut into florets, and blanched for three minutes
  • 1 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream, warmed
  • 110g/4oz cheddar cheese, grated
  • 2 free-range eggs, separated
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • pinch cayenne pepper
  • butter, to grease

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.

  2. Mix together the mustard, cream, cheese and egg yolks into a bowl and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Add the broccoli and mix well.

  3. Place the egg whites into a bowl and whisk until stiff peaks are formed when the whisk is removed.

  4. Fold through the egg whites and cayenne pepper.

  5. Heat the butter in a small ovenproof blini pan and add the soufflé mixture. Place into the oven to bake for 5-10 minutes, or until puffed up and golden.

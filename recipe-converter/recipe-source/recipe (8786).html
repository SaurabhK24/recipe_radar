Shredded sprout salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 orange, zest and juice
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 100g/3½oz Brussels sprouts, trimmed and shredded
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 50g/2oz brie, cut into 4 slices

Method

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the oil, vinegar, orange zest and juice, and the shallot.

  2. Add the shredded sprouts, toss together and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Serve with the brie slices arranged on top.

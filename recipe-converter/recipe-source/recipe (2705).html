Crab and Taleggio omelette

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Recommended by

1 person

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 4 free-range eggs, lightly beaten
  • 3 tbsp spring onions, finely chopped (1 tablespoon reserved for garnish)
  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 50g/1¾oz tinned crabmeat
  • 50g/1¾oz Taleggio, finely chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Place the eggs and spring onions into a bowl and whisk together.

  2. Melt the butter in a small frying pan until foaming. Add the egg mixture and cook for two minutes.

  3. Preheat the grill to high.

  4. Sprinkle the omelette with the crabmeat and Taleggio, season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and place under the grill for four minutes.

  5. To serve, turn the omelette out onto a plate and sprinkle with the remaining tablespoon of chopped spring onions.

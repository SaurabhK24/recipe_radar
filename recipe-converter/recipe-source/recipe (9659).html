Sticky chilli chicken

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

4 people

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 chicken breast, skin on
  • 2 garlic cloves, skin on, lightly crushed with the flat of a knife
  • ½ each green and red chilli, chopped
  • 2 tbsp clear honey

To serve

  • green beans, blanched
  • 1 tbsp toasted flaked almonds

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. Heat the oil in an ovenproof frying pan and sear the chicken breast on all sides until golden-brown, about 4-5 minutes.

  3. Add the garlic cloves, chopped chillies and honey to the pan, stir, and transfer to the oven to cook for 8-10 minutes, or until the chicken is completely cooked through.

  4. To serve, place the chicken onto a serving plate with the green beans and drizzle over the pan juices. Scatter with toasted almonds and serve.

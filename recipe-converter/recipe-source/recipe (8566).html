Scotch pancakes with blackberries and maple syrup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Recommended by

3 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 115g/4oz self-raising flour
  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 100ml/3½fl oz milk
  • 2 tbsp icing sugar, sifted
  • sunflower oil, for brushing
  • 115g/4oz blackberries, to serve
  • maple syrup, to serve

Method

  1. Preheat a large heavy-bottomed frying pan.

  2. Place the flour into a bowl and make a well in the centre. Break in the eggs and whisk, adding a little milk as you go, to form a smooth batter, then whisk in the icing sugar.

  3. Brush the pan with a little oil then add large spoonfuls of the batter. Cook for 1-2 minutes, until small bubbles appear on the surface, then turn over and cook for another 1-2 minutes, until lightly golden.

  4. Divide the pancakes between two warm serving plates. Top with the blackberries, drizzle over the maple syrup and serve.

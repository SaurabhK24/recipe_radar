Golden mojito

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 8 fresh mint leaves
  • 1 lemon wedge
  • 2 tsp sugar syrup (sold as 'gomme' syrup)
  • 50ml/2fl oz golden rum
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • ginger ale, to top up
  • sprig fresh mint, to garnish

Method

  1. Place the mint leaves, lemon wedge and sugar syrup into a cocktail shaker and mash (muddle) with the end of a rolling pin.

  2. Add a handful of ice and stir well.

  3. Add the rum and lemon juice and stir well.

  4. Top up with ginger ale and garnish with a sprig of mint.

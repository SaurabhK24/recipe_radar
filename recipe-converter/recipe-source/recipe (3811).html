Gin and IT

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Roll back to the 1940s and make a proper martini with this gin cocktail recipe.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 50ml/2fl oz gin
  • 25ml/1fl oz sweet vermouth
  • dash bitters

To serve

  • orange slice, twisted

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and stir.

  2. Strain the cocktail into a coupette glass and garnish with the orange.

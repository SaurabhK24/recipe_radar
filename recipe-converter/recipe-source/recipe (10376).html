Toad-in-the-hole

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Recommended by

2 people

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz plain flour
  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 2 tbsp double cream
  • 5 tbsp milk
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 Polish Kabanos sausages

Method

  1. Place the flour, eggs, cream and milk into a bowl. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and mix well to make a batter the consistency of double cream.

  2. Heat the olive oil in a small non-stick frying pan over a medium heat and place the sausages into the pan. Pour over the batter and cook for 6-8 minutes, or until the batter has set.

  3. To serve, cut the toad-in-the-hole into wedges and place onto small plates.

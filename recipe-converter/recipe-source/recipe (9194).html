Spiced clementine smoothie

You need JavaScript to view this clip.

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4-6

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This tastes amazing - spicy and fruity without adding much sugar.

By Emily Angle

Ingredients

  • 180ml/6fl oz clementine juice
  • 40g/1½oz porridge oats (not rolled oats)
  • 320g/11oz frozen blueberries
  • 2 ripe pears, peeled
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • ½ tsp mixed spice

Method

  1. Put all the ingredients in a food mixer and blend together. (If you have time, soak the oats in the clementine juice for 10 minutes to soften them for a super smooth smoothie.)

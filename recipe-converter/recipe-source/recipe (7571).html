Raw salsa

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

23 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This salsa recipe is a real crowd pleaser – fresh, healthy, low-fat and made in one step. Enjoy with Mexican corn chips.

By The Hairy Bikers

Ingredients

  • 250g/9oz fresh tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 mild chillies, finely chopped
  • bunch coriander, finely chopped
  • salt, to taste
  • lime juice, to taste
  • 1 tbsp water

Method

  1. To make the salsa, combine all the ingredients together in a bowl and serve immediately.

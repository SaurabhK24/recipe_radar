Eton mess with passion fruit and mango salsa and chocolate sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 3-4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the Eton mess

  • 6 ready-made meringues, roughly crushed
  • 125ml/4½fl oz double cream, whipped
  • 125ml/4½fl oz Greek-style yoghurt
  • 1 orange, zest only

For the passion fruit and mango salsa

  • ½ mango, peeled and chopped
  • 1 passion fruit, pulp scooped out
  • 1 lime, zest and juice

For the chocolate sauce

  • 50g/1¾oz dark chocolate, melted
  • 100ml/3½fl oz double cream

For the spun sugar

  • 100g/3½oz caster sugar
  • vegetable oil, for greasing

Method

  1. For the Eton mess, place all the ingredients into a mixing bowl and fold together. Spoon into a large serving bowl.

  2. For the salsa, place the mango, passion fruit pulp and lime zest and juice into a separate bowl and mix together.

  3. Spoon the salsa over the Eton mess.

  4. For the chocolate sauce, add the double cream to the melted chocolate and stir until smooth. Leave to cool slightly, then pour over the Eton mess.

  5. For the spun sugar, heat the sugar in a frying pan until it turns a dark caramel colour.

  6. Sit the base of the frying pan into cold water briefly to cool it slightly.

  7. Hold an oiled metal steel or ladle handle and wave spoonfuls of the caramel back and forth across it to make fine strands of caramel.

  8. To serve, gently gather the strands of caramel into a loose ball and place on top of the Eton mess.

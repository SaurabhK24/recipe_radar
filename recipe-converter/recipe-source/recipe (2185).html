Chocolate and chilli mousse

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

A little chilli adds warmth to chocolate dishes without making them really spicy. Try making it in advance for a dinner party.

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz milk chocolate, chopped
  • 1 red chilli, chopped
  • 300ml/½ pint double cream, whipped to soft peaks
  • icing sugar, to dust

Method

  1. Place the chocolate into a bowl set over a pan of gently simmering water and heat, stirring occasionally, until melted.

  2. Stir in almost all of the chilli. Remove from the heat and leave to cool slightly.

  3. Fold the melted chocolate into the whipped cream in a large bowl, then spoon into a serving dish. Chill until needed.

  4. To serve, scatter with the reserved chilli and dust with icing sugar.

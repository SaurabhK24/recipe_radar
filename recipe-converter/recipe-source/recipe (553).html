Bandera

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 1 litre

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Andy Pearson
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

For the sangrita

  • 700ml/ 1 pint 5fl oz tomato juice
  • 200ml/7fl oz orange juice
  • 75ml/3fl oz fresh lime juice
  • 8 dashes Tabasco sauce
  • 2 level teaspoons of cayenne pepper

To finish

  • 50ml/2fl oz sangrita
  • 50ml/2fl oz blanco Tequila
  • 50ml/2fl oz lime juice

Method

  1. For the sangrita, pour all of the sangrita ingredients into a jug and mix well.

  2. To serve, pour each of the three drinks into a shot glass, then line up the three glasses.

  3. Sip from each glass in turn - first the lime, then the tequila, and finally the sangrita.

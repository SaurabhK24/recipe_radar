Fred Said

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Fruity flavours combine to make this clever cocktail.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 10ml/2 tsp fresh lemon juice
  • 35ml/1½fl oz bourbon
  • 20ml/4 tsp apricot liqueur
  • 25ml/1fl oz clear apple juice

To garnish

  • orange twist

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake.

  2. Strain into a martini glass.

  3. Garnish with an orange twist.

Lemon cheescake with chocolate and ginger sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz ready mademacaroons, crumbled
  • ½ tub ricotta
  • zest and juice of half a lemon
  • 1 tsp icing sugar

For the sauce

  • 200ml double cream
  • 50g/1¾oz chocolate
  • small piece root ginger, peeled and grated
  • ½ orange, sliced
  • fresh mint leaves, to garnish

Method

  1. Using a fiveiinch chefs' ring, press the crushed macaroons into the bottom to make a base.

  2. Mix together the ricotta, lemon and icing sugar. Fill the chefs' ring to the top and level off neatly.

  3. To make the sauce, melt together the cream and chocolate in a small saucepan over a low heat. Once the chocolate has melted, stir in the ginger.

  4. Remove the chefs' ring and serve the cheesecake with orange slices, chocolate sauce and mint leaves.

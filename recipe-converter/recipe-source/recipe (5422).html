Mango dipped in white chocolate

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 40g/1½oz white chocolate
  • 1 handful dried mango
  • tinned mandarin segments, drained

Method

  1. Melt the white chocolate in a bowl placed over a pan of boiling water.

  2. Dip the mango into the melted chocolate and serve on a plate topped with the mandarin segments.

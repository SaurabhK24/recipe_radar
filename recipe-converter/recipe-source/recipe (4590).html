Hot spiced cider

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Stefan Gates

Ingredients

  • 1 litre/35fl oz cider
  • 4 tbsp honey
  • 1 orange, peel only
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • small handful of whole cloves
  • 2 tsp ground allspice
  • generous splash of Somerset Cider Brandy

Method

  1. Put all ingredients into a saucepan, heat gently and stir occasionally for around 15 minutes (do not boil).

  2. When hot, strain into heatproof cups (leaving the flavourings in if you want) and enjoy.

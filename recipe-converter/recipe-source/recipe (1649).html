Cheese and lentil pancake

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the pancake

  • 50g/1½oz plain flour
  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 50ml/2fl oz whole milk
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the filling

  • 50g/1½oz cooked lentils
  • 50g/1½oz cheddar cheese, grated
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the herb oil

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. For the pancake, place the flour, eggs and milk into a bowl and mix well to make a smooth batter.

  2. Heat the oil in a frying pan and pour in the batter, swirling the pan to coat the base. Fry the pancake for two minutes.

  3. Place the lentils and cheese onto the middle of the pancake and fold in half to enclose the mixture. Fry for a further 2-3 minutes.

  4. For the herb oil, place the oil and parsley into a bowl, mix well and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  5. To serve, place the pancake onto a plate and drizzle over the herb oil.

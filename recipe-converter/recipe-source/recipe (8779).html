Shortbread with lavender and lemon dip

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 6 tbsp lemon curd
  • small handful dried lavender leaves, crushed
  • 85g/3oz fresh blueberries
  • 1 tbsp double cream
  • 6 shortbread biscuits

Method

  1. Mix the lemon curd and crushed lavender leaves together in a mixing bowl and spoon into two small glass bowls.

  2. Sprinkle the blueberries over the mixture and drizzle over the double cream.

  3. To serve, arrange the shortbread around the edge of the glass bowl.

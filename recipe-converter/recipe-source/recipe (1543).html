Chargrilled bananas with chilli butterscotch sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the chargrilled bananas

  • 25g/1oz butter, melted
  • 2 bananas, peeled, halved lengthways

For the chilli butterscotch sauce

  • 55g/2oz butter
  • 4 tbsp soft brown sugar
  • 1 tsp chopped red chilli
  • 125ml/4fl oz double cream
  • pinch salt

Method

  1. For the bananas, heat a griddle pan, brush the bananas with the melted butter and griddle for 1-2 minutes on each side. Set aside.

  2. For the chilli butterscotch sauce, heat the butter in a saucepan, add the brown sugar and chilli and stir until well combined and melted. Pour in the cream and salt and bring to the boil. Turn down the heat and simmer for 2-3 minutes, or until smooth and thickened.

  3. To serve, place the griddled bananas onto a serving plate and pour over the butterscotch sauce.

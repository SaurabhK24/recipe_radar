Parmesan crusted salmon

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

5 people

By Ross Burden
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 30g/1¼oz breadcrumbs
  • 30g/1¼oz parmesan cheese
  • handful fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 150g/5¼oz salmon steak
  • 150g/5¼oz broccoli, cut into florets

Method

  1. Place the breadcrumbs, cheese, parsley and garlic in a food processor and blend until combined. The mixture should form fine crumbs.

  2. Coat the top of the salmon with the crumbs and bake in the oven for ten minutes or until the salmon is cooked through and the crumb is golden and crisp.

  3. Meanwhile, boil a saucepan full of salted water and drop in the broccoli.

  4. Cook the broccoli for four minutes or until just tender. Drain.

  5. Serve on a plate topped with the salmon.

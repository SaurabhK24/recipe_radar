Onion rings

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

3 people

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • oil for deep frying
  • ½ onion, sliced into rings
  • 50ml/1¾fl oz milk
  • 100g/3½oz plain flour

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a deep pan. (CAUTION: Hot oil can be very dangerous. Do not leave unattended.)

  2. Soak the onion rings in milk.

  3. Place the flour on the plate.

  4. Remove the onion rings from the milk and coat them in the flour.

  5. Deep fry in the oil for 2-3 minutes or until crisp and golden.

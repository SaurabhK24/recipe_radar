Strawberry gelati, Italian style

Preparation time

30 mins to 1 hour

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4-6

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Hugh Fearnley-Whittingstall

Ingredients

  • 1kg/2¼lb fresh strawberries
  • 250g/9oz icing sugar
  • 100ml/4fl oz lemon (juice of approx. two large lemons)

Method

  1. Crush the strawberries and rub through a nylon sieve to extract the seeds. Stir in the lemon juice then whisk in the icing sugar.

  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream machine and churn until frozen or make granita style if you don't have a machine.

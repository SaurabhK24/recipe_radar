Orange olives

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 orange, grated zest only
  • ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  • 110g/4oz black olives, pitted
  • cumin seeds, to serve

Method

  1. Pour the olive oil into a bowl and stir in the garlic and orange zest.

  2. Stir in the chilli flakes then add the black olives. Stir again to coat in the oil.

  3. Transfer the olives to a serving dish and sprinkle with a few cumin seeds, to serve.

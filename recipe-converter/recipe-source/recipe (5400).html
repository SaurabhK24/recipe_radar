Mangetout salad with mustard dressing

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 red onion, peeled and cut into rings
  • piece red chilli, chopped
  • drizzle olive oil
  • 1 lime, juice only
  • 110g/4oz mangetout, very finely shredded

For the dressing

  • 1 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • splash red wine
  • 4-6 tbsp olive oil
  • pinch chilli flakes
  • 2-3 tbsp balsamic vinegar

Method

  1. To make the salad, place the onion rings and chilli into a large bowl.

  2. Drizzle the olive oil over and add the lime juice and mangetout. Toss together.

  3. To make the dressing, whisk the mustard, wine, olive oil, chilli flakes and balsamic vinegar in a jug or bowl until combined.

  4. Transfer the salad to a serving plate or bowl. Drizzle the dressing over to serve.

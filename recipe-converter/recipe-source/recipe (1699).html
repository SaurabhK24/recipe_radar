Cheesy fondue

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1-2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 200ml/7fl oz ready-made cheese sauce
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 75ml/2½fl oz white wine
  • 1 free-range egg yolk, beaten
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh chives

To serve

  • toasted ciabatta bread, rubbed with garlic

Method

  1. Place the ready-made cheese sauce, garlic, wine, egg yolk and chives into a saucepan over a medium heat and bring to a simmer.

  2. To serve, place in a large bowl and dip with the ciabatta bread

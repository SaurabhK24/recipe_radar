Spicy beans on toast with tomato and basil garnish

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ¼ onion, finely chopped
  • pinch chilli flakes
  • 2 rashers vegetarian bacon, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, seeds removed and chopped
  • ½ tinned baked beans, sieved

To serve

  • 1 slice white toast
  • ½ tomato, sliced
  • 25g/1oz grated cheddar
  • 5 fresh basil leaves, torn

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a frying pan and gently fry the onion and chilli flakes for 1-2 minutes, or until softened, then add the vegetarian bacon. Fry for 3-4 minutes, or until golden-brown and crisp.

  2. Add the tomatoes and baked beans and cook for 2-3 minutes to warm through.

  3. Serve the beans and bacon on a slice of toast, with slices of tomato and cheddar on top, garnished with fresh basil.

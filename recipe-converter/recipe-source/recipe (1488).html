Cauliflower, egg and blue cheese gratin

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 small cauliflower, cut into small florets
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream
  • 50g/1¾oz blue cheese
  • 1 free-range egg yolk
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 230C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Place the cauliflower into a saucepan of boiling water and simmer for 3-5 minutes, or until tender, then drain and transfer to a small ovenproof dish.

  3. Pour the cream over the cauliflower and scatter over the blue cheese. Make a well in the middle of the mixture and place the egg yolk into the well, then season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  4. Transfer the dish to the oven and cook for eight minutes, or until the egg has set and the cheese is bubbling.

  5. To serve, remove from the oven and serve immediately.

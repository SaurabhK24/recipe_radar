Banana and blueberry ice cream

Preparation time

over 2 hours

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4

This simple, luscious ice cream recipe requires no ice-cream machine - perfect when whipping up a quick dessert.

By Kate Morris and Sally Brown
From I Can Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon, juice only
  • 125g/4½oz mascarpone, at room temperature
  • 6 tbsp icing sugar
  • 12 blueberries, plus extra to serve
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • wafer biscuits, to serve

Method

  1. In a bowl, mix together the lemon juice and mascarpone until well combined.

  2. Stir in the icing sugar, then the blueberries and mashed banana.

  3. Transfer the ice cream mixture to a freezeable, lidded container. Freeze for at least two hours, or until solid.

  4. To serve, scoop the ice cream into bowls and sprinkle with blueberries. Serve with a wafer biscuit.

Recipe Tips

This recipe can be frozen for up to three days. Do not use defrosted mascarpone to make this recipe.

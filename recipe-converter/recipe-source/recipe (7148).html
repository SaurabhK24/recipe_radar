Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 150ml/ 1/4 pint peach juice
  • ½ bottle champagne or sparkling wine, chilled
  • a good dash of brandy

Method

  1. Simply pour all the ingredients into a jug and mix through.

  2. Pour into champagne flutes and serve as an aperitif

Bloody Mary

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

By Sophie Dahl
From A Taste of My Life

Ingredients

  • 40ml/1½fl oz vodka
  • 75ml/3fl oz tomato juice
  • dash lemon juice
  • ½ tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2-3 drops Tabasco sauce
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • handful ice cubes
  • lime wedges, to serve

Method

  1. Pour all the ingredients, except for the lime wedges, into a cocktail shaker and shake well to mix.

  2. Strain into a tall glass over ice cubes and garnish with lime wedges.

Chicken al marsala

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

By Gino D'Acampo
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 5 tbsp of marsala wine
  • knob of butter
  • pinch of flour, salt and pepper to taste

To serve

  • potatoes, boiled and tossed in salt, pepper and extra virgin olive oil

Method

  1. Flatten the chicken breast with a rolling pin or meat mallet between two sheets of cling film. Fry the chicken in the olive oil for three minutes each side.

  2. Add the marsala wine and allow to flame. As the sauce flames, remove the chicken from the pan and blow out the flame.

  3. Coat the knob of butter in flour and add to the pan. Add salt and pepper to taste.

  4. When the sauce has thickened, serve with the chicken and potatoes.

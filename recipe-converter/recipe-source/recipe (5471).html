Marinated feta with panzanella

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

3 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the marinated feta

  • 125g/4½oz feta, cubed
  • ½ lemon, juice and zest
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • pinch chilli flakes
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the panzanella

  • 2-3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 slices crusty white bread, roughly torn
  • ½ onion, finely sliced
  • 100g/3½oz cherry tomatoes, halved
  • handful fresh parsley, roughly chopped
  • ½ lemon, zest and juice
  • 2 tsp balsamic vinegar

Method

  1. For the marinated feta, stick cocktail sticks into each cube of feta. Put the lemon juice, olive oil, chilli flakes, salt and freshly ground black pepper freshly ground black pepper into a bowl, add the feta and leave to marinate for 6-8 minutes, or longer if possible.

  2. To make the panzanella, heat a splash of olive oil in a frying pan, add the torn bread and fry until golden and crisp. Put the fried bread into a large bowl and add the rest of the panzanella ingredients. Mix well and leave to marinate for 6-8 minutes, or more if time permits.

  3. Serve the marinated feta with the panzanella.

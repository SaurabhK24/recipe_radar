Prosperity

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

A champagne cocktail with sweet fruity notes.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 25ml/1fl oz gin
  • 1 tbsp crème de cassis
  • 2 tsp elderflower cordial
  • 25ml/1fl oz fresh golden grapefruit juice
  • chilled Champagne, to top up

Method

  1. Place the gin, crème de cassis, elderflower and grapefruit juice into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.

  2. Strain into a flute and top up with champagne.

Sticky stir-fried beef

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 spring onions, sliced
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed to a paste in a mortar and pestle
  • 3 baby sweetcorn, chopped
  • 85g/3oz sirloin steak, sliced into strips
  • 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds

Method

  1. Heat the sesame oil in a pan and gently fry the spring onions and garlic for 2-3 minutes to soften, then add the sweetcorn and beef strips. Fry for a further 3-4 minutes, or until the beef is cooked to your liking.

  2. Stir in the ketchup, honey and sesame seeds until the beef is evenly coated, then pile onto a serving plate.

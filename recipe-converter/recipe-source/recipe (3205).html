Duck and sweetcorn broth

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Garrey Dawson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 300ml/½ pint hot chicken stock
  • ½ red onion, finely sliced
  • ½ duck breast, skinned, thinly sliced
  • 40g/1½oz baby sweetcorn, chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • small handful fresh coriander leaves

Method

  1. Pour the hot chicken stock into a small saucepan, add the onion and duck breast and simmer for 3-4 minutes or until the onion has softened and the duck has cooked all the way through.

  2. Add the baby sweetcorn and season, to taste, salt and freshly ground black pepper. Simmer for a further 2-3 minutes.

  3. Pour into a serving bowl, add the coriander leaves and serve.

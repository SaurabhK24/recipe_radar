Pea purée

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Stately Suppers

Ingredients

  • 450g/1lb defrosted or fresh peas
  • handful of basil leaves
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 50ml/1¾fl oz natural yoghurt
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients in a blender and purée.

  2. Just before serving, place the purée in a saucepan and heat through. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and place in the centre of the plate.

Sweetcorn relish

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 1 jar

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This recipe uses fresh corn-on-the-cob. But if it’s the wrong time of the year for it, try using drained tinned corn instead.

By Alex Lomax and Ed Bond
From The Big Allotment Challenge

Ingredients

  • dash oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 tsp grated ginger
  • 1-2 chillies, chopped
  • ½ red pepper, chopped
  • ½ green pepper, chopped
  • 2 cobs of sweetcorns, kernels removed
  • 1 large tomato, seeds removed, skinned
  • 2 tbsp dark brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp cider vinegar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • pepper, to taste
  • 3 tbsp chopped coriander

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and cook the onion, garlic and ginger until soft.

  2. Add the other vegetables to the pan and cook, allowing any liquid to evaporate.

  3. Add the remaining ingredients, except the coriander. Cook for five minutes.

  4. Add the coriander and cook for another minute. Transfer to a sterilised jar and leave to cool.

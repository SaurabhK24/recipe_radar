Sweet meringue

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This delicious cocktail uses falernum - a sweet syrup flavoured with ginger, cloves, lime and vanilla.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp fresh lime
  • 2 tsp falernum
  • 25ml/1fl oz coconut water
  • 75ml/3fl oz dark rum
  • 1 tbsp apricot liqueur

To garnish

  • slice fresh mango
  • fresh mint sprig

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.

  2. Strain the cocktail into a tumbler filled with ice and garnish with a slice of mango and a mint sprig.

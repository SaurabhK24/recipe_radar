China blue

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Citrus and lychee flavours burst through in this vodka cocktail recipe.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 25ml/1fl oz citrus vodka
  • 25ml/1fl oz blue curacao (available from online retailers)
  • 25ml/1fl oz golden grapefruit juice
  • 25ml/1fl oz lychee juice

To serve

  • orange slice
  • lychee, peeled

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients in a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.

  2. Strain into a tall glass over ice and garnish with an orange slice and lychee.

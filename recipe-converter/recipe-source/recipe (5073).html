Lemon and lime cheesecake

Preparation time

30 mins to 1 hour

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 2

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This no-bake cheesecake recipe is perfect for cooking with children. Try serving it with fresh raspberries on top.

By Kate Morris and Sally Brown
From I Can Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 oat digestive-style biscuits
  • 20g/¾oz butter, melted
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • ½ lime, juice only
  • 1 tbsp icing sugar
  • 125g/4½ oz mascarpone

Method

  1. Put the biscuits in sandwich bag and use a rolling pin to gently bash them until small crumbs.

  2. Tip the crushed biscuits into a bowl and pour in the melted butter. Stir together until well combined, then spoon the crumbs into the bottom of two small glasses and press down firmly.

  3. Mix the lemon and lime juice, mascarpone and icing sugar together in a bowl until well combined.

  4. Divide the mixture between the two glasses and chill in the fridge for two hours.

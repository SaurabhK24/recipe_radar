Preparation time

overnight

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Makes 1 large jar

Recommended by

8 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1kg/2¼lb small pickling onions or shallots, peeled
  • 30g/1oz salt
  • 1 tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 1l/1¾pt malt vinegar
  • 180g/6oz granulated sugar

Method

  1. Put the onions in a large bowl with the salt and mix together. Cover and leave overnight.

  2. Rinse well in cold water and allow to dry as much as possible.

  3. Pack the onions into sterilised jars.

  4. Put the vinegar, chilli flakes and sugar into a pan and slowly bring to the boil to dissolve the sugar.

  5. Pour the vinegar over the onions and seal the jars.

  6. Store in a cool, dark cupboard for two weeks before eating.

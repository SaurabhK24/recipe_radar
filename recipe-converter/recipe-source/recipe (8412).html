Sashimi of halibut

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½ very fresh skinless halibut steak, thinly sliced
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ tomato, cut into wedges, to garnish

Method

  1. Arrange the sliced halibut on a serving plate.

  2. Drizzle with lemon juice, season with freshly ground black pepper and garnish with tomato wedges. Serve chilled.

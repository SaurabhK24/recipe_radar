G&T

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Andy Pearson
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 80ml/3fl oz gin
  • 40ml/1½fl oz orange liqueur
  • 40ml/1½fl oz freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • scoop ice
  • 1 free-range egg white
  • 400ml/14fl oz flat tonic water
  • 2 tbsp sugar syrup
  • 2 pinches grated lemon zest, to garnish

Method

  1. Pour the gin, orange liqueur, lemon juice and ice into a cocktail shaker and shake well. Double strain the mixture into two chilled Martini glasses.

  2. Place the egg white, flat tonic water and sugar syrup into a clean cocktail shaker. Shake well until frothy. (If you have a cocktail strainer, remove the spring and add it to the mixture to help it to froth up.)

  3. Remove the spring, if using, and strain the egg and water mixture over the gin and orange mixture in the Martini glasses.

  4. To serve, sprinkle over the lemon zest.

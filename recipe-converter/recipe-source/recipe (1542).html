Chargrilled asparagus with sunblush tomatoes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Maria Elia
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 asparagus spears
  • 55g/2oz sunblush tomatoes
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh chives
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar

Method

  1. Heat up a griddle pan over a medium heat. Pour the olive oil into a shallow bowl and coat the asparagus with the oil.

  2. Place the oiled asparagus and sunblush tomatoes onto a griddle pan. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Griddle for 4-5 minutes, turning over halfway through cooking, or until golden-brown griddle marks appear on the asparagus and tomatoes.

  3. Scatter the chives and coriander over the asparagus and tomatoes. Transfer to a serving plate.

  4. Add the vinegar to the pan and simmer for 10-20 seconds, stirring until slightly reduced. Pour the reduced vinegar over the asparagus and tomatoes and serve.

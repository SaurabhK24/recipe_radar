Singapore sling

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Try our recipe for a classic Singapore sling using gin, cherry heering and ginger ale.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 25ml/1fl oz gin
  • 25ml/1fl oz cherry heering (available from online retailers)
  • 1 tsp benedictine liqueur
  • ginger ale, to top up

To serve

  • slice lime peel

Method

  1. Pour the ingredients into a tall glass and top up with ginger ale.

  2. Garnish with a lime peel and serve.

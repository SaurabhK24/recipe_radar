Chicken and pepper stew

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

6 people

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 75g/2½oz chicken fillets
  • ½ jar roasted red peppers, chopped
  • 75ml/2½fl oz white wine
  • 1 tbsp double cream
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a small saucepan.

  2. Add the chicken fillets and cook until golden on all sides.

  3. Add the red peppers and fry for two minutes.

  4. Add the wine, turn up the heat and bubble for one minute.

  5. Stir in the double cream and season. Cook for one minute, or until the chicken is cooked through.

  6. Pour into a serving bowl and serve.

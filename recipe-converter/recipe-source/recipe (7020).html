Porcini bread and butter pudding

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 5 slices ciabatta, crusts removed
  • butter, for greasing
  • ½ shallot, finely sliced
  • 3 free-range eggs
  • 55ml/2fl oz double cream
  • 25g/1oz dried porcini, rehydrated in warm water and finely chopped
  • 50g/2oz Pecorino, sliced
  • 2 tsp chopped fresh dill

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Layer the bread in a small oven dish, greased with butter.

  3. Place the shallot, eggs, double cream and porcini into a bowl and mix together. Pour the cream mixture over the bread.

  4. Place the Pecorino on top of the bread and place into the oven to bake for 12 minutes, or until golden and cooked through.

  5. Remove from the oven and serve garnished with the chopped dill.

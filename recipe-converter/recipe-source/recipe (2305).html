Chorizo and potato hash with poached egg

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

4 people

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 225g/8oz chorizo, sliced
  • 85g/3oz new potatoes, cooked and sliced
  • 1 spring onion, chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 free-range egg, poached, to serve

Method

  1. Heat a frying pan and fry the chorizo for 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently.

  2. Add the sliced cooked potatoes and the spring onion and cook gently, stirring occasionally, for five minutes.

  3. Slightly crush the potatoes with a fork and mix well to combine the ingredients. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  4. To serve, place the chorizo and potato hash onto a serving plate and top with a poached egg.

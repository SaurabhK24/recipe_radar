Snapper with pineapple salsa

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the snapper

  • 1 red snapper fillet
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the salsa

  • 100g/3½oz pineapple, finely chopped
  • 3 tbsp chopped coriander
  • 1 lime, juice only
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh mint
  • 5 salad onions, trimmed
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. For the snapper, heat a griddle pan until smoking. Rub the snapper fillet with oil and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  2. Place the snapper onto the hot griddle and char-grill for two minutes on each side, or until cooked through.

  3. To make the pineapple salsa, place all of the salsa ingredients into a large bowl and mix together well.

  4. To serve, place the salsa in the centre of a plate with the snapper fillet on top and garnish with the salad onions.

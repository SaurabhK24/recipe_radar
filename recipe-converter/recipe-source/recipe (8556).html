Scones with stewed plums

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the scones

  • 85g/3oz self-raising flour
  • Greek yoghurt, to bind

For the plums

  • 3 plums, halved and stones removed
  • 1 jar blackcurrant conserve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.

  2. In a bowl, mix together the flour and enough yoghurt to form a firm dough. Roll out on a flat surface to 2½/1in thickness and using a chefs' ring, cut into rounds.

  3. Heat a frying pan and add the dough rounds. Dry fry until the scones have puffed up and browned a little.

  4. For the plums, place the plums on a baking sheet. Place in the oven and bake for five minutes, until they have softened slightly.

  5. Meanwhile, place the blackcurrant conserve in a small saucepan with a splash of water and warm over a low heat.

  6. Remove the plums from the oven and cover with the warm blackcurrant conserve. Return to the oven for a further three minutes.

  7. To serve, place the scones onto a dish with the plum mixture poured over.

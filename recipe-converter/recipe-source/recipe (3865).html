Glazed gammon with sweet potato bubble and squeak

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 sweet potato, chopped
  • ½ onion, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 150g/5¼oz Brussels sprouts, shredded
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1 gammon steak
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Place the sweet potato in a saucepan of cold salted water and bring to the boil.

  2. Turn the heat down to a slow simmer and cook the potatoes for ten minutes.

  3. Meanwhile, gently fry onion and garlic in the oil for 3-5 minutes until softened.

  4. Add the Brussels sprouts and stir fry for a further three minutes or until they are softened.

  5. When the potatoes are cooked, strain them and place back in the pan. With a potato masher, mash the potatoes until they are lump-free. Add the butter and the salt and freshly ground black pepper and stir through.

  6. Add the onion and sprouts. Mix well. Set aside and keep warm while you cook the meat.

  7. Heat a griddle pan until smoking. With a pastry brush, brush the mustard and honey onto the gammon steaks.

  8. Griddle them for 2-3 minutes on each side.

  9. Finish them in the oven for a further three minutes.

  10. Serve the bubble and squeak on a plate and top with the glazed gammon.

Sausage patties

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 3 sausages
  • 1 lime, juice only
  • 1 handful fresh coriander, chopped
  • 50g/1¾oz, plain flour, to dust

For the fig jam

  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • 2 figs, sliced
  • 50g/1¾oz brown sugar
  • 25ml/1 fl oz white wine vinegar

Method

  1. Mix the sausage meat, the lime juice and chopped coriander in a bowl.

  2. Mould into three patties and dust in the flour.

  3. Fry in a frying pan for 6-8 minutes or until cooked through.

  4. For the jam, in a pan add the onions with the figs, brown sugar and the white wine vinegar.

  5. Cook for eight minutes on a low heat until soft and syrupy.

  6. To serve, place the patties on a plate with the fig jam on the side.

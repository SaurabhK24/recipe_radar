Spinach, orange and pine nut salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 200g/7oz spinach leaves
  • 2 oranges, peeled and segmented
  • 100g/3½oz pine nuts, toasted in a frying pan until golden
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. Mix all of the ingredients together in a bowl and serve.

Ginger apple fizz

Preparation time

30 mins to 1 hour

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 8-10

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By The Vegetarian Society

Ingredients

  • 1oz/25g raisins
  • 3½pts/2L apple juice
  • 3½pts/2L ginger beer
  • 1in/2cm piece root ginger (grated with skin on and squeezed for the juice - should yield about 1 tbsp/15ml)

Method

  1. Soak the raisins in the apple juice for 1 hour.

  2. Add the ginger beer and root ginger juice. Serve at once.

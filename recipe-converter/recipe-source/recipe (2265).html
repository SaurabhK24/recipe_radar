Chocolate, orange and fig mousse

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 85-110ml/3-4 fl oz double cream
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp icing sugar
  • 1 orange, juice only
  • 1 orange, segmented
  • 1 fig, sliced into rounds
  • 1 tbsp Greek-style yoghurt

Method

  1. Whisk the cream until just holding its shape.

  2. Fold in the cocoa, sugar and orange juice.

  3. In a glass, layer the mousse and orange segments.

  4. Top with fig rounds and a dollop of Greek-style yoghurt.

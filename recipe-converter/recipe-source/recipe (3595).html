French martini

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Toast the glamorous lifestyle with this enlivening French martini recipe.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 35ml/1½fl oz vodka
  • 1 tbsp raspberry liqueur
  • 1 lemon wedge
  • 25ml/1fl oz pressed pineapple juice

For garnish

  • 1 blackberry

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.

  2. Strain the drink into a cocktail glass, garnish with a blackberry and serve.

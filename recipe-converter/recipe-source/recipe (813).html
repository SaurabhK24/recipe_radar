Berry nice

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This mocktail recipe is perfect for any summer dinner party, and will be loved by children and adults alike!

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 15ml/½fl oz lemon juice
  • 5ml/1 tsp agave syrup (or plain sugar syrup)
  • 2 fresh blackberries
  • 2 fresh raspberries
  • 50ml/2fl oz cranberry juice

To garnish

  • 1 blackberry

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.

  2. Strain the cocktail into a martini glass, garnish with a berry and serve.

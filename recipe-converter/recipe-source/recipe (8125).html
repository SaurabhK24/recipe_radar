Roast sweet potato and lime soup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 25g/1oz butter
  • ½ sweet potato, peeled and cubed
  • 1 lime, cut in half
  • 1 clove garlic
  • ½ pint chicken or vegetable stock
  • 1 tbsp honey

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan. Add the potato, lime and the garlic. Cook for three minutes.

  2. Add the chicken or vegetable stock and honey. Cook for ten minutes. Remove the lime and discard. Blend the soup with a hand blender until smooth.

  3. Serve in a large soup bowl.

Penne with chilli, garlic, herbs and olive oil

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp fresh parsley leaves
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • a pinch of salt
  • 150g/5½oz penne pasta, cooked according to packet instructions
  • 6 fresh basil leaves, torn
  • a few shavings of fresh white truffle, to serve

Method

  1. Place the oil, parsley, garlic, chilli flakes and salt into a food processor and blend until smooth.

  2. Stir the mixture into the cooked penne pasta, and pile onto a plate. Garnish with fresh torn basil leaves and the shavings of white truffle.

Potato and leek frittata

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • ¼ potato, sliced
  • ½ leek, chopped
  • 2 free-range eggs, beaten
  • handful fresh basil leaves

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan. Add the potatoes and leeks sauté them for 4-5 minutes or until they are softened.

  2. Add the eggs and basil and gently stir around the pan until the eggs begin to set.

  3. Carefully turn the frittata over and cook for a further minute, or until cooked through.

  4. Transfer to a warmed plate and serve immediately.

Banana and rum milkshake

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Levi Roots
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 banana, peeled and chopped
  • 2 tbsp dark rum
  • 250g/9oz vanilla ice cream
  • 100ml/3½fl oz milk
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • ½ tsp grated nutmeg
  • 4 ice cubes, to serve

Method

  1. Place all of the milkshake ingredients, except the ice cubes, into a blender and pulse until smooth. Serve in a tall glass over ice cubes.

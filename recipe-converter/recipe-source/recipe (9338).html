Spicy tortilla nests

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 4 tortilla nests

Use up leftover cooked mince in this fun kids' recipe. It makes a great little starter or snack.

By Neil Strawson and Richard Hunt
From I Can Cook on the Go

Ingredients

  • olive oil, for greasing
  • 1 large flour tortilla
  • 2 tbsp cooked beef mince
  • 2 tbsp cooked kidney beans
  • 2 tbsp tinned ratatouille
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • 15g/½ oz mature cheddar, grated

Method

  1. Preheat the oven at 180C/350F/Gas 4. Grease four holes of a muffin tray.

  2. Cut the tortilla wrap into four, then press a quarter of the wrap into each muffin hole.

  3. Mix the mince, kidney beans, ratatouille and paprika together in a bowl. Divide the mixture between the muffin holes. Sprinkle over the cheese.

  4. Bake in the oven for 15 minutes.

Roquefort, rocket and honey bruschetta

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 slices ciabatta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ bunch rocket
  • 50g/1¾oz Roquefort, crumbled
  • 1 tbsp honey

Method

  1. Rub the ciabatta all over with olive oil. Heat a griddle pan and griddle the bread on both sides, until toasted.

  2. Place a slice of griddled ciabatta onto a serving plate and top with the rocket. Place the other slice of griddled ciabatta on top, followed by the Roquefort.

  3. To serve, drizzle with honey.

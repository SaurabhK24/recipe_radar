Chocolate fruits

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Rick Stein
From Fresh Food

Ingredients

  • 200g/7oz good quality white chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 210ml/7fl oz double cream
  • 700g/1½lb frozen mixed summer berries

Method

  1. Heat some water to boiling point in a saucepan big enough to sit a large heatproof bowl.

  2. Place the chocolate into the bowl and place it over the pan of boiling water. Don't allow the base of the bowl to come into contact with the water.

  3. Melt the chocolate and then add the double cream and incorporate it into the chocolate.

  4. Place the frozen berries in a serving dish and pour over the hot chocolate mixture and serve.

Chocolate pecan crunch cakes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 55g/2oz dark chocolate, chopped
  • 30g/1oz pecan nuts, chopped
  • 2 tbsp bran flakes, crushed

Method

  1. Place the chocolate into a heatproof bowl set over a pan of barely simmering water. Heat, stirring occasionally, until melted and smooth.

  2. Add the pecan nuts and bran flakes and stir to combine.

  3. Spoon the chocolate mixture into paper muffin cases and leave to cool. Place into the freezer to set completely.

  4. To serve, remove the chocolate pecan crunch cakes from the freezer and place onto plates.

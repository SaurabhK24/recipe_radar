Grilled fruit with mascarpone

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This dessert is so simple to pull together and tastes wonderful. A great dish to serve up for an evening with friends.

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 6 ripe plums
  • 2 meringue nests
  • 250g tub mascarpone cheese
  • 4 tbsp caster sugar

Method

  1. Preheat the oven 200C/180C Fan/Gas 6.

  2. Halve and stone the plums and put them, hollow side up, in a heatproof dish.

  3. Break up the meringues and sprinkle them over the top, making sure lots fall into the hollows of the fruit.

  4. Place dollops of mascarpone cheese on top of the meringues and fruit.

  5. Sprinkle with the sugar and place the dish in the oven.

  6. Cook for 20 minutes, removing from the oven when the fruit is tender with a crisp crust.

  7. Serve warm.

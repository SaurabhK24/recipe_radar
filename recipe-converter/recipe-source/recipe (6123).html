Grilled orange and apricot with champagne sabayon

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the orange and apricot

  • 2 oranges, peeled and segmented
  • 2 fresh apricots, stones removed, chopped
  • 2 tbsp honey

For the champagne sabayon

  • 2 free-range egg yolks
  • 1 vanilla pod, seeds only
  • 3 tbsp champagne
  • 2 tsp caster sugar

Method

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high.

  2. For the grilled orange and apricot, place the segmented oranges and chopped apricots into a small ovenproof dish. Drizzle over the honey and place under the grill for 3-4 minutes, or until the fruit is hot and the honey is bubbling. Set aside while you make the sabayon.

  3. For the sabayon, place the egg yolks into a heatproof bowl set over a pan of gently simmering water. Add the vanilla seeds, champagne and sugar and whisk until the mixture thickens.

  4. To serve, pour the sabayon over the fruit.

Tomato and Parma ham spaghetti

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz fresh spaghetti
  • 2 tomatoes, blanched, peeled and chopped
  • 3 slices Parma ham, roughly chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh basil

Method

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water according to packet instructions.

  2. Drain the spaghetti and stir in the chopped tomatoes, Parma ham, olive oil and black pepper.

  3. To serve, spoon into a bowl and sprinkle with fresh basil.

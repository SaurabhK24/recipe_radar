Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 50g/2oz butter
  • 100g/3½oz brown sugar
  • 4 ripe plums, halved and stoned
  • 2 ripe Conference pears, sliced
  • 2 Cox apples, cored and sliced
  • 227g/8¼oz tub clotted cream
  • 2 tsp Grand Marnier
  • ½ orange, zest only

Method

  1. Put the butter and sugar in a small, heavy-bottomed frying pan. Heat gently, stirring all the while.

  2. When the sugar has nearly melted, add the fruit. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the fruit is soft and caramelised.

  3. Meanwhile, stir the Grand Marnier into the cream. Serve the fruit with a spoon of cream and top with a sprinkling of orange zest.

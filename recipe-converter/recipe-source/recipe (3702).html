Frozen cherry margarita

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 50g/1¾oz caster sugar
  • 175g/6¼oz cherries, stemmed and stoned
  • 4 tbsp lime juice
  • 150ml/5¼fl oz tequila
  • 3 tbsp cherry liqueur or Cointreau
  • 275ml/10fl oz ice

Method

  1. Combine the sugar with three tablespoons of water and heat on a low heat until the sugar dissolves.

  2. Remove from the heat and allow to cool.

  3. Once cool, place the sugar in a blender with the cherries, lime juice, tequila, cherry liqueur or Cointreau, and the ice.

  4. Blend until smooth, and serve in martini glasses with a sugared rim.

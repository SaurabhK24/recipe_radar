Cosmopolitan

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

A strong sweet cocktail to get the party started.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 1 lime wedge
  • 50ml/2fl oz citrus vodka
  • 25ml/1fl oz orange liqueur
  • 25ml/1fl oz cranberry juice
  • dash lime cordial

For garnish

  • 1 slice orange peel

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.

  2. Strain the drink into a cocktail glass. Using a lit match or a cigarette lighter, carefully hold the flame against the orange peel and squeeze gently to release the oils. Garnish the drink with the flamed orange zest.

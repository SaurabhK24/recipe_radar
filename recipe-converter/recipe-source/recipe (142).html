Apple and prune crumble with brandy cream

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 15g/½oz butter
  • 15g/½oz brown sugar
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • 6-8 finely chopped prunes
  • ½ Bramley apple, peeled, cored and chopped into cubes

For the crumble

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 4 tbsp plain flour
  • 1 tbsp pistachio nuts, shells removed

For the brandy cream

  • 5 tbsp double cream, whipped
  • 2 tbsp brandy

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Melt the butter in a saucepan over a medium heat. Add the brown sugar and cook until melted and combined.

  3. Add the cinnamon, prunes and apples and cook until the apples become very soft.

  4. Transfer the fruit mixture to a shallow ovenproof dish.

  5. For the crumble, place all of the crumble ingredients together into a food processor and blend until combined. Sprinkle the crumble mixture on top of the fruit and transfer to the oven to bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crumble topping is crisp and golden-brown.

  6. For the brandy cream, add the brandy to the whipped cream in a bowl and fold together.

  7. Serve the crumble in the cooking dish with the brandy cream in a serving bowl alongside.

Mo-tea-to

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Andy Pearson
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp caster sugar
  • 1 tbsp freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 8 fresh mint leaves, plus 1 extra sprig, to garnish
  • scoop crushed ice
  • 50ml/2fl oz gin
  • 75ml/2¾fl oz chilled strong green tea

Method

  1. Place the sugar and lime juice into a highball glass and stir until dissolved.

  2. Wipe the mint leaves around the rim of the glass, then tear them in two and drop them into the glass.

  3. Crush the mint leaves lightly with the back of a teaspoon.

  4. Fill the glass with crushed ice.

  5. Add the gin and chilled green tea and stir well.

  6. To serve, garnish the glass with a sprig of mint.

Quick-fix omelette

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

When you need a good meal in a hurry, nothing beats a veggie omelette. Serve with salad.

By Charlotte Stirling-Reed
From Food: Truth or Scare

Ingredients

  • 2-3 new potatoes, halved
  • 1 tsp cooking oil or butter
  • 2 free-range eggs, beaten
  • 1 or 2 small mushrooms, sliced
  • ½ small red pepper, chopped
  • a little grated cheddar
  • side salad of your choice, to serve

Method

  1. Boil the potatoes for about 10 minutes, or until slightly soft

  2. Heat the oil in a frying pan. Once hot, add the cooked potatoes, mushrooms and peppers (or other vegetables of your choice). Once the vegetables are soft and slightly browned, transfer to a bowl.

  3. Add the beaten eggs to the pan. When the eggs have set slightly, return the vegetable mixture to the pan. Sprinkle some cheese on top

  4. Flip the omelette to cook the other side, or fold the omelette in half. Serve with salad.

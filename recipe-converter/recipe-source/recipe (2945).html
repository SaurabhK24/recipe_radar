Crunchy pepper salad with lemon and olive oil dressing

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • ¼ iceberg lettuce, shredded
  • ¼ red pepper, seeds removed, finely sliced
  • ¼ yellow pepper, seeds removed, finely sliced
  • ¼ onion, finely sliced
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • ½ lemon, zest and juice
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the lettuce, red and yellow pepper, onion and parsley.

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon zest, lemon juice and olive oil, then season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. To serve, drizzle the dressing over the salad and mix well to coat. Serve piled on a serving plate.

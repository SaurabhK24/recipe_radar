Sausage and sage potato patties

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

1 person

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 200g/7oz ready-made mashed potato
  • 500g/1lb 2oz sausagemeat
  • 1 tbsp plain flour
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 8 fresh sage leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. Mix the mashed potato, sausagemeat and flour together in a mixing bowl. Add salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste and mix well.

  2. Using your hands, shape the mixture into eight round patties and press one sage leaf onto the top of each patty.

  3. Heat the oil in a frying pan. Place the patties sage-side down into the oil and fry for 4-5 minutes each side until the patties are golden-brown and cooked through in the middle. Serve straightaway.

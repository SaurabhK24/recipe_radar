Coffee and chocolate meringues with chocolate sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the chocolate sauce

  • 2 tsp instant coffee granules
  • 3 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp boiling water
  • 3 tbsp caster sugar

For the meringues

  • 1 free-range egg white
  • 250g/8¾oz icing sugar
  • 55g/2oz cocoa powder

Method

  1. To make the chocolate sauce mix together all of the ingredients.

  2. For the meringues, mix together the ingredients and roll into small balls.

  3. Cook in the microwave for two minutes.

  4. Serve on a plate drizzled with the chocolate sauce.

Vanilla and orange rice pudding

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 200g/7oz long grain rice
  • 400ml/14fl oz milk
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1 vanilla pod, split, seeds scraped out
  • 1 orange, zest and juice only
  • 2 tbsp golden syrup, to serve

Method

  1. Place the rice into a pan with the milk, sugar and vanilla pod and seeds and simmer for 10-12 minutes, or until the rice is tender.

  2. Remove the vanilla pod, then stir in the orange zest and juice and cook for a further 1-2 minutes.

  3. Spoon the rice pudding into a serving bowl and drizzle over the golden syrup to serve.

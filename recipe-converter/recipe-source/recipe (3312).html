Elderflower and fruit jellies

Preparation time

over 2 hours

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

By Lesley Waters
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 7 tbsp elderflower cordial, plus extra for drizzling
  • 11g sachet powdered gelatine
  • 110g/4oz raspberries
  • 110g/4oz blueberries
  • 55g/2oz small black seedless grapes

Method

  1. Dilute the elderflower cordial with 570ml/1 pint cold water. Pour 4 tbsp into a small pan. Sprinkle over the gelatine and set aside for 10 minutes.

  2. Arrange the raspberries, blueberries and grapes into four whisky tumblers or wine glasses.

  3. Place the pan over a very gentle heat and swirl the pan until the gelatine has completely dissolved. Do not allow the gelatine to boil, as this will prevent the jelly from setting. Pour in the remaining diluted cordial and stir well over a low heat. Pour into the glasses.

  4. Chill for 3 hours or until softly set. Just before serving, spoon over a little extra cordial.

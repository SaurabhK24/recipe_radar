Asparagus and Parma ham wraps

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 6 asparagus spears, blanched for two minutes in boiling water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 slices Parma ham

For the dressing

  • 3 asparagus spears, blanched for two minutes in boiling water and chopped
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • juice of ½ lemon

Method

  1. Wrap the asparagus with the Parma ham in bundles of three.

  2. Heat a frying pan with the oil. Add the bundles and fry to colour for one minute on each side. Remove from the pan and set aside.

  3. In the same pan, add the chopped asparagus and remaining ingredients and fry for two minutes.

  4. To serve, place the wraps on a plate and pour over the sauce.

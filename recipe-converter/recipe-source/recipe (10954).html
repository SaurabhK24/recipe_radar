Warm winter salad with spicy dressing

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • ¼ butternut squash, peeled and chopped
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • ¼ red cabbage, shredded
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • juice of ½ orange
  • tsp salt
  • 2 tsp sesame seeds

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 225C/450F/Gas 7.

  2. Bring a pan of water to the boil and add the squash and cook for five minutes, or until soft. Drain and place on a baking sheet.

  3. Meanwhile, heat 1 tbsp of oil in a large pan and sauté the cabbage for 5-8 minutes, until soft. Transfer the cabbage to the baking sheet with the butternut squash.

  4. Drizzle the cabbage and butternut squash with the remaining tablespoon of olive oil. Sprinkle with the orange juice, brown sugar and a teaspoon of salt. Place in the oven and roast for ten minutes.

  5. To serve, place in a bowl and sprinkle with sesame seeds.

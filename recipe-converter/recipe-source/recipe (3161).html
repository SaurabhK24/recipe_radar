Double chocolate mousse

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz dark chocolate, melted
  • 150ml/¼ pint double cream
  • 100g/3½oz mascarpone

Method

  1. Drizzle one tablespoon of the melted chocolate into a large dessert glass. Chill in the fridge until set.

  2. Whisk the double cream until soft peaks form when the whisk is removed.

  3. Fold the whipped double cream into the mascarpone.

  4. Fold the remaining melted chocolate through the cream mixture until combined, and spoon into the chocolate drizzled glass.

  5. Chill until required.

Herbed roasted sprouts

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Garrey Dawson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 225g/8oz Brussels sprouts, trimmed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, roughly chopped

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. Boil the sprouts in salted water for about 3-4 minutes, then drain.

  3. Heat the oil in an ovenproof frying pan and fry the sprouts along with the herbs and garlic for 1-2 minutes. Transfer the pan to the oven and continue to cook for 10-12 minutes, or until the sprouts are tender.

  4. Serve in a gratin dish.

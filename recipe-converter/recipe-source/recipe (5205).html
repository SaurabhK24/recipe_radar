Lentil salad with herbs and Toulouse sausage

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the salad

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 100g/3½oz canned lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 50g/1¾oz fresh green beans, blanched and chopped
  • 50g/1¾oz each fresh parsley, coriander, chives
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the sausages

  • 100g/3½oz sausage meat shaped into a patty
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. For the lentil salad, mix all the salad ingredients together in a mixing bowl and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  2. For the Toulouse sausage, rub the patty with the olive oil and pan-fry for two minutes on each side, unitl lightly browned.

  3. Serve the lentil salad on a serving plate with the patty on top.

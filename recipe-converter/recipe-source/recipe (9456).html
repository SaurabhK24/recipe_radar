Spinach salad with cashew nut dressing and cheesy crispy croutes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the salad

  • 3 handfuls fresh spinach leaves
  • ¼ Romero pepper, deseeded, chopped
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tbsp cashew nuts, chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar

For the cheese croûtes

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 55g/2oz Gruy&egeave;re cheese, grated

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. For the salad, combine all the ingredients together in a bowl and stir well.

  3. For the croûtes, bake the slices of bread on a baking tray for 3-4 minutes. Top with the cheese then place back in the oven for a further 4-5 minutes or until the cheese has melted. Remove and cut each slice in half.

  4. Serve the croûtes as an accompaniment to the salad.

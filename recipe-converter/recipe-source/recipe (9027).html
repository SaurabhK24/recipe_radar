Smoked salmon mousse in croustades

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 6

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 30g/1oz smoked salmon
  • 1 tbsp double cream
  • 1 tbsp yoghurt
  • 6 ready-made croustade pastry cases
  • handful of fresh basil, chopped
  • olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika

Method

  1. In a blender, whiz together the smoked salmon, yoghurt and cream.

  2. Fill the croustades with the mixture. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with fresh chopped basil and paprika.

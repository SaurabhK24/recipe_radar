Oriental-style soy, chilli, green bean and steak broth

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Alex Mackay
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 150ml/¼ pint chicken stock
  • ½ red chilli, seeds removed, chopped
  • 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tbsp clear honey
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 85g/3oz green beans, trimmed
  • ¼ carrot, peeled and sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 85g/3oz beef steak, sliced
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh coriander, to serve

Method

  1. Place the stock, chilli, grated ginger, honey and soy sauce into a saucepan and bring to a simmer.

  2. Add the green beans and carrot to the pan and simmer for four minutes.

  3. Heat the olive oil in a non-stick frying pan and fry the steak for 2-3 minutes, or until cooked to your liking. Leave aside to rest for two minutes.

  4. Drain the steak on kitchen paper and place in the centre of a serving bowl.

  5. To serve, pour the broth over the steak and scatter over the chopped coriander.

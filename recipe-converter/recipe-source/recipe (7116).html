Pork skewer with chargrilled butternut squash

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the pork skewer

  • 200g/7oz pork tenderloin, cubed
  • 1 yellow pepper, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp clear honey

For the chargrilled butternut squash

  • 2 large slices butternut squash
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds, to garnish

Method

  1. For the pork skewer, thread the pork and the yellow pepper pieces onto a skewer and drizzle with oil.

  2. Heat a griddle pan and place the skewer into the pan. Drizzle with the soy sauce and honey and chargrill for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until golden-brown and completely cooked through.

  3. For the chargrilled butternut squash, heat a griddle pan and place the butternut squash slices into the pan. Brush with oil and cook for 5-6 minutes on each side, or until tender and golden-brown.

  4. To serve, place the butternut squash onto a plate, top with the pork skewer and sprinkle over the sesame seeds.

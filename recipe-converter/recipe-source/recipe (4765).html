Japanese-style carpaccio

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

By Ross Burden
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz sirloin steak
  • 1 lemon, segmented
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • handful fresh coriander, chopped
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • ½ leek, cut into thin strips and deep fried, to garnish

Method

  1. Slice the steak into really thin strips.

  2. Lay on a plate and top with the lemon segments, sesame seeds, coriander, soy sauce and deep-fried leeks.

  3. Serve raw.

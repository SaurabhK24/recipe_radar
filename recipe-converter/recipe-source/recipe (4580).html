Hot potato salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Ed Baines
From Housecall

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large potatoes, cut into cubes olive oil
  • wine vinegar
  • medium sized onion, chopped
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • handful fresh herbs, chopped
  • salt and pepper

Method

  1. Boil the potatoes until just cooked.

  2. Make up a simple viniagrette using 3 parts oil to 1 part vinegar.

  3. Add the onions, garlic, herbs and seasoning.

  4. Drain the potatoes and whilst they are still hot, tip them into the dressing. Stir well and serve.

Peach and raisin muffins

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Makes 6 muffins

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 125g/4oz self-raising flour
  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 100ml/3½fl oz whole milk
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • 50g/1½oz raisins
  • 6 tinned peach halves
  • 25g/1oz dark chocolate, melted, to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Place the flour, eggs, milk and sugar into a large bowl and mix together to make a smooth batter.

  3. Divide equal amounts of the raisins among the six muffin cases. Pour the batter into the cases and top each muffin with a peach half.

  4. Place the muffin cases into a muffin tin and bake in the oven for 8-10 minutes, or until golden-brown.

  5. To serve, place the muffins onto a plate and drizzle over the chocolate.

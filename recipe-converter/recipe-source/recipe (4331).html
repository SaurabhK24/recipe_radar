Toasted hemp seeds

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz hemp seeds
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt

Method

  1. Heat the hemp seeds in a frying pan over a medium heat and toast for five minutes, tossing frequently to ensure even cooking.

  2. Add the olive oil, season with plenty of salt and remove from the heat.

  3. To serve, pour into a bowl.

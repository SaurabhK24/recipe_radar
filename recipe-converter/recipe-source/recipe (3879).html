Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4-6

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 litre bottle cheap red wine
  • 30z/75g sugar (to taste)
  • 1 tsp whole cloves
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon or stick cinnamon
  • juice of 1 orange and 1 lemon

Method

  1. Pour wine into a saucepan and add remaining ingredients (you can put whole spices in muslin).

  2. Heat until nearly boiling and strain (if necessary) into a warm bowl.

  3. Serve warm.

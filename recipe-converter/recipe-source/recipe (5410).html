Mango and chocolate sundae

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Ross Burden
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz dark chocolate
  • 100ml/3½fl oz double cream
  • ½ mango, peeled and sliced
  • sprig of mint, to garnish

Method

  1. Place a bowl over a saucepan of simmering water. Add the chocolate and cream and stir until melted.

  2. Pour half of the chocolate sauce into a glass. Place some of the mango into the glass and top with the rest of the mixture.

  3. Decorate with a sprig of mint and place into the fridge to set for 15 minutes before serving.

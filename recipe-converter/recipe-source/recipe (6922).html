Plum cheesecake

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 200g/7oz mascarpone cheese
  • 142ml/5fl oz double cream, whipped
  • ½ vanilla pod, split, seeds scraped out
  • 2 tbsp icing sugar
  • 2 plums, halved, stones removed

To serve

  • sprig fresh mint
  • drizzle honey

Method

  1. In a bowl, mix together the mascarpone, cream, vanilla seeds and icing sugar.

  2. Place two chefs' rings onto two serving plates. Add the plums to the base of the rings and press down. Top up the rings with the mascarpone mixture and smooth down the top with a pallet knife.

  3. To serve, carefully remove the chefs' rings, garnish with mint and drizzle with honey.

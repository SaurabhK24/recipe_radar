Strawberry saffron dip (shrikhand)

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Manju Mahli’s easy version of this Indian dessert makes a wonderful dip for strawberries - the saffron turns the dessert a beautiful shade of pale yellow gold.

By Manju Malhi
From Tommy Sandhu

Ingredients

  • 3-4 saffron strands
  • 4 tbsp milk, warmed
  • 150g/5oz Greek-style yoghurt or low-fat crème fraîche
  • 1 tbsp cream
  • 1 tsp clear honey
  • pinch ground nutmeg
  • punnet of strawberries

Method

  1. Place the saffron into a cup with the milk and leave it to soak for a couple of minutes

  2. Whisk together the yoghurt (or crème fraîche), cream, honey and nutmeg in a separate bowl.

  3. Gently stir the milk and saffron into the yoghurt mixture.

  4. To serve, dip the strawberries into the shrikhand.

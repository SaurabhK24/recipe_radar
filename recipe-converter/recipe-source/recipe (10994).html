Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 3

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 150ml/5fl oz single malt whisky
  • 1 lemon, juice only
  • 30ml/1fl oz ginger cordial
  • honey syrup, to sweeten (can be made from equal measures of honey and hot water)
  • apple juice, to taste
  • apple slices, to finish

Method

  1. Pour the whisky into a jug full of ice.

  2. Add the lemon juice and cordial and honey syrup to taste.

  3. Top up with apple juice. Stir well, then pour into three whisky tumblers.

  4. Finish with the apple slices, and serve.

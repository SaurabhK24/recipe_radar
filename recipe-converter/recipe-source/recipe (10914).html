Warm melon 'boats' with ginger and cinnamon syrup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 Galia melon
  • small bunch fresh mint, chopped

For the syrup

  • 200g/7oz caster sugar
  • 4 tbsp water
  • 50ml/2fl oz champagne
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp ground ginger

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 170C/325F/Gas 3.

  2. Slice the melon in half and remove the seeds using a spoon.

  3. Slice each half into four wedges and place onto a baking sheet.

  4. Place the melon in the oven and cook for five minutes while you make the syrup.

  5. For the syrup, place the sugar and water into a pan. Dissolve the sugar over a low heat, before turning up the heat. Add the remaining syrup ingredients and reduce by a third.

  6. Arrange the warm melon on a serving platter, spoon over the syrup and sprinkle with chopped mint.

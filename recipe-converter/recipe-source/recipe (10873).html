Walnut and Stilton crostini

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 40g/1½oz watercress
  • 30g/1oz Stilton
  • 2 tbsp shelled walnuts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 slices baguette, toasted

Method

  1. Place the watercress, Stilton, walnuts, olive oil and black pepper into a mini food processor and blend until smooth.

  2. Place the baguette slices onto a serving plate. Spread over the walnut and Stilton mixture and serve.

Sicilian-style chocolate rice pudding with ricotta and mint cream

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the chocolate rice pudding

  • 150g/5½oz long grain rice
  • 100ml/3½fl oz milk
  • 100ml/3½fl oz double cream
  • 2 tbsp clear honey
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • butter, for greasing

For the ricotta and mint cream

  • 50g/1¾oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 tbsp double cream
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh mint
  • 2 tsp icing sugar

To serve

  • icing sugar, to dust
  • sprig fresh mint, to garnish

Method

  1. For the chocolate rice pudding, place the rice, milk and cream into a saucepan, bring to the boil, then reduce the heat and simmer gently for 8-10 minutes.

  2. Add the honey and cocoa powder and cook for 2-3 minutes, until thickened.

  3. Lightly grease a small glass bowl. Pour the rice pudding into the bowl, press it down, then turn out onto a serving plate.

  4. For the ricotta and mint cream, place all the ingredients into a bowl and mix well.

  5. To serve, place a dollop of the ricotta and mint cream onto the plate with the pudding. Dust with icing sugar and garnish with a sprig of mint.

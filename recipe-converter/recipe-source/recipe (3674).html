Fried christophine with corn on the cob

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Levi Roots
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 christophine (also called chow chow), peeled, cored and sliced
  • 55g/2oz butter
  • 1 red scotch bonnet chilli, de-seeded (or to taste)
  • 1 orange scotch bonnet chilli, de-seeded (or to taste)
  • 1 corn on the cob

Method

  1. Parboil the christophine slices in boiling water for 3-4 minutes, then drain.

  2. Melt half of the butter in a frying pan and fry the christophine and the whole scotch bonnet chillies for 4-5 minutes, turning the slices once, until the christophine is tender and golden-brown on both sides.

  3. Microwave the corn for 4-5 minutes on high, then remove and fry in another frying pan with the remaining butter for 3-4 minutes, or until the corn is tender.

  4. To serve, pile the fried christophine and chillies onto a serving plate, and place the corn on the cob on the side.

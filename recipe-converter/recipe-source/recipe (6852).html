Pineapple salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 55g/2oz pineapple, chopped
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • fresh mint leaves, to garnish

Method

  1. Mix together the pineapple, coriander and white wine vinegar.

  2. Serve in a bowl garnished with fresh mint leaves.

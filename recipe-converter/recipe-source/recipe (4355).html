Herb crusted salmon with sautéed garlic potatoes

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the salmon

  • 50g/1¾ oz fresh breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp each chopped chervil, dill, parsley
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 1x110g/4oz thin salmon fillet, skin on
  • 1 free-range egg, beaten
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the sautéed garlic potatoes

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 2 sprigs rosemary, leaves picked
  • 1 small potato, peeled, sliced and boiled for 5 minutes

Method

  1. For the salmon, place the breadcrumbs, herbs, garlic and salt and freshly ground black pepper into a bowl and mix together.

  2. Dip the salmon into the beaten egg and then dip into the breadcrumb mixture.

  3. Heat the oil in a frying pan over a high heat and fry the salmon on both sides for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown and cooked through.

  4. For the sautéed potatoes, in a separate frying pan, heat the olive oil over a medium heat. Add the garlic, rosemary and potato slices and sauté for five minutes until golden.

  5. To serve, place the sautéed potatoes onto a serving plate and place the salmon on top.

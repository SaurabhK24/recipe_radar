Roast pumpkin seeds

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 9

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz pumpkin seeds
  • ¼ tsp celery salt
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil

Method

  1. Brush a frying pan with a very small amount of oil and place over a medium heat. Add the pumpkin seeds, stirring frequently. They will expand and brown fairly quickly, and when this is done, place them in a bowl and add the salt, celery salt, freshly ground black pepper and paprika. This is delicious if the pumpkin seeds are reasonably fresh.

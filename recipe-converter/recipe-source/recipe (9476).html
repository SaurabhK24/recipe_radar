Spotted dick

Preparation time

30 mins to 1 hour

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 40

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This recipe makes five glorious puddings, enough for 40 people. They are at their best served with custard.

By The Hairy Bikers
From Hairy Bikers' Meals on Wheels

Ingredients

  • 250g/9oz golden syrup
  • 875g/1lb 15oz butter, softened, plus extra for greasing
  • 625g/1lb 6oz caster sugar
  • 5 lemons, zest only
  • 15 free-range eggs, lightly beaten
  • 875g/1lb 15oz self-raising flour
  • 250g/9oz currants

Method

  1. Generously butter the inside of five 1.2 litre/2 pint pudding basins.

  2. Beat the golden syrup, butter, sugar and lemon zest together in a mixer until light and fluffy.

  3. Beat in half the eggs followed by half the flour and when well combined, add the remaining eggs and flour then stir in the currants. The mixture should be at a just dropping consistency. Add a splash of milk if the mixture is too thick.

  4. Spoon the mixture into the pudding basins, only filling them three quarters full, then smooth the surface with the back of a spoon.

  5. Loosely cover with clingfilm, allowing room for the cling film to inflate as the mixture rises, and cook in the microwave for five minutes. The pudding is done when a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean.

  6. Leave to stand for five minutes then run a blunt knife around the edge of the pudding to loosen the sides, carefully invert onto a deep plate and remove the basin.

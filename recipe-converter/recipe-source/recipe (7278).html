Prawn on toast

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 4 cooked prawns, shells off
  • 1 spring onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • pinch of chilli flakes
  • ½ clove garlic, finely chopped
  • slice of white bread, toasted
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds

To serve

  • 2 lime wedges

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. In a small blender whiz together the prawns with the spring onion, sesame oil, chilli and garlic. Blend until smooth.

  3. Spread over the toasted bread and sprinkle over the sesame seeds. Place in the oven for 5 minutes.

  4. Cut the prawn toast into triangles and serve with lime wedges.

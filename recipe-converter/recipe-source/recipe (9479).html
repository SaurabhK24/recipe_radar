Spotted dick with banana

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 4-6

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Simon Rimmer
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 300g/10½oz plain flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 150g/5¼oz shredded suet (vegetarians can use vegetable suet)
  • 75g/2½oz sugar
  • 100g/3½oz currants
  • 1 orange, zest only
  • 2 bananas, peeled and chopped
  • 150ml/5¼fl oz whole milk
  • butter, for greasing
  • custard, to serve

Method

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, suet, sugar, currants and orange zest.

  2. Stir in the banana pieces and gradually pour in the milk to make a dough.

  3. Roll the dough into a cylinder about 15cm x 5cm/6in x 2in. Wrap loosely in buttered greaseproof paper, leaving room for it to rise, and seal at each end.

  4. Place the pudding into a steamer, cover and steam for an hour.

  5. To serve, remove the pudding from the steamer and cut into slices. Place into serving bowls and pour over some custard.

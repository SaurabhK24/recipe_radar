Cheese and cavolo nero frittata

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ shallot, peeled and sliced
  • 85g/3oz cavolo nero (black-leaf kale), chopped
  • ½ red romero pepper, seeds removed and sliced
  • 5 eggs, beaten
  • 55g/2oz Pont l'Évêque cheese

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.

  2. Heat the oil in a medium ovenproof, non-stick frying pan. Add the shallot and sauté for 2 minutes, to soften.

  3. Add the cavolo nero and the pepper and cook for a further 3-4 minutes.

  4. Add the eggs and cook over a low heat until the egg has set.

  5. Crumble the cheese over the top and then transfer to the oven and cook for 5-6 minutes, until the cheese has melted and is golden.

  6. To serve, remove from the oven, and carefully transfer onto a serving plate, cheese side upwards.

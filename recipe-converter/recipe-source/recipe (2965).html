Cucumber salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • ½ cucumber, diced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 25g/1oz anchovy fillets, chopped
  • 1 tsp fresh chopped dill
  • 1 tsp fresh chopped parsley
  • ½ clove garlic, finely chopped

Method

  1. Mix together all the ingredients and serve on a small plate.

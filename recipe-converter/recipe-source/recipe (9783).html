Strawberry chocolate cheesecake

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2-3

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 55g/2oz dark chocolate, melted
  • 45g/2½oz amaretti biscuits, crushed
  • 1 tbsp brandy
  • 150ml/¼ pint double cream
  • 1 tbsp icing sugar
  • 85g/3oz cream cheese
  • 1 tsp lime juice
  • 5 strawberries, hulled and sliced, plus 3 whole strawberries

Method

  1. Place a 7.5cm/3in chef's ring on a serving plate.

  2. In a bowl, mix one tablespoon of melted chocolate with the crushed biscuits and brandy and press into the base of the ring. Transfer to the fridge to chill.

  3. Whip the double cream and icing sugar together to form soft peaks when the whisk is removed.

  4. In another bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth and add the lime juice.

  5. Gently fold the double cream into the cream cheese mixture, along with the sliced strawberries.

  6. Fill the ring with some cream mixture and drizzle with some of the melted chocolate. Remove the ring and garnish with the strawberries.

  7. Repeat the process until the cream mixture and chocolate sauce is used up.

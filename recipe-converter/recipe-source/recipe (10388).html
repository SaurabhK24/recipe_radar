Toasted marshmallows in warm blueberry soup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the toasted marshmallows

  • 8 pink marshmallows

For the soup

  • 100ml/3½fl oz red wine
  • 50g/1½oz caster sugar
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 100g/3½oz blueberries
  • 1 tsp ground mixed spice
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground cloves

Method

  1. For the toasted marshmallows, colour the marshmallows all over using a mini-blowtorch until golden-brown.

  2. For the soup, place all of the ingredients into a saucepan, bring to the boil and simmer for 8-10 minutes.

  3. To serve, pour the soup into a bowl and top with the marshmallows.

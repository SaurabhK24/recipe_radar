Chocolate palmiers

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 55g/2oz ready-rolled puff pastry
  • icing sugar, to dust
  • cocoa powder, to dust
  • honey, to drizzle

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.

  2. Roll the pastry out into a very thin rectangle and dust with the cocoa and icing sugar.

  3. Roll up the pastry from both ends until they meet in the centre. Slice into 1cm/½in slices and place onto a baking sheet. Place into the oven and bake for 5-6 minutes, or until risen and cooked through.

  4. To serve, place onto a plate, dust with cocoa and drizzle with honey.

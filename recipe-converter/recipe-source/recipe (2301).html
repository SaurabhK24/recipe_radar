Chorizo guacamole

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Recommended by

1 person

As if lime-flavoured guacamole wasn’t tempting enough, Nigel Slater adds some chopped chorizo sausage to really get the taste buds going.

By Nigel Slater
From Nigel and Adam's Farm Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 lime, juice only
  • 3 tbsp roughly chopped chives
  • 150g/5½oz cooked chorizo sausage, roughly chopped

Method

  1. Scoop the avocado flesh into a mixing bowl.

  2. Squeeze the lime juice over the avocado to prevent it from discolouring.

  3. Lightly crush the avocado with a fork.

  4. Skin and roughly chop the chorizo.

  5. Roughly chop the chives and then fold everything together with the avocado and serve.

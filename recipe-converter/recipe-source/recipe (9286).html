Spiced potatoes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 100g/3½oz new potatoes, cooked and cut into quarters
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • ½ tsp tomato purée
  • 1 tbsp honey

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a pan and add the potatoes, vinegar and paprika and cook until golden.

  2. Finish by adding the tomato ketchup, tomato purée and honey. Cook for three more minutes.

  3. Transfer to a plate and serve.

Halloween punch

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 10

Recommended by

6 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This blood-red punch is perfect for a Halloween party. Just add a slug of gin or vodka for a grown-up version.

Ingredients

  • 500ml/18fl oz cranberry juice
  • 1.5 litres/2½ pints lemonade
  • 3 limes, juice only
  • large handful gummi worms

Method

  1. Pour all of the ingredients into a large glass bowl and mix until well combined.

  2. Drape the gummi worms over the edge of the bowl.

Recipe Tips

To make it extra spooky – rinse a food-grade rubber glove and turn it inside out. Fill with water, tie the top with an elastic band and freeze until solid. Turn the frozen ‘hand’ out into the punch.

Mediterranean-style stuffed Portobello mushroom

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Aldo Zilli
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Portobello mushroom, cleaned, stalk removed
  • 55g/2oz Dolcelatte cheese
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh basil
  • 25g/1oz butter, peeled, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped onion

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. Heat the olive oil in a small ovenproof frying pan and fry the mushroom for 1-2 minutes on both sides, or until golden-brown. Keep warm in the pan.

  3. In a small bowl, mash together the cheese, herbs, butter and garlic together until well combined, then use to stuff the mushroom. Sprinkle over the chopped onion and transfer the pan to the oven to roast for 5-6 minutes, or until the stuffing is bubbling and golden-brown.

  4. Transfer the stuffed mushroom onto a serving plate and serve immediately.

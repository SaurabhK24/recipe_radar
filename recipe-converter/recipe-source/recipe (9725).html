Stir-fried kale with onion, sesame and leek with wholegrain mustard

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • dash olive oil
  • ½ red onion, finely chopped
  • 200g/7oz kale, shredded
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the dressing

  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • handful fresh coriander, chopped
  • ½ lime, juice and zest only

For the sweet potato

  • dash olive oil
  • ¼ sweet potato, peeled, chopped
  • 1 tbsp wholegrain mustard, to serve

Method

  1. Heat a dash of olive oil in a frying pan, add the onion and fry until softened but not coloured.

  2. Blanch the shredded kale in a pan of boiling salted water for 1-2 minutes. Drain and refresh under cold running water and then add to the pan with the onions. Stir in the sesame seeds, season to taste with salt and freshly ground black pepper and set aside.

  3. For the dressing, in a small bowl whisk together the soy sauce, coriander, lime juice and zest and a dash of olive oil.

  4. For the sweet potato, heat a dash of olive oil in a frying pan, add the sweet potato and fry until golden-brown all over.

  5. To serve, pile the kale onto a serving plate, pour over the dressing and top with the crisp sweet potato. Dot the plate with the wholegrain mustard for garnish and serve.

Manhattan

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 50ml/2fl oz bourbon
  • 25ml/1fl oz sweet red vermouth
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 tsp syrup from a jar of maraschino cherries
  • twist orange zest, to garnish
  • maraschino cherry, to garnish

Method

  1. Place the bourbon, vermouth, bitters and syrup into a mixing glass filled with ice and stir well.

  2. Strain into a cocktail glass and garnish with an orange twist and a maraschino cherry.

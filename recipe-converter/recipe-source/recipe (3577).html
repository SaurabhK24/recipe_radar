Forest fruits cheesecake

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 3 ready-made ginger biscuits, crushed
  • 30g/1oz butter, melted
  • 55g/2oz mascarpone
  • 55g/2oz double cream
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • 85g/3oz frozen forest fruits

Method

  1. Mix the crushed biscuits with the melted butter in a bowl.

  2. Place a deep chefs' ring onto a plate and press the biscuit mixture into the base of the ring.

  3. In a clean bowl, mix together the mascarpone, cream, honey and lemon juice.

  4. Place three tablespoons of the frozen forest fruits over the biscuit base, then spoon over the cream mixture. Add the remaining fruit to fill the ring.

  5. Carefully remove the chefs' ring (you may need to use a mini-blowtorch to loosen the ring) and serve.

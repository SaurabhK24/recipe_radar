Spicy grilled Morrocan lamb chops with a bulgar wheat salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

By Mike Robinson
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 4 lamb chops
  • 1 tbsp turmeric
  • 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp dried ginger
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • salt
  • freshly ground black pepper

For the salad

  • 100g/3½oz bulgar wheat, cooked
  • bunch of coriander
  • bunch of basil
  • bunch of mint
  • 2 lemons, zest and juice
  • 1 roasted skinned red pepper
  • 50g/2oz green olives
  • 1 tbsp capers
  • 100g/3½oz cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 50ml/2fl oz olive oil

Method

  1. In a pestle and mortar, pound all the dry ingredients until they are a coarse powder.

  2. Rub the lamb chops with the mixture, then grill on a hot griddle pan for three minutes on each side. Reserve a small amount of the powder.

  3. Skin the pepper and slice it finely. Chop the olives into small pieces and finely chop all the herbs.

  4. Mix all the ingredients with the bulgar wheat and liberally douse it with lemon juice and olive oil. Season to taste.

  5. Pile the salad in the middle of the plate then lean the spiced chops against it.

  6. Dribble some good olive oil around the plate and garnish with a little of the spice mix.

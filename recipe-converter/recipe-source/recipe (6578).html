Pea hummus

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This quick, easy pea hummus is lighter and fresher-tasting than the traditional chickpea version.

By Rekha Mistry
From The Big Allotment Challenge

Ingredients

  • 200g/7oz fresh peas
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 1-2 tbsp freshly squeezed lime juice
  • ¼ tsp ground cumin
  • pinch salt, or to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
  • 1-2 spring onions, trimmed, finely chopped
  • vegetable crisps or tortilla chips, to serve

Method

  1. Boil the peas in a pan of lightly salted water for 2-3 minutes, or until tender. Drain well, then rinse under cold running water.

  2. Roughly crush half the peas and blend the remaining into a smooth purée using a hand-held blender.

  3. Mix the crushed and pureed peas together in a bowl, then stir in the tahini, lime juice, cumin and salt, to taste. Stir in the coriander and spring onions.

  4. Serve with vegetable crisps or tortilla chips.

Courgette pasta

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 courgette
  • 25g/1oz halloumi cheese, chopped
  • handful red Peppadew peppers
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • juice of ½ lemon

Method

  1. Using a vegetable peeler, peel the courgette into long 'ribbons'.

  2. Place the courgette ribbons into boiling water for 30 seconds and drain.

  3. To the courgette add the halloumi cheese pieces, red Peppadews, olive oil and lemon juice. Season to taste with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  4. Place in a bowl and serve.

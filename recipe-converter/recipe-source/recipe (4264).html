Halloumi cheese, lemon, olive oil

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By David Laris
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 150g/5oz halloumi cheese
  • ½ a lemon, juiced
  • 20ml/1oz olive oil
  • cracked black pepper to taste

Method

  1. Slice the halloumi into ½cm/¼in thick slices.

  2. Char-grill or pan-fry the cheese in the olive oil.

  3. Drizzle over the lemon juice and cracked black pepper and serve.

Oven-roasted tomatoes with feta

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

over 2 hours

Serves

Serves 6

Recommended by

5 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Oven-roasted tomatoes have a deliciously concentrated flavour and a wonderfully cherry texture. Serve simply with feta cheese and crusty bread for a summer starter.

By Rick Stein
From Fresh Food

Ingredients

  • 6 tbsp extra virgin oil
  • 750g/1½lb ripe plum
  • tomatoes
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme leaves
  • 1½ tsp sea salt flakes
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 450g/1lb good quality Greek feta cheese

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 240C/475F/Gas 9. Oil a shallow roasting tin with 2 tbsp of the oil.

  2. Cut the tomatoes in half lengthways and lay them cut-side up in the tin.

  3. Sprinkle over the thyme and some salt and pepper and roast for 15 minutes.

  4. Reduce the oven temperature to 150C/300F/Gas 2 and roast for a further 1½ hours until they have shrivelled to about half their original size and concentrated in flavour. Leave to cool.

  5. Arrange the tomatoes and the feta among four plates and drizzle over the remaining olive oil.

  6. Sprinkle with a bit more sea salt and some black pepper to serve.

Orange purée

Preparation time

30 mins to 1 hour

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 300g/10½oz

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This Christmassy spiced purée can be used to garnish both sweet and savoury dishes - but works particularly well with turkey.

By Raymond Blanc
From Christmas Kitchen with James Martin

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz orange zest
  • 50ml/2fl oz freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 150ml/5fl oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 20g/¾oz caster sugar
  • pinch salt
  • pinch cayenne pepper

Method

  1. Bring a saucepan of water and the orange zest to the boil over a high heat. Reduce the heat until the water is simmering, then simmer for 15 minutes. Drain the zest, replace the water and repeat three more times (this will remove the bitter taste from the orange zest).

  2. Drain the orange zest and transfer to a food processor. Blend the zest, orange juice and half of the oil to a smooth purée. Continue to add the oil, pulsing after each addition, until the purée reaches the consistency you desire.

  3. Season, to taste, with sugar, salt and pepper, then cover and chill in the fridge until needed.

Roasted vine tomatoes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 10 vine tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp soft brown sugar

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Place all of the ingredients into a small roasting dish, transfer to the oven and roast for 10-12 minutes.

  3. To serve, place onto serving plates.

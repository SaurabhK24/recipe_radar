Chocolate fondue with kebabs

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For fondue

  • 25g/1oz dark chocolate
  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 25ml/1fl oz brandy
  • 50g/1¾oz cocoa powder
  • 120ml/4 &frac14fl oz; double cream
  • 3 tbsp honey

For the kebabs

  • 2 pears, cut into cubes
  • 100g/3½oz raspberries

Method

  1. For the fondue, in a bain-marie over a low heat melt the chocolate and butter together. Add the brandy.

  2. Stir through the cream, honey and cocoa powder.

  3. Place into a serving dish.

  4. For the kebabs, thread the pears and raspberries onto the skewers, alternating one another.

  5. Place the kebabs around the bowl of chocolate fondue and serve.

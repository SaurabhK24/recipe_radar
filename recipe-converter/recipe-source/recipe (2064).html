Chilli, mango and coconut muffins

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 6

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Ross Burden
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 110g/4oz butter
  • 2 tbsp creamed coconut, grated
  • 110g/4oz caster sugar
  • 110g/4oz self-raising flour
  • 2 free-range eggs
  • ½ mango, peeled and roughly diced
  • ½ red chilli, de-seeded and finely chopped

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Grease a six-hole muffin tin with butter.

  3. Blend together the butter, coconut, sugar, flour and eggs in a food processor, until smooth.

  4. Transfer the mixture to a bowl and stir through the mango and chilli.

  5. Divide the mixture evenly into the muffin tin holes and place in the oven for 15 minutes, or until a skewer pushed into a muffin comes out clean.

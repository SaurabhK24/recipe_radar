Pan-fried tomatoes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • small bunch fresh basil
  • 2 tbsp olive oil, plus 1 tbsp extra
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tomatoes, thickly sliced
  • 25g/1oz plain flour

Method

  1. Place the basil, oil and garlic into a food processor and blend until smooth. Season, to taste, with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  2. Spoon the basil oil onto one side of the cut tomatoes.

  3. Place the extra tbsp oil into a frying pan over a high heat.

  4. Dredge the tomato slices in the flour, then place the tomatoes into the hot pan and fry for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden.

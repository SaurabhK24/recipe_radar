Italian-style veggie sausage, potato and tomato stew

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

3 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the tomato sauce

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ onion
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 50ml/2fl oz white wine
  • ½ x 400g/14oz can plum tomatoes, chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the potato and sausage

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ baking potato, chopped
  • 1 vegetarian sausage, cut in half lengthways
  • 1 sprig fresh chervil, to garnish

Method

  1. For the tomato sauce, heat the oil in a saucepan and gently fry the onion and garlic until the onion is translucent, about 4-5 minutes.

  2. Add the white wine and simmer for about one minute, until slightly reduced. Add the tomatoes and simmer for a further five minutes. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper and remove from the heat. Process until smooth with a hand blender.

  3. For the potato and sausage, heat the oil in a separate frying pan and fry the cubes of potato and the sausage halves until coloured and cooked through, about 7-8 minutes.

  4. To serve, pile the potatoes and sausages onto a serving plate and pour over the tomato sauce. Garnish with chervil.

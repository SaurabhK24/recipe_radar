Super green dream drink

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Cucumber, apple and ginger drink is really refreshing and a change from boring old squash. Serve with lots of ice.

By Neil Strawson and Richard Hunt
From I Can Cook on the Go

Ingredients

  • ½ cucumber
  • 1 tsp grated root ginger
  • 400ml/14fl oz apple juice
  • ½ lime, juice only

Method

  1. Grate the cucumber into a bowl, making sure you catch all the juice.

  2. Stir in the ginger, apple juice and lime juice.

  3. Seive the mixture into a jug, pressing down on the pulp to extract as much juice as possible.

  4. Pour into two tall glasses.

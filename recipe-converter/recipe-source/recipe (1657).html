Cheese and spinach soufflé

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 25g/1oz unsalted butter
  • 25g/1oz plain flour
  • 150ml/5fl oz milk
  • 100g/1¾oz Edam, grated
  • 100g/3½oz spinach
  • 3 free-range egg yolks
  • 3 free-range egg whites

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Melt the butter in a saucepan over a medium heat. Stir in the flour and cook for 30 seconds. Remove from the heat and gradually whisk in the milk.

  3. Bring to the boil and stir in the spinach and most of the cheese.

  4. Allow to cool slightly. Beat in the egg yolks and season well.

  5. In a large bowl whisk the egg whites to medium peak.

  6. Mix two large spoonfuls of egg to the milk mixture to slacken. Fold in the remaining egg whites and sprinkle with the Edam.

  7. Transfer to an oven-proof frying pan. Place in the oven and cook for 10 minutes.

  8. Serve the soufflé in the frying pan.

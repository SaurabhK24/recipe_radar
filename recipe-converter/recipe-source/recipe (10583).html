Tuna patties with cucumber, chilli and herb relish

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the tuna patties

  • 200g/7oz new potatoes, cooked, grated
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp fresh chives, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 150g/5½oz tinned tuna
  • 1 tbsp black olives, pitted
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp plain flour
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the cucumber relish

  • 1 small cucumber, diced
  • 50ml/2fl oz olive oil
  • 2 limes, juice only
  • ½ fresh red chilli, chopped
  • handful fresh coriander leaves, chopped

Method

  1. For the tuna patties, place all the patty ingredients into the bowl of a food-processor and blend until the mixture is well combined.

  2. Roll the patty mixture into four patties. Heat the olive oil in a small frying pan and fry for three minutes each side or until golden-brown.

  3. For the cucumber relish, place all the relish ingredients into a clean medium-sized bowl and mix well to combine.

  4. To serve, place the patties on a clean plate and serve with the cucumber relish in a small bowl on the side.

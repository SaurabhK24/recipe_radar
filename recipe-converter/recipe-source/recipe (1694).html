Cheesy bacon wraps with red pepper relish

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the wraps

  • 3 x 15g/ ½oz batons of Gruyère cheese
  • 3 slices bacon

For the red pepper relish

  • 1 red pepper, finely sliced
  • ¼ onion, finely sliced
  • 1 tsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp caster sugar

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.

  2. For the wraps, wrap the bacon around the cheese and place onto a baking sheet and place into the oven to bake for ten minutes, or until the bacon is cooked.

  3. For the red pepper relish, place all of the relish ingredients into a small saucepan over a medium heat and simmer for ten minutes, or until the peppers and onions are cooked.

  4. Serve the cheese and bacon wraps on a plate with the salsa in a bowl to the side.

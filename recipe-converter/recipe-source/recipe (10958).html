Watercress and potato soup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ¼ onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • ½ large potato, peeled and finely chopped
  • 150ml/5fl oz chicken stock (vegetarians can substitute vegetable stock)
  • ½ bunch watercress, chopped
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a medium saucepan. Add the onion and garlic and sauté gently until softened.

  2. Add the remaining ingredients and cook gently for 8 minutes.

  3. Blend with a hand blender until smooth and pour into a bowl to serve.

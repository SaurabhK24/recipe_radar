Lime syrup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Makes 4 Servings

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Saturday Brunch

Ingredients

  • 225g/8oz caster sugar
  • 8 green cardamom pods, seeds removed and crushed
  • 150ml/5¼fl oz water
  • 150ml/5¼fl oz lime juice
  • 1 tbsp grated lime zest or to taste

To serve

  • pineapple chunks or rings

Method

  1. Put the caster sugar, cardamom pods and water in a pan. Stir over a gentle heat until the sugar has dissolved.

  2. Take off the heat and allow to cool before adding the lime zest. Mix and pour over the pineapple chunks or rings.

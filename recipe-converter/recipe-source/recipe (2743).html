Cranberry and pear sparkle

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 2 litres/3½ pints

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By The Vegetarian Society

Ingredients

  • 6 tbsp pear concentrate
  • 1 litre/1¾ pint sparkling water
  • 1 litre/1¾ pint cranberry juice
  • 1 medium pear, cut into 1cm/½in pieces
  • sprigs of fresh mint

Method

  1. Mix the pear concentrate with the sparkling water.

  2. Add the cranberry juice and chill well.

  3. Just before serving, add the chopped pear, and garnish with fresh mint leaves.

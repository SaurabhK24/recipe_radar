Warm brandied cakes with pistachio cream and hot chocolate sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 3

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the pistachio cream

  • 100g/3½oz mascarpone
  • 3 tbsp pistachios, chopped
  • 2 tsp icing sugar
  • 3 tbsp double cream

For the warm brandied cakes

  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 3 small ready-made muffins, tops removed
  • 30ml/1fl oz brandy

For the hot chocolate sauce

  • 50g/1¾oz butter
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp hot water
  • 2 tbsp double cream
  • few drops Tabasco sauce

Method

  1. For the pistachio cream, place the mascarpone, pistachios, icing sugar and double cream into a bowl and mix well to combine.

  2. For the brandied cakes, melt the butter in a frying pan over a medium heat, then add the brandy and the muffins. Warm through for three minutes on each side, turning carefully.

  3. Meanwhile, for the hot chocolate sauce, place all of the ingredients into a small saucepan and warm through to melt.

  4. To serve, place the warm cakes onto a serving plate, top with the pistachio cream and pour over the hot chocolate sauce.

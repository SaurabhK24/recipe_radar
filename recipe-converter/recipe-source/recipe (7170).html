Potato salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Recommended by

3 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

An American-style potato salad that packs a tangy punch. Try it with a traditional meatloaf, or any cold meats.

By Nancy Davis
From The Hairy Bikers: Mums Know Best

Ingredients

  • 4-5 medium-sized red potatoes
  • 2 tsp milk
  • 2-3 tsp American mustard
  • ½ tsp vinegar
  • 1-2 tsp liquid from a jar of dill pickles (or gherkins)
  • pinch sugar
  • mayonnaise, to taste
  • chopped dill pickle (or gherkin), optional
  • 1 free-range egg, hard-boiled (optional)

Method

  1. Cut each potato into eight pieces and cook in boiling salted water until tender. Drain and set aside to cool slightly.

  2. Mix the milk, mustard, vinegar, pickle juice and sugar together in a small bowl to make a dressing.

  3. When the potatoes have cooled, pour over enough dressing to coat the potatoes. Add mayonnaise to taste and stir in the chopped pickle and egg, if using. Chill in the fridge for a couple of hours before serving.

Goats' cheese pâté

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Mike Robinson
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1 tub goats' cheese
  • olive oil
  • mixed chopped herbs
  • crusty bread

Method

  1. Place the cheese, oil and herbs in a bowl and stir.

  2. Spread liberally on the crusty bread and serve.

Frozen strawberry daiquiri

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This recipe for a frozen daiquiri takes no time to make and it's the perfect summer cocktail.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 35ml/1¼fl oz white rum
  • 1 tbsp strawberry liqueur
  • 1 tsp sugar syrup
  • 20ml/¾fl oz freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 4 fresh strawberries

To serve

  • 1 fresh strawberry

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients into a food processor with a handful of crushed ice and blend until smooth.

  2. Pour into a goblet glass, garnish with a fresh strawberry and serve.

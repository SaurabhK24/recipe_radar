Sausages wrapped in sage and ham with cheese-topped swede

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the sausages

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 pork sausages
  • 2 slices air-dried ham
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh sage

For the cheese-topped swede

  • 1 small swede, chopped
  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 1 tsp coarsely ground black pepper
  • pinch salt
  • 50g/1¾oz Cornish Yarg cheese, grated
  • sprig fresh rosemary, to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the grill to medium.

  2. For the sausages, heat the oil in a frying pan and fry the sausages for about 2-3 minutes, turning frequently, until lightly browned on all sides. Lay the slices of ham on a work surface and sprinkle with the chopped sage. Roll the sausages up in the ham and return to the pan. Fry for about ten minutes, or until completely cooked through.

  3. For the cheese-topped swede, boil the swede in salted water for about 12-15 minutes until tender. Drain and mash with the butter, pepper and a pinch of salt. Place in an ovenproof dish and top with the cheese. Grill for about 5-7 minutes, until the cheese is bubbling.

  4. To serve, place a spoonful of the swede onto a serving plate. Cut each sausage in half diagonally and arrange on top. Garnish with a sprig of rosemary.

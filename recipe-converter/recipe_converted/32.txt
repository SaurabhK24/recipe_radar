Title: Carrot and watercress salad with tomatoes
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 5 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Phil Vickery

Ingredients:
2 carrots
1 handful watercress
3 tomatoes
1 tbsp red wine vinegar
3 tbsp olive oil
salt
pepper

Instructions:
Mix the carrots, watercress and tomatoes together in a bowl.
In a small bowl, whisk together the red wine vinegar and olive oil and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Drizzle over the salad and toss well to coat.
Serve immediately.

Title: Cheesy celeriac mash
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 15 mins
Serves: 2
Author: Ross Burden

Ingredients:
200g celeraic
salt
pepper
2 tbsp double cream
nutmeg
55g double cream
chervil

Instructions:
Bring a large pan of water the boil. Add the celeriac and boil for ten minutes, or until tender.
Add the nutmeg, salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste, and mash the celeriac to a rough paste.
Add the cream and brie, stirring to mix well.
Place into a warm bowl and garnish with the chervil.

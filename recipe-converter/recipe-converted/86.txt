


Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 8-10


Dietary

 
Vegetarian





Ingredients

1kg/2¼lb floury potatoes, peeled and cubed
1kg/2¼lb orange sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 small tin pineapple chunks, drained and finely chopped
60g/2oz walnuts, chopped roughly
4 tbsp crème fraiche
salt and pepper




Method


Put the potatoes and sweet potatoes in a pan of salted water and boil until tender.


Drain well and mash with the pineapple and crème fraiche.


Season well and stir in the walnuts. Turn into a heatproof dish and put under a hot grill until the surface is golden brown.


Serve at once or make in advance and reheat in the oven, covered with foil.

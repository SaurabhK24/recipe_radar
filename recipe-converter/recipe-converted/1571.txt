

Caramelised apple pan bake with cream



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the caramelised apple

55g/2oz butter
2 apples, peeled, cored and chopped
55g/2oz caster sugar

For the batter

120g/4oz butter
120g/4oz caster sugar
115g/4oz self-raising flour
1 free-range egg
1 tbsp milk
squeeze of lemon juice

To serve

icing sugar
single or double cream




Method


Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.


For the caramelised apple, melt the butter in an ovenproof frying pan and add the chopped apple. Stir well, then sprinkle over the sugar and cook for 2-3 minutes, until lightly caramelised.


Meanwhile, for the batter, place all the batter ingredients into a food processor and blend to form a smooth batter.


Spoon the batter over the caramelised apples in the pan. Transfer to the oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, until well risen and golden-brown.


Dust generously with icing sugar. Serve hot with a jug of cream on the side.

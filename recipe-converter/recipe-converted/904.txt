

Venison kebabs



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2







By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

3 venison sausages, skin removed
6 prunes
3 wooden skewers
1 tbsp olive oil




Method


Preheat oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Start by rolling the venison meat into nine small balls.


Take a skewer and alternately place a venison ball then a prune; each skewer should have three venison balls and two prunes.


Heat a frying pan add the oil and gently brown the kebabs.


Place into the preheated oven for six minutes and serve.



Fruit compôte



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

400g frozen winter fruits
50g/1¾oz stem ginger, cut into julienne
2 pinches ground mixed spice
50ml/2fl oz double cream




Method


Place a small saucepan over a medium heat.


Add the frozen winter fruit, the stem ginger, and the mixed spice. Bring to the simmer and cook for five minutes.


To serve, pour into a bowl and drizzle with double cream.

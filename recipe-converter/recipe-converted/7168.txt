

Pan-fried tomatoes



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

small bunch fresh basil
2 tbsp olive oil, plus 1 tbsp extra
1 garlic clove, crushed
sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
2 tomatoes, thickly sliced
25g/1oz plain flour




Method


Place the basil, oil and garlic into a food processor and blend until smooth. Season, to taste, with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Spoon the basil oil onto one side of the cut tomatoes.


Place the extra tbsp oil into a frying pan over a high heat.


Dredge the tomato slices in the flour, then place the tomatoes into the hot pan and fry for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden.

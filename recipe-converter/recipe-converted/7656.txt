

Pineapple 'carpaccio' with cardamom syrup



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1-2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Paul Rankin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

½ pineapple, peeled and thinly sliced
25g/1oz caster sugar
100ml/3½fl oz water
3 cardomom pods, crushed
whipped cream, to serve




Method


Place the pineapple slices around a large plate in a fan shape and set aside.


In a saucepan, heat the sugar, water and cardamom and bring to the boil, then reduce the heat to simmer for 4-5 minutes to thicken the syrup.


To serve, pour the syrup over the pineapple and top with a dollop of cream.

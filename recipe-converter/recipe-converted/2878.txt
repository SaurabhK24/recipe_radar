

Coffee ice



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By James Martin






Ingredients

2 cardamom pods
1 scoop vanilla ice cream
1 cup strong coffee (with sugar if preferred)




Method


Crush the cardamom pods in a pestle and mortar to release the small black seeds.


Spoon the ice cream onto the coffee and scatter with the seeds.


Drink or spoon the coffee through the melting ice cream.




Recipe Tips
This recipes also works well using cinnamon as flavouring.

Title: Baked eggs
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 10 mins
Serves: 2
Author: Ed Baines

Ingredients:
35g butter
4 eggs
2 handfuls of grated cheese
salt
pepper

Instructions:
Turn the oven to the highest setting and smear butter over two small ovenproof dishes. Cover with a combination of ham, mushrooms and spinach, if liked.
Crack a couple of eggs over the top, followed by grated cheese, salt and pepper.
Cook for approximately 10 minutes.

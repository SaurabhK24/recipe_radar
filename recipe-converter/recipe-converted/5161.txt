

Hot Vacherin



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 3-4


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian







By Full on Food






Ingredients

1 x 500g/1lb Vacherin Mont d'Or cheese
fresh figs, chicory, biscuits and raisin bread, to serve




Method


Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.


Place the whole Vacherin, in its lidded box, on a baking tray in the oven and bake for 20-25 minutes until runny.


Place the box of melted cheese on a serving plate and serve with fresh figs and chicory and some savoury biscuits and raisin bread, like a fondue.

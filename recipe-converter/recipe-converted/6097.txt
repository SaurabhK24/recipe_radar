

Mango with lime



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Paul Rankin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

½ mango, peeled and sliced
icing sugar, to dust
½ lime, juiced and zested
double cream, to serve




Method


Place the mango in a bowl and dust heavily with icing sugar.


Add the lime juice and zest to the bowl and mix. Set aside and allow to marinate for up to ten minutes.


Meanwhile, whip the cream in a clean bowl to thicken.


Transfer the mango to a serving dish and spoon the whipped cream on top to serve

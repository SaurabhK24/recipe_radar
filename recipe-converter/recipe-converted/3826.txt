

Festive pilau rice



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for oiling
½ large onion, chopped
½ red pepper, chopped
1 garlic clove, chopped
50g/1¾oz walnuts, shelled, chopped
75g/2¾oz canned red kidney beans, rinsed and drained
200g/7oz basmati rice, cooked in vegetable stock or according to packet instructions
salt and freshly ground black pepper
1 large bunch fresh chervil, chopped




Method


Heat the oil in a large frying pan and fry the onion, red pepper and garlic until soft.


Add the walnuts and kidney beans to the pan and cook for a further 2-3 minutes.


Add the rice to the pan, warm through and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Remove the pan from the heat and stir in the chopped chervil.


Lightly oil a large bowl and pack the rice mixture into the bowl.


To serve, turn the rice out onto a plate to make a dome shape.

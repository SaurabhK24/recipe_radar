

Whisky sabayon



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

4 free-range egg yolks
2 tbsp icing sugar
2 tbsp whisky
½ tsp orange zest




Method


Whisk together the ingredients in a bowl over a saucepan of boiling water for five minutes, until warmed through.


Pour into a tall glass and serve.

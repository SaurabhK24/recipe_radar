

Nectarine fizz



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Ross Burden


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 nectarine
1 tbsp caster sugar
1 glass white wine
2 tbsp double cream
2 ice cubes




Method


Blend the nectarine with the caster sugar in a blender.


Add the white wine, cream and ice cubes and blend until smooth.


Serve.

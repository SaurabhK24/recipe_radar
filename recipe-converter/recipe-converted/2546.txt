

Chocolate fruits



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 4


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Rick Stein


From Fresh Food






Ingredients

200g/7oz good quality white chocolate, broken into pieces
210ml/7fl oz double cream
700g/1½lb frozen mixed summer berries




Method


Heat some water to boiling point in a saucepan big enough to sit a large heatproof bowl.


Place the chocolate into the bowl and place it over the pan of boiling water. Don't allow the base of the bowl to come into contact with the water.


Melt the chocolate and then add the double cream and incorporate it into the chocolate.


Place the frozen berries in a serving dish and pour over the hot chocolate mixture and serve.

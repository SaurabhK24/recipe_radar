

Celery soup




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
60 people



This easy celery soup recipe is surprisingly elegant and won't break the bank. A little cream wouldn't go amiss either.

  Each serving provides 401kcal, 17g protein, 47g carbohydrate (of which 9g sugars), 15g fat (of which 2g saturates), 6.5g fibre and 1.3g salt.







By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil
1 garlic clove, chopped
½ red onion, chopped
150g/5½oz celery, chopped
200ml/7fl oz hot chicken stock
2 slices toasted ciabatta, to serve




Method


Heat the oil in a saucepan and cook the garlic, onion and celery over a low heat for five minutes, until softened.


Add the chicken stock and simmer for 10-12 minutes.


Transfer the soup to a blender and liquidise.


To serve, place the soup into a bowl with the toasted ciabatta alongside.

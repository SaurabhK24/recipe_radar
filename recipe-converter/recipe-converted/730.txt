

Tzatziki



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes 300g/10oz


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian




Garlic, yoghurt, lemon and fresh mint make a wonderful dip to serve with pitta bread or your favourite kebab.






By Lisa Faulkner






Ingredients

2 garlic cloves
salt, to taste
300g/10oz natural yoghurt
1 lemon, juice only
small handful mint leaves, chopped




Method


Crush the garlic with a little salt in a pestle and mortar to make a paste.


Add the yoghurt and lemon juice and stir well. Stir through the mint. Set aside until needed.

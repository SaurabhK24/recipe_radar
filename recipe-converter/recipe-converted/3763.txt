

Baby potatoes with fennel



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Manju Malhi


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

310g/11oz baby salad potatoes
3 tbsp oil
¼ tsp fennel seeds (or ½ tsp sugar (if preferred)
¼ tsp salt
pinch of garam masala
¼ tsp chilli powder




Method


Par-boil the potatoes for 7-8 minutes, then drain them.


Leave them to cool.


In a frying pan heat the oil, add the spices and mix for 1 minute.


Tip in the potatoes and sauté for 3-4 minutes.

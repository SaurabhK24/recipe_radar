

Brandy snap baskets with strawberries



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Gino D'Acampo


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 ready-made brandy snap baskets
4 scoops vanilla ice cream
4 strawberries




Method


Place a brandy snap basket onto each of two serving plates.


Fill each basket with two scoops of ice cream.


Top each basket with two strawberries and serve.

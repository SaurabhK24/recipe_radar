

Ginger sponge pudding



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Ainsley Harriott


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

55g/2oz self-raising flour
55g/2oz butter, softened, plus extra for greasing
55g/2oz caster sugar
1 tbsp milk
1 tbsp clear honey
2.5cm/1in piece root ginger, peeled and finely grated
1 free-range egg
Greek-style yoghurt, to serve




Method


Lightly grease a small microwave-proof bowl.


Place all the ingredients except the egg and yoghurt into a food processor and blend to combine.


Crack in the egg and pulse briefly to mix.


Pour the mixture into the prepared bowl and microwave on high for 4-5 minutes, or until the pudding is set.


To serve, turn the pudding out onto a plate. Serve in bowls with a dollop of yoghurt.

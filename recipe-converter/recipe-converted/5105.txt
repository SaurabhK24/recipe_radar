

Hot artichoke sin



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 4


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Celia Brooks


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

1x400g can artichoke hearts, drained, chopped chunky
250ml/9fl oz mayonnaise
140g/5oz fresh parmesan, grated
2 green chillies, seeded and chopped
plain tortilla chips, for scooping




Method


Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.


In a bowl, mix together the artichokes, mayonnaise, cheese and chillies. Spread into a wide baking dish in a layer not more than 2cm/¾in thick.


Bake the mixture until bubbling and golden, about 15 minutes. Serve with tortilla chips for scooping.

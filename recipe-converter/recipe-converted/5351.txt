

Baked pear



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Gino D'Acampo


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp cranberry sauce from a jar
1 tbsp double cream
1 pear, peeled and cored
55g/2oz Stilton




Method


Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.


Stuff the pear with the Stilton and place onto a baking sheet. Place in the oven and bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the Stilton has melted.


Place the cranberry sauce and cream in a small pan over a low heat for 2-3 minutes.


To serve, place the baked pear on a serving plate and pour the cranberry sauce over.



Sweet baked mango and lime 'bruschetta'



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 slice crusty white bread
25g/1oz butter, melted
½ mango, peeled and sliced
1 tsp caster sugar
1 lime, zest and juice

To serve

1 tbsp double cream
icing sugar, to dust
lime wedges




Method


Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.


Dip the slice of bread into the melted butter to coat and place onto a baking sheet. Top with the sliced mango and sprinkle with caster sugar, lime zest and juice. Place into the oven and bake for five minutes.


Remove from the oven and place onto a serving plate. Drizzle with cream, dust with icing sugar and garnish with lime wedges. Serve immediately.

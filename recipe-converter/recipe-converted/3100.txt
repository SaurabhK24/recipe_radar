

Cranberry and pear sparkle



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes 2 litres/3½ pints


Dietary

 
Vegetarian







By The Vegetarian Society






Ingredients

6 tbsp pear concentrate
1 litre/1¾ pint sparkling water
1 litre/1¾ pint cranberry juice
1 medium pear, cut into 1cm/½in pieces
sprigs of fresh mint




Method


Mix the pear concentrate with the sparkling water.


Add the cranberry juice and chill well.


Just before serving, add the chopped pear, and garnish with fresh mint leaves.

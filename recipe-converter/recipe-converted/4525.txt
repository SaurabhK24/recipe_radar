

Green bean, red pepper and brazil nut salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian







By Garrey Dawson


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

75g/2½oz green beans, topped and tailed
1 red pepper, sliced
2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
2 tsp sesame seeds, toasted
30g/1oz brazil nuts, chopped
4 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
1 tbsp red wine vinegar
1 tsp Dijon mustard
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Place the green beans into a steamer and steam for 4-5 minutes, until tender. Transfer to a bowl, add the rest of the salad ingredients and season to taste with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Stir well to combine.


Spoon into a serving bowl and serve.

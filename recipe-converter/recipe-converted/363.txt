

The boss



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
1


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

5-6 fresh mint leaves, plus extra to garnish
25ml/1fl oz bourbon whiskey
25ml/1fl oz apple liqueur
25ml/1fl oz cranberry juice
splash American ginger ale
1 slice red apple, to garnish




Method


Roll the mint leaves in the palms of your hands to bruise them slightly and release the mint aromas.


Place the bruised mint leaves into a highball glass and cover with ice.


Pour over the bourbon, apple liqueur and cranberry juice, then top up with the ginger ale.


To serve, hook the apple slice over the edge of the glass. Garnish with a sprig of mint.

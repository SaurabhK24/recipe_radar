

Vegan chocolate fudge icing




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes enough for a 9in cake


Recommended by
3 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian




Just because you’re avoiding dairy, doesn’t mean you don’t get the pleasure of irresistible chocolate fudge icing on your cakes.





By Charlotte O'Leary






Ingredients

55g/2oz soya margarine
3 tbsp soya milk
200g/7oz icing sugar
30g/1oz cocoa powder




Method


Place the soya margarine, soya milk, icing sugar and cocoa in a bowl over a saucepan of simmering water.


Stir well until the icing is smooth and glossy.


Remove it from the heat and let it cool completely, then use to ice your cake.

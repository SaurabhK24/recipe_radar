

Pisco sour



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian




A delicious recipe for a pisco sour cocktail, with brandy, lemon juice, egg white and sugar






By Andy Pearson






Ingredients

50ml/1¾fl oz pisco (grape brandy)
50ml/1¾fl oz lime juice
25ml/1fl oz sugar syrup
1 dash bitters
½ free-range egg white

For the garnish

1 cherry, stem attached
1 lemon slice




Method


Place all of the ingredients except the cherry and lemon slice into a cocktail shaker and shake.


Strain into a rocks glass and garnish with a fresh cherry on the stem and a lemon slice.

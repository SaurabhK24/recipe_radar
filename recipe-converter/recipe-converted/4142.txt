

Fried halibut steaks



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Recommended by
4 people







By Nigel Slater


From A Taste of My Life






Ingredients

30g/1oz flour
2 halibut steaks, about 2.5cm/1 inch thick
juice of 1 lemon
olive oil, for frying
chopped parsley
lemon wedges, to serve




Method


Scatter a think layer of flour onto a plate. Dip the halibut steaks into the lemon juice, then into the flour on both sides.


Fry the fish until firm and cooked right through, about five minutes on each side.


Serve hot scattered with parsley and served with large wedges of lemon.

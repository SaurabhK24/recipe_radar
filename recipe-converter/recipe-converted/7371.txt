

Pea and mint soup



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1







By Nick Nairn


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

30g/1oz butter
½ onion, chopped
1 garlic clove, finely chopped
300ml/½pint chicken stock
300g/10½oz peas
2 tbsp double cream
2 tbsp fresh mint, chopped
Fresh chives, to garnish




Method


Melt the butter in a saucepan over a medium heat, and when bubbling add the onion and garlic and cook, for five minutes, or until softened.


Add the chicken stock, peas, cream and mint to the saucepan and simmer for two minutes.


Blend the soup mixture with a hand blender and pour into a clean bowl.


To serve, garnish with chives.

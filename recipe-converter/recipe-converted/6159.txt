

Marshmallow and banana chocolate skewers



Preparation time
1-2 hours


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 4







By Maria Elia


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

2 small bananas, sliced
1 200g/7oz packet marshmallows
200g/7oz chocolate, good quality dark chocolate




Method


Break the chocolate into small pieces and melt in a bowl over a pan of boiling water.


Peel and cut the bananas into 2cm/Â¾in slices.


Skewer a marshmallow then add a banana onto the skewers.


Remove the chocolate from the heat.


Dip the banana and marshmallow into the chocolate.


Place on parchment paper and put in the fridge until the chocolate sets.


Serve in a vase standing upright!



Thai salmon and lemongrass broth



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves







By Paul Rankin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

200ml/7fl oz coconut milk
1 spring onion, thinly sliced
1 stick lemongrass, roughly chopped
125g/4Â½oz salmon fillet, sliced
small handful fresh coriander
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Place the coconut milk, spring onion and lemongrass into a small saucepan and bring to a gentle simmer.


Add the salmon and cook for 1-2 minutes. Stir in the coriander, salt and freshly ground black pepper


Serve straightaway poured into a soup bowl.

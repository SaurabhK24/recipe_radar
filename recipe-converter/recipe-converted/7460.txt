

Pears poached in red wine and rosemary with Northumberland cheese



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

150ml/¼ pint red wine
85g/3oz caster sugar
1 pear, peeled, cored and quartered
sprig fresh rosemary
55g/2oz Northumberland cheese, crumbled, to serve




Method


Heat the wine and sugar in a pan until the sugar dissolves.


Add the pear pieces and the rosemary and simmer for 10-15 minutes, or until the pears are soft and the sauce has thickened.


To serve, place a spoonful of the sauce onto a serving plate and top with the pears and the cheese.



Grilled sesame prawns with red pepper and garlic dressing



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1







By Richard Phillips


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the red pepper and garlic dressing

1 tbsp olive oil
½ onion, finely chopped
1 garlic clove, chopped
1 red pepper, chopped
100ml/3½fl oz white wine
½ tsp sea salt

For the prawns

2 king prawns, shells removed, de-veined
1 tbsp sesame seeds
1 tbsp olive oil




Method


For the red pepper and garlic dressing, heat the oil in a frying pan over a medium and fry the onion and garlic for 4-5 minutes, until soft but not coloured.


Add the red pepper and stir well, then pour in the wine and add the salt. Boil for 5-7 minutes, until the dressing is beginning to reduce and the red pepper is tender.


For the prawns, roll the prawns in the sesame seeds to completely coat.


Heat a griddle pan and drizzle with the oil. Add the prawns and cook for two minutes on each side, until completely cooked through .


To serve, spoon the red pepper and garlic dressing onto a plate and place the sesame prawns on top.



Thai noodles



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Mike Robinson


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

250g packet medium egg noodles
1 tsp sesame oil
2 tsp soy sauce
1 tsp peeled and finely grated galangal
2 bird's-eye red chillies, chopped
2 tsp chopped fresh holy basil
1 tsp chopped fresh coriander
1 lime, juice only
1 tsp clear honey
1 tsp sesame seeds




Method


Cook the noodle according to the packet instructions.


In a large bowl mix together the remaining ingredients.


Drain and toss the noodles in the Thai mixture.


Serve.



Lemon cheesecake cocktail




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian




If you don’t fancy cheese or a pudding then a dessert cocktail is a naughty alternative to dessert.






By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

35ml/1¼fl oz vanilla vodka
1 tbsp limoncello
1 tbsp vanilla syrup
pinch lemon zest
1 tbsp natural yoghurt
1 heaped tbsp mascarpone

To garnish

digestive biscuit, crumbled




Method


Place all the ingredients in a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.


Strain the cocktail into a coupette glass and garnish with the crumbled biscuit.

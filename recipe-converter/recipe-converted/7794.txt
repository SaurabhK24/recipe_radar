

Poached peaches in brandy butterscotch sauce



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 peaches, skinned and halved
100ml/3½fl oz brandy
splash of water
85g/3oz caster sugar
1 tbsp butter
splash cream




Method


Heat the oven to 250C/500F/Gas 9.


Place the peaches, brandy, a splash of water and sugar in an oven-proof frying pan and place in the hot oven.


Bake for ten minutes.


Remove from the oven and set the peaches aside leaving the syrup in the pan.


Place on a hob at a high heat and add the butter and cream and reduce for 1-2 minutes.


Pour the sauce over the peaches and serve.

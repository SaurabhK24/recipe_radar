

Pan-fried tuna with salsa rossa



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2







By Mitch Tonks


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

2 tuna steaks

For the salsa

1 garlic clove, pasted
1 red pepper, roasted, peeled and diced
2 leaves radicchio, shredded
handful basil, chopped
handful mint, chopped
1 tsp capers
1 small chilli, seeded and finely chopped
2 anchovy fillets, roughly chopped
1 tomato, seeded and chopped into small dice
1 lemon, juice only
pinch sea salt




Method


Fry the tuna steaks for a couple of minutes on each side.


Mix the salsa ingredients in a bowl; add a squeeze of lemon and a pinch of crunchy sea salt. Serve.

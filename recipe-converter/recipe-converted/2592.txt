

Chocolate sponge pudding with chocolate sauce and ice cream



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the Chocolate pudding

75g/2¾oz brown sugar
75g/2¾oz butter
2 free-range eggs
125g/4oz self raising flour
1 tsp vanilla essence
chocolate bar crumbled
2 chocolate cookies crushed

For the chocolate sauce

100ml/3½fl oz water
3 tbsp cocoa powder
3 tbsp caster sugar

To serve

vanilla ice cream




Method


To make the sponge pudding put the butter and sugar in a food processor and blend.


Add the eggs, flour and vanilla essence and blend again.


Butter a small pudding dish and add the crumbled chocolate, cookies and then top with the pudding mixture.


Cover with cling film and cook on high in the microwave for four minutes.


To make the chocolate sauce, put the water in a small pan, add the cocoa powder and sugar and reduce to thicken it.


To serve, place the pudding upside and turn out. Drizzle the sauce over and serve with ice cream.

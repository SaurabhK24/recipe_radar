

Bruschetta



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian




With a snack this simple, quality ingredients are key - use ripe tomatoes, full-bodied olive oil and farmhouse bread.






By Gino D'Acampo






Ingredients

5 tomatoes, sliced in half
3-4 mushrooms, sliced
1 clove garlic, chopped
3 tbsp olive oil
salt and freshly ground black pepper
1 slice bread, toasted
basil to garnish




Method


Pan-fry the tomatoes, mushrooms and garlic in the oil for three to four minutes until cooked through.


Season, place on the toast and serve garnished with basil.

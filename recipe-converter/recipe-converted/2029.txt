

Apple sponge pudding



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Makes 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

110g/4oz butter
110g/4oz caster sugar
zest of 1 lemon
2 free-range eggs, beaten
55g/2oz self-raising flour
55g/2oz ground almonds
2 apples, each halved and cut into eight pieces

To serve

icing sugar
double cream




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


In a food processor blend together the butter and sugar until pale.


Add the eggs and lemon zest and blend to combine.


Add the self-raising flour and ground almonds. Pulse with the blender until just combined.


Mix the apples and the sponge mix and place into a greased, small, deep, oven-proof frying pan. Place in the the oven for 15 minutes, or until risen and golden brown.


Serve dusted with icing sugar and drizzled with cream.



Banana smoothie



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
5 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 banana, peeled and sliced
½ pint milk
handful of ice
1-2 tbsp honey




Method


Place the banana, milk, ice and honey in a blender and then blend until smooth.


Transfer to a serving glass and serve at once.



Deep fried strawberries and ice cream



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1







By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

300m/10Â½fl oz vegetable oil
125g/4oz self-raising flour
fizzy water, enough to mix
4 large strawberries, thickly sliced

To serve

mini marshmallows
1 scoop vanilla ice cream




Method


Place the flour in a bowl and whisk in enough water until it forms a thick batter.


Coat each strawberry slice in batter, and deep fry for one minute until golden. Drain on kitchen paper.


To serve, place the deep fried strawberries on a dish and top with marshmallows and ice cream.

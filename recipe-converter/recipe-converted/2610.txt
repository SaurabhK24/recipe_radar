

Chorizo guacamole



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 2


Recommended by
1 person



As if lime-flavoured guacamole wasn’t tempting enough, Nigel Slater adds some chopped chorizo sausage to really get the taste buds going.






By Nigel Slater


From Nigel and Adam's Farm Kitchen






Ingredients

1 ripe avocado
1 lime, juice only
3 tbsp roughly chopped chives
150g/5½oz cooked chorizo sausage, roughly chopped




Method


Scoop the avocado flesh into a mixing bowl.


Squeeze the lime juice over the avocado to prevent it from discolouring.


Lightly crush the avocado with a fork.


Skin and roughly chop the chorizo.


Roughly chop the chives and then fold everything together with the avocado and serve.

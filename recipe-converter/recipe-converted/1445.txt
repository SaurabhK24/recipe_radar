

Buttered mint sugar snap peas



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

75g/3oz sugar snap peas
25g/1oz unsalted butter
2 tsp chopped fresh mint
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Boil the sugar snap peas in boiling salted water for three minutes.


Drain and mix in the butter, mint, salt and freshly ground black pepper. Serve in a bowl.

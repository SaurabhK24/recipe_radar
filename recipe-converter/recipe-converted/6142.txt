


Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Recommended by
8 people




Ingredients

2 x 180g/6oz salmon fillet steaks
2.5ml/½ tsp chilli sauce
juice and zest of 1 lime
2.5ml/½ tsp clear honey
ground black pepper
sprigs of fresh coriander




Method


Mix together the chilli sauce, honey and the juice and zest of the lime. Stir in plenty of ground black pepper.


Pour over the salmon fillets and leave to marinate for 5-10 minutes.


Place under a hot grill and cook for 2-3 minutes each side, depending on the thickness of the pieces of fish. They are ready when the salmon becomes opaque all the way through.


Serve sprinkled with the fresh coriander on top



Bruschetta with tomato and red onion



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Gino D'Acampo


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 thick slices of bread
1 tbsp olive oil

For the topping

25g/1oz chopped red onion
75g/2¾oz tinned tomatoes, chopped
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Heat a griddle pan and rub the bread with olive oil. Place the bread onto the griddle and cook for two minutes on each side, or until toasted.


For the topping, place the red onion, tomatoes and olive oil into a bowl and mix well.


Add the parsley and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.


To serve, place the toasted bread on the plate and spoon over the topping.

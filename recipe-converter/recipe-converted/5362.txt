

Baked pear with mint, feta and honey



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian







By Garrey Dawson


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 pear, cored, topped and tailed, thickly sliced
55g/2oz feta, crumbled
olive oil
1 tbsp honey
fresh mint sprig, to garnish




Method


Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.


Place the pear slices onto a baking sheet, top with the feta and drizzle with olive oil. Place into the oven to bake for 6-8 minutes, or until tender and golden-brown.


Remove the pears from the oven, transfer onto a serving plate and drizzle over the honey. Garnish with the mint sprig, and serve.

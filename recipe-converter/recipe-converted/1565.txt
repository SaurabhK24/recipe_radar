

Caramel brazils



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery






Ingredients

150g/5¼oz caster sugar
150g/5¼oz brazil nuts




Method


Heat the sugar in a frying pan. When it melts and reaches a dark caramel stage, add the brazil nuts and stir to coat well.


Pour onto baking parchment and leave to cool.


Break up and serve.

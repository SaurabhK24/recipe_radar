

'No butter' brandy butter



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes enough for one pudding


Dietary

 
Vegetarian




Free from: dairy, gluten, wheat, nuts and eggs





By Michelle Berriedale-Johnson






Ingredients

100g/3½oz dairy-free margarine
75g/2½oz pale muscovado sugar, or to taste
4 tbsp brandy, or to taste




Method


Beat the margarine hard with the sugar until it's light and fluffy.


Add the brandy to taste.

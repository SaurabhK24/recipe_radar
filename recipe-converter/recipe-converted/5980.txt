

Lychee and lime syrup



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Paul Rankin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

150g/5½oz caster sugar
50ml/2fl oz water
150g/5½oz fresh lychees, peeled
½ lime, juice and zest only




Method


Place the sugar and water in a small saucepan over a medium heat. Once the sugar has dissolved remove from the heat.


Place the lychees, lime juice and lime zest into a bowl and mix together.


Add the sugar syrup to the bowl of lychees and lime and mix together.


Serve in a bowl.

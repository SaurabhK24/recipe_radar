

Hot potato salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 4


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Ed Baines


From Housecall






Ingredients

2-3 large potatoes, cut into cubes olive oil
wine vinegar
medium sized onion, chopped
1-2 cloves garlic, crushed
handful fresh herbs, chopped
salt and pepper




Method


Boil the potatoes until just cooked.


Make up a simple viniagrette using 3 parts oil to 1 part vinegar.


Add the onions, garlic, herbs and seasoning.


Drain the potatoes and whilst they are still hot, tip them into the dressing. Stir well and serve.



Cardamom and maple syrup bread and butter pudding



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Richard Phillips


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

150ml/5½fl oz double cream
2 cardamom pods, seeds only
3 free-range egg yolks
2 tbsp maple syrup
pinch ground cinnamon
6 circles sliced white bread (to fit ramekins)
icing sugar, to dust




Method


Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.


Place the double cream, cardamom seeds, egg yolks, maple syrup and cinnamon into a pan and heat gently, stirring continuously.


Press a bread circle into the base of two ramekins, then pour over some of the egg mixture. Repeat with two more layers of bread and egg mixture, ending with a layer of egg mixture.


Place in the oven and bake for ten minutes, or until set and golden-brown on top. Dust with icing sugar and serve.

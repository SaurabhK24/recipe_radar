

Cucumber and mint raita




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 4


Recommended by
13 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian




A cool, fresh chutney that takes seconds to make. Use it to top fiery curries or as a dip for poppadoms.






By Jo' Pratt


From The Nation's Favourite Food






Ingredients

250ml/8fl oz natural yoghurt
½ cucumber, grated or finely chopped
large handful mint leaves, chopped
large pinch salt
½-1 green chilli, de-seeded and finely chopped (optional)




Method


Wrap the grated cucumber in a tea towel and squeeze out any excess water.


Mix together all the ingredients and serve chilled as an accompaniment to any curry or as a dip for poppadoms.



Chickpea, spinach and onion fattoush



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

½ red onion, finely chopped
1 clove garlic, finely chopped
½ beef tomato, de-seeded and finely chopped
3 mini white pitta breads, torn into small pieces
2 tbsp canned cooked chickpeas, rinsed and drained
55g/2oz spinach leaves
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp balsamic vinegar, plus extra to serve
6 fresh basil leaves, torn




Method


Combine all of the ingredients in a bowl and mix thoroughly to combine. Serve on a plate with an extra drizzle of balsamic vinegar.

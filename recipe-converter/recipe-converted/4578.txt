

Griddled lamb with eggy mash



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1







By Gino D'Acampo






Ingredients

Â½ sweet potato
3 tbsp double cream
1 tbsp chives, chopped
2 eggs
2 tbsp olive oil
1 lamb chump steak
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


To make the mash, peel and dice the sweet potato.


Boil until tender and drain.


Mash the sweet potato, mash in the cream and chives and season.


Boil the eggs for five to six minutes.


Peel and cut the eggs into quarters.


Drizzle oil over the lamb and season.


Griddle in a hot pan for two minutes either side.


Cut the steak into two pieces and serve on the mash with the egg.

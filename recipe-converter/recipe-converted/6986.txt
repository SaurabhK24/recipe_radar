

Pancakes



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Makes 6







By Nick Nairn


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

100g/3½oz self-raising flour
½ pint milk
pinch salt
1 free-range egg
butter for frying
honey, to serve




Method


Mix the flour, milk, salt and egg in a food processor.


Heat the butter in a pan.


Add the batter to the pan in two batches, frying for 1-2 minutes on each side, until golden.


Serve with honey.

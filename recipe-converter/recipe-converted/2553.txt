

Chocolate hazelnut pudding



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Ed Baines


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

50g/1¾oz chocolate
100ml/3½fl oz double cream
3 free-range egg yolks
25g/1oz icing sugar
splash brandy
50g/1¾oz hazelnuts, chopped
fresh orange segments (optional)




Method


Melt the chocolate with the cream in a small pan then leave to cool for a few minutes.


Whisk together the egg yolks and icing sugar.


Pour this into the chocolate mixture and continue to whisk until light and fluffy.


Stir in the brandy and hazelnuts.


Serve in a bowl or glass and top with the orange segments, if you like.



Fat chips with cheese fondue



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Martin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 potato, sliced into thick chips
2 tbsp olive oil
½ tsp paprika
salt and freshly ground black pepper
200ml/7fl oz double cream
250g/9oz red cheddar cheese
1 tbsp mustard powder
½ tsp ground cumin




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Place the potato chips onto a baking tray. Drizzle with the olive oil and sprinkle with the paprika. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Place into the preheated oven and cook for 15 minutes.


<strong> For the fondue </strong>


Heat the double cream in a saucepan. Add the cheese, mustard and cumin and cook until the cheese melts.


Serve the fondue with the fat chips.




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
1 to 2 hours


Serves
Serves 6-8


Recommended by
18 people


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian





Ingredients

1 medium red cabbage, quartered, cored and sliced
2 red onions, peeled and sliced
80g/3oz dried cranberries
2 Bramley apples, peeled, cored and grated
1 tsp ground cinnamon
25g/1oz demerara sugar
3 tbsp red wine vinegar
Salt and pepper




Method


Preheat the oven to 170C/3325F/Gas 3.


Mix the cabbage with the rest of the ingredients and put in an ovenproof dish.


Season well and cover with foil.


Bake in the oven for 60-90 minutes until the cabbage is tender and juicy. Mix the ingredients together 2-3 times during cooking.


Allow to cool and store in the fridge. Reheat when needed.

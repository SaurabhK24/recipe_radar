

Honey-glazed coriander carrots



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Recommended by
3 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 bunch baby carrots, washed and trimmed
1 tbsp butter
1 tbsp honey
1 tbsp chopped fresh coriander, to serve




Method


Bring a saucepan of water to the boil and blanch the carrots for 3-4 minutes, then drain.


Heat the butter in a small frying pan and add the carrots and honey. Sauté for 2-3 minutes, until the carrots are glazed.


To serve, stir in the coriander.

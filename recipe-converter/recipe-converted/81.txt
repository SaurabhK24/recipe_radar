

Sweet potato crisps



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Paul Rankin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 pint vegetable oil
½ sweet potato
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


To make the crisps, heat the oil in a large pan or in a deep fat fryer. (CAUTION: Hot oil can be very dangerous. Do not leave unattended.)


Slice the sweet potato on a mandolin. They should be round, thin discs.


Once the oil is heated, fry the sweet potatoes.


Drain on kitchen paper.


Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Serve in a large bowl.



Deep fried cashews



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Ross Burden


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

100g/3½oz cashew nuts
500ml/18fl oz vegetable oil
salt




Method


Heat the oil in a deep saucepan to 180C/350F (Caution: Hot oil can be dangerous. Do not leave unattended).


Carefully pour in the cashew nuts (beware of splatters) and deep fry until golden.


Remove the nuts with a slotted spoon, drain on kitchen paper and sprinkle with salt.


Serve in a bowl.

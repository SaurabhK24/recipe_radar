

Pancakes with fudge sauce and ice cream



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Martin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

100g/3½oz self-raising flour
½ tsp baking powder
2 free-range eggs
60ml/2¼fl oz milk
25g/1oz butter

For the fudge sauce

25g/1oz brown sugar
50ml/1¾fl oz double cream
2 scoops ice cream




Method


In a bowl, mix the self-raising flour, baking powder, eggs and milk to form a thick batter.


Melt the butter in a frying pan and ladle in the pancake mix a ladleful at a time. Cook until golden-brown on both sides.


Make a stack of pancakes and place on a plate.


For the fudge sauce, place the brown sugar in a saucepan with the butter and cream cook for three minutes to form a sauce.


Serve with the pile of pancakes and drizzle the sauce on top.



Caramelised apple with fried egg



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

15g/½ oz butter
1 small apple, cored and sliced
1 free-range egg




Method


Heat the butter in a non-stick frying pan. Add the apple slices and fry on both sides until golden and caramelised.


Transfer to a serving plate.


In the same pan, fry the egg, adding extra butter if needed.


Serve the fried egg on top of the apple slices.

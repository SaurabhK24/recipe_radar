

Cup cakes



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Makes 12-15


Recommended by
22 people


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian




Simple and easy small cakes perfect for a lunch box or tea-time.






By James Martin


From Housecall






Ingredients

110g/4oz unsalted butter
110g/4oz caster sugar
30g/1oz flavouring: lemon zest, grated or cocoa powder or 6 drops vanilla essence
2 eggs, whisked
110g/4oz self-raising flour
pale coloured icing




Method


Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.


Cream the butter and the sugar together until light and fluffy. Add your chosen flavoring of lemon zest cocoa or vanilla.


Add the whisked egg to the mixture. Slowly fold in the self-raising flour and spoon the mixture into paper cases sitting in a bun tin.


Cook for approximately 10 minutes until risen and bouncy to touch.


Decorate with icing in pale colours eg white, pink, blue and yellow.



Honey and lime tofu



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

½ packet firm tofu, dried and cut into three 1cm/½in slices
1 lime, zest and juice
2 tsp honey
1 garlic clove, sliced
olive oil, to drizzle
1 handful peanuts
toasted bread, to serve




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Place the tofu in an ovenproof dish and then drizzle over the remaining ingredients.


Place into the oven to bake for five minutes, or until cooked through.


Serve with toasted bread.

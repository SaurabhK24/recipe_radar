

Chicken and herb salad with onion mayonnaise



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1







By Paul Rankin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the salad

1 ready-cooked chicken breast, roughly chopped
handful fresh sage
handful fresh coriander
handful fresh parsley
handful fresh mint
handful fresh dill
handful fresh basil

For the onion mayonnaise

3 free-range egg yolks
2 tbsp caramelised onions from a jar
Â½ tsp white wine vinegar
salt and freshly ground black pepper
150ml/5fl oz olive oil




Method


For the salad, mix together the chicken and herbs in a clean bowl, and set aside.


For the onion mayonnaise, place the egg yolks, onion and white wine vinegar into a food processor and blend until smooth. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Once it looks slightly thickened begin to gradually add in the olive oil, blending constantly.


To serve, mix the onion mayonnaise into the salad.



Pineapple traybake



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

110g/4oz self-raising flour
110g/4oz brown sugar
110g/4oz butter
4 free-range eggs
1 baby pineapple, peeled and sliced
drizzle of honey




Method


Preheat the oven to 220C/428F/Gas 7.


Line a rimmed baking tray with baking parchment.


Mix the flour, sugar and butter in a food processor until combined. Add the eggs, one at a time, until the batter is smooth.


Lay the pineapple slices on the baking tray and drizzle with honey.


Pour the batter over the top and level out.


Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes or until cooked through and springy to the touch.



Teriyaki mushrooms



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Ross Burden


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 tbsp sesame oil
2 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbsp honey
12 button mushrooms, stems removed
1 Savoy cabbage leaf, to garnish




Method


Place the sesame oil, honey and soy sauce into a bowl and mix together.


Add the mushrooms and leave to marinate for ten minutes.


Heat a griddle pan until smoking. Thread the mushrooms onto skewers and place onto the griddle to cook for two minutes on each side.


To serve, place a Savoy cabbage leaf on the base of a plate and top with the mushroom skewers.

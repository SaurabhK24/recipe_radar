

Crisp onion and rocket salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Nick Nairn


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

55g/2oz rocket
1 tbsp olive oil
½ lemon, juice only
½ red onion, finely sliced
1 tsp brown sugar




Method


Place the rocket leaves into a bowl with half a tablespoon of olive oil and the lemon juice and mix well.


Heat the remaining olive oil in a small frying pan and fry the onion with the sugar until crisp and caramelised.


To serve, place the dressed rocket onto a serving plate and top with the onion.

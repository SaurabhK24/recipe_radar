

Classic champagne cocktail




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
6 people



This decadent cocktail of champagne laced with cognac is perfect for special occasions. Treat yourself this Christmas.






By Andy Pearson


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

1 white sugar cube
2 dashes bitters
20ml/Â¾fl oz cognac
enough champagne to fill the glass




Method


Place the sugar cube onto a spoon and add the bitters.


Drop the soaked sugar cube into a champagne flute and add the cognac.


Top up the glass with champagne and serve.

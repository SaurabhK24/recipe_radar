

Chorizo and bean quesadilla



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1







By Paul Rankin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

85g/3oz cheddar cheese, grated
55g/2oz chorizo, diced
85g/2oz mixed beans, diced
handful fresh coriander, chopped
salt and freshly ground black pepper
2 Mexican tortillas

To serve

2 tbsp olive oil
1 garlic clove, peeled and chopped
150g/5¼oz tin tomatoes




Method


Mix the cheese, chorizo, beans, coriander and salt and freshly ground black pepper in a bowl.


Spread the mixture onto one of the tortillas and top with the other.


Heat a little oil in a frying pan and pan-fry the quesadilla for 2-3 minutes on each side.


Meanwhile, heat the olive oil in a pan and sauté the garlic for one minute. Stir in the tomato and heat for a few minutes. Blend until smooth.


To serve, transfer the quesadilla to a plate and serve the tomato sauce alongside.

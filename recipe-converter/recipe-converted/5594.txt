

Langoustine cocktail



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1







By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the sauce

100g/3½oz Greek yoghurt
2 tbsp double cream
2 tbsp tomato ketchup
½ tsp Worcestershire sauce
½ tsp Tabasco sauce
1 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
½ lemon, juice only
salt and freshly ground black pepper

To serve

handful fresh coriander
4 langoustines, cooked and peeled
2 lemon wedges




Method


For the sauce, place all the ingredients into a bowl and mix together well.


To serve, place the coriander in a small bowl, add the langoustines, spoon over the sauce and garnish with the lemon wedges.

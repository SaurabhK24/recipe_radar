

Grilled peaches with honey and yoghurt



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Nick Nairn


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 peaches
1 tbsp runny honey
1 tbsp Greek-style yoghurt




Method


Heat a griddle pan and grill the peaches for 2-3 minutes on both sides.


Serve with a drizzle of honey and a dollop of Greek-style yoghurt.

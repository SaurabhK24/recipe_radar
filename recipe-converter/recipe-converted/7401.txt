

Peach mousse



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Gino D'Acampo


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the mousse

½ peach, peeled and finely chopped
½ orange, juice only
150ml/5fl oz double cream, whipped until stiff peaks form when the whisk is removed
½ tsp cinnamon powder
1 tbsp icing sugar

To serve

1 tsp cocoa powder
1 tbsp Greek yoghurt
1 sprig fresh mint




Method


For the mousse, place the peach and orange juice into a food processor and blend until smooth.


In a large bowl mix together the cream, cinnamon and icing sugar, then fold in the peach mixture. Place this mixture into a tall glass.


To serve, dust with cocoa powder, top with Greek yoghurt and garnish with a sprig of mint.

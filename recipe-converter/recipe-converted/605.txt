

Tropical fruit smoothie



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

250g/9oz frozen tropical fruit, defrosted
½ lime, juice only
100ml/3½fl oz Greek yoghurt
handful ice
85g/3oz raspberries
2 tsp clear honey




Method


Place all of the ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth.


To serve, pour the smoothie into a tall glass.

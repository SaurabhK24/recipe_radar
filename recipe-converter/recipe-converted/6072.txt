

Mango and lime shots



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes 4


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

½ mango peeled, stone removed and sliced
½ lime, zest only
1 lime, juice only
50g/1¾oz ice cubes
55ml/2fl oz sparkling mineral water
1 tbsp honey




Method


Place all the ingredients into a food processor and blend into a smooth purée.


Pour the mixture into four shot glasses and serve immediately.

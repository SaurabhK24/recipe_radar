

Broad bean salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Paul Rankin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

85g/3oz podded broad beans, cooked, skins removed
¼ red onion, peeled and chopped
1 tbsp chives, chopped
1 tbsp olive oil
½ lemon, juice only
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


To make the salad, mix the broad beans, red onion and chives in a bowl.


Drizzle over the olive oil and lemon juice and then add the salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Toss together and then transfer to a serving plate.

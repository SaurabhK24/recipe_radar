


Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 4


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian





Ingredients

125g/4oz cream cheese
120ml/4fl oz natural yoghurt
1 tbsp white wine vinegar
Salt and pepper
1 small cucumber, deseeded and very finely diced
2 tbsp freshly chopped mixed herbs such as basil, coriander, tarragon, chives, parsley




Method


Beat together the cream cheese, yoghurt and white wine vinegar and season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Fold in the cucumber and herbs.


Serve with hot flatbreads, crisps or crudities.

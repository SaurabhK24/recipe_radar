

Garlic and herb cheese salad with pitta bread



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

140g/4oz cream cheese
1 garlic clove, crushed and chopped
1 tablespoon mixed fresh herbs (flatleaf parsley, coriander, mint), finely chopped
1 spring onion, chopped

For the salad

2 handfuls mixed herbs (flatleaf parsley, coriander, mint)
2 tbsp olive oil
griddled pittas, cut into triangles




Method


Beat the cream cheese in a bowl until soft.


Add the garlic, herbs and spring onion and beat to combine.


For the herb salad, toss the herbs in the oil in a bowl.


Serve the garlic and herb cheese and the herb salad in bowls with griddled pittas to the side.

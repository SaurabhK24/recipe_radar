

Turkey mince kebabs



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Makes 3


Recommended by
1 person







By Ross Burden


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the curry paste

1 chilli, de-seeded and finely chopped
½ leek, finely chopped
3 garlic cloves, chopped
1 bunch coriander root, chopped
zest of 1 lime
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp fish sauce (nam pla)

For the turkey

125g/4½oz turkey mince
1 tbsp olive oil

To serve

little gem lettuce, broken up
lime wedges




Method


To make the curry paste, blend together all of the paste ingredients in a food processor.


Add one tablespoon of the curry paste to the turkey mince and mix through thoroughly in a bowl. Divide the mixture into thirds and mould around three skewers.


Heat a grill pan and add the oil. Grill the skewers, turning occasionally for 8-10 minutes.


Serve on the baby gem lettuce with the lime wedges.

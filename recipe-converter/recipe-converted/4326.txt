

Ginger, orange and vanilla rice pudding



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

500ml/18fl oz whole milk
250g/9oz pudding rice
50g/2oz caster sugar
1 vanilla pod, split and seeds scraped out
1 orange, juice and zest
2 pieces stem ginger, finely chopped
1 tbsp ginger syrup (from jar of stem ginger)

To serve

1 tbsp blueberries
1 orange, sliced




Method


Place the milk into a pan over a low heat and heat gently until simmering.


Add the rice, sugar, vanilla pod and seeds, orange juice and zest, ginger pieces and ginger syrup to the pan. Simmer for 8-10 minutes, or until the rice is tender.


To serve, pour into bowls and garnish with the blueberries and orange slices.



Vegetable stir-fry with chilli and garlic pork



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1






By Garrey Dawson


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

150g/5½oz pork fillet, sliced
pinch chilli flakes
dash olive oil
salt and freshly ground black pepper
2 garlic cloves, chopped
½ onion, finely sliced
1 carrot, very finely sliced
2 small leeks, very finely sliced




Method


Place the sliced pork into a bowl with the chilli flakes, a dash of olive oil, salt and freshly ground black pepper and garlic. Stir well and leave to marinate for ten minutes.


Heat a wok, add a dash of olive oil and stir fry the pork for 2-3 minutes, then remove to a plate.


Add a little more olive oil to the wok and return to the heat. Add the onion, carrot and leeks and stir fry for three minutes. Return the pork to the wok and stir well.


To serve, pile the stir-fry into a serving bowl.

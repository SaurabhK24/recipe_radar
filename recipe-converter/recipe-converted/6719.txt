

Basil and parsley-dressed pasta



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
3 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Gino D'Acampo


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

55g/2oz basil leaves, finely chopped
30g/1oz flatleaf parsley, finely chopped
2 tbsp olive oil
salt and freshly ground black pepper
150g/5½oz dried pasta of your choice




Method


Place the chopped herbs in a bowl with the olive oil and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Leave to infuse.


Cook the pasta according to the instructions on the packet. When the pasta is cooked, drain and stir in the oil and herbs. Serve immediately.

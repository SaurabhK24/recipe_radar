

Vegetable stir-fry



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Paul Rankin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp vegetable oil
½ onion, sliced
1 tbsp chopped fresh root ginger
1 garlic clove, chopped
½ red pepper, cut into strips
½ yellow pepper, cut into strips
1 leaf choi sum, shredded
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp sesame oil




Method


Heat the oil in a pan and gently fry the onion, ginger and garlic for 3-4 minutes, or until softened. Stir in the peppers and cook for a further 2-3 minutes, then add the choi sum, soy sauce and sesame oil. Stir well and cook for a further 2-3 minutes, or until the choi sum is wilted.


Spoon the stir-fry into a bowl and serve.



Pears poached in red wine



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Nick Nairn


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

200ml/7fl oz red wine
50g/1¾oz brown sugar
2 whole star anise
1 pear, peeled
3 tbsp Greek yoghurt, to serve




Method


In a pan heat together the red wine, sugar and star anise until gently simmering.


Add the pear and poach for 5-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until tender.


To serve, place the pear into a bowl with the cooking liquor and Greek yoghurt spooned alongside.

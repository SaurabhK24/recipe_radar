

Homemade lemonade




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes 1 litre


Recommended by
9 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian




This simple recipe for homemade lemonade is perfect for picnics and barbecues. Add a splash of gin for a grown-up drink.






By Richard Corrigan


From Full on Food






Ingredients

100g/3½ oz fruit sugar
4 lemons, preferably Sicilian, juice only
ice
about 1 litre/15¼fl oz sparkling mineral water




Method


Put the sugar and lemon juice in a jug.


Add some ice and top up with the mineral water.


Stir to combine.



Cinnamon French toast with honey, cream and orange



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 free-range egg
150ml/5fl oz milk
1 tbsp caster sugar
½ tsp ground cinnamon
2 slices white bread
25g/1oz butter

To serve

1 orange, peeled and sliced
icing sugar, for dusting
2 tbsp clear honey
3 tbsp double cream




Method


Whisk the egg, milk, sugar and cinnamon together in a bowl until well combined, then soak the bread slices in the mixture.


Melt the butter in a frying pan, add the bread slices and fry for 1-2 minutes on both sides, or until golden-brown all over.


To serve, arrange the orange slices on a serving plate, place the toast on top and dust with icing sugar. Drizzle over the honey and cream.

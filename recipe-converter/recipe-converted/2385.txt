Try infusing your cocktails with tea for a sophisticated tipple.






By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

50ml/2fl oz Scotch whisky
1 Lady Grey tea bag
1 tsp peach syrup
2 dashes angostura bitters
twist lemon peel, to garnish




Method


Pour the Scotch into a mixing jug with a handful of ice. Dip the teabag in for about 20 seconds to infuse, remove and add the remaining ingredients.


Stir until well combined, then strain the cocktail into a tumbler filled with ice. Garnish with the lemon twist.

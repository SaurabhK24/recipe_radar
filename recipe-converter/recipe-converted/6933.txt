

Oven-roasted chips



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By James Martin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 large potato, peeled and sliced into long chips
4 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
2 cloves garlic, sliced
3 tbsp olive oil




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/390F/Gas 6.


Place the potato chips flat on a baking tray. Sprinkle with the thyme leaves and garlic and season.


Drizzle with the olive oil and cook into the oven for 15 minutes, or until crisp and golden.


Serve.



Honey, vanilla and orange rice pudding



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

75g/2½ oz rice
150ml/5fl oz milk
1 orange, segmented, with juice and zest
1 vanilla pod, seeds only
50ml/1¾fl oz double cream
drizzle honey




Method


Place the rice, milk, orange juice, vanilla seeds, double cream and honey in a pan and bring to the boil.


Turn down to a low heat and cook gently for 8-10 minutes or until the rice has absorbed all the liquid.


Serve hot topped with the orange segments.

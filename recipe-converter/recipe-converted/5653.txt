

Leek and potato soup



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

¼ potato, diced
¼ leek, sliced
1 tbsp butter
150ml/5fl oz milk
150ml/5fl oz double cream
handful of fresh chives, chopped




Method


Place all the ingredients, except the chives into a saucepan and cook for 15 minutes or until cooked through.


Add the chives and serve.

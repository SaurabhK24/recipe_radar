

Pistachio syllabub



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

75ml/3fl oz whipped cream
75ml/3fl oz mascarpone
75g/3oz pistachio nuts, finely chopped
2 tbsp icing sugar
1 orange, juice and zest only
3 ready-made sponge fingers, to serve




Method


Place all the ingredients except the sponge fingers into a bowl and mix together.


Spoon the mixture into a tall glass on a plate with the sponge fingers served alongside.

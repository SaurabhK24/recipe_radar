

Chinese-style crispy pork with onion



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person







By Ed Baines


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

oil for deep frying
50g/1¾oz plain flour
50g/1¾oz cornflour
½ tsp paprika
½ tsp cumin
½ tsp chilli powder
½ pork fillet, cut into strips
½ onion, cut into rings
50g/1¾fl oz milk




Method


Heat the oil ready for deep frying. (CAUTION: Hot oil can be very dangerous. Do not leave unattended.)


Mix together the flours and the spices and coat the pork.


Soak the onion in the milk then toss through the flour mix.


Deep fry both the pork and the onion rings for 2-3 minutes or until crisp and golden. Serve.

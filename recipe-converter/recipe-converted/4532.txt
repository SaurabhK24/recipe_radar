

Green beans with spring onions and parsley butter



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Tony Tobin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

150g/5½oz green beans, trimmed
25g/1oz butter
3 spring onions, sliced
salt and freshly ground black pepper
1 tbsp honey
2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley




Method


Cook the beans in a saucepan of boiling, salted water for 3-4 minutes, or until they have softened slightly but are not cooked through, then drain.


Melt the butter in a frying pan until foaming, then add the beans, sliced spring onions, and salt and pepper. Cook for 2-3 minutes.


Drizzle the vegetables with the honey and stir in the parsley, then serve.

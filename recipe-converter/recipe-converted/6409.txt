

Mini fry-up



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1







By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 tbsp olive oil
½ Portobello mushroom
½ red onion, sliced into thick rings
½ vine tomato
2 rashers smoked bacon
2 quails' eggs
2 slices white bread, toasted




Method


Heat the oil in a pan and fry the mushroom and red onion for 5-6 minutes, or until browned. In the same pan, fry the tomato half and the bacon for 3-4 minutes, or until the bacon is crisp, then add the quails' eggs and cook to your liking.


Serve the mushrooms and onions on the toast, with the tomato, bacon and eggs on top.

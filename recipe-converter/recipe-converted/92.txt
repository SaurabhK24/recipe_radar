Title: Leek and potato soup
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 15 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Brian Turner

Ingredients:
1/4 leek
1 tbsp butter
150ml milk
150ml double cream
handful fresh chives

Instructions:
Place all the ingredients, except the chives into a saucepan and cook for 15 minutes or until cooked through.
Add the chives and serve.



Vegetable broth



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Nick Nairn


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

½ onion, roughly chopped
2 carrots, roughly chopped
¼ potato, peeled and roughly chopped
300ml/½ pint chicken or vegetable stock
salt and freshly ground black pepper
2 tbsp chopped fresh chives, to serve




Method


Place the vegetables and stock into a saucepan and bring to the boil.


Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper and simmer for 10-15 minutes, or until all of the vegetables are tender.


To serve, ladle the broth into two soup bowls and sprinkle over the chives.



Tomato mozzarella salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Tony Tobin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

8 cherry tomatoes, halved
1 thick slice mozzarella cheese
2 tbsp olive oil

To serve

2 tbsp chopped fresh basil




Method


Place the cherry tomatoes onto a plate.


Heat the oil in frying pan and when hot, add the mozzarella and fry for one minute on each side, until golden.


To serve, place the mozzarella on top of the tomatoes and sprinkle with basil.

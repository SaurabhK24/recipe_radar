

Honey and sultana-glazed croissants



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Gino D'Acampo


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 ready-made croissants, sliced in half lengthways
4 tbsp honey
2 tbsp sultanas
icing sugar, to dust




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Place the croissants cut-side up on a non-stick baking sheet. Drizzle with honey and sprinkle over the sultanas.


Transfer to the oven and bake for five minutes, or until warmed through.


To serve, place the warm croissants onto serving plates and dust with icing sugar.



Caramelised oranges with amaretti and cream



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Gino D'Acampo


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

30g/1oz butter
2 tbsp brown sugar
1 orange, peel and pith removed, sliced into rounds
½ miniature bottle of brandy
2 tbsp amaretti biscuits, crushed
1 tbsp grated dark chocolate
fresh mint sprig, to garnish
double cream, to serve




Method


Heat the butter and sugar together in a small frying pan until the sugar has dissolved.


Add the sliced oranges and cook for 2-3 minutes, until they are coated and caramelised.


Add the brandy and carefully allow to flame.


When the flames have died out, remove from the heat and arrange the orange slices around a shallow serving dish.


Pour the sauce from the frying pan over the oranges.


Sprinkle over the crushed biscuits, grated chocolate and garnish with a sprig of mint.


To serve, drizzle over some double cream.

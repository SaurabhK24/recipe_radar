

Watermelon cooler



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian




You don't need a juicer to make this tasty watermelon drink. Make it a few hours in advance as it's best served chilled.





By Neil Strawson and Richard Hunt


From I Can Cook on the Go






Ingredients

550g/1lb 4oz watermelon, peeled, chopped
¼ lemon, juice only
fresh mint leaves




Method


Mash the watermelon in a bowl, then stir in the lemon juice.


Sieve the watermelon into a jug and press down gently on the pulp to extract all the juice.


Pour the juice into glasses filled with ice and garnish with the mint.



Pea, mint and cheddar frittata



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Gino D'Acampo


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil
15g/½oz butter
½ onion, finely chopped
1 tomato, chopped
200g/7oz frozen peas, defrosted
salt and freshly ground black pepper
55g/2oz cheddar, grated
small handful fresh mint leaves, chopped
3 free-range eggs, lightly whisked




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


In a small ovenproof non-stick frying pan, heat the olive oil and butter together and sauté the onion until soft, but not coloured.


Add the tomato and peas and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Pour over the eggs and cheese and cook over a low/medium heat until the base is set.


Transfer to the oven for 8-10 minutes, or until cooked through.


To serve, scatter with mint.



Cucumber ribbon salad with olive and caper dressing



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Maria Elia


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 red peppers from a jar
1 tbsp black olives
2 tbsp capers, drained
3 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp white wine vinegar
½ lemon, juice only
2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
½ cucumber, de-seeded and cut into ribbons with a peeler




Method


Place all of the ingredients except the cucumber in a food processor and pulse until smooth.


Place the cucumber ribbons into a bowl and drizzle over the dressing. Toss well to coat then transfer to a plate to serve.

Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 6


Recommended by
5 people

Â 
Vegetarian





Ingredients

3 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp wine vinegar
1 tsp French mustard
pinch of caster sugar
salt and pepper




Method


Put all the ingredients into a clean jar with a secure lid. Shake well to emulsify the dressing and serve. If the dressing separates, just give it another shake.


Add a crushed clove of garlic or 1 tbsp fresh herbs for variety.

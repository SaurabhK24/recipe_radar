

Frosted fruit cake



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 6-8


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By James Martin


From Housecall






Ingredients

1 plain iced fruit cake
2 egg whites
55g/2oz caster sugar
1 bunch of mint
selection of fruit e.g. cherries, grapes, cape gooseberries, figs




Method


Cut the figs into quarters and brush with egg white.


Brush the remaining fruit and mint with the egg white and dust with caster sugar.


Top the cake with the sugared fruits and serve.



Carrot and herb salad with Parma ham



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1







By Paul Rankin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

150g/5Â½oz carrot, finely sliced
1 tsp chopped fresh parsley
1 tsp chopped fresh chives
1 tbsp olive oil
50g/2oz Parma ham
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Place the carrots, herbs and oil into a bowl. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper and mix well.


To serve, place the Parma ham flat onto a plate and arrange the carrot and herb salad on top.

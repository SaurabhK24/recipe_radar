

Hot cherries in Kirsch



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By James Martin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 miniature Kirsch
1 tbsp caster sugar
1 x 400g/14oz can of cherries




Method


Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.


Heat the Kirsch and sugar in an ovenproof frying pan until the sugar has dissolved.


Add the cherries and then transfer to the oven and bake for 5-6 minutes.


Pour into a bowl and serve.



Chickpea dip



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

200g/7oz tinned chickpeas, rinsed
salt and freshly ground black pepper
glug olive oil
1 garlic clove, peeled and chopped

To serve

crusty bread
crudités




Method


To make the dip, blend the chickpeas, seasoning, olive oil and garlic in a food processor until you have a smooth, creamy texture. Add more olive oil if necessary.


Spoon the dip into a serving dish and serve with bread and crudités.




Recipe Tips
If it's crucial this recipe is dairy-free, check the label on the bread as some contain dairy products.

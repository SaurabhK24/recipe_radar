

Gruyère dauphinoise



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Tony Tobin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

½ large potato, very finely sliced
1 garlic clove, very finely sliced
200ml/7fl oz double cream
50ml/2fl oz milk
55g/2oz Gruyère cheese, grated




Method


Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.


Place the potato, garlic, cream and milk into a saucepan and heat gently for five minutes.


Pour into a gratin dish and sprinkle over the Gruyère. Place into the oven and bake for 12 minutes, remove from the oven, then let it sit for five to ten minutes before serving.

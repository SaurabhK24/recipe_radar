

Mediterranean-style salad with tapenade



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the tapenade

1 garlic clove, chopped
50g/1¾oz black olives, stones removed
2 tbsp olive oil

For the Mediterranean-style salad

2 tomatoes, chopped
1 handful fresh parsley
1 handful fresh basil, torn
1 tsp balsamic vinegar
1 tbsp olive oil




Method


For the tapenade, place the garlic, olives and oil into a mini food processor and blend until smooth.


For the Mediterranean-style salad, place all of the salad ingredients into a bowl and mix well.


To serve, place the salad onto a plate with the tapenade drizzled over.

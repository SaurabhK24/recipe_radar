

Marbled chocolate mousse



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Tony Tobin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

100ml/3½fl oz double cream
55g/2oz dark chocolate, melted




Method


Place the cream into a bowl and whip until thickened.


Add three-quarters of the melted chocolate and fold in to create a marbled mixture.


Scoop out balls of the chocolate cream mixture with an ice cream scoop and place onto a plate. Drizzle with the remaining melted chocolate and serve.

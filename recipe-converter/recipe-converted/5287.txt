

Irish coffee



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian




Don't stir the cream into the coffee, but drink it through the cream and for a change, you can use your favourite liquer instead of whiskey.



Ingredients

a good glug of Irish whiskey
1 tsp brown raw cane sugar
1 heaped tbsp whipped cream
hot strong coffee to fill the glass




Method


Use warmed coffee glasses and add the whiskey.


Add the sugar and dissolve in the whiskey.


Add the coffee and stir well.


Float the whipped cream on top and serve.

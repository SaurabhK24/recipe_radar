

Moroccan rice with grapes



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 tbsp vegetable oil
½ tsp turmeric
½ tsp oregano
½ tsp mixed spice
½ tsp ground ginger
½ tsp chilli powder
5 baby leeks, finely chopped
¼ red pepper, diced
150g/5½oz long grain rice, cooked according to packet instructions
4 tbsp fresh coriander, chopped
50g/1¾oz grapes, sliced




Method


Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan and fry all the spices together for 2-3 minutes.


Add the leeks and red pepper and continue to cook for three minutes until the vegetables are soft.


Add the cooked rice and the fresh coriander and stir together.


Transfer to a serving dish and garnish with the sliced grapes.


Serve.

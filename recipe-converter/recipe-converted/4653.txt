

Grilled chilli mango with vanilla ice cream and chocolate sauce



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the mango

½ mango, scored diagonally with skin turned inside out
1 tsp chilli powder
1 tbsp icing sugar
1 scoop vanilla ice cream

For the sauce

150g/5½oz cocoa powder
75g/2½oz caster sugar
2 tbsp boiling water




Method


For the mango, preheat the grill to its highest setting.


Place the mango into an ovenproof saucepan, sprinkle over the chilli and icing sugar and place under the grill until the surface is golden.


For the sauce, place the sauce ingredients into a heavy- bottomed saucepan over a medium heat, and cook until the sauce thickens.


To serve, put the mango and the ice cream into a serving bowl and drizzle over the chocolate sauce.



Chicken skewers



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person







By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

4 chicken mini-fillets
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 orange, zest only
2 tbsp olive oil
You'll need 4 wooden skewers, soaked in water for ten minutes before using




Method


Place the chicken in a small bowl and add the soy sauce, orange zest and olive oil. Leave aside for five minutes to marinate.


Thread each chicken mini-fillet onto a skewer. Heat a griddle pan and fry the chicken skewers for three minutes on each side, or until cooked through. Serve immediately.

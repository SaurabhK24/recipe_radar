

Ice creams



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 4


Dietary

 
Vegetarian







By Peter Gottgens


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients
For black pepper ice cream

2 tbsp cracked black pepper
1 litre/1¾ pint vanilla ice cream

For the peri-peri (chilli powder)ice cream

2 tbsp peri-peri powder
1 litre/1¾ pint vanilla ice cream

For the passion fruit (granadilla) ice cream

juice of 4 passion fruit (granadilla)
1 tbsp caster sugar
25ml/1fl oz water
1 litre/1¾ pint vanilla ice cream




Method


For the black pepper ice cream, soften the vanilla ice cream and mix in the cracked black pepper until evenly spread.


Taste the ice cream at this stage, if you prefer a stronger taste of black pepper add some more. Mix thoroughly and re-freeze.


For the peri-peri (chilli powder) ice cream, soften the vanilla ice cream and mix in the peri-peri until evenly spread.


Taste the ice cream at this stage, if you prefer a stronger taste of peri-peri add some more. Mix thoroughly and re-freeze.


For the passion fruit (granadilla) ice cream, soften the vanilla ice cream.


Put the remaining ingredients in a pan and reduce until it becomes the consistency of syrup. Let the syrup mixture cool.


When the mixture has cooled add to the vanilla ice cream until evenly spread and then re-freeze.

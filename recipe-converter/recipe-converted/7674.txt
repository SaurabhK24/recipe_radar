

Pink caipirinha




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian




Add a brazen blush to the classic caipirinha cocktail with pomegranate liqueur.






By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

4 segments pink grapefruit
1 tsp caster sugar
1 tbsp pomegranate liqueur
50ml/2 fl oz cachaça

To garnish

slice pink grapefruit
pomegranate seeds




Method


Place the pink grapefruit wedges, sugar and pomegranate liqueur into a tumbler glass and mash (muddle) with the end of a rolling pin. Add some crushed ice and stir well


Pour over the cachaça and stir again. Garnish with a slice of pink grapefruit, some pomegranate seeds and serve.

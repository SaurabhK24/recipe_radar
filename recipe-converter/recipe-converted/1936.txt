

Cheesy dauphinoise potatoes



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian







By Garrey Dawson


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

250ml/9fl oz double cream
2 cloves garlic, sliced
½ baking potato, peeled and thinly sliced on a mandoline
salt and freshly ground black pepper
85g/3oz mature cheddar cheese, grated




Method


Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.


Heat the cream and garlic in a pan until gently simmering. Add the sliced potato, and season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Stir in half the cheese, then transfer the mixture to a gratin dish and sprinkle over the remaining cheese. Bake in the oven for 15 minutes, or until golden-brown and bubbling on top. Serve immediately from the dish.



Avocado with tomato salsa



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Tony Tobin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp white vine vinegar
1 tsp honey
1 tsp finely chopped shallot
100g/3½oz cherry tomatoes, chopped
1 tbsp finely shredded fresh basil
salt and freshly ground black pepper
½ ripe avocado, stone removed




Method


Place the oil, vinegar, honey and shallot in a large bowl and mix together well.


Add the tomato and basil, season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper and toss together.


To serve, place the avocado in the centre of a plate and spoon in some of the salsa mixture.



Chocolate mousse with caramelised oranges



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Makes 1


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Ainsley Harriott


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the chocolate mousse

75g/2¾oz chocolate, melted
200ml/7fl oz double cream, whipped

For the caramelised oranges

2 tbsp butter
3 tbsp caster sugar
2 oranges, peeled and segmented




Method


To make the chocolate mousse, melt the chocolate in a glass bowl over simmering water.


Whip the cream until soft peaks form.


Mix the two together to form a marble effect. Be careful not to overmix them.


Put into a tall glass.


For the oranges, in a medium saucepan heat the butter and the sugar together to form a caramel.


Add the oranges and continue to cook for two minutes.


Place in a small glass dish and serve with the chocolate mousse.



Cheesy refried barbecue beans



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Martin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil
1 red chilli, seeds removed and chopped finely
1 garlic clove, crushed
1 tbsp brown sugar
2 tsp soy sauce
3 tbsp tomato sauce
150g/5½oz cannellini beans from a can, rinsed and drained
1 ready-made flour tortilla
2 tbps grated Emmental cheese




Method


Preheat the grill to its highest setting.


Heat the oil in a frying pan and add the chilli and garlic and fry for one minute.


Add the brown sugar and cook for two minutes, or until the sugar starts to melt.


Add the soy sauce, tomato sauce and cannellini beans. Bring to the boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for four minutes.


Place the tortilla into a clean ovenproof frying pan. Pour the beans mixture into the middle and fold the tortilla to cover.


Top with the grated cheese and place under the grill. Cook for two minutes, or until the cheese is melted and golden.

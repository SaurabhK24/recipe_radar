

Apple Martini




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes 1



This cocktail is like boozy apple juice and wonderfully refreshing on a hot day.






By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

25ml/1fl oz pink lady apple juice
50ml/2fl oz apple vodka
20ml/1fl oz green apple flavoured Sourz, or similar sour apple liqueur (available from specialist drinks suppliers)

To garnish

slice of fresh apple




Method


Mix ingredients in a cocktail shaker.


Strain into a tall coupette glass and garnish with the apple slice.

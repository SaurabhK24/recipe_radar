

Pan-fried sea bass with green bean sauce vierge



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1







By Paul Rankin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the sea bass

2 tbsp olive oil
1 fillet sea bass, skinned

For the green bean sauce vierge

1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp balsamic vinegar
salt and freshly ground black pepper
1 tomato, seeds removed and chopped
100g/3Â½oz green beans, trimmed, blanched and drained




Method


For the sea bass, heat the oil in a frying pan over a medium heat and cook the sea bass for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.


For the green bean sauce vierge, place the oil and vinegar into a bowl, season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and mix well. Add the chopped tomato and stir.


To serve, place the beans onto a serving plate and pour over the dressing. Place the sea bass alongside.

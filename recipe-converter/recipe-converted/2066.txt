

Chicken en croûte



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
30 mins to 1 hour


Serves
Serves 4


Recommended by
13 people



These individual puff pastry parcels make an impressive dinner party dish. Serve with buttery mashed potato and a tomato relish.






By Simon Rimmer


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

225g/8oz buffalo mozzarella, chopped
12 fresh basil leaves, shredded
20 sun-blush tomatoes, chopped
4 x chicken breasts
4 pieces puff pastry, 15cm/6in square, 3mm/⅛in thick
1 egg, beaten




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Mix the mozzarella, basil and sun-blush tomatoes together in a bowl.


Cut a pocket in each of the chicken breasts and stuff with the mozzarella mixture.


Place a stuffed chicken breast into the middle of each square of puff pastry and roll up into parcels.


Brush each parcel with the beaten egg and bake in the oven for 25-30 minutes, or until risen and golden-brown.

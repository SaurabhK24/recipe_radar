

Millefeuille with ice cream



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 sheets filo pastry
5 tbsp melted butter
5 tbsp icing sugar
2 balls vanilla ice cream
25g/1oz pistachios
5 apricots diced
3 tbsp honey




Method


Pre-heat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Brush one sheet pastry with butter and place the other on top. Cut the pastry intp three slices.


Place the three pastry slices onto the baking tray and dust with icing sugar. Put in the oven for four minutes until golden brown, then leave to cool on a plate for a few minutes.


Put a piece of the filo pastry. Layer on some of the ice cream, then sprinkle with nuts and apricots, and continue to do alternative layers until you have used up all three sheets of pastry.


Drizzle with honey and serve.



Blue cheese carbonara



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
2 people







By Paul Rankin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the carbonara

4 slices bacon, cut into thin strips
100ml3½fl oz double cream
75g/2¾oz blue cheese
100g/3½oz pasta, cooked (such as tagliatelle)




Method


For the carbonara, fry the bacon in a saucepan for two minutes until crisp and add the cream.


Cook for a further two minutes.


Add the blue cheese to the sauce to melt, and then add the pasta and serve.

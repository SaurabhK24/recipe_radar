Title: Fresh mango sorbet
Prep time: 2 hours
Cook time: 5 mins
Serves: 4
Author: James Martin

Ingredients:
3 mangos
250 icing sugar
2 lemons

Instructions:
Roughly chop the mango flesh and place into a food processor with the icing sugar and lemon juice. Blend to a pur√©e.
Transfer the mixture into an ice cream machine and churn to freeze, according to manufacturer's instructions.
Once frozen, remove and place into a container. Cover and store in the freezer until ready to serve. It will keep for a couple of months.

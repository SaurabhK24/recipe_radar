

Creamy pea soup



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Ross Burden


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the creamy pea soup

½ red onion, diced
25g/1oz butter
200g/7oz peas (tinned or frozen, thawed)
300ml chicken or vegetable stock
100ml double cream
2 tbsp parsley, chopped




Method


Melt the butter in a saucepan. When foaming add the onions and sweat for three minutes.


Add the peas and cook for a further four minutes.


Add the stock and cream and cook for one minute.


Blend the soup in a food processor and serve in a large bowl garnished with the chopped parsley.



Carrot and apple salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp white wine vinegar
½ apple, core removed, cut into thin strips
½ carrot peeled and sliced into thin strips
25g/1oz leek, white part only, finely sliced
25g/1oz finely chopped mixed herbs (parsley, chives, dill)
salt and freshly ground black pepper
1 tbsp cheddar, finely chopped




Method


Place the oil and vinegar into a large bowl and whisk together.


Add the apple, carrot and leek and toss together to coat in the dressing.


Sprinkle over the mixed herbs and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.


To serve, place onto a plate and sprinkle over the cheddar pieces.



Pineapple porridge



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

600ml/1 pint whole milk
4 tbsp rolled oats
1 tbsp caster sugar
½ lime, juice only
2 rings pineapple from a can, chopped (a little saved for garnish)




Method


Place the milk, oats, sugar and lime juice into a small saucepan over a medium heat. Bring to the boil, stirring, then reduce the heat to simmer gently for five minutes, stirring regularly.


Add the chopped pineapple and heat through for one minute.


To serve, pour into a bowl and top with a little extra chopped pineapple.



Brownie and peach knickerbocker glory



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Tony Tobin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

3 ready-made brownies, broken into pieces
100g/3½oz mascarpone
½ vanilla pod, seeds removed
15g/½oz icing sugar
50ml/2fl oz double cream, whipped
1 peach, skinned and cut into cubes
sprig of fresh mint, to garnish




Method


Place half of the brownies in a tall ice cream glass.


Place the mascarpone, vanilla seeds and sugar in a bowl, mix well and fold through the cream.


Place half of the mixture onto the brownies and top with half of the peaches.


Repeat with a layers of brownie, mascarpone mixture and peaches.


Garnish with mint and serve.

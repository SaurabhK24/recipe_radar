

Pisco amour



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

25ml/1fl oz lemon juice
35ml/1fl oz pisco
15ml/½fl oz Galliano (or similar herbal liqueur)
2 tsp Parfait amour (or similar rose liqueur)

To garnish

lemon slice
mint sprig




Method


Place all the ingredients, apart from the Parfait amour, into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.


Strain into a wine goblet over ice and drizzle the Parfait amour on top.


Garnish with a lemon slice and mint sprig and serve.

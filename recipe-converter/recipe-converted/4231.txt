

Gammon with pineapple salsa



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 4







By James Martin


From Housecall






Ingredients

4 slices gammon
6 rings pineapple, fresh or tinned with natural juice, chopped
2 limes
1 red chilli, de-seeded and chopped
5g/Â¼oz mint, roughly chopped
2 tbsp olive oil
salt and crushed black pepper




Method


Grill or fry the gammon in a little oil and butter.


Prepare the salsa by mixing together the remaining ingredients and stirring well.


Serve the gammon with chips, potato wedges or vegetables and a generous spoonful of salsa on the side. Season with salt and pepper.

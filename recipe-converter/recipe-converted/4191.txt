

Fruit smoothie



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

250g/9oz frozen forest fruits, defrosted
2 tbsp caster sugar
100g/3½oz cream cheese
splash water
3 tbsp crushed digestive biscuits, to serve




Method


Place the fruit, sugar, cream cheese and a splash of cold water into a blender and blend until smooth.


To serve, pour the smoothie into a tall glass and top with the crushed biscuits.

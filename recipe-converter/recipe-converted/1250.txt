Lemon curd gives this whisky cocktail a sherbety kick






By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

2 tsp lemon curd
2 dashes angostura bitters
1 tbsp fresh lime
1 tsp vanilla syrup
1 tbsp white cacao
35ml/1fl oz whisky

To serve

Freshly ground nutmeg




Method


Place all the ingredients into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.


Strain the cocktail into a martini glass and garnish with nutmeg.

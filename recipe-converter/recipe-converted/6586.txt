

Mushroom cappuccino



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

100g/3½oz mixed mushrooms
1 tbsp olive oil
1 garlic clove chopped
25g/1oz butter
75ml double cream
100ml/2½fl oz vegetable stock
pinch of nutmeg




Method


In a saucepan heat the butter and oil together. Cook the mushrooms and garlic for three minutes.


Add the stock and cook for five minutes.


Finish by adding the cream and nutmeg and season.


Using the hand blender blend until frothy.


Serve in a cappucino cup.



Plum cheesecake



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Martin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

200g/7oz mascarpone cheese
142ml/5fl oz double cream, whipped
½ vanilla pod, split, seeds scraped out
2 tbsp icing sugar
2 plums, halved, stones removed

To serve

sprig fresh mint
drizzle honey




Method


In a bowl, mix together the mascarpone, cream, vanilla seeds and icing sugar.


Place two chefs' rings onto two serving plates. Add the plums to the base of the rings and press down. Top up the rings with the mascarpone mixture and smooth down the top with a pallet knife.


To serve, carefully remove the chefs' rings, garnish with mint and drizzle with honey.

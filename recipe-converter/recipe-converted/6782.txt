

Olly’s sham-pagne




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes 2


Recommended by
13 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian




Cutting back on calories? You don't have to give up the bubbly at a social occasion with this elderflower mocktail.





By Olly Smith


From Good Food Friday with Michel Roux Jr and Olly Smith






Ingredients

60ml lime cordial
60ml elderflower cordial
3 drops orange blossom water
250ml ice cold soda water




Method


Pour all the ingredients into a jug and give it a stir. Serve in champagne flutes to blend in at a party.

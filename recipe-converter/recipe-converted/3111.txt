

Crayfish lettuce baskets



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1







By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

200g/7oz crayfish, cooked
Â¼ onion, finely chopped
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp honey
1 tsp sesame oil
1 spring onion, chopped
6 Little Gem lettuce leaves
3 lime wedges, to garnish




Method


Place the crayfish, onion, soy sauce, honey, sesame oil and spring onion into a pan and heat gently for 4-5 minutes.


To serve, place the lettuce leaves onto a plate, pour over the crayfish mixture and garnish with lime wedges.

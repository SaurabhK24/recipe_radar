

Omelette



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Gino D'Acampo


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

3 free-range eggs
1 tbsp whole milk
1 tbsp double cream
salt and freshly ground black pepper
1 tbsp olive oil




Method


In a bowl mix together the eggs, milk and cream, then season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Heat the oil in a frying pan, and pour in the egg mixture. After one minute, fold the omelette over from one side into the middle. Then fold the top edge over to the bottom.


To serve, immediately place the omelette onto a plate.

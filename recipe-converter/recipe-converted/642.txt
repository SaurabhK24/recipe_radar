

Tuna and tomato pasta



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person







By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil
Â½ red onion, finely chopped
1 clove garlic, chopped
85g/3oz fresh tuna, chopped
1 tomato, chopped
1 tbsp soy sauce
small handful chopped fresh parsley
salt and freshly ground black pepper
125g/4oz penne pasta, cooked according to packet instructions




Method


Heat the olive oil in a saucepan, add the onion and garlic and fry for 2-3 minutes, or until softened but not coloured. Add the tuna and fry for 1-2 minutes. Stir in the chopped tomato, reduce the heat and simmer until the tomato has softened.


Add the soy sauce and a splash of water, stir in the parsley and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and stir well to coat.


Spoon the pasta into a bowl and serve.



Onion rings



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
3 people







By Nick Nairn


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

oil for deep frying
½ onion, sliced into rings
50ml/1¾fl oz milk
100g/3½oz plain flour




Method


Heat the oil in a deep pan. (CAUTION: Hot oil can be very dangerous. Do not leave unattended.)


Soak the onion rings in milk.


Place the flour on the plate.


Remove the onion rings from the milk and coat them in the flour.


Deep fry in the oil for 2-3 minutes or until crisp and golden.



Mixed salad of apricots and strawberries



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Ed Baines


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

100g/3½oz apricots, chopped
100g/3½oz strawberries, chopped
1 lime, juice only
handful fresh mint, chopped
handful fresh basil, chopped
1 tsp caster sugar
dollop yoghurt, to serve




Method


Mix all the ingredients apart from the yoghurt together.


Serve in a bowl and place a dollop of yoghurt on top.



Plum compôte




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Recommended by
4 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian




Serve this plum compôte with Greek yoghurt for a healthy dessert or spoon it onto your morning porridge.






By Sophie Dahl


From A Taste of My Life






Ingredients

300ml/½ pint boiling water
150g/5½oz caster sugar
500g/1lb 2oz plums, stones removed
2 cinnamon sticks
3-4 whole star anise




Method


Heat a large saucepan and pour in the boiling water. Add the sugar and let the mixture bubble for 30 seconds.


Add the plums, cinnamon sticks and star anise and simmer for 8-10 minutes.


Remove the plums, and simmer the sauce, stirring gently until it thickens, then pour over the plums and leave to cool slightly before serving.

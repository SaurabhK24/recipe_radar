

Creamed spinach with nutmeg and garlic



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil
1 garlic clove, peeled
125g spinach, washed
200ml/7¼fl oz double cream
ground nutmeg, to taste
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Heat the olive oil in a frying pan over a medium heat and fry the garlic for 1-2 minutes. Add the spinach and continue to fry until the spinach has wilted. Pour in the cream and season, to taste, with nutmeg, salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Continue to heat for 2-3 minutes, or until the volume of the liquid has reduced slightly.


Blend the mixture together with a stick blender. Spoon into a bowl and garnish with extra grated nutmeg, to taste.

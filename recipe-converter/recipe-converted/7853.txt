

Porcini bread and butter pudding



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Makes 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

5 slices ciabatta, crusts removed
butter, for greasing
½ shallot, finely sliced
3 free-range eggs
55ml/2fl oz double cream
25g/1oz dried porcini, rehydrated in warm water and finely chopped
50g/2oz Pecorino, sliced
2 tsp chopped fresh dill




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Layer the bread in a small oven dish, greased with butter.


Place the shallot, eggs, double cream and porcini into a bowl and mix together. Pour the cream mixture over the bread.


Place the Pecorino on top of the bread and place into the oven to bake for 12 minutes, or until golden and cooked through.


Remove from the oven and serve garnished with the chopped dill.



Virgin Mary



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1







By Tony Tobin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

200ml/7oz passata
1 lime, juice only
½ tsp Tabasco sauce
½ tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp Dijon mustard

To serve

handful ice cubes
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Place all the ingredients into a food processor and blend.


To serve, pour into a glass filled with ice cubes and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.



Pea 'guacamole' bruschetta



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1-2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By James Martin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

50g/2oz frozen peas, defrosted
50g/2oz soya yoghurt
pinch chilli flakes
salt and freshly ground black pepper
2 slices crusty white bread, drizzled with olive oil and griddled both sides, to serve




Method


Place the peas, soya yoghurt, chilli flakes and seasoning into a mini blender and blitz until almost smooth.


To serve, spoon into a bowl with the griddled bread on the side.

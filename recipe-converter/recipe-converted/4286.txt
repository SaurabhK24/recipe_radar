Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required

Serves 1

Â

A very popular drink served during the Victorian gin palace era, particularly in London!






By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

35ml/1fl oz gin
35ml/1fl oz Lillet blanc




Method


Place the gin and Lillet in a mixing glass with a handful of ice and stir.


Strain into a coupette glass (margarita glass) and garnish with a lemon twist.

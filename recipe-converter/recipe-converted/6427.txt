Ingredients

25g/1oz fresh mint, chopped
1 tsp caster sugar
1 tbsp hot water
2 tbsp white wine vinegar




Method


Mix all the ingredients together and stand for 20 minutes to allow the mint flavour to develop. The classic mint sauce to serve with roast lamb.

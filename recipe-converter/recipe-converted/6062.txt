

Mango and chilli pudding



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

½ mango, chopped into large cubes
2 pinches of chilli powder
1 tbsp icing sugar
50ml/2fl oz double cream




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Place the mango in a bowl and toss with the chilli and sugar. Transfer to a small baking tray and roast for 10 minutes.


To serve, place in a warm bowl and drizzle with the cream.



Egyptian dukkah



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 4


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Jill Dupleix


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

100g/4oz sesame seeds
100g/4oz blanched almonds
50g/2oz coriander seeds
10g/½oz cumin seeds
1 tsp sea salt
½ tsp pepper
Turkish bread
olive oil




Method


Toast all the seeds and almonds together in a hot dry pan. Keep stirring until fragrant.


Cool, then coarsely grind with the salt and pepper.


To serve, dip the Turkish bread into the olive oil and then into the dukkah.

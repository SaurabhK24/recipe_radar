

Party eggs



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes 4



This is a fun recipe to make with children and they can be served as part of a kid's party spread.





By Neil Strawson and Richard Hunt


From I Can Cook on the Go






Ingredients

2 free-range eggs, hard-boiled, peeled, halved lengthways
2 tsp salad cream

For the toppings

cherry tomatoes, chopped
cooked bacon bits
cress
spring onion, sliced




Method


Carefully remove the yolk from the eggs and put in a bowl. Mash in the salad cream and a little of the toppings.


Spoon the yolk mixture back into the halved eggs and place on a serving plate.



Marinated red cabbage and walnut salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 2


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

½ red cabbage, thinly sliced
3 tbsp balsamic vinegar
5 tbsp olive oil
50g/1¾oz walnuts, chopped
1 handful fresh coriander, chopped
2 oranges, peeled, segmented




Method


Place the red cabbage, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, walnuts, and coriander into a bowl. Mix well and leave to marinate for five minutes.


To serve, place the salad onto a plate and top with the orange slices.

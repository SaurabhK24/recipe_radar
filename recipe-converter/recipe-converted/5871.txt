

Lime and vodka cocktail



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 lime, peeled and sliced
140g/5oz ice
½ miniature vodka
55g/2oz icing sugar
Lime slice, to garnish




Method


Blend together the cocktail ingredients in a food processor.


Pour into a Martini glass with a lime slice to garnish.

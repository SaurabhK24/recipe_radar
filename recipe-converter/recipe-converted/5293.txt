

Irish stout punch



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 2


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Ainsley Harriott


From Take on the Takeaway






Ingredients

500ml/17fl oz Irish stout
200ml/7fl oz condensed milk
10 ice cubes
ground cinnamon or freshly grated nutmeg, for sprinkling




Method


Pour the Irish stout and the condensed milk into a blender and add the ice. Pulse for one minute until light and frothy.


To serve, pour into glasses and sprinkle with a little ground cinnamon or freshly grated nutmeg.

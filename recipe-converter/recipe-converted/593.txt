

Ginger trifle



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Alex Mackay


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

3 slices ready-made ginger cake
150ml/¼ pint ready-made custard
150ml/¼ pint double cream, whipped until soft peaks form when the whisk is removed
3 tbsp raisins
3 tbsp pecans, roughly chopped, plus extra, to serve




Method


Cut the cake into pieces and place in the base of a small glass bowl. Pour over the custard.


Place the cream into a separate large bowl and fold in the raisins and pecans.


Spread the cream mixture on top of the custard.


To serve, sprinkle over the remaining pecans and place servings of the trifle into bowls.

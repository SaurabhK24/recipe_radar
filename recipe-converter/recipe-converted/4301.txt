

Ginger alert



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian







By Salvatore Calabrese


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

40ml/1½fl oz whisky
2 tsp clear apple juice
1 tsp clear pear juice
20ml/½fl oz fresh lemon juice
2.5cm/1in piece fresh ginger, peeled
ginger ale, to top up
apple slice, to garnish




Method


Pour the whisky, apple juice, pear juice, and lemon juice into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice.


Grate the ginger into the shaker, then shake well.


Strain into a highball glass filled with ice cubes.


Top up with ginger ale and garnish with an apple wedge.

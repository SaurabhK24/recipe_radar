

Cheese baked with thyme



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Makes 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Martin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 cheese in a box (such as Pont l'Évêque)
1 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
½ tbsp olive oil
salt and freshly ground black pepper
½ red romero pepper, seeds removed and chopped
rustic white bread, broken up, to serve




Method


Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.


Place the cheese in a box on a baking sheet and sprinkle with the thyme leaves.


Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Place in the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until just beginning to melt.


Remove from the oven and serve on a plate with the chopped pepper pieces and rustic bread pieces beside.



Orange crumble with cranberries



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 orange, peeled and chopped
100g/3½oz dried cranberries
3 tbsp honey
25g/1oz caster sugar

For the topping

100g/3½oz butter
50g/1¾oz brown sugar
100g/3½oz plain flour
50g/1¾oz oats




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


In a blini pan or a small frying pan place the orange, cranberries, honey and sugar. Cook on a high heat for two minutes until golden.


To make the topping, using a food processor blend together the butter, brown sugar, flour and oats until lumpy.


Scatter this mixture over the crumble base.


Place in the preheated oven for eight minutes.


Serve hot from the oven.

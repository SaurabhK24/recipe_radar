

Breakfast Martini



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

 
Vegetarian







By Salvatore Calabrese


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

50ml/2fl oz gin
1 tbsp Cointreau, or similar orange liqueur
1 tbsp lemon juice
1½ tsp orange marmalade
orange peel spiral, to garnish




Method


Place the gin, Cointreau, lemon juice and marmalade into a cocktail shaker with some ice and shake well to combine.


To serve, strain into a Martini glass and garnish with a spiral of orange peel.

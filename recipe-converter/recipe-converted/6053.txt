

Mangetout salad with mustard dressing



Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 red onion, peeled and cut into rings
piece red chilli, chopped
drizzle olive oil
1 lime, juice only
110g/4oz mangetout, very finely shredded

For the dressing

1 tsp wholegrain mustard
splash red wine
4-6 tbsp olive oil
pinch chilli flakes
2-3 tbsp balsamic vinegar




Method


To make the salad, place the onion rings and chilli into a large bowl.


Drizzle the olive oil over and add the lime juice and mangetout. Toss together.


To make the dressing, whisk the mustard, wine, olive oil, chilli flakes and balsamic vinegar in a jug or bowl until combined.


Transfer the salad to a serving plate or bowl. Drizzle the dressing over to serve.

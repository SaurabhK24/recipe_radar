

Pasta with tomato and chilli sauce



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Gino D'Acampo


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 tbsp olive oil
½ red onion, sliced
1 garlic clove, chopped
150g/5½oz tinned tomatoes, chopped
½ tsp dried chilli flakes
150g/5½oz penne pasta, cooked according to packet instructions




Method


Place a frying pan over a medium heat. Add the olive oil, red onion and garlic and cook for five minutes, until the onion has softened.


Add the tomatoes and chilli flakes and continue to cook for five more minutes.


To serve, place the pasta in a bowl and pour over the tomato sauce.

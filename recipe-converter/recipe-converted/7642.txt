

Pina colada



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Andy Pearson


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

35ml/1¼fl oz gold rum
1 tbsp coconut-flavoured rum
50ml/2fl oz coconut cream
50ml/2fl oz pineapple juice
3-4 pineapple chunks

To serve

2 pineapple leaves
1 maraschino cherry




Method


Pour the two rums, the coconut cream, pineapple juice and chunks of pineapple into a blender and blend until smooth.


To serve, pour into a cocktail glass and garnish with pineapple leaves and a maraschino cherry.

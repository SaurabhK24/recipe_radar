

Chimayo



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

1 scoop ice
¼ lime, juice only
35ml/1¼floz tequila reposado
15ml/½fl oz crème de cassis
100ml/3½fl oz cider

To serve

1 slice apple
1 sprig fresh mint




Method


Place the ice into a sling glass and squeeze over the lime juice.


Pour in the tequila, crème de cassis and cider. Stir once.


To serve, hook the slice of apple over the side of the glass. Garnish with the mint sprig.

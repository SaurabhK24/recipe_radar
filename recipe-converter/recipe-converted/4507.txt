

Greek salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Ross Burden


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 handfuls fresh flatleaf parsley leaves, roughly chopped
2 celery stalks, finely chopped
3 tomatoes, deseeded and chopped
100g/3½oz feta, cut into cubes
2 tbsp tinned cannellini beans, rinsed and drained
1 lemon, juice only
1 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp black olives, pitted




Method


Place all of the ingredients into a large bowl. Toss together well and serve immediately.



Mojito mint tea



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Andy Pearson


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

20 fresh mint leaves
2 tsp brown sugar
20ml/¾fl oz lime juice
50ml/1¾fl oz white rum
200ml/7fl oz hot water




Method


Place the mint and brown sugar into the bottom of a tall heatproof glass.


Add the lime juice and mix (muddle) together with the end of a clean rolling pin or wooden spoon.


Add the rum, then the hot water and serve.

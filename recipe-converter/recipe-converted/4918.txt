

Baked chocolate-stuffed banana with vanilla cream



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Makes 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the banana

1 flaky chocolate bar, crumbled
1 banana, skin-on, split lengthways

For the vanilla cream

½ tsp vanilla extract
100ml/3½fl oz double cream, whipped until soft peaks form when the whisk is removed




Method


Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.


For the banana, push the crumbled chocolate into the slit in the banana.


Place on a baking tray, transfer to the oven and bake for ten minutes, or until the banana is soft and the chocolate has melted.


For the vanilla cream, gently fold the vanilla extract into the whipped cream.


To serve, place the baked banana onto a serving plate with a dollop of vanilla cream.

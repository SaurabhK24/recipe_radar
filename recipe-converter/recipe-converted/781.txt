

Vanilla porridge



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

100g/3½oz oats
1 vanilla pod, seeds removed
200ml/7fl oz milk
50ml double cream

For the caramel sauce

50g/1¾oz butter
50g/1¾oz caster sugar
50ml/1¾fl oz double cream
1 orange, peeled and sliced




Method


Place the oats, vanilla seeds, milk and cream in a heavy-bottomed saucepan. Simmer for four minutes until thick.


To make the caramel sauce, in a hot pan add the butter and sugar and cook for 3-4 minutes. Next add the cream and orange segments.


Serve the vanilla oats with the caramel and oranges.

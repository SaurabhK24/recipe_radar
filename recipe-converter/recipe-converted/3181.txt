

Creamy pasta with mussels



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person







By Tony Tobin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

50ml/2fl oz white wine
100ml/3½fl oz double cream
pinch turmeric powder
150g/5½oz fresh pasta, cooked according to packet instructions
75g/2¾oz cooked mussels from a jar, rinsed and drained
2 tbsp chopped fresh chives




Method


Place the white wine, double cream and turmeric into a pan over a medium heat. Bring up to the boil then reduce the heat to simmer for three minutes.


Add the cooked pasta and mussels and cook for a further three minutes, until the mussels are warmed through.


To serve, pour into a bowl and top with the chopped chives.

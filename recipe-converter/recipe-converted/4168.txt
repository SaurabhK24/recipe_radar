

Frozen mango and lime shots



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 4


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Nick Nairn


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 mango, peeled, stone removed and chopped
1 lime, zest and juice
2 oranges, juice only
3-4 ice cubes
fresh mint leaves and lime wedges, to garnish




Method


Place the mango, lime zest and juice, orange juice and ice into a food processor and blend until smooth.


To serve, pour into four shot glasses and garnish each with mint leaves and lime wedges.




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 4


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian





Ingredients

225g/8oz dark chocolate, broken into pieces
80g/3oz caster sugar
120ml/4fl oz double cream
60ml/2fl oz hot water




Method


Put all the ingredients into a thick-bottomed saucepan.


Put on a gentle heat and stir until the chocolate has melted and the sauce is hot.

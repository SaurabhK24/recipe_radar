

Warm potato, mustard and dill salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By James Martin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

250g/9oz new potatoes
2 tbsp Dijon mustard
drizzle of olive oil
2 tbsp dill, chopped
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Place the new potatoes into a large saucepan of boiling salted water and boil for 15 minutes, or until just cooked through.


Drain the potatoes and place into a large bowl with all of the other ingredients.


Mix well and serve warm.

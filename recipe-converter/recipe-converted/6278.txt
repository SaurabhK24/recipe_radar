

Melon with parma ham



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 2







By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

Â½ cantaloupe melon, seeds and skin removed, quartered
8 fresh basil leaves, plus an extra sprig, to garnish
4 slices parma ham




Method


Place two basil leaves on top of each piece of melon.


Wrap a slice of parma ham around each piece of melon.


To serve, place the parma ham-wrapped melon onto a serving plate and garnish with a sprig of basil.

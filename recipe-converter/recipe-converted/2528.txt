

Chocolate crispy cakes



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Gino D'Acampo


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

30g/1oz shredded wheat cereal, lightly crushed
85g/3oz chocolate, melted
2 tbsp clear honey
1 orange, peeled, pith removed and flesh sliced into rounds




Method


Stir the crushed shredded wheat, melted chocolate and clear honey together in a mixing bowl. Chill for 5-10 minutes until it's set.


Arrange the orange slices on a serving plate and top with the crispy chocolate to serve.

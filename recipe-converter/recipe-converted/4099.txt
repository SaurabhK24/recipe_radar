

Fresh pea and mint soup



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil, plus extra to serve
25g/1oz butter
½ onion, finely chopped
1 garlic clove, finely chopped
100g/3½oz fresh peas
200ml/7fl oz vegetable stock
2 tbsp chopped fresh mint




Method


Heat the oil and butter in a frying pan and fry the onion and garlic until softened but not coloured.


Add the peas and stock and bring to a gentle simmer. Cook until the peas are tender, about 5-8 minutes.


Remove from the heat. Stir in the fresh mint and blend until smooth with a hand blender. Serve in a bowl garnished with a drizzle of olive oil.

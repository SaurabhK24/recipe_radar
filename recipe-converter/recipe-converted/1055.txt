

Warm sun-blushed tomato and broccoli salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil
½ head broccoli, cut into florets
100g/3½oz sun-blushed tomatoes
75ml/2½fl oz white wine
25g/1oz butter
1 tbsp chopped fresh tarragon, to serve




Method


Heat the oil in a wok and gently fry the broccoli and sun-blushed tomatoes for 3-4 minutes.


Add the wine and simmer until the wine is reduced and the broccoli is tender, about five minutes. Stir in the butter.


Sprinkle with chopped fresh tarragon and serve immediately.

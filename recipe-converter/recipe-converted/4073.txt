

Fresh mint sauce



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian




Use runny honey or golden syrup in this fresh mint sauce, perfect with roast lamb.






By Mary Berry


From Mary Berry Cooks






Ingredients

1 small bunch fresh mint
2 tbsp clear honey or golden syrup
2 tbsp white wine vinegar or cider vinegar




Method


Pick the leaves off the mint stalks and chop them as finely as possible, then mix with the honey or syrup and the vinegar.



Chocolate-dipped strawberries



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

55g/2oz dark chocolate, chopped
5 whole strawberries




Method


Place the chocolate into a bowl set over a pan of simmering water (make sure the bottom of the bowl doesn't touch the water surface). Leave to melt for 5-6 minutes.


Dip the strawberries into the chocolate and place on a silicone mat set on a baking sheet. Place into the fridge for 15-20 minutes to set. Serve.

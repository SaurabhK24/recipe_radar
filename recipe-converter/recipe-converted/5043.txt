

Honey, garlic and red pepper dressing



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

1 red pepper, chopped
2 garlic cloves, chopped
4 tsp clear honey
salt and pepper
1 cup hot water
olive oil, to blend




Method


Sweat down the red pepper and garlic in a hot pan for 2-3 minutes.


Add the honey and then the hot water, simmer for about 1 minute.


Blend the mixture and slowly add the olive oil to a smooth sauce consistency. Pass the dressing through a sieve, season and use.

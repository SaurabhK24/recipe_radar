

Tomato, apple and soft duck egg salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tomato, sliced
1 spring onion, sliced
1 apple, sliced
1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
3 tbsp olive oil
salt and freshly ground black pepper
large handful fresh coriander
1 duck egg, poached, to serve




Method


Mix the tomato, spring onion, apple, balsamic vinegar, and olive oil together in a large bowl and season to taste with salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Spoon into a serving bowl and top with the poached duck egg.

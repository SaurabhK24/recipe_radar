Title: Vegan chocolate fudge icing
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 5 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Charlotte O'Leary

Ingredients:
55g soya margarine
3 tbsp soya milk
200g icing sugar
30g cocoa powder

Instructions:
Place the soya margarine, soya milk, icing sugar and cocoa in a bowl over a saucepan of simmering water.
Stir well until the icing is smooth and glossy.
Remove it from the heat and let it cool completely, then use to ice your cake.

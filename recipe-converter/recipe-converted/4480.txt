

Grape spritzer



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Allegra McEvedy


From Economy Gastronomy






Ingredients

180-200g/6½-7oz ripe grapes
2 tbsp elderflower cordial
8 ice cubes, plus extra to serve
150ml/5fl oz white wine
4 mint leaves
splash sparkling water, to serve




Method


Blend all of the ingredients except for the sparkling water in a food processor until smooth and well combined.


To serve, half-fill two tall glasses with ice cubes. Pour over the grape mixture. Top up each glass with the sparkling water.

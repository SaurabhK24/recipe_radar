

Vanilla and orange rice pudding



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

200g/7oz long grain rice
400ml/14fl oz milk
1 tbsp caster sugar
1 vanilla pod, split, seeds scraped out
1 orange, zest and juice only
2 tbsp golden syrup, to serve




Method


Place the rice into a pan with the milk, sugar and vanilla pod and seeds and simmer for 10-12 minutes, or until the rice is tender.


Remove the vanilla pod, then stir in the orange zest and juice and cook for a further 1-2 minutes.


Spoon the rice pudding into a serving bowl and drizzle over the golden syrup to serve.

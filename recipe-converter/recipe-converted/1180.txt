

Breaded cheese



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Tony Tobin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

oil for deep-frying
150g/5¼oz Wensleydale cheese, cut into triangles
100g/3½oz breadcrumbs
1 free-range egg, whisked




Method


Heat the oil ready for deep frying. (CAUTION: Hot oil can be very dangerous. Do not leave unattended.)


Dip the cheese in the egg and then coat in the breadcrumbs.


Carefully drop the cheese into the hot oil and deep-fry for 2-3 minutes or until crisp and golden.


Drain on kitchen paper and serve hot.

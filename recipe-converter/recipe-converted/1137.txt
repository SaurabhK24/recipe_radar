

White chocolate mousse with cherry trifle



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Paul Rankin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

100g/3½oz white chocolate, melted
100ml/3½fl oz double cream, whipped
100g/3½oz tinned cherries
30ml/1¼fl oz red wine
28g/1oz sugar
2 chocolate muffins, chopped
100ml/3½fl oz double cream, whipped




Method


To make the chocolate mousse, mix the melted white chocolate with the double cream and set aside.


Heat together the cherries, red wine and sugar in a small pan. Cook until the sugar has melted.


Lay the chocolate muffin pieces in a tall glass and pour over some of the cherry and wine juice.


Top with some mousse and repeat this process three times.


Top with the cherries and serve.

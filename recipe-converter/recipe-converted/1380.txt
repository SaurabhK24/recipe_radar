

Bruschetta of roasted pepper and Dolcelatte



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Nick Nairn


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 slice ciabatta
dash olive oil
1 roasted red pepper from a jar, drained, sliced
55g/2oz Dolcelatte cheese, crumbled




Method


Preheat the grill to medium.


Drizzle a slice of ciabatta with olive oil and then griddle for 1-2 minutes on each side.


Warm the sliced roasted red peppers in a small pan and then spoon onto the griddled bread. Place the crumbled Dolcelatte cheese on top of the peppers and place under the grill for 1-2 minutes or until bubbling.


Slide onto a plate and serve.



Mango with chilli



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

½ mango
½ chilli, finely chopped
½ tsp mixed spice
1 tbsp runny honey




Method


Preheat the grill to high.


Criss-cross the mango flesh, then turn the mango half inside out to make a 'hedgehog' shape.


Sprinkle over the chopped chilli, mixed spice and honey.


Place the mango onto a baking sheet then place under the grill for 2-3 minutes.


To serve, place the mango onto a plate.

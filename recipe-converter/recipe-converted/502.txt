

Braised celery with oregano and garlic



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

200g/7oz celery, roughly chopped
100ml/3½fl oz hot vegetable stock
½ onion, finely chopped
1 garlic clove, chopped
1 tsp dried oregano
30g/1oz butter
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Place the celery, stock, onion, garlic and oregano into an ovenproof pan. Bring to the boil, then reduce to a simmer and cover with foil.


Transfer to the oven and cook for 6-8 minutes, or until the celery is tender. Remove from the oven, whisk in the butter and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.


To serve, place the braised celery into a serving dish.



Wrapped mozzarella



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1







By Nick Nairn


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

freshly ground black pepper
shredded basil leaves
1 ball of fresh mozzarella
2 slices of prosciutto
olive oil, for drizzling




Method


Scatter the freshly ground black pepper and shredded basil leaves on the mozzarella ball.


Wrap the seasoned ball in the prosciutto slices.


Heat a little olive oil in a non-stick frying pan and fry the wrapped mozzarella for a few minutes, until the prosciutto is crisp and golden.


Serve.



Poached pears with chocolate sauce



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

255ml/9fl oz white wine
1 vanilla pod, seeds scraped out
50ml/2fl oz cherry juice from a tin of cherries
1 pear, peeled

For the chocolate sauce

100g/3½oz dark chocolate
150ml/5½fl oz double cream

To garnish

1 tbsp sesame seeds




Method


For the poached pears, place the wine, vanilla seeds and cherry juice into a pan over a medium heat and bring to a simmer.


Add the pear and poach for ten minutes, until softened.


For the chocolate sauce, place the chocolate and double cream into a heat-safe bowl, placed over a saucepan of just simmering water. Heat, stirring occasionally until the chocolate has completely melted into the cream.


To serve, place the poached pear onto a plate and spoon over the chocolate sauce. Sprinkle with the sesame seeds to finish.

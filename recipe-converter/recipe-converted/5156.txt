

Hot sweet and sour strawberries with vanilla ice cream



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1-2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Tony Tobin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp red wine vinegar
1 tbsp caster sugar
2 tbsp red wine
55g/2oz strawberries, tips left on
vanilla ice cream, to serve




Method


Heat the oil, vinegar, sugar and red wine in a small frying pan for 2-3 minutes, until thickened, then add the strawberries. Stir well to coat the strawberries and cook for a further minute.


To serve, transfer to a serving bowl, pour over some of the pan syrup and serve with a large scoop of ice cream.

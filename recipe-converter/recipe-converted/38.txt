Title: Cherry negroni
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 5 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Wayne Collins

Ingredients:
25 ml cherry brandy
25 ml gin
25 ml sweet vermouth
25 ml campari

Instructions:
Place all the ingredients into a tumbler glass with a handful of ice and stir until well combined.
Garnish with a slice of orange and serve.

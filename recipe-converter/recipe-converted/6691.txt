

Nectarines poached in honey and wine



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 tbsp brown sugar
1 nectarine cut in half, stone removed
50ml/2fl oz white wine
1 tbsp clear honey
1 tbsp double cream




Method


Heat the brown sugar in a frying pan over a high heat, until melted.


Place the nectarines cut-side down into the sugar and cook for one minute.


Add the wine and honey and boil, unitl the liquid has reduced in volume by half.


Remove the nectarines and keep warm, then add the cream to the sauce, bring to the boil and stir until thickened.


To serve, place the nectarines cut side down onto a plate and drizzle over the sauce.

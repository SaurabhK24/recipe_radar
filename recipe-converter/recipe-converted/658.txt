

Tuna, tomato and olive stew



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1







By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil
¼ onion, chopped
100g/3½oz tuna steak, chopped
2 tsp honey
2 tbsp white wine
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 plum tomato, chopped
few kalamata olives, stones removed




Method


Heat the olive oil in a small saucepan, add the onion and fry until softened but not coloured.


Add the tuna and fry for two minutes.


Add the honey, white wine, soy sauce and chopped tomato and simmer for five minutes. Stir in the olives.


Pour into a bowl and serve.

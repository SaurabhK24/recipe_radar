

Grilled broccoli with chilli soy dressing



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

85g/3oz Tenderstem broccoli, blanched and drained
1 tbsp vegetable oil
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 red chilli, chopped
3 tbsp chopped fresh chervil
1 tbsp sesame oil
1 tbsp balsamic vinegar




Method


Heat a griddle pan until smoking, then drizzle the broccoli with the vegetable oil and add to the pan. Chargrill for 2-3 minutes, turning occasionally, or until the broccoli is golden-brown all over.


Combine the remaining ingredients in a small bowl for the dressing.


Place the chargrilled broccoli on a serving plate and drizzle over the dressing. Serve immediately.

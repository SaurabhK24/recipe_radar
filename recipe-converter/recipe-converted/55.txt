

Sweet eggy bread



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Ross Burden


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

4 free-range eggs
2 free-range egg yolks
25g/1oz caster sugar
50ml/2fl oz double cream
25g/1oz unsalted butter
3 thick slices brioche




Method


Place the eggs, egg yolks, caster sugar and cream into a large bowl and whisk together.


Melt the butter in a large frying pan over a medium heat.


Dip the brioche in the egg mixture then place into the frying pan and fry for two minutes on each side, until golden.


To serve, place onto a warm plate.

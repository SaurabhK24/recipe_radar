

Carpaccio of salmon with a soy dressing and sesame seeds



Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1







By Maria Elia


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp sesame oil
1 tbsp soy sauce
Â½ lime, juice only
85g/3oz salmon fillet, skin removed, thinly sliced

To serve

1 tbsp chopped fresh coriander leaves
1 tsp sesame seeds




Method


Combine the sesame oil, soy sauce and lime juice in a bowl to make a soy dressing.


Arrange the thinly sliced salmon onto a plate and pour over the soy dressing.


Set the salmon aside to marinate in the dressing for 5-10 minutes.


To serve, garnish the salmon with the coriander leaves and sesame seeds.

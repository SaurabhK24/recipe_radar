

Carrot and watercress salad with tomatoes



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 carrots, peeled and grated
1 handful watercress, roughly chopped
3 mi-cuit (semi-dried) tomatoes, in oil, cut in half
1 tbsp red wine vinegar
3 tbsp olive oil
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Mix the carrots, watercress and tomatoes together in a bowl.


In a small bowl, whisk together the red wine vinegar and olive oil and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Drizzle over the salad and toss well to coat.


Serve immediately.

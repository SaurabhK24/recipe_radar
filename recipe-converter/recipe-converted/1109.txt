

Whisky flapjacks



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Makes 3


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

55g/2oz oats
2 eggs, beaten
55g/2oz self-raising flour
55g/2oz caster sugar
vegetable oil
1 miniature whisky

To serve

2 segments orange
fresh mint sprig




Method


Mix together the main ingredients, except the oil and whisky, and shape into three patties.


Heat a pan with a drop of oil and fry for two minutes on each side.


Add the whisky to the pan, warm and carefully set alight to flambé for a few seconds.


Serve on a warm plate with the orange segments and mint sprig.

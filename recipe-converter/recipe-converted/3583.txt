

Drop scones with butterscotch sauce



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Makes 6


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner






Ingredients
For the drop scones

150g/5½oz self-raising flour
55ml/2fl oz milk
2 free-range eggs
55g/2oz caster sugar
1 tbsp mixed spice
knob of butter

For the butterscotch sauce

55ml/2fl oz double cream
55g/2oz butter
55g/2oz caster sugar
1 tbsp honey




Method


For the scones, mix together all of the scone ingredients except for the butter. Bring together to make a dough. Divide into six pieces and shape into rounds.


Melt the butter in a frying pan over a high heat. Add the scone rounds to the pan and fry for one minute on each side or until risen and golden. Transfer to a plate.


Meanwhile, to make the sauce, heat the double cream in small pan. Add the butter, caster sugar and honey and cook for five minutes over a high heat.


Drizzle the hot cream mixture over the scones and serve.



Fruit and yoghurt with homemade granola



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
30 mins to 1 hour


Serves
Serves 40


Dietary

 
Vegetarian




You could substitute the apples for other stewed fruit. The granola will keep for up to a week in an airtight container.






By The Hairy Bikers


From Hairy Bikers' Meals on Wheels






Ingredients

4kg/9lbs eating apples, peeled, cored and chopped
250g/9oz demerara sugar
2 litres/3½ pints water
200g/7oz unsalted butter
800g/1lb 12oz oats
200g/7oz desiccated coconut
1 tbsp ground cinnamon
150g/5½oz honey
2kg/4lb 8oz plain yoghurt




Method


Preheat the oven 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Place the apples, sugar and water in a large saucepan and cook gently for 10-15 minutes, or until the apples start to break up. Remove from the heat and set aside to cool.


Melt the butter in a microwave. Place in a large bowl and stir in the oats, coconut, cinnamon and honey. Spread the mixture onto large baking trays and cook in the oven for 7-8 minutes, or until golden-brown. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool.


To serve, place the cooled apples into individual serving bowls, top with the yoghurt and sprinkle over the granola mix.



Mustard and butterbean pâté with garlic toasts



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

100g/3½oz butter beans from a can, rinsed and drained
1 tbsp wholegrain mustard
3 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp lemon juice
sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
2 slices french bread
1 garlic clove, peeled and halved




Method


Place the butterbeans, mustard, olive oil and lemon into a food processor and blend until smooth. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Heat a griddle pan until smoking and place the bread onto it. Griddle for two minutes on each side, then remove and rub with the cut side of the garlic.


To serve, place the toasts onto a plate and top with the bean pâté.

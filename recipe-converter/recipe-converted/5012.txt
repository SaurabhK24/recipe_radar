

Honey and sesame-roasted carrots with raita



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the carrots

1 tbsp olive oil
50ml/2fl oz clear honey
2 tbsp sesame seeds
5 carrots, blanched and drained

For the raita

5 tbsp Greek-style yoghurt
1 tbsp chopped fresh mint leaves
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


For the honey and sesame-roasted carrots, place the oil, honey and sesame seeds into a bowl and mix well. Place the carrots into the bowl and stir to coat.


Place the carrots onto a baking sheet and roast in the oven for 15 minutes, or until lightly browned.


For the raita, place the yoghurt and mint into a small bowl, season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper and mix well.


To serve, place the honey and sesame-roasted carrots onto a plate with the bowl of raita alongside.



Ginger cake bread and butter pudding



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Nick Nairn


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

butter, for greasing
1 slice ready-made ginger cake
4-6 tbsp ready-made custard
icing sugar, to dust
fresh mint sprig, to garnish




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6. Lightly grease a ramekin.


Slice the cake into smaller pieces and place into the base of the ramekin.


Pour over enough custard to fill the ramekin.


Place the ramekin into the oven and bake for 6-8 minutes, or until warmed through.


To serve, dust the pudding with icing sugar and garnish with a sprig of mint.

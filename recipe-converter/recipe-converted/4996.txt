

Baked eggs




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Recommended by
3 people



This easy weekend treat can be tweaked to include mushrooms, bacon or spinach. Serve with toast for dipping.






By Ed Baines


From Housecall






Ingredients

35g/1Â¼oz butter
4 eggs
2 handfuls of grated cheese
salt and pepper
additions: ham or mushrooms cooked, drained spinach (optional)




Method


Turn the oven to the highest setting and smear butter over two small ovenproof dishes. Cover with a combination of ham, mushrooms and spinach, if liked.


Crack a couple of eggs over the top, followed by grated cheese, salt and pepper.


Cook for approximately 10 minutes.



White peach mojito



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

6 fresh mint leaves, plus 1 sprig mint, to garnish
25ml/1fl oz fresh white peach purée
20ml/¾fl oz fresh lime juice
1 tsp sugar syrup
35ml/1¼fl oz dark rum
2 tsp peach liqueur
dash ginger ale
1 slice white peach, to garnish




Method


Place the mint leaves, peach purée, lime juice, sugar syrup, rum and peach liqueur into a sling glass. Stir with a long-handled spoon.


Three-quarters fill the glass with crushed ice, then top up with ginger ale.


To serve, hook the slice of white peach and the mint sprig over the side of the glass.



Griddled spiced mango with honey, lime and chilli



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

½ mango, stone removed and flesh scored
2 tsp olive oil
¼ tsp chilli flakes
1 tbsp clear honey
½ lime, juice only
2 lime wedges, to garnish
icing sugar, for dusting




Method


Preheat the grill to the highest setting.


Place the mango on a small non-stick baking tray.


Drizzle the mango with the olive oil and sprinkle over the chilli flakes, honey and lime juice.


Place the mango under the hot grill for 3-4 minutes, or until golden-brown.


To serve, garnish with lime wedges and dust with icing sugar.

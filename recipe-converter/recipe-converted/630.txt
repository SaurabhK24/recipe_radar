

Tuna and sweetcorn pasta salad




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 4



You can find fun wholemeal pasta shapes that will please kids when you add this to their lunchbox. Ready in 10.

  Each serving provides 230kcal, 16g protein, 28g carbohydrate (of which 3g sugars), 5g fat (of which 0.6g saturates), 5g fibre and 0.1g salt.



Ingredients

150g/5oz whole wheat pasta, cooked according to packet instructions
150g/5oz frozen or tinned sweetcorn
150g/5Â½oz tinned tuna in spring water, drained
2 tbsp plain yoghurt
1 tbsp mayonnaise
freshly ground black pepper
squeeze lemon (optional)




Method


Mix all the ingredients together until thoroughly combined. Transfer to a sealed plastic tub and keep in the fridge.




Recipe Tips
If you're serving this pasta salad straightaway, cool the cooked pasta under cold running water to cool.

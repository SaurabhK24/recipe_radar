

Poached vanilla meringue with fruit compôte



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 4


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients
For the meringues

4 egg, whites only
110g/4oz vanilla sugar
570ml/1 pint milk
1 vanilla pod

For the fruit compote

1 bag mixed berries, defrosted
vanilla sugar, to taste




Method


Whisk egg whites adding the sugar slowly.


Cut and split the vanilla pod. Bring the milk and vanilla to gentle simmer.


Using 2 large dessertspoons, quenelle the meringue on to the milk and gently poach each side for around 30-40 seconds. Remove and put on fruit compote and serve.


Sprinkle the mixed berries with vanilla sugar to taste. Warm berries and sugar in a pan and serve with poached meringues.


If using fresh seasonal fruit, cook for longer until the fruit is softened.

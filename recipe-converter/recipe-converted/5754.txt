

Lemon and orange Chinese-style beef



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1







By Ross Burden


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp oil
knob fresh ginger, peeled and sliced
170g/6oz beef, thinly sliced
3 lemon leaves, shredded (or subsitute lime leaves)
2 tsp sesame oil
2 tsp sesame seeds
2 tbsp lemon, zest only
honey, to drizzle




Method


Heat the oil in a small wok over a high heat, until smoking, and add the ginger and beef. Stir fry for two minutes, to brown.


Turn the heat down slightly, add the remaining ingredients and stir fry for a further two minutes before transferring to a bowl and serving.

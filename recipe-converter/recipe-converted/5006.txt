

Honey and pistachio-roasted carrots with herb salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1-2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the carrots

1 bunch baby carrots
1 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp honey
2 tbsp pistachios, shelled and chopped

For the herb salad

small handful each fresh parsley, coriander and chives
1 tbsp olive oil
2 tsp balsamic vinegar




Method


Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.


Place the carrots onto a baking sheet and drizzle with the olive oil. Transfer to the oven and roast for 15 minutes.


Remove from the oven, drizzle over the honey and sprinkle over the pistachios. Return to the oven for 2-3 minutes, until the carrots are tender and lightly browned.


For the herb salad, place all of the salad ingredients into a large bowl and stir.


To serve, place the salad onto a serving plate and top with the carrots.

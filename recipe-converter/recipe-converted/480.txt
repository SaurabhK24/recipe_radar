

Braised buttered leeks



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1-2


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian







By Garrey Dawson


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

30g/1oz butter
2 small leeks, finely sliced
splash boiling water
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Heat the butter in a small saucepan.


Add the sliced leeks and a splash of boiling water and fry gently for 3-4 minutes, or until the leeks are just tender. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper, then spoon into a bowl and serve.

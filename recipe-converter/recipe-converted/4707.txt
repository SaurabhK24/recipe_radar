

Baked apple with raisins and custard



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
3 people







By Tony Tobin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the apple

1 tbsp soft brown sugar
30g/1oz butter
20g/¾oz raisins
honey, to drizzle
½ apple, cored

For the custard

150ml/6fl oz milk
150ml/6fl oz double cream
40g/1½oz caster sugar
1 tsp vanilla essence
2 free-range egg yolks




Method


Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.


Place the butter, raisins, brown sugar and honey into a bowl and mix together.


Place the apple onto a baking sheet and stuff with the butter mixture.


Cook in the oven for eight minutes.


For the custard, warm the milk and cream in a saucepan over a medium heat.


Place the sugar, vanilla and eggs into a bowl and thoroughly mix together. While stirring, gradually add the creama and milk the over a low heat. Stir constantly, until the custard slightly thickens.


To serve, place the apple on a serving plate and pour the hot custard around.

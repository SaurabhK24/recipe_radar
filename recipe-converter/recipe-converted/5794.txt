


Preparation time
1-2 hours


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 4




Ingredients

1 400g/14¼oz jar lemon curd
4 pre bought meringue nests
600g/1lb 5¼oz low-fat fromage frais
150g/5¼oz fresh raspberries




Method


Mix the lemon curd and fromage frais in a large bowl and crumble into it the meringue nests.


Give a good mix and place in individual timbales or large ramekin dishes.


Place in the freezer for an hour.


Take out five minutes before serving so they just start to melt at the sides.


Empty onto a place a garnish with fresh raspberries.

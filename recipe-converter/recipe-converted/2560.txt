

Chocolate mousse



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
3 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian







By Garrey Dawson


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

50g/1¾oz cocoa powder, plus extra to dust
2 tbsp water
3 tbsp whole milk
1 tbsp icing sugar
15g/½oz butter
75ml/2½fl oz double cream




Method


Heat the cocoa powder, water, milk, icing sugar and butter in a saucepan and stir until smooth and combined.


In a large mixing bowl, whisk the cream until soft peaks form when the whisk is removed. Gently fold in the cocoa mixture.


Serve in a wine glass, sprinkled with a dusting of cocoa powder.

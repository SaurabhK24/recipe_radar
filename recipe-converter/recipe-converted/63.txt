Title: Avocado with tomato salsa
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 5 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Tony Tobin

Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp white vine vinegar
1 tsp honey
1 tsp finely chopped shallot
100g cherry tomatoes
1 tbsp basil
salt
pepper
1/2 avocado

Instructions:
Place the oil, vinegar, honey and shallot in a large bowl and mix together well.
Add the tomato and basil, season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper and toss together.
To serve, place the avocado in the centre of a plate and spoon in some of the salsa mixture.

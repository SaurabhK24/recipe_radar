

Cream of broccoli soup



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
½ onion, finely chopped
1 garlic clove, finely chopped
1 head broccoli, cut into small florets
500ml/18fl oz chicken or vegetable stock
100ml/3½fl oz double cream




Method


Heat the oil in a pan and gently fry the onion and garlic for 3-4 minutes until softened. Add the broccoli and stir.


Pour in the hot stock and simmer for 4-5 minutes, or until the broccoli is tender.


Stir in the cream and bring to a simmer. Allow to cool slightly, then blend until smooth with a hand-blender.


To serve, pour the soup into two soup bowls and drizzle with olive oil.



Easy garlic bread



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Makes about 250g






By Michael Moore


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

250g/9oz butter
30g/1oz tomato paste
1 clove garlic, crushed
½ tsp thyme, chopped
½ tsp rosemary, chopped
seasoning
toast or hot baguette for serving




Method


Beat butter with wooden spoon until soft, and then add the remaining ingredients and beat until smooth.


Alternatively add all ingredients to a food processor and blend until smooth.


Scrape onto cling film or foil and roll to reform. Refrigerate until ready to use.


Serve on the toast or hot baguette.



Madeira cake with mulled wine sauce



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

140ml/5fl oz red wine
pinch ground cinnamon
pinch ground ginger
100ml/3½fl oz cherry juice from a tin
2 tbsp caster sugar
½ orange, zest only
1 orange, juice only
3 slices of ready-made Madeira cake




Method


Place the red wine with the spices, cherry juice, sugar, orange zest and orange juice into a saucepan over a high heat. Bring to the boil and then reduce the heat to simmer for ten minutes until the liquid has reduced by a half.


To serve, pour the mulled wine into a bowl and top with slices of Madeira cake.

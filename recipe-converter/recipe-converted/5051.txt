

Almond dipping sauce with pitta bread fingers



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

3 tbsp almond butter, from a jar
½ lemon, juice only
1 tbsp vegetable oil
2 wholemeal pitta breads, toasted, to serve




Method


Place the almond butter, lemon juice and oil into a bowl and mix well.


Cut the pitta bread into fingers and serve with the almond dipping sauce.

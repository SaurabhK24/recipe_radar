

Eton mess with berry coulis



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Martin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the Eton mess

150ml/¼ pt double cream, whipped until soft peaks form
75g/2½oz cornflakes
3 tbsp frozen mixed berries, defrosted
sprig fresh mint

For the berry coulis

100g/3½oz frozen mixed berries, defrosted
1 lime, juice only
1 tbsp icing sugar




Method


Place the whipped double cream, cornflakes and two tablespoons of the mixed berries into a large bowl and mix together well.


Spoon half of the cream mixture into a tall glass.


To make the coulis, place the mixed berries, lime juice and icing sugar into a small food processor and blend until smooth.


Pass the berry mixture through a fine sieve.


Spoon two tablespoons of the coulis over the cream mixture in the glass.


Spoon the rest of the cream mixture into the glass, then drizzle with another spoonful of coulis.


To serve, decorate with the remaining one tablespoon of mixed berries and a sprig of mint.



Coffee and chocolate meringues with chocolate sauce



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the chocolate sauce

2 tsp instant coffee granules
3 tbsp cocoa powder
3 tbsp boiling water
3 tbsp caster sugar

For the meringues

1 free-range egg white
250g/8¾oz icing sugar
55g/2oz cocoa powder




Method


To make the chocolate sauce mix together all of the ingredients.


For the meringues, mix together the ingredients and roll into small balls.


Cook in the microwave for two minutes.


Serve on a plate drizzled with the chocolate sauce.

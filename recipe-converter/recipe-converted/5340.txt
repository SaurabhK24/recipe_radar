

Baked peaches with crushed amaretti



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 4


Recommended by
3 people


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian




Baking peaches will make even slightly hard and unsweet peaches delicious! Warm, soft with their sugars concentrated - they are a joy.





By Katie and Giancarlo Caldesi


From Return to Tuscany






Ingredients

50g/2oz butter
12 amaretti biscuits, crushed
2 tbsp honey, plus extra for drizzling
good dash vin santo, or similar sweet dessert wine, plus extra for drizzling
4 peaches, halved and stones removed
demerara sugar, to sprinkle




Method


Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.


Melt the butter in a saucepan, but do not let it brown.


Mix together the crushed amaretti biscuits, the melted butter, honey and a dash of vin santo in a bowl.


Place a heaped teaspoon of the mixture onto the flat side of each of the peach halves and place on a baking tray.


Sprinkle with demerara sugar and drizzle with more honey and vin santo.


Bake in the oven for 15-30 minutes until golden. Serve.

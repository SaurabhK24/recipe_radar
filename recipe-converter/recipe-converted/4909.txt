

Herby rice



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Nick Nairn


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

200g/7oz rice, cooked
2 tsp soy sauce
handful fresh coriander, chopped
1 lime, juice and zest




Method


Mix together all the ingredients and serve.

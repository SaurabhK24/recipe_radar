

Tomato, cucumber and onion relish



Preparation time
30 mins to 1 hour


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 6


Dietary

 
Vegetarian







By The Vegetarian Society






Ingredients

225g/8oz tomatoes, chopped into small pieces
225g/8oz cucumber, peeled and cut into small pieces
100g/4oz red onion, finely diced
2 green chillies, deseeded and finely chopped
2.5ml/½ tsp salt
1.25ml/¼ tsp sugar
3 tbsp fresh lemon juice
2 tbsp fresh coriander leaves, chopped




Method


Mix all the ingredients together and chill for one hour.


Serve with bread and salad.

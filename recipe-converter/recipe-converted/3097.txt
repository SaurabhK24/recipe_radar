


Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
30 mins to 1 hour


Serves
Serves 6-8


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian





Ingredients

150g/5oz butter
150g/5oz caster sugar
3 eggs
225g/8oz self raising flour
55g/2oz stem ginger, finely chopped
1 orange, zest and juice
1 tsp ground ginger
175g/6oz fresh cranberries
55g/2oz dried cranberries




Method


Heat the oven to 190C/375F/Gas 5.


Line a 23cm/9inch tin with baking parchment.


Cream together the butter and the sugar and beat in the eggs.


Fold in the flour and the ground ginger.


Stir in the chopped stem ginger, the orange zest and juice, and the fresh and dried cranberries.


Turn into the tin and bake for 35-45 minutes until an inserted skewer comes out clean.


Serve hot or cold with custard or vanilla ice cream (or make in advance and reheat).

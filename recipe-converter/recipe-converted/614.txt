

Trout en papillote



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
2 people







By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil
½ red pepper, sliced
½ red onion, sliced
1 trout fillet
1 lemon, sliced
½ chilli, chopped
1 tbsp soy sauce




Method


Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.


Heat the oil in a wok and when hot add the red onion and red pepper. Stir fry for one to two minutes or until softened.


Spread out a large piece of baking parchment and place the stir fried vegetables onto the middle.


Top with the trout fillet and place the lemon slices and chopped chilli on top. Pour over the soy sauce.


Fold the parchment around the trout fillet and seal tightly.


Lay on a baking sheet and cook in a hot oven (220C) for ten minutes.

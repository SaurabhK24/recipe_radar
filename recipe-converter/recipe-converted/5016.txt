

Honey and vanilla rice pudding with oranges



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian




Traditional rice pudding gets a little twist with the addition of honey and orange, but you can use whatever fruit you have available.






By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

100g/3½oz long grain rice
400ml/14fl oz milk
2 tbsp double cream
1 vanilla pod, split, seeds only
3 tbsp honey

To serve

1 orange, peeled and sliced
1 tbsp honey




Method


Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.


Place the rice, milk, cream, vanilla seeds and honey into a pan over a medium heat and bring to the boil. Transfer to an ovenproof gratin dish and place into the oven to cook for 8-10 minutes, or until cooked through and golden-brown on top.


Serve garnished with orange slices, drizzled with honey.

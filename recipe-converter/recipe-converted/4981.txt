

Honey, feta and thyme pancakes




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 4



Add a Greek twist to your pancakes for Shrove Tuesday. This topping sits on the fence between sweet and savoury and is so moreish.



Ingredients

2-3 tbsp clear honey
100g/3Â½oz feta
2-3 sprigs thyme, leaves picked
4 pancakes, to serve




Method


Put the pancakes on serving plates and crumble over the feta.


Drizzle with honey and scatter the thyme leaves over the top.


Serve hot or cold.



Coconut caramel biscuits



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Makes 10


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

150g/5½oz caster sugar
150g/5½oz coconut biscuits, crushed
½ lemon, juice only
1 tsp vegetable oil




Method


Place the caster sugar into a frying pan and melt over a medium heat until beginning to turn golden-brown.


Add the crushed biscuits and mix well. Stir in the lemon juice.


Grease a baking sheet with vegetable oil, then pour the mixture onto the baking sheet. Leave to cool, then chop into pieces.

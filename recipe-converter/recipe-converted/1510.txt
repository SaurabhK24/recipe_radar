Swap your after-dinner coffee for this potent coffee cocktail topped with a liqueur foam.






By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

25ml/1fl oz vodka
25ml/1fl oz Galliano ristretto, or other coffee liqueur
3 squirts Bols cacao white foam




Method


Place the vodka and Galliano into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.


Strain into a small cocktail glass and top with the foam.

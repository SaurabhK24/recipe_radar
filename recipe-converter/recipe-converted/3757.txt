

Eton mess




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 4


Recommended by
5 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian




This quintessential British dessert is strawberries and cream at its best. Make it in seconds with AWT’s one-step recipe.






By Antony Worrall Thompson


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

1 punnet strawberries
a dash of sugar
a dash of port
meringues, broken up
cream, softly whipped




Method


Mash the strawberries with a little sugar and port, and fold in broken meringues and softly whipped cream.

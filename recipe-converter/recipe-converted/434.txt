

Toasted seeds



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Ross Burden


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

100g/3½oz oats, toasted
55g/2oz pumpkin seeds
2 tbsp sesame seeds
55g/2oz sunflower seeds
1 tsp salt
½ tsp chilli flakes

To serve

1 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp fresh mint leaves, chopped




Method


Place the oats, all the seeds, salt and chilli flakes into a saucepan over a high heat and 'toast' for five minutes. Shake the pan regularly to ensure even cooking.


Add the oil and chopped mint, heat and stir for a further minute.


Serve warm in a bowl.

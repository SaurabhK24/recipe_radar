By Jim Wrigley


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

50ml/2fl oz blended scotch
25ml/1fl oz amaretto
twist of orange peel, to garnish




Method


Place a few ice cubes into a rocks glass. Add the whisky and the amaretto, stir and garnish with a twist of orange peel.

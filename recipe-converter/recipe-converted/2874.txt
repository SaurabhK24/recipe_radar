

Asian-style noodle salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Nick Nairn


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

85g/3oz udon noodles, cooked according to packet instructions and drained
½ red pepper, seeds removed, chopped
55g/2oz fresh pineapple flesh, chopped
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp soy sauce
2 tsp sesame oil
2.5cm/1in piece root ginger, finely chopped
½ tsp chilli flakes




Method


Place the cooked noodles into a large bowl with the red pepper and pineapple.


In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger and chilli flakes until well combined.


To serve, pour the dressing over the noodles and stir. Transfer to a serving bowl.



Griddled quesadillas with BBQ beans and cheese with cherry tomato and avocado salsa



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 4






By Andy Campbell


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

1 tbsp vegetable oil
1 pack of 15cm/6in flour tortillas
1 tin refried beans
1 smoked sausage, chopped
BBQ flavouring
1 ripe avocado
soured cream
1 tbsp lemon juice
400g/14oz grated cheese (cheddar, Monterey jack, mozzarrella)
1 punnet cherry tomatoes
salt and pepper
sprigs of coriander




Method


Mash avocado with lemon juice, add 1 tbsp of soured cream, and season to taste.


Heat the beans with the sausage, and add the BBQ flavouring.


Brush two tortillas with oil, and mark crossways on a griddle pan.


Cover one tortilla with the beans and cheese and add the avocado mixture to the other tortilla, press both together.


Cook in preheated oven 200C/400F/Gas 6 for about 5 minutes, until the cheese melts and binds it together.


Allow to cool slightly then cut into triangles from the centre like a pizza.


Garnish with cherry tomatoes, dollop of soured cream and coriander sprigs.


Serve on slates or tiles.

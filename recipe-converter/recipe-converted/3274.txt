

Attica fix




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian




Looking for ouzo coktails? Try this tasty recipe.






By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

½ lemon, cut into wedges
2 tsp caster sugar
3 small sprigs fresh oregano
50ml/2fl oz vanilla vodka
2 tsp coffee liqueur
2 tsp ouzo

To serve

lemon slice
fresh oregano leaves




Method


Place the ingredients into a cocktail shaker and mash (muddle) with the end of a rolling pin. Add a handful of ice and shake hard.


Strain the cocktail into a tumbler filled with crushed ice, garnish with a lemon slice and oregano leaves.

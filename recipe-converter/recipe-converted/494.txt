

Tomato, avocado and mango salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
4 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

½ mango sliced into thin strips
½ avocado diced
4 cherry tomatoes, sliced
1 lime, juice only
3 tbsp parsley, chopped
3 tbsp coriander, chopped
3 tbsp red onion, diced
4 tbsp olive oil




Method


Mix the mango with the avocado and cherry tomatoes.


Add the lime juice, chopped herbs, red onion and olive oil and mix together.


Serve.

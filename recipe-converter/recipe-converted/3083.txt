

Cranachan



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

½ packet ready-made honey and oat biscuits, crushed
½ tin blackcurrants, drained, juice reserved
3 tbsp clear honey
150ml/¼ pint double cream, whipped until soft peaks form when the whisk is removed




Method


Place a quarter of the crushed biscuits into the base of a tall dessert glass.


Drizzle over one tablespoon of honey, two tablespoons of blackcurrants and one tablespoon of the juice from the blackcurrants. Top with a third of the whipped cream.


Repeat the layering process twice with the remaining ingredients.


To serve, sprinkle the remaining crushed biscuits over the top of the dessert.

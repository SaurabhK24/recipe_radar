

Sweet potato and leek bubble and squeak



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 small sweet potato, peeled and cubed
2 baby leeks, finely chopped
olive oil




Method


Bring a saucepan of water to the boil. Add the sweet potato and boil for eight to ten minutes, or until soft, then drain.


Add the baby leeks to the potatoes and mash together.


Heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a very small frying pan and add the potato mixture. Fry for four to five minutes over a medium heat and then turn out onto a plate to serve.

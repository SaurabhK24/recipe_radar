

Open toasted savoury cheese sandwich



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Makes 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 slices farmhouse-style bread
1 tbsp yeast extract
100g/3½oz cheddar, sliced




Method


Toast bread and spread with the yeast extract.


Arrange the cheddar slices on top of the yeast extract and place under a hot grill for one minute or until the cheese has melted. Serve.

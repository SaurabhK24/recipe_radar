

Calypso swizzle




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian




This Caribbean carnival cocktail is perfect for a celebration.






By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

1 tbsp fresh lime juice
1 tsp vanilla syrup
25ml/1fl oz aloe vera juice
25ml/1fl oz guava juice
2 dashes angostura bitters
25ml/1fl oz white rum
25ml/1fl oz sourz pineapple liqueur
1 tbsp dark rum

To garnish

2 pineapple leaves
slice fresh guava




Method


Place all of the cocktail ingredients into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and stir until well combined.


Strain the cocktail into a highball glass filled with crushed ice and garnish with the pineapple leaves and a slice of guava.

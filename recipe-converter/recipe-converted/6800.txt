

Onion bhaji



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Makes 1


Recommended by
3 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 onion, peeled and sliced
100ml/3½fl oz milk
2 tbsp medium curry powder
2 tbsp plain flour
oil for deep frying




Method


Soak the onion in the milk for five minutes.


Heat the oil ready for deep frying (CAUTION: Hot oil is very dangerous. Do not leave unattended).


Mix the curry powder and flour together in a bowl. Remove the onion from the milk and coat it in the curry powder and flour mix.


Deep fry the onion for five minutes or until soft and golden brown. Drain and serve.

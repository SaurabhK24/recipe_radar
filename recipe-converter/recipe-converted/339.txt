

Thai-style steamed sea bass



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person







By Tony Tobin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 sea bass fillet
1 tsp olive oil
2 garlic cloves, crushed
1 lemongrass stalk, finely chopped
1 tsp fresh thyme leaves
handful fresh coriander, chopped
Â½ chilli, chopped

For the dressing

1 tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp sesame oil
1 tsp chilli, chopped




Method


Bring a large pan of water to the boil and place a bamboo steamer or colander over the top.


Rub the sea bass fillet with the olive oil, garlic, thyme, lemongrass, coriander and chilli. Place the fillet onto a piece of foil and transfer into the steamer or colander. Cover and steam for 3-5 minutes, or until just cooked through.


For the dressing mix the soy, sesame oil and chilli together.


To serve, place the sea bass on a plate and pour over the dressing.



Braised broccoli with cheese sauce



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

85g/3oz Blue Vinnie cheese, or similiar hard, crumbly blue cheese
200ml/7fl oz double cream
1 tsp Dijon mustard
salt and freshly ground black pepper
100g/3½oz purple sprouting broccoli, blanched, drained




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Heat a small saucepan over a low to medium heat, crumble in the cheese and add the cream and mustard. Stir until the cheese is melted, then season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Arrange the blanched broccoli in an ovenproof gratin dish and pour over the cheese sauce.


Bake in the oven for 5-10 minutes, or until the cheese mixture is golden-brown and bubbling.

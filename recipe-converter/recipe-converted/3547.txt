

Pomegranate molasses salad dressing



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes 8 tbsp


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian







By Miranda Richardson


From A Taste of My Life






Ingredients

1 tbsp pomegranate molasses
6 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp white wine vinegar
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Place the pomegranate molasses, olive oil and white wine vinegar in a clean jam jar.


Put the lid on the jar and shake well. Season with salt and freshly ground pepper to taste and use the dressing on your favourite salad.



Griddled pork with apple salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1







By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 pork chop
Â½ apple, cored and chopped
25g/1oz cheddar cheese, grated
25g/1oz raisins
3 tbsp fresh parsley leaves
1 tbsp white wine vinegar
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp sesame seeds




Method


Griddle the pork chop in a hot griddle pan for 3-4 minutes on each side or until cooked through. Remove from the pan and leave to rest for five minutes.


Meanwhile, mix together the apple, cheese, raisins and parsley leaves.


Cut the pork into slices and mix with the apple and cheese mixture.


Dress with the vinegar, olive oil and sesame seeds and serve.

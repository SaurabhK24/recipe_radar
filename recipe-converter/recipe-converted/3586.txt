

Drunken strawberries



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

8 strawberries
200ml/7fl oz sparkling wine
2 sprigs fresh mint, to garnish




Method


Place four strawberries into two Martini glasses and top up with sparkling wine.


To serve, garnish with a sprig of mint.

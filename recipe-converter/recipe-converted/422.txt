

Toad in the hole salsa



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
30 mins to 1 hour


Serves
Serves 2






By Michael Moore


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

4 pork sausages
100g/3½oz flour
4 eggs
½ litre milk
25g/1oz butter
50g/2oz tomato concasse
50g/2oz celery
50g/2oz apple
25g/1oz white wine vinegar
20g/¾oz brown sugar
10g/½oz tomato paste
10g/½oz sweet chilli sauce
herbs of your choice




Method


Take some sausages and cook in pan then slice lengthways (only half way through though). Take the flour, egg and milk and mix into a batter.


Plate sausages into dish and pour batter around them.


Place in oven 180C/350F/Gas 4.


Cook it for 35 to 40 minutes. Take it out of the oven when its golden brown and let it rest.


Take diced vegetables (celery, tomatoes and apples) and place in a bowl all together.


Take butter, melt in a pan, add brown sugar, white wine vinegar, sweet chilli sauce and tomato paste.


Cook out for five minutes, add the diced vegetables to the salsa, allow to cool and then add chopped herbs.


Pour over sausages and serve immediately.



Chicken bhuna with pilau rice



Preparation time
over 2 hours


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 4


Recommended by
7 people







By Paul Merrett


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

225g/½lb sliced chicken breast
1 tbsp vegetable oil
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp garam masala

For the marinade

6 tbsp natural yoghurt
1 lemon, juiced
2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
½ tsp turmeric
1 tbsp paprika
3 cardamoms, crushed with husk removed
½ tbsp salt
1 tbsp ground chilli
1 tbsp cumin
handful of fresh coriander

For the rice

2 cups basmati rice, washed well
1 onion, chopped
1 clove of garlic, chopped
½ cinnamon stick
2 cardamom pods
1 tbsp fennel seeds
1 tbsp vegetable oil




Method


Mix the marinade ingredients together and marinade the chicken for 2 hours.


Heat 1 tbsp vegetable oil in a pan and fry the onion in a pan.


Add the rice, garlic, cinnamon, cardamom and fennel seeds.


Add cold water to 1in above the level of the rice and bring to the boil.


Boil for 5 minutes and cover with a lid.


Turn off the heat and leave for 20 minutes.


In a non-stick pan heat the oils and fry the chicken until tender and cooked through.


When the chicken is cooked, add the garam masala and chopped coriander and serve with the rice and a warm naan bread.

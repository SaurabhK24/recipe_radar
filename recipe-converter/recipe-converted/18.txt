

Sweet and spicy nuts and cherries



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2-3


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

3 tbsp caster sugar
pinch dried chilli flakes
100g/3½oz brazil nuts, shells removed
1 lime, juice only
1 orange, juice only
handful fresh mint, chopped
50g/2oz canned black cherries, stones removed, drained
1 orange, cut into wedges, to serve




Method


Place the sugar into a frying pan and heat until melted and golden. Add the chilli flakes and the nuts and cook over a low heat, stirring occasionally, for 2-3 minutes.


Add the lime and orange juice, the chopped mint and cherries and stir well to coat in the caramel.


To serve, pour the sweet and spicy nuts and cherries into a bowl and garnish with the orange wedges.



Tapenade



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Nick Nairn


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

3 garlic cloves, peeled and chopped
85g/3oz olives, pits removed, chopped
4-6 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
ciabatta, sliced, to serve




Method


Place the garlic cloves, olives, olive oil and parsley in a mini food processor and blend until smooth.


Heat a grill pan until hot and chargrill the ciabatta slices on both sides.


Transfer tapenade to a serving bowl and serve with the chargrilled ciabatta slices.

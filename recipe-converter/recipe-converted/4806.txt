

Ham, cheese and fig skewers



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 4







By Maria Elia


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

6 slices of ham, cut in half
1 ball buffalo mozzarella, torn into 12 pieces
12 basil leaves
4 char-grilled figs, quartered
olive oil
sea salt




Method


On the end of each ham slice, place a piece of mozzarella, basil leaf and a fig quarter.


Drizzle with olive oil and season with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Roll up and secure with cocktail stick.


Refrigerate until required.

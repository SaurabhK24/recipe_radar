

Tomato and yellow pepper crostini



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 thick slices of bread
1 tbsp olive oil
1 clove garlic, crushed
½ yellow pepper, de-seeded and sliced
4 mini mozzarella balls
handful fresh basil
4 cherry tomatoes, sliced in half
sea salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Preheat the grill to its highest setting.


Brush the bread with the oil and rub in the garlic.


Layer the remaining ingredients onto the bread, season and place under the grill for 2-3 minutes, until cheese is bubbly. Serve.

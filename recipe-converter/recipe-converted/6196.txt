

Bandera



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes 1 litre


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Andy Pearson


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients
For the sangrita

700ml/ 1 pint 5fl oz tomato juice
200ml/7fl oz orange juice
75ml/3fl oz fresh lime juice
8 dashes Tabasco sauce
2 level teaspoons of cayenne pepper

To finish

50ml/2fl oz sangrita
50ml/2fl oz blanco Tequila
50ml/2fl oz lime juice




Method


For the sangrita, pour all of the sangrita ingredients into a jug and mix well.


To serve, pour each of the three drinks into a shot glass, then line up the three glasses.


Sip from each glass in turn - first the lime, then the tequila, and finally the sangrita.

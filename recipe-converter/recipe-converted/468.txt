

Tomato spirals



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Makes 12


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian




These cheese and tomato pastries would go down well at a children's party and kids can help make them too.





By Kate Morris and Sally Brown


From I Can Cook






Ingredients

125g/4½oz ready-made puff pastry
4 tsp tomato puree
30g/1oz cheddar, grated
4 fresh basil leaves, shredded




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6. Line a baking tray with greaseproof paper.


Roll the dough out on a floured worked surface to a 3mm/⅛in thickness.


Spread the tomato puree all ove the pastry. Sprinkle over the cheddar and basil.


Roll the pastry up tightly, then cut it into 12 slices. Place the slices, cut-side up onto the baking tray and bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden-brown.



Tamarillo pudding



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Ross Burden


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tamarillo, halved
25g/1oz caster sugar
50ml/2fl oz red wine
2 tbsp Greek yoghurt, to serve




Method


Heat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.


Place the tamarillo in a roasting dish and sprinkle with sugar and red wine. Roast for 15 minutes until soft and syrupy.


To serve, spoon into a bowl and serve with Greek yoghurt.

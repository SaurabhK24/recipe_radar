

Fried edamame beans with chilli



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Nick Nairn


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 tbsp olive oil
150g/5½oz edamame beans, in their pods
pinch chilli flakes
2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Heat the olive oil in a frying pan and add the beans, chilli flakes and garlic. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and sauté for 2-3 minutes, until the beans are tender.


To serve, place into a serving bowl.

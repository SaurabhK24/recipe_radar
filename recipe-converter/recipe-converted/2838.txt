

Coconut rice pudding



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Ed Baines


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

100g/3½oz butter, melted
100g/3½oz rice
290ml/½pt milk
200g/7oz coconut milk
50g/2oz caster sugar
drizzle double cream
orange segments




Method


Place all the ingredients into a pan over a medium heat and bring to the boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 8-10 minutes, or until the rice has absorbed all the liquid.


Transfer to a bowl, drizzle with cream and top with orange segments to serve.

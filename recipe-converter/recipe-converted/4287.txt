Roll back to the 1940s and make a proper martini with this gin cocktail recipe.






By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

50ml/2fl oz gin
25ml/1fl oz sweet vermouth
dash bitters

To serve

orange slice, twisted




Method


Place all the ingredients into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and stir.


Strain the cocktail into a coupette glass and garnish with the orange.

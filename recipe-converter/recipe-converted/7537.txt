


Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2




Ingredients

2 tsp black peppercorns, crushed, but still in quite large pieces
2 sirloin steaks, cut 1.5cm/½in thick
7.5ml/1½ tsp olive oil
sea salt




Method


Rub the steaks lightly with the oil, then press the crushed peppercorns firmly into the meat.


Heat the grill or grill pan, until very hot. Cook the steaks quickly for about 2 minutes on each side.


Season with a little salt and serve immediately.

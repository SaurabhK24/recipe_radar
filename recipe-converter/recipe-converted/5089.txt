

Hot buttered toddy



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian




Stay warm as the nights draw in with this comforting whisky cocktail.






By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

1 tsp brandy butter, softened
pinch ground allspice
2 tsp vanilla syrup
25ml/1fl oz whisky
orange peel studded with cloves
hot cider, to top up




Method


Place all of the ingredients except the cider into a latte-style glass.


Top with hot cider and serve.

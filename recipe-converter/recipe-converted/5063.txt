

Baked figs with Stilton



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Makes 2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By James Martin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 figs, cut open
55g/2oz Stilton cheese
balsamic vinegar
olive oil
mixed salad leaves, to serve




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Stuff each fig with a wedge of Stilton and press halves back together.


Place on a baking tray and drizzle with balsamic vinegar and olive oil. Place into the oven for eight to ten minutes, until hot and bubbling.


Remove from the oven and serve with a mixed salad.



Fried green beans



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Tony Tobin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

3 tbsp olive oil
150g/5½oz green beans, finely sliced
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Heat a deep frying pan and add the olive oil. Add the green beans and fry for five minutes, until cooked through and golden. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Serve in a bowl as a side dish.



Indian-style sautéed potatoes



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
30 mins to 1 hour


Serves
Serves 2-3


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp vegetable oil
250g/9oz new potatoes
½ onion, chopped
4 garlic cloves, crushed
2 tsp curry powder
2 tsp ground cumin
2 tsp ground turmeric
fresh coriander, to garnish




Method


Heat the oil in frying pan. Add the new potatoes, onion and garlic and fry for two minutes.


Add the curry powder, cumin and turmeric and fry over a high heat.


Cover the pan with a lid, reduce the heat and cook for 30-35 minutes until the potatoes are cooked.


Serve on a plate garnished with fresh coriander.

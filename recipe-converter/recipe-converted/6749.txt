

Nutty mayonnaise



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 8-10


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Rick Stein


From Fresh Food






Ingredients

90ml/6tbsp mayonnaise
25g/1oz toasted cashew nuts, finely chopped
2 sun-dried tomatoes, finely chopped
1 tbsp parsley, chopped
1 tbsp toasted cashew nuts, whole




Method


Place the mayonnaise in a bowl and add the chopped nuts, sun-dried tomato and parsley. Mix well.


Sprinkle over the whole nuts and serve.

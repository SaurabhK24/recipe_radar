less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

250ml/9fl oz Greek yoghurt
3 tbsp double cream
2 tsp icing sugar
3 tbsp damson jam




Method


Whisk the yoghurt, cream and icing sugar together.


Spoon the jam into the base of a glass serving dish and top with the yoghurt mixture. Refrigerate until serving.

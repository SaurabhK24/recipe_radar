

Avocado, tomato, feta and brown rice grain bowl




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian




This tasty lunch is a really easy way to work more vegetables into your daily diet. If you’re taking it to work you might want to take the avocado whole and cut just before eating.





By Rebecca Sargent






Ingredients

50g/1¾oz brown rice
1 spring onion, sliced
1 tomato, sliced
½ avocado, sliced
30g/1oz feta, crumbled
2 sprigs coriander, leaves picked
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Cook the rice according to packet instructions, then drain.


Place the rice in the bowl you plan to eat from. Add the spring onion, tomato, avocado, feta and coriander.


Drizzle liberally with the oil and season with salt and pepper. Eat straight away or pack in your lunchbox ready to take to work.



Peach soufflé



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Gino D'Acampo


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

½ peach, peeled and finely chopped
½ orange, juice only
2 free-range egg whites
2 tbsp caster sugar
1 tbsp icing sugar




Method


Preheat the oven 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Place the peach and orange juice into a food processor and blend until smooth.


In a large bowl whisk the egg whites until stiff peaks form when the whisk is removed. Add the caster sugar and whisk again.


Fold the peach mixture into the egg white mixture and spoon into a ramekin.


Transfer to the oven and bake for 7-8 minutes, or until risen.


Serve on a plate with a dusting of icing sugar.



Marzipan and orange plait with mascarpone cream



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the orange plait

250g/9oz puff pastry
150g/5½oz marzipan, cut
2 oranges, peeled, segmented and sliced
into bite-sized pieces
1 tbsp honey
½ tsp of cinnamon
1 free-range egg, beaten
2 tbsp of icing sugar

For the mascarpone cream

55ml/2fl oz double cream, whipped
125g/4oz mascarpone




Method


To make the marzipan orange plait, preheat oven 180C/350F/Gas 4.


Roll out the puff pastry to form a large rectangle.


Place the marzipan down the centre of the puff pastry and top with the orange slices.


Drizzle with honey and sprinkle with cinnamon.


Cut the sides of pastry making the cuts about one inch markings apart. Plait the pastry across the middle.


Brush with the beaten egg and place plait onto baking tray.


Cook in a pre-heated oven for 20 minutes until golden.


Dust with icing sugar and serve.


To make the mascarpone cream, mix the whipped cream and mascarpone together in a bowl to a smooth thick consistency. Serve with the orange plait.

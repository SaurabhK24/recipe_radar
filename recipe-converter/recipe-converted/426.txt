

Toad-in-the-hole with roasted tomatoes



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Makes 1-2







By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the toad-in-the-hole

2 eggs
splash milk
6 tbsp flour
1 tsp vegetable oil
2 sausages

For the tomatoes

1 tbsp oil
4 garlic cloves, crushed
handful cherry tomatoes
splash balsamic vinegar
small bunch fresh basil, chopped
2 tbsp fresh coriander, chopped




Method


Preheat the oven 225C/425F/Gas 7.


Whisk together the eggs and milk and then gradually whisk in the flour to make a smooth batter.


Heat the oil in a small oven-proof pan over a medium heat. Add the sausages and cook on all sides until browned.


Pour the batter into the pan with the sausages and transfer to the oven to cook for 15 minutes, or until risen and golden.


For the tomatoes, heat a separate oven-proof pan with the oil. Add the garlic cloves and tomatoes and saut√© for three minutes over a medium heat.


Transfer to the oven and roast for five minutes.


Remove the tomato pan from the oven and add the balsamic vinegar.


Add the basil and coriander and stir through when ready to serve.


Serve the toad-in-the-hole with the tomatoes beside.

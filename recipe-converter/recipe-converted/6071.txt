

Mango and lime fool



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Nick Nairn


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

½ mango, skin and stone removed, chopped
150ml/5¼fl oz double cream, lightly whipped
1 tbsp honey, plus extra for drizzling
½ lime, juice and zest only
lime wedges and fresh mint sprigs, to garnish




Method


In a small bowl, mix together the chopped mango, whipped cream, honey, lime juice and zest and spoon into a serving glass. Garnish with an extra drizzle of honey, lime wedges and mint sprigs.

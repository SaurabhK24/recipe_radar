

Garlic roasted mushroom on toast




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian




This is easy enough to make on a weekday, just pop the mushrooms in the oven while you get ready.





By Rebecca Sargent






Ingredients

2 portobello mushrooms, whole
1 garlic clove, thinly sliced
1 tbsp olive oil
1 slice sourdough, toasted
sea salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/180C Fan/Gas 5.


Place the mushroom on a baking tray and sprinkle the garlic over them. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper and place in the oven for 20 minutes.


To serve, place the mushrooms on top of the toast and season to taste.



Marinated courgette, lemon, gorgonzola and sesame salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

½ courgette, finely sliced
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
½ lemon, juice only
55g/2oz gorgonzola (or a similar vegetarian blue cheese), crumbled
1 tbsp chopped fresh coriander leaves
1 tbsp chopped fresh mint leaves
1 tsp sesame seed, to serve




Method


Combine all the ingredients except the sesame seeds in a bowl and mix until combined.


Leave to marinate for 5 minutes.


To serve, sprinkle over the sesame seeds.

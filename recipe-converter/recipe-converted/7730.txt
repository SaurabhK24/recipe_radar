

Ploughman’s pickle



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
30 mins to 1 hour


Serves
Makes about 2 jars


Dietary

 
Vegetarian




This veg-packed pickle is great with cold meats or in a cheese and pickle sandwich.






By Gary Murdock and Pete Taylor


From The Big Allotment Challenge






Ingredients

120g/4½oz carrots, diced
1 medium onion, diced
1 medium turnip, diced
1 medium courgette, diced
1 medium cucumber, seeds removed, diced
3 garlic cloves, sliced
150g/5½oz dark brown sugar
salt
30ml/1fl oz lemon juice
180ml/6fl oz malt vinegar
1 tsp ground allspice
½ tsp cayenne pepper




Method


Place all the ingredients into a preserving pan and simmer until the turnips have reached a firm, but just-cooked consistency.


Transfer to sterilised jars.




Recipe Tips
Store this chutney in the fridge and eat within a week.

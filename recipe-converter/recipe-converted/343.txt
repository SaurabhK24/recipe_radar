Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

 
Vegetarian




A simple recipe for a delicious blackberry cocktail.






By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

35ml/1½fl oz gin
20ml/¾fl oz lemon juice
1 tsp sugar syrup
15ml/½ fl oz blackberry liqueur
slice of lemon and a blackberry, to garnish




Method


Place all of the ingredients into a tumbler filled with crushed ice and stir well.


Garnish with a slice of lemon and a blackberry.

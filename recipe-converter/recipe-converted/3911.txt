

Filo pastry scrunches with fruit



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

3 sheets filo pastry
5 tbsp butter, melted
25g/1oz icing sugar
5 tbsp honey
8 dried apricots, chopped
25g/½oz pistachios, chopped
2 tbsp sesame seeds




Method


Preheat oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Brush the filo pastry sheets with butter and dust with the icing sugar. Scrunch and place onto a baking trays.


Place into a pre-heated oven for three minutes.


Take out and leave to cool for a few minutes.


Place the filo pastry onto a plate and drizzle with honey, then cover with the chopped apricots and pistachios. Finally sprinkle them with the sesame seeds.

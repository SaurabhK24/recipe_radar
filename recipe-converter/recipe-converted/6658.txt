

Mutton 'hotpot'



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
2 people







By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 tbsp olive oil
3 cooked pink fir apple potatoes, sliced
½ onion, chopped
1 garlic clove, crushed
3 baby carrots, chopped
100g/3½oz mutton, chopped
salt and freshly ground black pepper
75ml/2½fl oz beef stock
50ml/2fl oz red wine
25g/1oz butter




Method


Heat the olive oil in a frying pan and fry the potato slices gently until beginning to colour on both sides. Remove from the pan and set aside.


In the same pan, gently fry the onion, garlic and carrots for 3-4 minutes, then add the chopped mutton. Season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper and fry for ten minutes, or until the mutton is tender and golden-brown.


In the same pan, heat the beef stock and red wine and simmer until reduced slightly. Stir in the butter.


Serve the mutton and carrot mixture in a serving dish, pour over the wine sauce and top with the fried potato slices.



Grilled broccoli with water chestnut dressing



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the broccoli

110g/4oz purple sprouting broccoli, trimmed

For the dressing

2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp honey
4 water chestnuts, diced




Method


Bring a large pan of water to the boil. Add the broccoli and par-boil for two to three minutes and drain.


Heat a grill pan and chargrill the broccoli until nicely coloured.


To make the dressing, mix together the honey, soy sauce and water chestnuts.


Serve the broccoli warm, drizzled with the dressing.



King prawns and cabbage en papillote



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1







By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 tbsp olive oil
100g/3½oz cabbage, shredded
50ml/2fl oz white wine
½ lemon, juice only
50ml/2floz clear honey
3 king prawns
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Preheat a baking sheet in the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Place all the ingredients into a bowl, mix together and season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Place a large sheet of greaseproof paper on top of an equal-sized sheet of aluminium foil.


Pour the mixture onto one side of the paper, arranging the prawns on top.


Fold the paper in half and seal the edges by rolling up tight.


Carefully place the sealed bag on the baking tray and bake for about 15 minutes


To serve, transfer the bag onto a plate and tear open at the table.

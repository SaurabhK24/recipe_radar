

Tapenade



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Ross Burden


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

3 garlic cloves, roughly chopped
1 large handful black olives, pitted
handful fresh flatleaf parsley leaves
1 lemon, zest only
1 tbsp olive oil
freshly ground black pepper, to taste
toasted ciabatta, to serve




Method


Place the ingredients into a food processor and blend together to form a coarse paste.


Spread the tapenade onto toasted ciabatta bread and serve.

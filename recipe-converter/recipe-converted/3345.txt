

Cucumber ribbon salad with lime and sesame



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Maria Elia


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 cucumber, cut into ribbons using a potato peeler
2 tbsp sesame oil
½ lime, juice only
1 tsp sesame seeds
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Combine the cucumber, sesame oil, lime juice and sesame seeds together in a bowl. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and serve immediately.



Cheesy celeriac mash



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Ross Burden


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

200g/7oz celeriac, peeled and chopped
salt and freshly ground black pepper
pinch of nutmeg
2 tbsp double cream
55g/2oz brie
fresh chervil, to garnish




Method


Bring a large pan of water the boil. Add the celeriac and boil for ten minutes, or until tender.


Add the nutmeg, salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste, and mash the celeriac to a rough paste.


Add the cream and brie, stirring to mix well.


Place into a warm bowl and garnish with the chervil.

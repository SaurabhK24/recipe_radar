

Maple cream with glazed oranges



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

200ml/7fl oz double cream
1 tbsp icing sugar
1 orange, peeled and segmented
2 tbsp maple syrup




Method


Whip the cream with the icing sugar in a bowl until it forms soft peaks when the whisk is removed.


To serve, spoon the cream mixture into a serving glass, place the orange segments on top and drizzle with the maple syrup.

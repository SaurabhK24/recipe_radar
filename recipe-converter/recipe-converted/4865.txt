

Toasted hemp seeds



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Gino D'Acampo


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

100g/3½oz hemp seeds
2 tbsp olive oil
salt




Method


Heat the hemp seeds in a frying pan over a medium heat and toast for five minutes, tossing frequently to ensure even cooking.


Add the olive oil, season with plenty of salt and remove from the heat.


To serve, pour into a bowl.

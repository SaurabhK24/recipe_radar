

Onion bhajis



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 onion, sliced
2 tbsp plain flour
1 free-range egg
1 tbsp curry powder
1 tbsp Greek-style yoghurt
oil for frying




Method


Mix all of the ingredients together except for the oil.


Heat the oil in a large frying pan. Place a large spoonful of the mixture in the pan.


Fry for 2-3 minutes on each side or until crisp and golden.


Repeat with the rest of the mixture.


Drain on some kitchen paper and serve.

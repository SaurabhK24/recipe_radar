

Pear and Cointreau cocktail



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes 1







By Tony Tobin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 pear, peeled and chopped
1 handful ice
1 miniature bottle Cointreau
2 tbsp double cream




Method


In a blender, mix the pear, ice, and Cointreau together.


Pour into a Martini glass and drizzle with a little cream.

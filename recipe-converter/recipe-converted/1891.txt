

Spinach mash



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Paul Rankin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil
2 handfuls fresh spinach
2 garlic cloves, chopped
200g/7oz ready-made mashed potato




Method


Heat the oil in a pan and add the spinach and garlic. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the spinach has wilted.


Stir in the cooked mashed potato and cook for a further 3-4 minutes, or until warmed through. Serve.

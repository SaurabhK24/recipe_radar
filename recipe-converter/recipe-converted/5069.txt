

Honey-roasted fig and red onion crostini



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Paul Rankin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil
¼ red onion, finely sliced
1 garlic clove, chopped
1 tsp soft brown sugar
2 tsp balsamic vinegar
1 fig, halved
1 tbsp clear honey
2 slices baguette, toasted




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Heat the olive oil in a pan, add the red onion and fry over a gentle heat for five minutes until softened.


Add the garlic and sugar and cook for 1-2 minutes. Add the balsamic vinegar and cook for three minutes.


Place the halved fig onto a baking sheet and drizzle with honey. Roast for five minutes in the oven.


To serve, spread the caramelised onions onto the toasted baguette slices and top with the honey-roasted figs.



Roasted broccoli



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 medium head of broccoli, broken up into florets
1 tbsp olive oil




Method


Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.


Heat an ovenproof griddle pan until smoking hot. Place the broccoli into the pan and drizzle with the oil.


Grill for two minutes on each side then transfer to the oven for five minutes, until cooked through.

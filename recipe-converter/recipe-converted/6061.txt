

Mango and bacon salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1







By Paul Rankin






Ingredients

4 slices pancetta
1 tsp sesame oil
5 stems choi sum
1 tsp soy sauce
Â½ mango, thinly sliced
1 tbsp fresh coriander




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


On a baking tray lay the pancetta flat and put into a hot oven for four minutes until crispy.


Drain on kitchen paper.


In a hot wok add the sesame oil and choi sum, and stir-fry for two minutes. Next add the soy sauce and then take off the heat.


Place the choi sum into a bowl and mix with the mango and coriander.


Serve on a plate and top with the crispy pancetta.

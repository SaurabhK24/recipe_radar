

Crushed new potatoes



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 4


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian







By Bonnie and Peter Woods


From Recipe for Success






Ingredients

600g/1 lb 5oz new potatoes
1 tbsp butter
1 tbsp crème fraîche
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Bring a large pan of water to the boil and add the new potatoes. Boil until tender


Drain and crush them with the back of a fork.


Stir in the butter and crème fraîche and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Serve.

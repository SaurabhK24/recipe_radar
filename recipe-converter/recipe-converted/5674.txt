

Bakewell pudding



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
30 mins to 1 hour


Serves
Serves 4 - 6


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Mrs Beeton






Ingredients

450ml/¾ pint breadcrumbs
600ml/1 pint milk
3 eggs
55g/2oz sugar
8og/3oz butter
25g/1oz ground almonds
jam




Method


Butter a pie dish, add the breadcrumbs, covering them with a layer of strawberry or any other kind of jam.


Mix the milk with the beaten eggs, the ground almonds, and the butter and sugar.


Beat all the ingredients together, pour into the dish and bake for 1 hour at a moderate heat.



Goats' cheese and sun-blushed tomato salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil, plus extra to grease
100g/3½oz round goats' cheese, cut in half
½ tsp cracked black pepper
small bunch fresh basil
small bunch fresh coriander
55g/2oz sun-blushed tomatoes, chopped
½ tsp balsamic vinegar




Method


Heat a griddle pan until smoking, then brush with the oil.


Place the cheese onto the griddle and cook for two minutes on each side.


Meanwhile mix together the remaining ingredients in a bowl, then place onto a serving plate.


To serve, place the griddled goats' cheese on top of the salad.

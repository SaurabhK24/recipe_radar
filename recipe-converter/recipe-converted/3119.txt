

Cream cheese dip with toast



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

50g/1¾oz cream cheese
½ tsp Dijon mustard
2tbsp olive oil
30ml/1fl oz tomato passata
1 tsp white wine vinegar
2 spring onions, sliced
2 slices bread, toasted




Method


In a bowl, mix the cream cheese with the Dijon mustard, olive oil, white wine vinegar and tomato passata to form a thick paste.


Serve in a dish with the toast, sprinkled with the spring onions.

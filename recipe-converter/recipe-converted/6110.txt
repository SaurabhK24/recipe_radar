

Maple, lime and mango syllabub



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

125ml/4fl oz double cream, softly whipped
3 tbsp Greek-style yoghurt
½ lime, juice only
1 tbsp maple syrup, plus extra to serve
½ mango, peeled and chopped
lime wedges, to garnish




Method


In a bowl, mix the whipped cream, yoghurt, lime juice and maple syrup together.


To serve, spoon the mixture into a dessert glass. Top with the chopped mango, drizzle with extra maple syrup and garnish with lime wedges.

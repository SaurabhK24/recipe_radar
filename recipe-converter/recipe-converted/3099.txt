

Cranberry and pear relish



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Makes approx. 400g relish


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Lotte Duncan






Ingredients

7oz (200g) light brown muscovado sugar
10fl oz (300ml) water
1 orange, grated zest and juice
8oz (225g) fresh cranberries
2 pears, peeled, cored and diced




Method


Dissolve the sugar in the water and then boil for 5 minutes.


Add the orange zest and juice and the cranberries and cook for 10 minutes, or until the berries are starting to burst.


Add the pears and gently cook for another 5 minutes. Cool.

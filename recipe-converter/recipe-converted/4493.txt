

Gravlax with dill yoghurt



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes 10







By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

50g/1½oz Greek yoghurt
50g/1½oz ready-made gravlax, chopped
1 tbsp chopped fresh dill
salt and freshly ground black pepper
10 ready-made mini canapé shells




Method


Place the yoghurt, gravlax and dill into a bowl and season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Mix the ingredients well.


Spoon the mixture into the canapé shells.


To serve, arrange the shells neatly onto a clean plate.

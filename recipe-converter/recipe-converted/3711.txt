

Eggs and spinach en cocotte



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

30g/1oz butter
55g/2oz fresh spinach leaves
2 medium free-range eggs
50ml/2fl oz double cream
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.


Melt the butter in a small pan. Add the spinach and cook until wilted. Place into a small ovenproof dish.


Crack the eggs into a bowl and carefully slide on top of the spinach. Pour over the cream and season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Place in the oven and bake for about 7-10 minutes, or until the eggs are just set. Serve immediately.

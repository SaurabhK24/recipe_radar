

Fruit salad



Preparation time
1-2 hours


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 4


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian




The Italian name for this recipe is Macedonio di Frutta. Any other fruits can be used for this recipe too.






By Gino D'Acampo


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

5 tbsp amaretto liquor
200g/7oz strawberries, halved
200g/7oz blueberries
200g/7oz melon, cubed
mint, to garnish (optional)




Method


Soak all the fruit in the amaretto for at least 1 hour.


Serve the fruit in a glass and spoon over a bit of the amaretto used to marinate the fruit in.


Garnish with a sprig of mint.

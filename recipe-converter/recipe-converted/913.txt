

Brandy butter




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 6


Recommended by
8 people


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian




Try this easy recipe for traditional brandy butter; perfect for slathering over mince pies or Christmas pudding.



Ingredients

125g/4oz unsalted butter, softened
125g/4oz icing sugar
2 tbsp boiling water
3 tbsp brandy




Method


Cream together the butter and the icing sugar.


Beat in the boiling water and brandy until smooth.


Chill until needed and serve with mince pies or Christmas pudding.

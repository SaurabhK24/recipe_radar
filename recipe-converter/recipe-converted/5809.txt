

Lemon posset



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By James Martin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

50g/2oz double cream, whipped
50g/2oz ready-made lemon curd
25g/1oz strawberries, thinly sliced
25g/1oz cantuccini biscuits, crushed
25g/1oz flaked almonds, toasted
mint leaf, to garnish




Method


In a small bowl mix together the whipped cream and lemon curd.


Layer a glass with the strawberries, biscuits and almonds. Spoon in the lemon curd and cream mixture and garnish with the mint sprig.



Gnocchi with pesto



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person







By Gino D'Acampo

For the pesto

Method


Stir together all the pesto ingredients.


Add the gnocchi and stir to coat.

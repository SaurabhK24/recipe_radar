

Parsley pesto



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes 1 portion


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Darina Allen


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients
For the parsley pesto

30g/1oz flatleaf parsley leaves (no stalks)
1-2 garlic cloves, crushed
40g/1½oz freshly grated parmesan
30g/1oz pine nuts
75ml/2½fl oz extra virgin olive oil
salt




Method


Put all the ingredients except the oil and salt into the food processor. Whiz for a second or two, add the oil and a little salt. Taste and correct seasoning.



Cherry negroni




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

25ml/1fl oz cherry brandy
25ml/1fl oz gin
25ml/1fl oz sweet vermouth
25ml/1fl oz campari

To garnish

orange slice




Method


Place all the ingredients into a tumbler glass with a handful of ice and stir until well combined.


Garnish with a slice of orange and serve.

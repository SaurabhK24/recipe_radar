

Lemon oregano potatoes



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
1 to 2 hours


Serves
Serves 4


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

 
Vegetarian







By David Laris


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

2.5kg/5½lb potatoes
80ml/5tbsp olive oil
80ml/5tbsp water
handful fresh oregano (or ½ tsp dried oregano)
1 lemon, juiced.




Method


Preheat oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.


Peel and slice the potatoes lengthways. Place in roasting tray.


Cover with olive oil, water, lemon juice and oregano.


Season to taste and mix.


Place the baking tray in the oven for 1½ hours. If cooking with Thyme, Sage and Garlic Roast Chicken, put the tray under the chicken to catch all the juices.

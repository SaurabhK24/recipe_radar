

Goats' cheese pâté



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 4


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Mike Robinson


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

1 tub goats' cheese
olive oil
mixed chopped herbs
crusty bread




Method


Place the cheese, oil and herbs in a bowl and stir.


Spread liberally on the crusty bread and serve.



Whisky sour




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Makes 1


Recommended by
2 people



When life gives you lemons (and leftover egg whites) you should make whisky sours.






By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

1 small free-range organic egg, white only
2 tsp caster sugar
1 tsp vanilla syrup
1 small lemon, juice only
50ml/2fl oz Bourbon
2 dashes bitters




Method


Place all the ingredients into a cocktail shaker and shake.


Strain into a tumbler with ice.

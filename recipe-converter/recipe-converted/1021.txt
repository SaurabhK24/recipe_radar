

Warm pak choi salad with ginger and chilli dressing



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Phil Vickery


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 pak choi, quartered
25g/1oz piece root ginger, peeled, finely chopped
1 red chilli, finely chopped
1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
1 orange, peeled, segmented
3 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp fresh chopped chives
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Place the pak choi into a steamer and steam for five minutes, or until wilted.


Whisk the rest of the ingredients together in a small bowl to make a dressing.


To serve, pile the pak choi onto a serving plate and drizzle over the dressing.

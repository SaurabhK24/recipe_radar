

Lettuce with oyster sauce



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2-4







By Ken Hom


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

750g/1Â½lb Cos or Iceberg lettuce
3 tbsp oyster sauce
1 tbsp groundnut (peanut) oil




Method


Separate the lettuce leaves and blanch them in a pan of boiling, salted water for about 30 seconds or until they have wilted slightly.


Remove them and drain well. Mix the oyster sauce with the oil.


Arrange the lettuce leaves on a warm serving dish, pour the oyster sauce mixture over it and serve immediately.

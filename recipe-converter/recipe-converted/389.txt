

Thyme and garlic-roasted vegetables



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian







By Garrey Dawson


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

2 tbsp olive oil
½ small swede, peeled and cut into small cubes
½ baking potato, peeled and cut into small cubes
2 garlic cloves, peeled, chopped
sprig fresh rosemary
3 sprigs fresh thyme
handful broccoli florets
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Heat the oil in an ovenproof pan and gently fry the swede, potato, garlic and herbs for 6-8 minutes, stirring regularly.


Add the broccoli florets, season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper, then transfer to the oven to cook for a further 8-10 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.

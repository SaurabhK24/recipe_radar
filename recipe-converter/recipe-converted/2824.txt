

Coconut and mango smoothie



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
1 person


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Nick Nairn


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

100g/3½oz mango, peeled and chopped
2 tbsp runny honey
50g/1¾oz Greek yoghurt
100ml/3½floz milk
100ml/3½floz coconut milk
1 small handful ice
½ lime, juice only

To serve

sprig of mint




Method


Blend the mango, honey, yoghurt, milk, coconut milk, ice and lime juice in a liquidiser until smooth.


Pour into a tall smoothie glass and garnish with a mint sprig.



Tomato rubbed toast




Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian




A Mediterranean classic. Toast has never tasted so delicious!





By Emily Angle






Ingredients

1 ripe tomato
1 tbsp olive oil
salt and freshly cracked black pepper
1 slice sourdough, toasted




Method


Cut the tomato in half and rub the flesh side into the toast. Season with salt and pepper and drizzle with olive oil.

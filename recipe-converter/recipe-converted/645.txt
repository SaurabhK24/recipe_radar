

Tuna burger



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
2 people







By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

55g/2oz tuna steak, finely chopped
Â¼ onion, finely chopped
1 tsp Dijon mustard
1 tsp sesame seeds
2 tsp olive oil




Method


Place the tuna, onion, mustard and sesame seeds into a bowl and mix well. Using your hands, mould the mixture into a patty.


Heat the oil in a frying pan over a medium-high heat and fry the patty for 1-2 minutes on each side, or until cooked to your liking.


To serve, place the tuna burger onto a serving plate.



Devonshire vanilla ice cream and summer fruit milkshakes



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 4


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Michael Caines


From Countryfile Summer Diaries






Ingredients

200g/7oz good quality vanilla ice cream, preferably Devonshire
200g/7oz fresh strawberries, hulled, or raspberries
3 tbsp clear honey
140ml/5fl oz whole milk




Method


Place all the ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth.


To serve, pour into chilled glasses.



Mo-tea-to



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Andy Pearson


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

2 tsp caster sugar
1 tbsp freshly squeezed lime juice
8 fresh mint leaves, plus 1 extra sprig, to garnish
scoop crushed ice
50ml/2fl oz gin
75ml/2¾fl oz chilled strong green tea




Method


Place the sugar and lime juice into a highball glass and stir until dissolved.


Wipe the mint leaves around the rim of the glass, then tear them in two and drop them into the glass.


Crush the mint leaves lightly with the back of a teaspoon.


Fill the glass with crushed ice.


Add the gin and chilled green tea and stir well.


To serve, garnish the glass with a sprig of mint.

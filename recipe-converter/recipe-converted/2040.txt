

Apple sponge pudding with toffee sauce



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Recommended by
2 people


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Tony Tobin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the apple

1 apple
75g/2¾oz butter
75g/2¾oz sugar

For the pudding

75g/2¾oz self raising flour
25g/1oz butter
25g/1oz brown sugar
1 egg
25ml/1fl oz milk

For the toffee sauce

75g/2¾oz butter
75g/2½oz dark brown sugar
100ml /3½fl oz double cream




Method


Pre-heat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Peel and slice the apple into wedges. Melt the butter and sugar in an oven-proof frying pan. Cook until golden. Add the apple wedges.


For the pudding, mix together the flour, butter, sugar, egg and milk to form a smooth paste.


Place the mixture on top of the apples place into an oven proof frying pan and place in the oven and cook for 8-10 minutes.


For the toffee sauce, melt the butter and sugar for four minutes slowly add the cream.


Serve with the apple sponge pudding.

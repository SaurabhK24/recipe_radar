

Marzipan and peach filo crown



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 x 100g/3½oz/12-inch squares of ready-made filo pastry
2 x 100g/3½oz/12-inch square slices marzipan
50g/1¾oz unsalted butter, melted
2 peach halves
icing sugar for dusting




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas6


Roll the marzipan squares to 1cm/½in thickness.


Place the two 12-inch squares of layered filo pastry onto a floured surface. Brush each pastry square with melted butter.


Place one marzipan square in the centre of each pastry square.


Place a peach half flat-side down on top of each marzipan square.


Fold in the corners, making sure each peach in the centre is visible.


Brush with the melted butter and transfer to a baking tray. Bake for six minutes.


To serve, place on a plate with a dusting of icing sugar.

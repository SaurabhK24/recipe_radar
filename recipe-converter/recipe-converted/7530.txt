

Peppered, griddled goats' cheese



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

100g/3½oz goats' cheese, with rind
1 tsp olive oil
freshly ground black pepper

To serve

mixed herb salad (coriander, basil, flatleaf parsley)
olive oil, to dress salad




Method


Heat a griddle pan and drizzle with the oil.


Grind fresh black pepper generously all over the goats' cheese and place onto the griddle for 3-4 minutes, turning once.


Place on the herb salad and dress with olive oil.



Poached plums with chocolate mousse



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Gino D'Acampo


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the poached plums

2 plums, halved and stones removed
1 lemon, juice only
100ml/3½oz red wine

For the chocolate mousse

150ml/5fl oz double cream, whipped until stiff
50g/1¾oz dark chocolate, grated




Method


To make the poached plums, place all of the ingredients into a small saucepan over a medium heat and bring to the boil. Reduce the heat and simmer the plums for ten minutes.


Drain the plums and place in the freezer for three minutes to cool quickly.


To make the chocolate mousse, mix together the cream and grated chocolate in a bowl.


Place a 7cm/3in chefs' ring in the centre of a plate and fill the ring with the chocolate and cream mixture. Heat the sides of the chefs' ring with a mini-blowtorch and carefully remove.


To serve, place the cooled plums around the chocolate cream mousse.

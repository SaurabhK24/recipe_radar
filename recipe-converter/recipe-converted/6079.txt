

Mango fool



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Martin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

½ mango, puréed
150ml/5fl oz double cream, whipped
2 tbsp icing sugar
squeeze of lime juice
sprig of fresh mint




Method


Mix all the ingredients together.


Serve in a long glass.

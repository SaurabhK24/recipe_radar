

Ginger punch



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

4 x 2.5xm/1in cubes pineapple (fresh or canned)
2cm/1in piece fresh ginger, peeled.
2 tsp pineapple juice
1 tbsp falernum syrup (spiced syrup, available from specialist suppliers)
35ml/1¼fl oz Jamaican rum
2 tsp lime juice
2 tsp vanilla syrup (available from specialist suppliers)
2 fresh pineapple leaves, to garnish




Method


Place the pineapple into a cocktail shaker and mash (muddle) with the end of a rolling pin.


Add the ginger and mash well again.


Add the pineapple juice, falernum syrup, rum, lime juice, vanilla syrup and a handful of ice. Shake well and strain into a glass.


Garnish with two fresh pineapple leaves and serve.



Chocolate fondue with kebabs



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For fondue

25g/1oz dark chocolate
25g/1oz butter
25ml/1fl oz brandy
50g/1¾oz cocoa powder
120ml/4 &frac14fl oz; double cream
3 tbsp honey

For the kebabs

2 pears, cut into cubes
100g/3½oz raspberries




Method


For the fondue, in a bain-marie over a low heat melt the chocolate and butter together. Add the brandy.


Stir through the cream, honey and cocoa powder.


Place into a serving dish.


For the kebabs, thread the pears and raspberries onto the skewers, alternating one another.


Place the kebabs around the bowl of chocolate fondue and serve.

A variation on the classic whisky mac, this cocktail is a great one for kicking off your Burns night supper.






By Wayne Collins


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

dash whisky bitters
1 strip orange zest
25ml/1fl oz Scotch whisky
25ml/1fl oz ginger liqueur
lemon zest, to garnish




Method


Place all the ingredients into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and stir to mix.


Strain into a whiskey tumbler over ice, garnish with lemon zest and serve.

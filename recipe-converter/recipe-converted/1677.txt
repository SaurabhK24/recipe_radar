

Carrot, horseradish and cheese salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 carrot, grated
2 tbsp grated, fresh horseradish
55g/2oz spinach, chopped
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp wholegrain mustard
1 tbsp white wine vinegar
sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
25g/1oz cheddar cheese, grated




Method


In a bowl, mix together the carrot, horseradish and spinach.


In a new bowl mix together the oil, mustard and vinegar and season, to taste, with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Pour the dressing over the salad mixture and toss well to coat.


Sprinkle over the cheese and serve.



Cashew nut pesto



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Tony Tobin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 garlic cloves
1 large handful fresh basil
1 large handful flatleaf parsley
1 tbsp breadcrumbs
2 tbsp olive oil
salt
1 handful cashew nuts
toasted ciabatta, to serve




Method


Place all of the ingredients, except the ciabatta, into a food processor and blend until smooth.


Serve spread thickly onto the toasted ciabatta.

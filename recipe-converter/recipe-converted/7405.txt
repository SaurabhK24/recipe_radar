

Peach soup with a mascarpone and chocolate chip quenelle



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Paul Rankin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients
For the peach soup

1 peach, peeled, stoned and chopped
55g/2oz caster sugar
2 limes, juice only
1 vanilla pod
splash water

For the quenelle<strong>

55g/2oz mascarpone
2 tbsp double cream
25g/1oz dark chocolate, chopped
½ peach, finely chopped




Method


For the soup, place the peach pieces, sugar, lime juice, vanilla pod and water into a small saucepan and bring to the boil.


Cook over a gentle heat for two minutes then remove the vanilla pod (you can save the pod for another occation) and blend until smooth with a hand-held blender. Allow to cool.


For the quenelle, mix the mascarpone, cream and chopped chocolate together.


To serve, pour the peach soup into a bowl. Using two spoons make a quenelle of the mascarpone mix and place on top of the soup. Scatter over the chopped peach.

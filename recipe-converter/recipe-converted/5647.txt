

Leek and lamb koftas



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Makes 3







By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

250g/8¾oz lamb mince
½ leek, chopped
handful breadcrumbs
½ tsp paprika
½ tsp chilli flakes
½ tsp ginger
1 free-range egg yolk
1 tbsp oil
herb salad, to serve




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Mix together all of the ingredients thoroughly and mould around three skewers.


Heat the oil in a frying pan and cook the lamb skewers for 1-2 minutes to seal on all sides.


Place into the oven for ten minutes, until golden and cooked through.


Serve with a herb salad.

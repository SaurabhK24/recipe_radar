

Harvey wallbanger



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian








By Andy Pearson


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

50ml/2fl oz vodka
orange juice, to top up
1 tbsp Galliano, or similar anise and herb-flavoured liqueur
1 orange slice, to garnish
1 cherry, to garnish




Method


Pour the vodka into a highball glass and top up with orange juice.


Carefully add the Galliano to the top of the drink to create a separate layer.


Serve garnished with an orange slice and a cherry.

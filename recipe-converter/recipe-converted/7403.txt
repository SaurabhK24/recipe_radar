

Peach soufflé



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

3 free-range egg whites
200g/7oz/½tin tinned peaches
1 tsp sugar




Method


Heat the oven to 250C/500F/Gas 9.


Whisk the egg whites to stiff peak stage.


Blend the peaches in a food processor and add the sugar.


Fold the peach coulis into the egg whites and bake in the oven for ten minutes or until risen.



Macadamia nut brittle



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
less than 10 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

150g/5½oz caster sugar
150g/5½oz macadamia nuts, toasted




Method


Heat the sugar in a saucepan for 4-5 minutes, until melted and turning golden-brown.


Add the macadamia nuts, stir well and take the pan off the heat.


Pour the mixture onto a non-stick baking sheet and leave to cool completely.


To serve, break the brittle up into pieces.



Caipirosca



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Andy Pearson


From Something for the Weekend






Ingredients

60ml/2¼fl oz vodka
1½ limes, cut into quarters
1 tbsp granulated sugar
handful ice
1 lime wedge, to garnish




Method


Place the vodka, limes (except the garnish) and sugar into a highball glass and carefully 'muddle' or pound with the end of a clean rolling pin.


Add a handful of ice, garnish with a lime wedge and serve.

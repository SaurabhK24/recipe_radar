

Teriyaki chicken on a bed of griddled spring onions



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 2


Recommended by
2 people







By Mike Robinson


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

6 chicken fillets
1 tbsp vegetable oil
1 tsp sesame oil
2 tsp clear honey
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 lime, juice only
splash of water
6 spring onions
2 tsp sesame seeds




Method


Heat the vegetable oil in a medium frying pan.


Fry the chicken fillets for 2-3 minutes.


Stir in Â½ tsp sesame oil, the honey and soy and cook until the chicken caramelises.


Place a griddle pan on the heat.


Griddle the spring onions on both sides until well charred.


Add the lime juice and water to the chicken. Remove the pan from the heat.


Drizzle the remaining sesame oil over the spring onions, then sprinkle over the sesame seeds.


Serve the teriyaki chicken on a bed of griddled spring onions.

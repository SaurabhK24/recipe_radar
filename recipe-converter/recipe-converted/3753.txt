

Espresso prunes



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
30 mins to 1 hour


Serves
Serves 4


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Jill Dupleix


From Saturday Kitchen






Ingredients

2 tbsp ground espresso coffee
300g/10½oz sugar
2 tbsp brandy
450g/1lb large prunes, dried and pitted




Method


Dissolve the coffee in 125ml/4fl oz boiling water, then strain.


Add the sugar, brandy and 500ml/17fl oz water and bring to the boil, stirring.


Add the prunes, bring to the boil and cook at quite a fast simmer for 30-45 minutes until the liquid is thick and syrupy. Serve.

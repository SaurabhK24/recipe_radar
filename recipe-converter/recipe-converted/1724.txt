

Celeriac and Stilton gratin



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Aldo Zilli


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil
25g/1oz butter
½ celeriac, peeled and grated
100ml/3½fl oz double cream
55g/2oz Stilton cheese, crumbled




Method


Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.


Heat the olive oil and butter together in a pan and gently fry the celeriac for 4-5 minutes, or until beginning to brown. Stir in the cream and cheese, then transfer to an ovenproof gratin dish. Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes, or until golden-brown on top and bubbling.

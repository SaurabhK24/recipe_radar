

Chicken and pineapple salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Serves
Serves 1







By Tony Tobin


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

2 slices pineapple from a can, chopped
½ lime, juice only
1 tbsp olive oil
¼ red chilli, finely chopped
½ ready-cooked chicken breast, chopped
handful of mixed fresh herbs e.g. mint, basil, coriander and parsley




Method


Place all of the ingredients into a bowl, stri well and serve.

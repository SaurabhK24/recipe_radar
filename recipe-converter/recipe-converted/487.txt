

Tomato and spinach soup



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Brian Turner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

dash olive oil
¼ onion, finely chopped
75ml/2½fl oz tomato passata (sieved tomatoes)
300ml/½ pint hot vegetable stock
125g/4½oz fresh spinach leaves
salt and freshly ground black pepper




Method


Heat the olive oil in a saucepan, add the onion and fry until softened.


Add the passata, stock, spinach, salt and freshly ground black pepper and simmer for 3-4 minutes. Blend with a hand blender until smooth.


To serve, pour into a serving bowl.



Mince pie ice cream



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required


Dietary

Â 
Vegetarian




Mix leftover mince pies with vanilla ice cream for a delightfully simple dessert.






By Gregg Wallace


From Eat Well for Less?






Ingredients

leftover mince pies
vanilla ice cream




Method


Remove the ice cream from the freezer and leave to soften.


Break the mince pies up into small pieces and mix into the ice cream. Return the ice cream to the freezer.

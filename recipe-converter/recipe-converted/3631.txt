

Duck salad



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
no cooking required






By John Barrowman


From A Taste of My Life






Ingredients

4 carrots, cut into thin strips
1 bunch spring onions, cut into thin strips
Â½ cucumber, seeds removed, cut into thin strips
1 crisp lettuce, such as cos or iceberg, sliced
2 pre-cooked duck breasts, shredded into small slices
hoisin sauce, from a jar, to taste
handful sesame seeds




Method


Combine all the prepared vegetables in a salad bowl.


In a separate bowl, toss the cooked duck pieces in the hoisin sauce.


Place the hoisin duck on top of the salad and sprinkle with sesame seeds. Serve at once.

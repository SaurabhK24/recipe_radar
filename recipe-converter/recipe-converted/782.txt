

Vanilla rice pudding



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Serves 1-2


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By James Tanner


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

300ml/10fl oz whole milk
100ml/3½fl oz double cream
2 tbsp caster sugar
1 vanilla pod, split lengthways
150g/5½oz jasmine rice
1 slice orange, to serve




Method


Place the milk, cream, sugar and vanilla pod in a saucepan and bring to simmering point. Add the rice and simmer gently for ten minutes, or until the rice is tender.


Serve in a bowl, garnished with a twisted slice of orange.

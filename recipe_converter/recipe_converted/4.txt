Title: Sweet plums in red wine
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 10 mins
Serves: 2
Author: Ross Burden

Ingredients:
150g butter
4 plums
100ml red wine
2 tbsp honey
2 cardamon pods

Instructions:
Melt the butter in a small frying pan over a medium heat.
Add the remaining ingredients and cook over a high heat for six minutes, stirring frequently.
Serve in warm bowls.

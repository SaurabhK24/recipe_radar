Italian-style trifle

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the chocolate cream

  • ½ miniature bottle of whisky
  • 100ml/3½oz double cream, whipped
  • 100g/3½oz mascarpone
  • 50g/1½oz dark chocolate, melted

For the amaretti cream

  • ½ miniature bottle of whisky
  • 100ml/3½oz double cream, whipped
  • 100g/3½oz mascarpone
  • 100g/3½oz crushed amaretti biscuits

For the biscuit topping

  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder, for dusting
  • 7 whole amaretti biscuits, to garnish

Method

  1. For the chocolate cream, place all of the ingredients into a bowl and mix well.

  2. For the amaretti cream, place all of the ingredients into another bowl and mix well.

  3. Spoon half of the amaretti cream into a tall glass, then place half of the chocolate cream on top. Repeat the layers with the remaining cream.

  4. To serve, dust with cocoa powder and top with the amaretti biscuits.

Fennel and feta linguine

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Recommended by

42 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

A light fresh dish, full of sweet fried flavour - makes a great weeknight dinner.

By Nigel Slater
From Nigel Slater's Dish of the Day

Ingredients

  • 200g/7oz linguine
  • 1 fennel bulb
  • 1 banana shallot
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 15 basil leaves
  • 100g/3½oz feta cheese

Method

  1. Cook the pasta in a pan of boiling, salted water, according to packet instructions.

  2. Slice the fennel and shallot, and fry in the oil until soft.

  3. Tear the basil over the fennel and shallot and crumble in the feta.

  4. When the pasta is cooked, drain and combine with the shallot and fennel.

Cheesy celeriac mash

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Ross Burden
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 200g/7oz celeriac, peeled and chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • pinch of nutmeg
  • 2 tbsp double cream
  • 55g/2oz brie
  • fresh chervil, to garnish

Method

  1. Bring a large pan of water the boil. Add the celeriac and boil for ten minutes, or until tender.

  2. Add the nutmeg, salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste, and mash the celeriac to a rough paste.

  3. Add the cream and brie, stirring to mix well.

  4. Place into a warm bowl and garnish with the chervil.

Chocolate pudding with raspberry coulis and pear

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz mascarpone
  • 50g/1¾oz chocolate, melted
  • 50g/1¾oz biscuits, crushed

For the coulis

  • 100g/3½oz raspberries
  • 25g/1oz icing sugar
  • 1 lemon, juice only

For the pear

  • 1 pear, sliced
  • 25g/1oz butter, melted
  • 2 tbsp runny honey

Method

  1. For the chocolate pudding, in a bowl mix together the mascarpone with the melted chocolate. Fold through the biscuits.

  2. Place the chocolate in a chef's ring on a plate and place into the fridge for ten minutes to set.

  3. For the coulis, blend together the raspberries, icing sugar and lemon juice.

  4. For the pear, in a frying pan add the pear, butter and honey. Cook until golden.

  5. To serve, remove the chef's ring using a blow-torch. Place the pears around the pudding and drizzle over the coulis.

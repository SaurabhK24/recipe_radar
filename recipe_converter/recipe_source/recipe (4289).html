Haricot, herb and ricotta dip

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 3

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Simplicity itself. This fresh-tasting ricotta dip is a great vegetarian starter along with slices of carrot and breadsticks for dipping.

By Nigel Slater
From Nigel and Adam's Farm Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 400g/14oz can haricot beans
  • 20g/¾oz fresh coriander, chopped
  • 15g/½oz mint, chopped
  • 200g/7oz ricotta
  • 3 tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. Lightly crush the beans with a potato masher in a large mixing bowl.

  2. Fold the beans together with the chopped herbs and the ricotta, without over mixing.

  3. Trickle over the olive oil and serve.

Eggy bread

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 3 tbsp condensed milk
  • handful blueberries
  • 2 slices brioche
  • knob of butter

Method

  1. Place the eggs in a bowl with the condensed milk and whisk together.

  2. Place a brioche slice onto a plate and place the blueberries on the top, put the second brioche slice on the top to make a sandwich. Push down on the sandwich to slightly squash the blueberries.

  3. Carefully pick up the brioche sandwich and place it into the egg mixture, turning it to completely coat.

  4. Meanwhile, heat a frying pan with butter on a medium to high heat, and when bubbling, place the brioche sandwich into the frying pan. Fry for two minutes on each side or until golden.

  5. Remove the brioche from the frying pan and serve immediately.

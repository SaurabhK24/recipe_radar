Mac daddy

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

A variation on the classic whisky mac, this cocktail is a great one for kicking off your Burns night supper.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • dash whisky bitters
  • 1 strip orange zest
  • 25ml/1fl oz Scotch whisky
  • 25ml/1fl oz ginger liqueur
  • lemon zest, to garnish

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and stir to mix.

  2. Strain into a whiskey tumbler over ice, garnish with lemon zest and serve.

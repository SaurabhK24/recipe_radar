Crostini with black olive tapenade and creamy garlic cheese

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white crusty bread
  • olive oil, for drizzling
  • 55g/2oz soft white cheese with herbs, plus extra for garnish
  • 150g/5½oz black olives, stones removed
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Preheat the grill to medium.

  2. Brush the bread with a little olive oil, then toast in a hot frying pan for two minutes on both sides, or until golden-brown and crisp.

  3. Spread the cheese over each slice of toasted bread, then place under the grill for 1-2 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and golden-brown.

  4. Place the olives, garlic, freshly ground black pepper and a little olive oil into a mini food processor and pulse to a coarse paste. Slowly add a little more olive oil, continuing to blend, until the mixture comes together to form a smooth paste.

  5. To serve, spread a little of the olive tapenade over each slice of toast and dot with a bit more cheese. Serve immediately.

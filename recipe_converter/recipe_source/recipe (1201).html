Bruschetta with tomatoes and rosemary

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Ainsley Harriott
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 150g/5½oz cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp rosemary
  • 2 tbsp thyme
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 2 large slices of ciabatta bread, toasted

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Place the tomatoes on a baking sheet, drizzle with oil and sprinkle with rosemary, thyme and garlic.

  3. Place into the preheated oven for 10 minutes.

  4. Serve the hot tomatoes on the toasted bread and enjoy.

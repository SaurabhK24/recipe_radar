Chicken wrapped in bacon with red pepper chutney

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the chicken

  • 4 rashers smoked streaky bacon
  • 1 chicken breast, cut into 4 strips
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the red pepper chutney

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ red pepper, chopped
  • 2 tsp red wine vinegar
  • 2 tsp caster sugar
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • dash Tabasco sauce
  • small handful fresh basil and mint, chopped

Method

  1. For the chicken, tightly wrap a bacon rasher around each chicken strip. Heat the oil in a frying pan and fry the wrapped chicken strips for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until golden-brown and completely cooked through.

  2. For the chutney, heat the oil in a small saucepan over a medium heat and fry the peppers for 2-3 minutes. Add the vinegar, sugar, ketchup and Tabasco sauce and simmer for 6-8 minutes. Stir in the herbs.

  3. To serve, place the bacon-wrapped chicken strips onto a serving plate with the chutney in a small bowl alongside.

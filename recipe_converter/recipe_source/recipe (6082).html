Citrus onion, herb and toasted cashew nut salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 100g/3½oz cashew nuts
  • 3 spring onions, chopped
  • large handful each fresh chervil, coriander and mint
  • ½ lime, zest and juice

Method

  1. Heat the oils in a wok and gently toast the cashew nuts for 2-3 minutes. Add the spring onions and fry for two minutes.

  2. To serve, mix the herbs together, pile onto a serving plate, top with the cashew nuts and spring onions and squeeze over the lime juice and zest.

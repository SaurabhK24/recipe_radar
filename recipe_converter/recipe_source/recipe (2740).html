Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 6-8

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 150g/5oz butter
  • 150g/5oz caster sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 225g/8oz self raising flour
  • 55g/2oz stem ginger, finely chopped
  • 1 orange, zest and juice
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 175g/6oz fresh cranberries
  • 55g/2oz dried cranberries

Method

  1. Heat the oven to 190C/375F/Gas 5.

  2. Line a 23cm/9inch tin with baking parchment.

  3. Cream together the butter and the sugar and beat in the eggs.

  4. Fold in the flour and the ground ginger.

  5. Stir in the chopped stem ginger, the orange zest and juice, and the fresh and dried cranberries.

  6. Turn into the tin and bake for 35-45 minutes until an inserted skewer comes out clean.

  7. Serve hot or cold with custard or vanilla ice cream (or make in advance and reheat).

New potatoes with beans and olives

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 200g/7oz cooked new potatoes, quartered
  • 100g/3½oz green beans, trimmed
  • 75g/2¾ oz black olives, pits removed, chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Heat the oil and butter in a frying pan and fry the potato quarters until they turn golden-brown.

  2. Add the green beans to the pan and cook for 4-5 minutes, until the beans are tender.

  3. Stir in the olives and salt and freshly ground black pepper. Serve as a side dish.

Classic tarte tatin

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

3 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Garrey Dawson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 3 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1 apple, peeled and sliced
  • 85g/3oz ready-rolled puff pastry
  • double cream, to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Heat the butter in a small ovenproof frying pan, add the sugar and cook until the sugar has melted and caramelised. Add the apple slices and stir to coat them in the caramel.

  3. Cut a circle of puff pastry to fit over the frying pan. Place the pastry circle on top of the apples and push down around the edges to seal in the apples.

  4. Transfer to the oven and bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the pastry is golden-brown and risen. Remove from the oven and allow to cool slightly.

  5. To serve, carefully turn the tarte tatin out onto a plate and pour over the cream.

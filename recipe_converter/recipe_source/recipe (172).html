Apple, pork and cheddar burger with chips

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the chips

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 150g/5&frac12oz potato (about 1 large potato), peeled and cut lengthways into thin chips

For the burger

  • 2 sausages, skins removed
  • 30g/1oz cheddar, crumbled
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp wholegrain mustard
  • 3 sage leaves, finely chopped
  • ¼ apple, grated
  • ¼ tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

To serve

  • 30g/1oz cheddar, sliced
  • small bunch chives, chopped

Method

  1. For the chips, heat the oil in a non-stick frying pan and fry the chips for 15 minutes or until golden and cooked through.

  2. For the burger, combine the sausage meat, crumbled cheddar, onion, mustard, sage leaves, apple and Worcestershire sauce and mix well. Shape into a patty.

  3. Heat the oil in a non-stick frying pan and fry for 4-5 minutes on each side or until cooked through.

  4. To serve, top the burger with the sliced cheddar. Serve on a plate with the chips and garnish with the chives.

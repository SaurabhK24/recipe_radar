Fresh asparagus, tomato and pine nut salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 5 cherry tomatoes, quartered
  • ½ red chilli, seeds removed, finely chopped
  • 1½ tbsp pine nuts
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 bunch asparagus, blanched, to serve

Method

  1. Heat the butter in a pan and sauté the tomatoes and chilli for 2-3 minutes.

  2. Add the pine nuts and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. To serve, place the asparagus onto a plate and pour over the tomato sauce.

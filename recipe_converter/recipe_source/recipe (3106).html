Deep-fried stuffing balls

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 300ml/10½fl oz vegetable oil for deep-frying
  • 150g/5½oz stuffing
  • 50g/1¾oz cooked turkey, chopped
  • 2 handfuls fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce

Method

  1. Heat a heavy bottomed saucepan, a third full of vegetable oil, until a breadcrumb sizzles in it. (CAUTION: hot oil can be dangerous! Do not leave unattended.)

  2. In a bowl mix the stuffing with the cooked turkey and roll out into six small balls.

  3. Add the stuffing balls to the hot vegetable oil and cook for four minutes, until golden. Remove from the oil with a slotted spoon and drain on kitchen towels.

  4. To serve, place the chopped parsley on a plate, top with the stuffing balls and sprinkle over the soy sauce.

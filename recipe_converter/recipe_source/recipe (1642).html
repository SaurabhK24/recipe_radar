Cheese and asparagus soufflé

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1-2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 75ml/2½fl oz double cream
  • 3 asparagus spears, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh chives
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 100g/3½oz Double Gloucester cheese, grated
  • 3 free-range eggs, separated
  • butter, for greasing
  • flour, for dusting

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Place the double cream into a small saucepan and bring to the boil. Add the asparagus spears and chives, season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and cook for 1-2 minutes. Add the grated cheese and stir until melted. Remove from the heat and leave to cool slightly.

  3. Add two of the egg yolks to the mixture and beat well (the third egg yolk can be used in another dish).

  4. Place the egg whites into a large, clean bowl and whisk until stiff peaks form when the whisk is removed. Gently fold the egg whites into the cheese mixture.

  5. Grease a small ovenproof pan with butter, dust with plain flour and shake out the excess. Spoon the soufflé mixture into the pan, then transfer to the oven and bake for 6-8 minutes, or until risen and golden-brown.

  6. Serve immediately in the pan or turned out onto a plate.

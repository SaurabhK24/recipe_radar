Spiced hot coconut drink

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 6

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Hemsley + Hemsley’s low-sugar take on a bedtime drink is an indulgent night-time treat.

This meal provides 130 kcal, 2g protein, 5g carbohydrate (of which 2g sugars), 11g fat (of which 10g saturates), 0.2g fibre and 0.2g salt per portion.

By Hemsley + Hemsley

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp carob (available from health food stores)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp almond extract
  • 1-2 tsp date syrup (available from delicatessens or online specialists) or maple syrup
  • 1-2 tsp maca powder (optional; available from health food stores)
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 400g tin full-fat coconut milk

Method

  1. Blend all of the ingredients and 300ml/10½fl oz water together in a food processor until super-smooth and well combined.

  2. Transfer the mixture to a pan and warm over a gentle heat.

  3. Serve immediately in mugs.

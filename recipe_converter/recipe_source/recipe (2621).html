Corn on the cob with lime and cayenne butter

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • ½ corn on the cob
  • 85g/3oz butter
  • ½ tsp cayenne pepper
  • ½ lime, zest only
  • 1 tbsp coriander, finely chopped

Method

  1. Place the corn on the cob into a saucepan of boiling water and boil for 8-10 minutes or until the sweetcorn is cooked and tender. Remove the corn on the cob from pan and keep warm.

  2. In another saucepan, heat the butter, cayenne pepper, lemon zest and coriander until melted together.

  3. To serve, immediately pour the sauce over the hot corn on the cob.

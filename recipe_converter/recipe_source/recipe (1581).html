Chargrilled steak and sweet garlic celeriac chips with roast button mushrooms and red wine jus

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Garrey Dawson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the steak

  • 125g/4oz rump steak
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the celeriac chips

  • ½ celeriac, peeled and cut into chips
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 garlic cloves, crushed

For the roast button mushrooms

  • 15g/½oz butter
  • 5 chestnut mushrooms

For the jus

  • 100ml/3½fl oz red wine

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. For the steak, brush the meat with the oil and season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Heat a chargrill pan until smoking, then griddle the steak for 2-3 minutes on both sides (for rare), or until cooked to your liking. Remove from the pan and allow to rest on a warm plate.

  3. For the celeriac chips, place the celeriac slices on a baking sheet and drizzle over the olive oil. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper, scatter over the garlic cloves and roast in the oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the celeriac slices are golden-brown and crisp.

  4. For the mushrooms, melt the butter in an ovenproof frying pan and fry the mushrooms, whole, for 2-3 minutes, or until lightly golden. Transfer to the oven to roast for 8-10 minutes, or until golden-brown all over.

  5. For the jus, pour the wine into the steak pan to deglaze and reduce over a high heat for 3-4 minutes, or until the liquid has reduced by half.

  6. Serve the steak with the celeriac chips and mushrooms on the side and spoon the jus around the edges of the plate.

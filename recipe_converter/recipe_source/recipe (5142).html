Lemon cheese fondue

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Ross Burden
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 140ml/5fl oz double cream
  • 200g/7oz Port Salut cheese
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • splash white wine
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • char-grilled bread, to serve

Method

  1. Place all of the ingredients into a heatproof bowl placed over a saucepan of simmering water or double boiler to gently melt.

  2. Serve with char-grilled bread.

Chocolate-stuffed prunes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Ross Burden
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 10 dried prunes, stones removed and incisions made lengthways
  • 50g/1¾oz dark chocolate, cut into 10 equal pieces

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Stuff the 10 prunes with the 10 pieces of chocolate. Place onto a baking sheet and place into the oven to bake for six minutes, until warmed through.

  3. Remove from the oven and serve on a warm plate.

Deep-fried goats' cheese with rocket salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 250ml/9fl oz vegetable oil
  • 100g/3½oz goats' cheese, cut into rounds
  • 100g/3½oz plain flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 100g/3½oz breadcrumbs mixed with 2 tsp fresh thyme leaves, chopped
  • 2 handfuls of rocket

Method

  1. Preheat the oil in a deep pan for deep-frying (CAUTION: Hot oil can be dangerous, do not leave unattended).

  2. Dip the goats' cheese in the flour, then the egg and then in the thyme breadcrumbs. Place in the fridge to chill for ten minutes.

  3. Meanwhile, deep fry the rocket for 30 seconds and drain on kitchen towel.

  4. Deep-fry the crumbed goats' cheese for two minutes or until golden. Drain on kitchen towel.

  5. Serve the goats' cheese with the rocket scattered around the plate.

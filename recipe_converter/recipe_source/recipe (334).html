Baby potatoes with fennel

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Manju Malhi
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 310g/11oz baby salad potatoes
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • ¼ tsp fennel seeds (or ½ tsp sugar (if preferred)
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • pinch of garam masala
  • ¼ tsp chilli powder

Method

  1. Par-boil the potatoes for 7-8 minutes, then drain them.

  2. Leave them to cool.

  3. In a frying pan heat the oil, add the spices and mix for 1 minute.

  4. Tip in the potatoes and sauté for 3-4 minutes.

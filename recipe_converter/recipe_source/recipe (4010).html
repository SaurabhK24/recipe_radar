Greek salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz feta, chopped
  • 75g/2½oz black olives, pits removed
  • 3 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp white wine vinegar
  • 2 tsp red wine vinegar
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Mix all the ingredients together and serve.

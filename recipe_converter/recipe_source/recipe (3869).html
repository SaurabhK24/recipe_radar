Glazed oranges with vanilla yoghurt

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Ainsley Harriott
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 oranges, peeled and sliced
  • 1 pear, peeled and sliced
  • pinch of cinnamon
  • 25ml/1fl oz brandy
  • 100g/3½oz Greek yoghurt
  • 1 vanilla pod, seeds scraped out

Method

  1. Melt the butter with the brown sugar in a saucepan and cook for one minute until golden.

  2. Add the sliced oranges and pears and the ground cinnamon and stir well to coat.

  3. Add the brandy and carefully allow to flame. Cook for two minutes.

  4. In a bowl, mix the Greek yoghurt and the vanilla seeds together.

  5. To serve, place the caramelised oranges and pears on a serving plate and spoon over the vanilla yoghurt.

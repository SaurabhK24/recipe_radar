Classic Daiquiri

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Andy Pearson
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 50ml/2fl oz white rum
  • 35ml/1½fl oz fresh lime juice
  • 4 tsp sugar syrup (often sold as 'gomme' syrup)
  • 1 wedge lime

Method

  1. Place the rum, lime juice and sugar syrup into a cocktail shaker and shake well to combine.

  2. Pour into a Martini glass and serve garnished with a wedge of lime.

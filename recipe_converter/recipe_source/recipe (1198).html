Bruschetta with fresh cherry tomatoes and basil

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 small ciabatta loaf, split lengthways
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 150g/5½oz cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 large sprig basil, leaves only
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Drizzle the cut side of the ciabatta bread with one tablespoon of the olive oil and toast in a large, non-stick frying pan until golden-brown.

  2. Mix the remaining tablespoon of olive oil, cherry tomatoes, basil and salt and pepper in a small bowl and leave aside to infuse.

  3. To serve, divide the tomato mixture between the two pieces of toasted ciabatta bread.

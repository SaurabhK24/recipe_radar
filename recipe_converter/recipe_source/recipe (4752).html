Ginger cake bread and butter pudding

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • butter, for greasing
  • 1 slice ready-made ginger cake
  • 4-6 tbsp ready-made custard
  • icing sugar, to dust
  • fresh mint sprig, to garnish

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6. Lightly grease a ramekin.

  2. Slice the cake into smaller pieces and place into the base of the ramekin.

  3. Pour over enough custard to fill the ramekin.

  4. Place the ramekin into the oven and bake for 6-8 minutes, or until warmed through.

  5. To serve, dust the pudding with icing sugar and garnish with a sprig of mint.

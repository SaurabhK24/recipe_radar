Lollipop chicken with honey and sesame seeds

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz chicken breast, cut into strips
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds, toasted
  • 1 small handful fresh parsley leaves

Method

  1. Soak two wooden skewers in a bowl of cold water for ten minutes.

  2. Thread the chicken onto the skewers and drizzle over half of the olive oil.

  3. Heat a griddle pan until smoking and cook the chicken skewers for 4-5 minutes on both sides, or until golden-brown and cooked through. Drizzle over the honey and sprinkle over the sesame seeds.

  4. To serve, place the chicken skewers onto a serving plate. Toss the parsley leaves with the remaining olive oil and drizzle on the plate next to the chicken skewers.

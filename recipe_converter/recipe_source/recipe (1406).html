Caramelised pear sponge pudding with whipped cream

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the sponge

  • 50g/1¾oz unsalted butter
  • 50g/1¾oz self-raising flour
  • 50g/1¾oz caster sugar
  • 1 tbsp ground mixed spice
  • 2 free-range eggs

For the carameisedl pear

  • 25g/1oz unsalted butter
  • 25g/1oz caster sugar
  • ½ pear, cored and chopped
  • 1 tsp ground mixed spice
  • icing sugar, to dust
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. For the sponge, mix all the sponge ingredients together in a food processor to produce a thick smooth batter.

  3. For the caramelised pear, melt the butter with the sugar in an ovenproof pan over a medium heat until golden-brown.

  4. Add the pear and mixed spice to the caramel and mix together.

  5. Pour the batter over the caramelized pear and place in the oven. Bake for 10 minutes, or until the sponge is risen and cooked through.

  6. To serve, turn the pudding out onto a serving plate, dust with icing sugar and drizzle cream all over.

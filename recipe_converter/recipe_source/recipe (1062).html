Braised rice with mushrooms

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 150g/5¼oz rice
  • ½ pint/290ml chicken or vegetable stock
  • 75g/2½ oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 75g/2½ oz Romero pepper, sliced
  • handful fresh mixed herbs, chopped
  • 30g/1¼oz butter

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.

  2. Bring the rice and chicken stock to the boil in a large ovenproof pan. Place in the oven for ten minutes.

  3. Add in the mushrooms and peppers. Cover with a lid and return to the oven for three minutes.

  4. Remove the rice from the oven and stir in the herbs and butter.

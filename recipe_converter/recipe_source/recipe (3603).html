French martini

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

3 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Mix together vodka, raspberry liqueur and pineapple juice for this delicious cocktail.

By Andy Pearson
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 50ml/2fl oz vodka
  • 15ml/½fl oz raspberry liqueur
  • 35ml/1½fl oz fresh pineapple juice

Method

  1. Place the vodka, raspberry liqueur, pineapple juice into a cocktail shaker with some ice and shake vigorously.

  2. Strain the mixture into a Martini glass and serve.

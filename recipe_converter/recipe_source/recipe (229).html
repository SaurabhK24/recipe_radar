Art of Bourbon

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Try a classic bourbon cocktail recipe. Hailing from Berlin, this tipple has zingy flavor with lime and fresh apple juice.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • Ingredients:
  • 35ml/1½fl oz bourbon
  • 15ml/1 tbsp créme de pêche
  • 10ml/½fl oz fresh lime juice
  • 25ml/1fl oz fresh apple juice

To garnish

  • apple slice
  • mint sprig

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients in a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.

  2. Strain the cocktail into a coupette glass and garnish with an apple slice and mint sprig.

Baked figs with Stilton

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 figs, cut open
  • 55g/2oz Stilton cheese
  • balsamic vinegar
  • olive oil
  • mixed salad leaves, to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Stuff each fig with a wedge of Stilton and press halves back together.

  3. Place on a baking tray and drizzle with balsamic vinegar and olive oil. Place into the oven for eight to ten minutes, until hot and bubbling.

  4. Remove from the oven and serve with a mixed salad.

Coconut tart with passion fruit cream

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 6-8

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

For the tart

  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 2 lemons, juice and zest
  • 200g/7oz caster sugar
  • 375ml/13fl oz double cream
  • 300g/11oz desiccated coconut
  • 25cm/10in sweetened shortcrust pastry tart shell, blind baked (you can use ready-made)

For the passion fruit cream

  • 250g/9oz mascarpone
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream
  • 2 tbsp icing sugar
  • 3 passion fruit, sliced in half and pulp removed

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 160C/320F/Gas 2.

  2. For the tart, mix the eggs, lemon zest and caster sugar in a clean bowl throughly, for a minute.

  3. Gently mix in the cream, then the lemon juice and finally the desiccated coconut.

  4. Pour into the pastry shell and bake until golden all over. This should take about 40 minutes.

  5. Remove from the oven and leave for one hour to cool and firm up.

  6. For the cream, let the mascarpone warm to room temperature before putting it into a bowl with the double cream and icing sugar.

  7. Whip until it begins to thicken, then add the passion fruit pulp and beat until firm.

  8. To serve, simply cut the coconut tart into wedges and place on a serving plate with a spoonful of the passion fruit cream.

Indulgent chocolate truffle with raspberry sauce

Preparation time

over 2 hours

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2-3

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

For the truffle

  • 125g/4½oz dark chocolate
  • 125g/4½oz milk chocolate
  • 250g/8¾oz whipping cream
  • 1 tsp brandy
  • ¼ tsp instant coffee

For the bubble wrap garnish

  • 25g/1oz dark chocolate
  • 25g/1oz white chocolate
  • 1 piece of clean bubble wrap

For the raspberry sauce

  • 1 punnet of raspberries
  • 2 tsp icing sugar

Method

  1. To make the truffle, place chocolate in a bowl over a pan of simmering hot water until it slowly melts. Alternatively, place in a plastic bowl and melt in the microwave for 4-5 minutes on the defrost setting.

  2. Half whip the cream in a large bowl, then warm the brandy in a pan, add the coffee and mix until smooth.

  3. Finally fold together the chocolate, coffee mix and cream and spoon into ramekins.

  4. Chill for at least 2-3 hours.

  5. To make the chocolate 'bubble wrap', melt the dark and white chocolate separately then fold together slightly to create a marbled effect.

  6. With a pallete knife, spread the marbled chocolate over a sheet of clean, plastic bubble wrap and set in the fridge for an hour.

  7. After chilling, gently peel the chocolate of the bubble wrap and break into chunks (discard the plastic). You will be left with chocolate that looks like bubble wrap.

  8. For the raspberry sauce, blend the raspberries and icing sugar in a liquidiser, pass through a sieve and put into a sauce bottle.

  9. Zigzag raspberry sauce on a serving plate, centre the chocolate on the plate and top with chocolate bubble wrap.

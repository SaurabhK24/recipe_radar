Green beans with garlic and almonds

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4-6

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Jane Sen
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 450g/1lb French green or bobby beans
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, sliced thinly
  • 100g/4oz slivered or whole almonds
  • 2 tbsp tamari soy sauce

Method

  1. Drop the beans into boiling water and boil for 10 minutes. Drain and pile into a warm serving dish.

  2. Gently heat the oil with the garlic until it is just turning golden, quickly toss in the almonds and the tamari, stir and brown a few minutes longer. Tip over the green beans. Serve immediately.

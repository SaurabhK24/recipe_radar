Cherry cheesecake

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 200ml/7fl oz double cream, whipped
  • 140g/5oz quark cheese (or other soft cheese)
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • 2 slices ready-made Madeira cake
  • 2 tbsp white wine
  • 100g/3½oz tinned cherries, stones removed
  • mint leaf, to garnish

Method

  1. Place the cream, quark and caster sugar into a bowl and mix together well.

  2. Using a deep chefs' ring, cut out two rounds from the Madeira cake slices. Press one round of the Madeira cake into the base of the chefs' ring on a plate and drizzle with the white wine.

  3. Add half the cream and quark mixture into the chefs' ring, then add a layer of cherries. Then press the other Madeira round into the ring and top with another layer of the cream and quark mixture, finishing with the cherries on top.

  4. Carefully remove the chefs' ring and garnish with a mint leaf.

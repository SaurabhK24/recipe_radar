Carrot and herb salad with Parma ham

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 150g/5½oz carrot, finely sliced
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh chives
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 50g/2oz Parma ham
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Place the carrots, herbs and oil into a bowl. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper and mix well.

  2. To serve, place the Parma ham flat onto a plate and arrange the carrot and herb salad on top.

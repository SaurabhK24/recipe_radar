Mincemeat palmiers

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 350g/12oz ready-made, ready-rolled puff pastry
  • 1 jar mincemeat
  • 75g/2½oz dried cranberries
  • 1 apple, peeled and diced
  • oil, to grease

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 230C/450F/Gas 8.

  2. Spread the mincemeat over the ready-made, ready-rolled pastry sheet and sprinkle with the cranberries and apple.

  3. Roll up the sheet from both ends, so that they meet in the middle.

  4. Slice into 1cm/½in thick pieces and place onto an oiled baking sheet. Place in the oven for 10-15 minutes, or until cooked and golden.

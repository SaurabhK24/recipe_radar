Ginger fizz

You need JavaScript to view this clip.

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 1 large jug

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Cocktails don't need to be sickly sweet or wildly strong. This cranberry, apple and ginger combination is exceptionally Christmassy.

By Nigel Slater
From Nigel Slater's 12 Tastes of Christmas

Ingredients

  • 200g/7oz ice
  • 400g/14oz frozen cranberries, plus extra for decoration
  • 2-4 knobs of preserved stem ginger (depending on how gingery you like it)
  • 3 tbsp syrup from the stem ginger jar
  • 500ml/18fl oz cider

Method

  1. Blend all ingredients except for the cider in a blender.

  2. Pour in the cider and blend again.

  3. Serve in a jug with a few extra frozen cranberries for decoration.

Harvey wallbanger

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Andy Pearson
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 50ml/2fl oz vodka
  • orange juice, to top up
  • 1 tbsp Galliano, or similar anise and herb-flavoured liqueur
  • 1 orange slice, to garnish
  • 1 cherry, to garnish

Method

  1. Pour the vodka into a highball glass and top up with orange juice.

  2. Carefully add the Galliano to the top of the drink to create a separate layer.

  3. Serve garnished with an orange slice and a cherry.

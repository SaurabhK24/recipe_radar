Thai noodles

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Mike Robinson
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 250g packet medium egg noodles
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp peeled and finely grated galangal
  • 2 bird's-eye red chillies, chopped
  • 2 tsp chopped fresh holy basil
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh coriander
  • 1 lime, juice only
  • 1 tsp clear honey
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds

Method

  1. Cook the noodle according to the packet instructions.

  2. In a large bowl mix together the remaining ingredients.

  3. Drain and toss the noodles in the Thai mixture.

  4. Serve.

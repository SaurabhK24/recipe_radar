Sweet and sour stir fry with honey squid and parsley oil

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Ross Burden
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • ½ romero pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • ½ tsp chilli, chopped
  • 1 squid tube, cleaned and chopped
  • 2 tbsp honey

For the parsley oil

  • 2 handfuls fresh parsley leaves
  • 55ml/2fl oz olive oil
  • 1 lime, juice only

Method

  1. Heat a wok to a high temperature.

  2. Add the oil and when smoking add the romero pepper, vinegar, tomato ketchup, brown sugar and chilli. Stir fry for five minutes.

  3. Add the squid and honey and cook for a further two minutes.

  4. For the parsley oil, place the parsley, olive oil and lime juice in a small food processor and blend until smooth.

  5. To serve, place the squid stir-fry on a plate and drizzle over the parsley oil.

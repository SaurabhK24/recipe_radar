Oriental-style salmon and noodle broth

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 200ml/7fl oz hot vegetable stock
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 x 50g/1½oz pieces salmon fillet, skinned
  • 50g/1¾oz rice noodles
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds, to garnish
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh chives, to garnish

Method

  1. Place the olive and sesame oil, stock and soy sauce into a saucepan and bring to the boil.

  2. Reduce the heat to a simmer. Add the salmon pieces to the pan and poach for 3-4 minutes, or until almost cooked through.

  3. Add the noodles to the pan and remove from the heat. Cover with a lid and leave for a few minutes, until the noodles have softened.

  4. To serve, pour the broth into a bowl and sprinkle over the sesame seeds and chives.

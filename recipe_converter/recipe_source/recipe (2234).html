Chocolate-dipped strawberries

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Makes 8

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 85g/3oz dark chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 8 strawberries

Method

  1. Place the chocolate pieces in a heatproof bowl over a pan of simmering water. Do not let the base of the pan touch the water. Stir occasionally until the chocolate has melted.

  2. Lay the strawberries on a piece of silicone or greaseproof paper. Drizzle over the melted chocolate. Allow to set, remove from the paper and serve.

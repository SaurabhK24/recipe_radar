Fruit spring rolls

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • ¼ Bramley apple, finely chopped
  • 50g/2 oz dates, finely chopped
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • 4 sheets ready-made filo pastry
  • 1 free-range egg, beaten
  • 500ml/18fl oz vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp honey, to drizzle

Method

  1. Place the apple, date and lemon juice into a bowl and mix together.

  2. On a clean surface lay two sheets of filo pastry out alongside each other on a clean, flat surface. Brush with the egg and lay another sheet over each.

  3. Spoon a little of the fruit mixture into the middle of each sheet at one end. Roll up as you would a spring roll, whilst brushing with the egg so it sticks.

  4. Heat the oil in a deep saucepan until a breadcrumb sizzles in it (CAUTION: Hot oil can be dangerous do not leave unattended) and add the parcels and deep-fry until golden, then carefully remove with a slotted spoon and drain on kitchen paper.

  5. To serve, place the fruit roll onto a plate and drizzle with honey.

Breakfast rosti

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 small potato, peeled
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 15g/½oz butter
  • 2 free-range eggs, fried
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh chives, to garnish

Method

  1. Grate the potato into a clean tea towel over a bowl and squeeze out the excess liquid. Season the grated potato with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  2. Heat the oil in a small frying pan. Place the grated potato into the pan, press down and fry for 3-4 minutes. Push half of the butter down the edges of the rösti to crisp the edges.

  3. Carefully turn the rösti over using a fish slice and cook for a further 2-3 minutes, or until beginning to turn golden-brown. Push the remaining butter down the edges of the rösti and cook for a further minute.

  4. To serve, place the rösti onto a plate, top with two fried eggs and sprinkle over the chives.

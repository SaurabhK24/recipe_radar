Oriental-style omelette with shiitake mushrooms

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 3 free-range eggs
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 100g/3½oz shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 choy sum leaves, roughly chopped

Method

  1. Lightly whisk the eggs and soy sauce together in a bowl.

  2. Heat the olive oil in a non-stick frying pan and sauté the mushrooms until tender.

  3. Stir in the choy sum leaves and cook for one minute, until just wilted.

  4. Pour in the egg mixture and cook gently until the base sets, then turn over and cook for a further minute. Serve immediately.

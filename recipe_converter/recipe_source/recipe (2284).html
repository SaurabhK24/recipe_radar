Chocolate soufflé with mango sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the chocolate soufflé

  • 3 free-range eggs, separated
  • 50g/1¾oz caster sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 200g/7oz plain chocolate, melted
  • butter, for greasing

For the mango sauce

  • 100g/3½oz mango, diced
  • 1 tbsp icing sugar

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6. Place a baking sheet in the oven to heat.

  2. To make the soufflé place the egg whites into a bowl and whisk until stiff.

  3. Gradually add the caster sugar and continue to whisk until glossy stiff peaks are formed when the whisk is removed.

  4. Place the cocoa powder and egg yolks into a bowl with the melted chocolate and mix together well.

  5. Add a little of the egg white mixture to the chocolate mixture and mix together well.

  6. Add the rest of the soufflé mixture to the chocolate mixture and fold in, taking care to maintain volume.

  7. Grease a deep oven proof dish with the butter. Spoon in the meringue mix and place on the hot tray in the oven to bake for ten minutes, or until risen and cooked through.

  8. To make the mango sauce, place the mango and icing sugar into a food processor and blend to a smooth purée.

  9. Serve the soufflé fresh from the oven in its dish with the mango sauce in a bowl alongside.

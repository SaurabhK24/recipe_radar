Cherry negroni

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 25ml/1fl oz cherry brandy
  • 25ml/1fl oz gin
  • 25ml/1fl oz sweet vermouth
  • 25ml/1fl oz campari

To garnish

  • orange slice

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients into a tumbler glass with a handful of ice and stir until well combined.

  2. Garnish with a slice of orange and serve.

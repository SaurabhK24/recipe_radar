Lamb and apple koftas

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz lamb steak, trimmed
  • 1 tsp ground mixed spice
  • pinch chilli powder
  • 2 tbsp grated apple
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 30g/1oz butter

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Place the lamb, spices and apple into a food processor and blend to combine. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Using your hands, shape the mixture into two balls.

  4. Heat the butter in an ovenproof frying pan and cook the koftas, turning frequently, until browned all over.

  5. Transfer to the oven and bake for 6-8 minutes, or until the koftas are completely cooked through.

  6. To serve, thread the koftas onto a skewer.

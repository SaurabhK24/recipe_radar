Mascarpone grapes and wine

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100ml/3½fl oz white wine
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar
  • 110g/4oz grapes
  • 85g/3oz mascarpone
  • 1 tbsp lemon curd
  • ½ lemon, zest only
  • sprig of fresh mint, to garnish

Method

  1. Heat the wine, lemon juice and sugar in a small frying pan over a high heat. When boiling, reduce to simmer for 3-4 minutes to thicken.

  2. Add the grapes and simmer for three minutes. Remove from heat and pour into a shallow bowl.

  3. In a clean bowl, beat the mascarpone, until soft, then fold through the lemon curd and zest.

  4. To serve, top the grape mixture with a dollop of the cream and garnish with mint.

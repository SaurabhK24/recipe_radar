Lemon syllabub

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100ml/3½fl oz double cream, lightly whipped
  • ½ lemon, zest only
  • 1 tbsp white wine
  • 2 tbsp icing sugar
  • squeeze fresh lemon
  • 1 sprig fresh mint, to garnish

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients, except the mint into a large bowl and whisk until the mixture forms soft peaks, when the whisk is removed.

  2. To serve, place in a glass bowl and garnish with a sprig of fresh mint.

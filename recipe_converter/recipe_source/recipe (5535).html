Marzipan-wrapped ginger cake with cinnamon fingers

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the ginger cake

  • 200g/7oz cream cheese
  • 50g/1¾oz stem ginger, finely chopped
  • 1tbsp stem ginger syrup
  • 1 vanilla pod, seeds scraped out
  • 150g/5½oz marzipan
  • 2 peach halves

For the cinnamon fingers

  • 75g/2¾oz ready-made filo pastry
  • 25g/1oz unsalted butter, melted

To garnish

  • 2 mint leaves
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon for dusting

Method

  1. To make the cinnamon fingers, preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Lay two squares of filo on a floured surface. Brush one with melted butter and sprinkle with cinnamon. Place the other square over and repeat the brush and sprinkle process.

  3. Cut into three inch by one inch fingers. Place on a non-stick baking-sheet and bake for five minutes.

  4. To make the ginger cake, mix together the cream cheese, stem ginger, syrup and vanilla seeds in a large bowl.

  5. Set a three inch chef's ring in the centre of a plate. Spoon in the mixture packing it down well. Use a palette knife to flatten the top.

  6. Release and remove the ring by gently warming the edges with a mini blow torch or sliding a knife around the inner edges.

  7. Roll the marzipan into a long thin strip. Wrap the marzipan strip around the cake. Lightly caramelise the marzipan wrap with a mini blow torch.

  8. To serve, place the cinnamon fingers on top of the cake. Garnish with the cake with 2 mint leaves and place the peach halves beside.

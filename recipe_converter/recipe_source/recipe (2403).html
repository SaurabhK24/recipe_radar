Cinnamon-roasted apple with butterscotch sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the cinnamon-roasted apple

  • 30g/1oz butter
  • ½ apple, cored
  • 1 tbsp white wine
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp icing sugar

For the butterscotch sauce

  • 100g/3½oz caster sugar
  • 55g/2oz butter
  • 4 tbsp double cream
  • 1 tbsp water

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. For the cinnamon-roasted apple, heat the butter in a small ovenproof frying pan and fry the apple cut-side down until golden-brown.

  3. Add the white wine and simmer for one minute. Sprinkle over the cinnamon and icing sugar.

  4. Transfer to the oven and bake for ten minutes, or until softened.

  5. For the butterscotch sauce, heat the caster sugar in a non-stick frying pan until it turns a deep caramel colour.

  6. Remove from the heat, add the butter, cream and water and whisk to make a thick, smooth sauce.

  7. To serve, place the apple onto a serving plate and drizzle with the sauce.

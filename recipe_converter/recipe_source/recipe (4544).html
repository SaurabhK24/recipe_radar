Hot and cold ice cream with griddled figs and bananas

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

You can make these filo parcels in advance and freeze them with the ice cream in them and then just cook them on the day you need them - very handy at this time of year when you're trying to take the hassle out of Christmas preparations

By James Martin
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, peeled and halved
  • 4 figs, halved
  • 30g/1oz butter, melted
  • 4 sheets filo pastry
  • icing sugar
  • 4 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 4 tbsp honey
  • fresh mint, to garnish

Method

  1. First, cut each sheet of filo pastry in half and overlap them on top of each other.

  2. Brush the filo pastry with the melted butter and add a scoop of vanilla ice cream in the centre.

  3. Bunch up the filo to make a parcel, dust with icing sugar and place in a preheated oven at 220C/425F/Gas 7 for 2-3 minutes.

  4. While the filo parcels are cooking, preheat a griddle pan and brush the bananas and figs with a little of the butter.

  5. Griddle the bananas and figs on both sides and when cooked, place on a plate.

  6. Remove the parcels from the oven and serve on the side of the plate. Drizzle with the honey, a further dusting of icing sugar and garnish with a sprig of mint.

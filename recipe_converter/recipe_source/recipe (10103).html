Syllabub

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 free-range egg whites
  • 150ml/5fl oz double cream
  • 25g/1oz icing sugar
  • 30ml/1fl oz brandy

Method

  1. In a clean bowl, whisk the egg whites until stiff peaks form when the whisk is removed.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the double cream and icing sugar until it forms soft peaks. Fold the whisked egg whites into the double cream mixture and spoon into a piping bag.

  3. Pour the brandy into a glass serving dish, then pipe the syllabub mix on top of the brandy and serve.

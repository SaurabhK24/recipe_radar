Forest fruit soufflé pancakes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the pancakes

  • 2 free-range eggs, beaten
  • 4 tbsp plain flour
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 2 tsp vegetable oil

For the soufflé filling

  • 2 free-range egg whites
  • 3 tbsp caster sugar
  • 75g/2½oz frozen forest fruits, defrosted
  • 50g/1¾oz digestive biscuits, crushed
  • icing sugar, to dust

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. For the pancakes, place the eggs, flour and milk into a bowl and mix together to make a batter the consistency of single cream.

  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a small frying pan and remove the excess with kitchen paper. Add one tablespoonful of the pancake batter and swirl to coat the base of the pan. Cook for 1-2 minutes on each side. Remove the pancake from the pan and repeat the process to make three more pancakes.

  4. For the soufflé filling, place the egg whites into a large, clean bowl and whisk until stiff peaks form when the whisk is removed. Gradually whisk in the sugar one tablespoonful at a time until the mixture is glossy.

  5. Gently fold the fruit and crushed biscuits into the egg white mixture.

  6. Place a spoonful of the mixture onto each of the four pancakes. Fold each pancake to make a half moon shape, then place onto a baking sheet and bake in the oven for 8-10 minutes.

  7. To serve, place the pancakes onto four plates and dust with icing sugar.

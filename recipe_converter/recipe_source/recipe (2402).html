Cinnamon puff with caramelised oranges

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz ready-rolled puff pastry
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2½ tbsp caster sugar
  • 150ml/¼pt double cream
  • squeeze orange juice
  • 2 tsp icing sugar
  • ½ vanilla pod, seeds scraped out
  • 1 orange, peeled and segmented

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Cut a 6in triangle out of the ready-rolled puff pastry. Place onto a baking sheet and sprinkle with the cinnamon and half a tablespoon of the caster sugar. Place into the oven to cook for ten minutes.

  3. Place the cream, orange juice, icing sugar and vanilla seeds into a bowl, and whisk until the mixture forms soft peaks when the whisk is removed.

  4. Place the orange segments onto a baking sheet, sprinkle with the rest of the sugar and using a mini-blow torch,caramalise the sugar for two minutes, or until golden.

  5. To serve, place the oranges onto a serving plate, place some cream on top and then the cooked cinnamon pastry.

Lime and blueberry iced smoothie

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz blueberries
  • 1 lime, juice and zest only
  • 2 vanilla pods, seeds only
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 75ml/2½fl oz dairy-free cream substitute (or single cream)
  • 3 ice cubes

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients into a blender and process until smooth. Serve in a tall glass.

Lamb with herb crust

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

By James Martin
From Housecall

Ingredients

  • 4 lamb cutlets

For the crust

  • 1 slice of bread, toasted
  • handful of fresh mixed herbs (parsley, mint, basil)
  • 80g/3oz parmesan, finely grated
  • salt and pepper to taste

Method

  1. Preheat the grill to high

  2. Tip all the crust ingredients into a processor and blitz briefly.

  3. Cook the lamb cutlets under the grill and serve scattered with the herb crust.

Crumbed lamb steak with swede mash

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

13 people

Lamb steak grilled in crunchy breadcrumbs flavoured with parmesan and mustard. Serve with buttery swede mash.

By Nigel Slater
From Nigel Slater's Dish of the Day

Ingredients

For the mash

  • ½ swede
  • 100g/3½oz butter
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the lamb

  • 1 lamb steak
  • 2 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • 30g/1oz breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 2 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
  • oil for frying

Method

  1. For the mash, peel and roughly chop the swede. Boil in water until soft when tested with the point of a knife.

  2. Preheat the grill to a high heat.

  3. Massage the lamb all over with the mustard.

  4. On a plate combine the breadcrumbs, parmesan and thyme. Take the steak and roll in the breadcrumb mix so that it is evenly covered.

  5. Grill the steak for six minutes on each side. Drain the swede, mash with the butter and season. Serve together.

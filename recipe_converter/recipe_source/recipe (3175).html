Honey and soy dressing

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 4 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tsp fresh coriander, chopped

Method

  1. Mix all the ingredients together and serve.

Easy jam ice cream

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 1 tub

Recommended by

4 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Saturday Brunch

Ingredients

  • 500ml/18fl oz double cream
  • 500ml/18fl oz plain yoghurt
  • 450g/16oz pot high quality raspberry jam or preserves
  • cones, to serve

Method

  1. Mix all the above ingredients together and transfer to a freeze-safe container.

  2. Freeze for about four hours or overnight. Remove from freezer one hour before serving.

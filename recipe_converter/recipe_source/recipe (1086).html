Brandy snap baskets with white chocolate

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 8

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Mary Berry
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 8 bought brandy snap baskets
  • 200g/8oz Continental white chocolate
  • 200ml/7floz double cream
  • 200m/7fl oz full fat créme fraîche
  • 200g/8oz fresh raspberries or strawberries and other mixed soft fruit

Method

  1. Melt white chocolate with the double cream together in a bowl over hot water (not boiling) until the chocolate just melts (do not allow to get too hot or it will become grainy and will not set firm when cold).

  2. Set aside to cool.

  3. Mix with the créme fraîche until smooth.

  4. Pour into the brandy snap cases.

  5. Top with the fruits to serve.

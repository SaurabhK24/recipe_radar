Creamy leeks with feta, griddled potato and red pepper skewers

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Maria Elia
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the leeks

  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 1 leek, thinly sliced lengthways
  • 85g/3oz feta cheese, crumbled
  • 200ml/7fl oz double cream
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the skewers

  • 6 new potatoes, cooked and drained
  • ½ red pepper, cut into chunks
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. For the leeks, melt the butter in a frying pan and gently fry the leeks for 4-5 minutes, or until softened. Stir in the cheese and cream and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Simmer for a further 4-5 minutes, or until the cheese is melted.

  2. For the skewers, soak two wooden skewers in cold water for ten minutes. Drain.

  3. Thread the potatoes onto the skewers, alternating with the chunks of red pepper and rosemary. Brush with the oil and griddle in a hot chargrill pan for 4-5 minutes, turning occasionally, until the peppers are tender. Serve on top of the creamy leeks.

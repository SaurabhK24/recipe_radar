Hot sweet and sour skewered strawberries served with sweet mascarpone

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 8 strawberries, hulled
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1 vanilla pod, seeds scraped out
  • 100g/3½oz mascarpone
  • 2 tbsp icing sugar
  • sprig fresh mint

Method

  1. Thread two strawberries onto a bamboo skewer. Repeat with three more skewers to create four skewers with two strawberries each.

  2. Heat the olive oil, vinegar, caster sugar and half of the vanilla pod seeds in a non-stick frying pan. Once the sugar has dissolved, bring to the boil, then reduce the heat to simmer for two minutes or until thickened to a syrup.

  3. Add the strawberry skewers and cook for one minute, turning occasionally to coat the strawberries in syrup. Remove the strawberry skewers from the pan and set aside.

  4. Place the remaining vanilla seeds, the mascarpone and the icing sugar into a clean bowl and stir well to combine.

  5. To serve, arrange the skewers onto a plate, drizzle over any remaining syrup from the pan and serve with a spoonful of the mascarpone.

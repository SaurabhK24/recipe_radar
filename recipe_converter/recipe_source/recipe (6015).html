No-olives Greek salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Aldo Zilli
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz feta, cut into 1cm/½in cubes
  • 10 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • small handful rocket
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ garlic clove, peeled, crushed
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Put the feta and cherry tomatoes into a bowl.

  2. Make a dressing by mixing the olive oil with the garlic in another bowl.

  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and serve at once.

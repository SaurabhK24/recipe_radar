Mince pie ice cream

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Mix leftover mince pies with vanilla ice cream for a delightfully simple dessert.

By Gregg Wallace
From Eat Well for Less?

Ingredients

  • leftover mince pies
  • vanilla ice cream

Method

  1. Remove the ice cream from the freezer and leave to soften.

  2. Break the mince pies up into small pieces and mix into the ice cream. Return the ice cream to the freezer.

Green beans with ginger and mustard

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Manju Malhi
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp mustard seeds
  • 200g/7oz green beans, trimmed and par-boiled
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp peeled and grated root ginger
  • ¼ tsp salt

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a saucepan. Add the mustard seeds.

  2. A few seconds later tip in the beans with the cumin and fry for 1 minute.

  3. Stir in the ginger and salt and sauté over a low heat for 5 minutes.

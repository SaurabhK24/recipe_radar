Crispy duck and cashew noodle stir-fry

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • dash olive oil
  • 100g/3½oz duck breast, cut into strips
  • ¼ onion, sliced
  • ½ fresh red chilli, finely sliced
  • 2 tbsp chopped cashews
  • 1 orange, zest and juice only
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice only
  • 1 lime, zest and juice only
  • splash soy sauce
  • 150g/5½oz fresh egg noodles
  • 2 tbsp edamame beans

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a wok, add the duck strips and fry over a high heat for 5-6 minutes.

  2. Add the onion and cook for 2-3 minutes. Add the rest of the ingredients, stir well and cook for a further 2-3 minutes.

  3. To serve, pile the noodles onto a serving plate.

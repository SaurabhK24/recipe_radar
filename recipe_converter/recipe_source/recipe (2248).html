Chocolate fudge sponge pudding with hot chocolate sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

4 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the chocolate fudge sponge pudding

  • 100g/3½oz butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 100g/3½oz caster sugar
  • 2 free-range eggs, beaten
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 100g/3½oz self-raising flour
  • 55g/2oz ready-made fudge, broken into pieces
  • 55g/2oz dark chocolate, broken into pieces

For the hot chocolate sauce

  • 85g/3oz dark chocolate
  • 150ml/5fl oz double cream

Method

  1. Cream the butter and sugar together in a mixing bowl until pale and fluffy.

  2. Gradually mix in the beaten egg, then the milk and finally the flour.

  3. Grease a microwaveable bowl with butter. Place the fudge and chocolate into the bowl. Spoon the sponge mixture on top and microwave for about 4-5 minutes on full power.

  4. Meanwhile, for the chocolate sauce, heat the chocolate and cream together in a pan over a low heat, stirring continuously until combined.

  5. Turn the sponge pudding out onto a plate and serve drizzled with the hot chocolate sauce.

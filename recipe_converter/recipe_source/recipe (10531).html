Ginger trifle

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Alex Mackay
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 3 slices ready-made ginger cake
  • 150ml/¼ pint ready-made custard
  • 150ml/¼ pint double cream, whipped until soft peaks form when the whisk is removed
  • 3 tbsp raisins
  • 3 tbsp pecans, roughly chopped, plus extra, to serve

Method

  1. Cut the cake into pieces and place in the base of a small glass bowl. Pour over the custard.

  2. Place the cream into a separate large bowl and fold in the raisins and pecans.

  3. Spread the cream mixture on top of the custard.

  4. To serve, sprinkle over the remaining pecans and place servings of the trifle into bowls.

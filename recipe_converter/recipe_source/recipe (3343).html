Eve's pudding

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This traditional baked pudding dates back to the early nineteenth century. Serve it with cream or custard.

By Michelle Wardingley
From Hairy Bikers' Meals on Wheels

Ingredients

For the filling

  • 450g/1lb cooking apples, peeled, cored and sliced
  • 15g/1/2oz butter
  • 85g/3oz sugar
  • ½ lemon, juice and zest

For the topping

  • 115g/4oz flour
  • pinch salt
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 55g/2oz butter, softened
  • 55g/2oz sugar
  • 1 free-range egg
  • 1 tbsp milk

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. For the filling, place the apples, butter, sugar and lemon juice and zest in a saucepan with a little water and cook gently until the apples have softened.

  3. Put the apple mixture in the bottom of a pie dish, packing the apples down to provide a firm base for the topping (this helps it rise evenly during baking).

  4. For the topping, sieve the flour together with a pinch of salt and the baking powder. In a separate bowl, cream together the butter and sugar, beat in the egg and then lightly fold in the flour. Add a little milk if the mixture is too stiff.

  5. Pour the mixture on top of the apples and bake in the oven for 45-55 minutes or until the top is golden-brown and cooked through.

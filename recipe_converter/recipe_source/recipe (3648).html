Fresh spaghetti with tomato and basil sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the spaghetti

  • 100g/3½oz plain flour
  • 1 free-range egg
  • pinch salt

For the sauce

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 200g/7oz fresh chopped tomatoes
  • handful fresh basil, chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. To make the spaghetti, mix the flour with the egg and salt in a blender until combined.

  2. Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead for one minute.

  3. Wrap the dough in cling film and rest in the fridge for five minutes.

  4. Roll the dough through a pasta machine according to the manufacturer's instructions until very thin.

  5. Roll the dough through the spaghetti setting on the pasta machine and set the fresh spaghetti aside until you are ready to cook it.

  6. To make the sauce, heat the oil in a frying pan. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened, but not browned.

  7. Add the tomatoes and cook until broken down.

  8. Cook the pasta in a large pan of salted boiling water for 30 seconds, before draining well.

  9. Stir the basil through the tomato sauce and season to taste with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  10. Serve the pasta in a serving bowl with the sauce spooned over.

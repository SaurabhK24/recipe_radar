Knickerbocker glory

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the knickerbocker glory

  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 25g/1oz brown sugar
  • 100g/3½oz greengages
  • ½oz tsp cinnamon
  • ½oz vanilla pod
  • 3 scoops of vanilla ice cream

To garnish

  • 25g/1oz almonds, toasted
  • 25g/1oz chocolate, grated
  • 25g/1oz oats, toasted

Method

  1. In a medium saucepan on a high heat melt the butter and sugar and add the greengages, cinnamon and the vanilla pod.

  2. Simmer for four minutes on a low heat.

  3. In a knickerbocker glory glass add some of the stewed greengages, then add a scoop of vanilla ice cream. Repeat this process until the glass is full.

  4. To finish sprinkle the nuts, the chocolate and the oats on top.

Caramelised apple with fried egg

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 15g/½ oz butter
  • 1 small apple, cored and sliced
  • 1 free-range egg

Method

  1. Heat the butter in a non-stick frying pan. Add the apple slices and fry on both sides until golden and caramelised.

  2. Transfer to a serving plate.

  3. In the same pan, fry the egg, adding extra butter if needed.

  4. Serve the fried egg on top of the apple slices.

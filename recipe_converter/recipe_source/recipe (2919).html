Croissant 'bread and butter pudding'

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 200ml/7fl oz double cream
  • 100ml/3½oz whole milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ orange, zest only
  • 1 free-range egg
  • 3 free-range egg yolks
  • 50g/1½oz caster sugar
  • butter, for greasing
  • 1 ready-made croissant, cut into slices
  • 1 tbsp sultanas
  • icing sugar, to dust

Method

  1. Place the cream, milk, vanilla extract and orange zest into a saucepan and bring to a simmer.

  2. Place the egg, egg yolks and sugar into a large bowl and whisk together.

  3. Gradually pour the cream mixture onto the eggs, whisking continuously to make a smooth custard.

  4. Lightly grease a microwave-proof dish and place the croissant slices in the base of the dish. Sprinkle over the sultanas and pour over the custard mixture.

  5. Place in the microwave and cook on full power for four minutes, or until set.

  6. To serve, dust the pudding with icing sugar and caramelise with a mini-blowtorch. Place servings of the pudding into two bowls.

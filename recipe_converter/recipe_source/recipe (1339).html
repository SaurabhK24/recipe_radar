Tomato, cucumber and herb salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tomato, roughly chopped
  • 2 spring onions, finely chopped
  • ½ cucumber, roughly chopped
  • small handful fresh basil, torn
  • small handful fresh flatleaf parsley, torn
  • 2-3 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Mix all the ingredients together in a large bowl and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Serve immediately.

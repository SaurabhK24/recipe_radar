Irish coffee

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Patrick Williams

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp Irish whiskey
  • 25g/1oz brown sugar
  • 425ml/3/4pt fresh strong black coffee
  • 90ml/3fl oz double cream

Method

  1. Divide the whisky and brown sugar between two heatproof serving glasses.

  2. Pour the hot coffee into the glasses to come about 4cm/1½in from the top.

  3. Very carefully and steadily pour the cream over the back of a spoon to float on top of the coffee. Drink at once!

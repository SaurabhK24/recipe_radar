Light Oriental-style broth

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 300ml/½ pint hot fish stock (vegetarians can substitute vegetable stock)
  • 3 stalks tenderstem broccoli
  • 2 tbsp fresh peas, or frozen peas, defrosted
  • 1 bird's-eye chilli, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 tbsp cooked basmati rice
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • handful fresh coriander, chopped, to serve

Method

  1. Place the stock into a pan and bring to the boil. Add the broccoli, peas and chilli and boil until the broccoli and peas are just tender.

  2. Add the remaining ingredients, except the coriander, and simmer gently to warm through.

  3. To serve, pour the broth into a serving bowl and sprinkle over the chopped coriander.

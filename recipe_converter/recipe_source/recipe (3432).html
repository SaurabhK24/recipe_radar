Figs wrapped with crispy bacon

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 3 figs
  • 50g/3½oz Camembert, cut into 3 pieces
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp butter
  • 3 rashers streaky bacon

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Make a criss-cross in the top of each fig, pinch the base to expand the opening at the top, and stuff each with a Camembert piece. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Heat the butter in a small ovenproof frying pan and fry the figs for one minute. Place the pan into the oven and roast for six minutes, or until the cheeseis melted and golden.

  4. In a separate pan, fry the bacon on both sides for 4-5 minutes, or until crisp. Remove the figs from the oven and wrap each one with a rasher of bacon.

  5. To serve, place the bacon-wrapped figs onto a plate.

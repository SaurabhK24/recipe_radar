Coffee and hazelnut milkshake

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream
  • 50ml/2fl oz condensed milk
  • 100ml/3½fl oz whole milk
  • 1 tbsp instant coffee
  • 2 tbsp hazelnuts, chopped
  • 50g/1¾oz chocolate, melted
  • handful ice

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients into a food processor with ½ the ice and blend until smooth.

  2. To serve, transfer the mixture into a tall glass and add the remaining ice.

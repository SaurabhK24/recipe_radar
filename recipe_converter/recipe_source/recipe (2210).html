Chocolate cakes with chilli chocolate sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 6

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the cakes

  • 25g/1oz cocoa
  • 100g/3½oz self-raising flour
  • 100g/3½oz butter
  • 100g/3½oz caster sugar
  • 2 free-range eggs

For the chilli chocolate sauce

  • 55g/2oz dark chocolate
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes

To serve

  • icing sugar
  • double cream

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Mix together the cake ingredients in a food processor and spoon even portions into a six-hole, non-stick muffin tin.

  3. Place in the oven and bake for 10 minutes.

  4. For the chocolate sauce, melt the chocolate and chilli flakes in a heat-proof bowl set over boiling water.

  5. To serve, turn out the cakes onto plates and dust with icing sugar. Pour the chocolate sauce over each muffin and drizzle with cream.

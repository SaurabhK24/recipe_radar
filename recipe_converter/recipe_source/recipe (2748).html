Cranachan

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 50g/1½oz oats , toasted
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream, whipped
  • 150g/5&frac12oz Greek yoghurt
  • 50g/1¾oz blueberries
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • mint sprig, to garnish

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients into a bowl and mix together.

  2. To serve, transfer into a serving bowl and place the mint sprig on top to decorate.

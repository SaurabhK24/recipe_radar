Taleggio and rocket pesto tart

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the tart

  • 4 x 10cm/4in squares ready-made filo pastry
  • 1 free-range egg, beaten
  • 85g/3oz Taleggio cheese, sliced
  • 1 tbsp chopped rocket

For the rocket pesto

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp pine nuts
  • 1 garlic clove, peeled
  • large handful rocket
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

To serve

  • 1 free-range egg, poached

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Place one square of filo pastry onto a non-stick baking sheet, crumple slightly and brush with beaten egg. Place another piece of pastry on top and again brush with egg. Repeat with the remaining pastry and egg.

  3. Place the baking sheet into the oven and bake the pastry for 5-6 minutes, or until golden-brown and crisp.

  4. Sprinkle the top of the pastry with the Taleggio slices and rocket and set aside.

  5. For the rocket pesto, place all the pesto ingredients into a small food processor and blend until smooth.

  6. To serve, place the tart onto a serving plate, drizzle with the rocket pesto and top with a poached egg.

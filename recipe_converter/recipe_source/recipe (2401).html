Cinnamon oranges with baked meringue

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 3 oranges, peeled and segmented
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 3 free-range egg whites
  • 100g/3½oz caster sugar
  • icing sugar, to dust
  • fresh mint sprigs, to garnish

Method

  1. Divide the oranges between two large dessert glasses and sprinkle over the cinnamon.

  2. In a large clean bowl, whisk the egg whites until stiff peaks form when the whisk is removed.

  3. Gradually add the caster sugar, whisking continuously until the mixture is thick and glossy.

  4. Pipe or spoon the meringue mixture into the glasses to cover the oranges. Use a mini-blowtorch to brown the meringue all over.

  5. To serve, dust with icing sugar and garnish with a sprig of mint.

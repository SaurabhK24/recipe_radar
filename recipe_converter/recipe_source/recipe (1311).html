Café foam

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Swap your after-dinner coffee for this potent coffee cocktail topped with a liqueur foam.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 25ml/1fl oz vodka
  • 25ml/1fl oz Galliano ristretto, or other coffee liqueur
  • 3 squirts Bols cacao white foam

Method

  1. Place the vodka and Galliano into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.

  2. Strain into a small cocktail glass and top with the foam.

Oriental broth with noodles

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the noodles

  • 150g/5¼oz plain flour
  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 25ml/1oz water

For the broth

  • 500ml/17fl oz chicken or vegetable stock
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 lime, juice only
  • 2 tbsp coriander, chopped

Method

  1. Mix the plain flour with the eggs and water and mix to form a smooth dough.

  2. Using a pasta machine, place the noodle dough onto the fine noodle setting. Create your noodles by feeding the pasta through slowly.

  3. To make the broth, place the chicken or vegetable stock in a deep saucepan with the soy, chilli flakes, lime juice and coriander. Simmer for five minutes then add the noodles. Cook for a further three minutes and serve in a large bowl.

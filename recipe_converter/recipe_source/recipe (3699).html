Frozen pea ice cream with bananas and mint

Preparation time

overnight

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Pop the bananas in the fridge the night before and you can have this healthy dessert ready in less than 5 minutes.

By Leila Lindholm
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 4 frozen sliced bananas
  • 1 handful fresh mint
  • 250g/9oz frozen peas
  • ½ lime, juice only
  • 2 tbsp agave syrup
  • 5 drops liquorice essence, or a pinch of liquorice powder

Method

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a food processor until smooth.

  2. Serve immediately in glasses or bowls.

Mulled cider and blackcurrant

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 2 litres/3½ pints

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By The Vegetarian Society

Ingredients

  • 2 litres/3½ pint sweet cider
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 lemon, juice only
  • 3fl oz/100ml crème de cassis
  • 1 tsp soft brown sugar
  • 1½ tsp ground cinnamon

Method

  1. Simmer the cider, cinnamon stick, lemon juice and crème de cassis for 10 minutes.

  2. When the punch is almost boiling, remove the cinnamon stick and pour mixture into strong glasses or mugs.

  3. Mix the sugar and ground cinnamon together and sprinkle on top. Serve at once.

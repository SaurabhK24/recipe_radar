Calf's liver with sweet and sour onions

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 2

Recommended by

27 people

Simon Hopkinson’s calf’s liver recipe is best served with softly creamed potatoes, or polenta.

By Simon Hopkinson
From The Good Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 2 red onions, thinly sliced
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 thin slices of calf's liver, cut into thin strips
  • 1 tbsp sherry vinegar
  • 1 tbsp crème de cassis
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Method

  1. Heat one tablespoon of the olive oil and all of the butter in a heavy-based frying pan. Fry the onions over a low heat for 30 minutes, or until soft. Season lightly with salt and freshly ground black pepper, place into a bowl and set aside.

  2. Heat the remaining olive oil in the frying pan until hot. Season the calf’s liver with salt and freshly ground black pepper and fry for 30 seconds (you should keep it pink inside). Remove and add to the onions in the bowl and mix together.

  3. Place the liver and onions back into the frying pan. Quickly heat through and add the vinegar, crème de cassis and parsley.

  4. Place the liver and onions onto hot plates and serve at once.

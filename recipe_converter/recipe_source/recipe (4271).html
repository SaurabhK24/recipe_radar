Ham and eggs with grilled tomato

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1 hungry person

Recommended by

1 person

Serve a few slices of sourdough bread with prosciutto, creamy scrambled eggs and grilled tomato for a lazy weekend breakfast or brunch.

By Paul Hollywood
From Paul Hollywood's Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 big beef tomato
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • dash olive oil
  • 3 slices prosciutto
  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 3 free-range eggs
  • 50ml/2fl oz cream
  • knob of butter

Method

  1. Preheat the griddle to hot. Slice the tomato in half, season with salt and freshly ground black pepper, drizzle with oil and place on a grill pan with the prosciutto. Grill until the tomato is softened and the prosciutto is crisp.

  2. Lightly toast the sourdough bread.

  3. Crack the eggs into a bowl. Season them with salt and pepper and beat well. Beat in the cream.

  4. Pour the mixture into a non-stick pan and cook the scrambled eggs until they reach a sloppy consistency. Remove from the heat.

  5. Pour the scrambled eggs onto the sourdough toast and place the grilled prosciutto on top. Arrange the grilled tomatoes on the side and serve.

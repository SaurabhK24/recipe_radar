Godfather

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Jim Wrigley
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 50ml/2fl oz blended scotch
  • 25ml/1fl oz amaretto
  • twist of orange peel, to garnish

Method

  1. Place a few ice cubes into a rocks glass. Add the whisky and the amaretto, stir and garnish with a twist of orange peel.

Citrus and honey microwave sponge

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon, zest peeled and flesh finely sliced
  • 1 orange, zest peeled and flesh finely sliced
  • 110g/4oz caster sugar
  • 110g/4oz self-raising flour
  • 125g/4½oz soft butter, plus extra to grease
  • 1 free-range egg
  • 2 tbsp honey, plus 1 tbsp extra

Method

  1. Place the orange and lemon zests, sugar, flour, butter, egg and honey into a food processor and blend until a smooth sponge batter is formed.

  2. Grease a small oven proof bowl with butter and brush with the extra honey. Line the bowl with the orange and lemon slices.

  3. Pour the sponge batter into the lined bowl and cover with cling film.

  4. Place the bowl in a microwave and cook on full power for five minutes, or until the sponge is cooked through.

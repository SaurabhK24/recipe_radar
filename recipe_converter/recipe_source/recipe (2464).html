Clementine and cranberry crêpes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the clementine

  • 1 clementine, skin on, cut in half
  • 1 tbsp honey

For the crêpes

  • 150g/7oz plain flour
  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 75ml/2½fl oz whole milk
  • 75ml/2½fl oz double cream
  • 25g/1oz butter

For the cranberry compote

  • 100g/3½oz cranberries
  • 1 orange, juice only
  • 50g/1¾oz brown sugar

Method

  1. For the clementine, heat a frying pan, place the clementine halves into the pan, drizzle with honey and cook over a medium heat for 2-3 minutes, or until beginning to turn golden-brown.

  2. For the crêpes, place the flour, eggs, milk and double cream into a bowl and mix to make a smooth batter.

  3. Heat half of the butter in a non-stick frying pan, place a ladleful of batter into the pan, swirl around to coat the base of the pan and cook for one minute on each side, or until golden-brown. Remove from the pan and repeat with the remaining mixture to make a second pancake.

  4. For the cranberry compote, place the cranberries, orange juice and brown sugar into a saucepan and cook over a medium heat for 4-5 minutes, until thickened.

  5. To serve, place the pancakes onto a plate with the clementines. Place the cranberry compote into the middle of each pancake and roll up like a cigar.

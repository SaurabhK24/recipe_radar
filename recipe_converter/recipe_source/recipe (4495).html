Honey-glazed chicken skewers with spicy stir-fried noodles

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the chicken skewers

  • ½ chicken breast, cut into strips
  • olive oil
  • 2 tbsp clear honey
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the noodles

  • dash olive oil
  • 2 spring onions, sliced
  • ¼ green pepper, sliced
  • ½ fresh red chilli, chopped
  • 150g/5½oz egg noodles, cooked and drained
  • 3 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • splash boiling water

Method

  1. For the chicken skewers, thread the chicken onto two skewers and drizzle with olive oil. Heat a griddle pan, add the skewers and chargrill until golden-brown on all sides and completely cooked through. Drizzle with honey, salt and freshly ground black pepper and set aside.

  2. For the noodles, heat the olive oil in a pan, add the spring onions, green pepper and chilli and fry for 2-3 minutes. Add the cooked noodles and stir in the coriander and soy sauce and a splash of boiling water.

  3. To serve, pile the noodles onto a serving plate and top with the chicken skewers.

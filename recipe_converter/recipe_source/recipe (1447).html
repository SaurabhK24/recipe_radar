Carrot and coriander soup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

5 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic
  • ½ onion finely, chopped
  • 100g/3½oz carrots, chopped
  • 25g/1oz fresh coriander, chopped
  • 200ml/7oz hot chicken stock (vegetarians may substitute vegetable stock)
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • fresh coriander leaf, to garnish

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a saucepan over a medium heat. Add the garlic and onions and fry for 2-3 minutes, until softened.

  2. Add the carrots and fresh coriander and cook for five minutes.

  3. Add the stock and simmer for five minutes.

  4. Add the cream, season to taste with salt and freshly ground black pepper and mix until smooth with a hand blender.

  5. To serve, pour into a warm bowl and garnish with the fresh coriander leaf.

Baked haddock gratin

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

3 people

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 150ml/¼ pint milk
  • ½ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 30g/1oz butter
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • ¼ head broccoli, cut into florets
  • 150g/5½oz haddock fillet
  • 55g/2oz parmesan, grated

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. In a saucepan, heat the milk, cayenne pepper and butter until the butter has melted and the mixture is well combined. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Add the broccoli and fish to the pan and simmer for five minutes, or until almost cooked through. Lift the broccoli and fish out with a slotted spoon and place into a small ovenproof dish.

  4. Spoon over 2-3 tablespoons of the milk mixture and sprinkle over the grated cheese. Discard the remaining milk mixture.

  5. Bake in the oven for five minutes, or until the cheese is melted and golden-brown. Serve in the dish.

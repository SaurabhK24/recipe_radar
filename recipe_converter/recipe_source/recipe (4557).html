Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2-4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 2 chopped, deseeded fresh red chillies
  • 1 clove crushed garlic
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 tbsp freshly chopped parsley

Method

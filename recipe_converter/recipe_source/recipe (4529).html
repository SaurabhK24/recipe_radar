Horseradish omelette

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 4 free-range eggs, beaten
  • 2 tsp chopped fresh chives
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 25g/1oz fresh grated horseradish
  • 50g/1¾oz rocket

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a small ovenproof frying pan over a medium heat. Add the eggs, stir gently and then leave to settle.

  2. Sprinkle over one teaspoon of chives, parsley and the horseradish. Cook for two minutes and then fold over one half onto itself and cook for another minute, until golden underneath.

  3. To serve, place the omelette onto a small plate, sprinkle with the remaining teaspoon of chopped chives and place the rocket on the side.

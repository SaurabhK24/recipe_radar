Warm duck and lettuce salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 50g/2oz smoked duck breast, chopped
  • 1 orange, juice only
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 small Little Gem lettuce leaves, shredded
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 50g/2oz roasted hazelnuts, chopped

Method

  1. Heat half the olive oil in a frying pan. Fry the duck until crisp and golden.

  2. Add the orange juice, balsamic vinegar and the remaining olive oil. Stir through the lettuce and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Serve on a warm plate and sprinkle over the chopped hazelnuts.

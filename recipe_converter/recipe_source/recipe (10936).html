Warm salad of potatoes, sweet red onions and oregano (insalata di patate, cipolle rosse e oregano)

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gennaro Contaldo
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 8 medium-sized potatoes
  • 2 small sweet red onions, sliced very thinly
  • 2 tbsp green olives, pitted and quartered lengthways
  • ½tbsp dried oregano
  • 120ml/8tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • salt

Method

  1. Cook the potatoes in their skins until tender. Drain and remove the skins immediately.

  2. Quarter them lengthways and place in a large bowl together with the sliced onions and green olives.

  3. Add the oregano, olive oil, vinegar and salt. Mix well and serve immediately.

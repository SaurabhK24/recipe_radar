Brandy snap baskets with strawberries

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 ready-made brandy snap baskets
  • 4 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 4 strawberries

Method

  1. Place a brandy snap basket onto each of two serving plates.

  2. Fill each basket with two scoops of ice cream.

  3. Top each basket with two strawberries and serve.

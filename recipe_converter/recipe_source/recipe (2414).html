Citrus sizzler frisée - lettuce, watercress, cucumber and orange segments

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By The Vegetarian Society

Ingredients

  • 4 frisée lettuce leaves
  • 2.5cm/1in piece of cucumber, sliced
  • 4-5 orange segments

For the dressing

  • mayonnaise (check it uses free range eggs or is egg free) or a drizzle of
  • sesame oil

Method

  1. Toss salad ingredients together and fill roll.

  2. Add dressing.

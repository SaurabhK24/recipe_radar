Mango skewers with yoghurt

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Ed Baines
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • vegetable oil for deep frying
  • 1 tortilla, cut into strips
  • ½ mango, cut into 2cm/1in dice
  • 4 tbsp honey
  • icing sugar

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/428F/Gas 7.

  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan for deep frying. (CAUTION: hot oil can be dangerous. Do not leave unattended).

  3. Deep-fry the tortilla strips for 3-4 minutes or until crisp and golden. Remove from the oil with a slotted spoon and drain on kitchen paper.

  4. Thread the mango cubes onto a skewer and sprinkle with icing sugar. Bake in the oven for ten minutes.

  5. To serve, arrange the mango skewer on top of the tortilla crisps and dust with icing sugar.

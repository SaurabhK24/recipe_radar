Ceviche of sea bass

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 sea bass fillet, skin on
  • 1 lime, zest and juice
  • ½ tsp caster sugar
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Slice the sea bass fillet thinly on the diagonal using a very sharp knife.

  2. Spread out the bass slices on a plate and sprinkle over the lime zest and juice, sugar and vegetable oil. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Set aside to marinate in a cool place for about 15 minutes, but do not leave any longer or the fish flesh will begin to break down. Serve immediately.

Cheesy chips

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1-2

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • large potato, cut into chunky chips and parboiled
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 5 tbsp olive oil

For the cheesey sauce

  • 100ml/3½fl oz white wine
  • sprig fresh thyme
  • 142ml/5fl oz double cream
  • 55g/2oz cheddar cheese, grated

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.

  2. For the chips, place the parboiled chips onto a baking tray, season to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper and drizzle with the oil.

  3. Place into the oven and roast for 15 minutes, or until cooked thoroughly and golden.

  4. For the sauce, pour the wine into a saucepan on a high heat and boil to reduce by half. Add the thyme, cream and cheese and simmer, stirring continously, until the cheese has melted.

  5. To serve, place the chips into a serving bowl and pour over the cheese sauce.

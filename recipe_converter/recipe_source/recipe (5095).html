Lemon curd, mascarpone and almond pancakes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

A fresh take on the classic sugar and lemon, using lemon curd and sweetened mascarpone with flaked almonds for crunch.

Ingredients

  • 90g/3¼oz mascarpone
  • 1 tbsp icing sugar, to taste
  • 6 tbsp lemon curd
  • 30g/1oz toasted flaked almonds
  • 4 pancakes, to serve

Method

  1. Beat the icing sugar into the mascarpone a little at a time until smooth and sweet enough to suit your tastes.

  2. Spread the lemon curd over the pancakes. Top with a dollop of mascarpone and the flaked almonds.

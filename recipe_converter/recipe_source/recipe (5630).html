Mexican soup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By James Martin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • pinch cumin seeds
  • pinch dried chilli
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tin of cherry tomatoes
  • 200g/7oz mixed beans
  • ¼ chilli chopped
  • pinch sugar
  • squeeze lemon juice
  • splash of water
  • Greek-style yoghurt
  • handful coriander
  • ½ avocado, sliced

Method

  1. In a pestle and mortar, grind the spices.

  2. Heat the oil in a pan and cook out the spices for 1-2 minutes.

  3. Add the tomatoes, beans, chilli, sugar, lemon juice and a splash of water and simmer gently for 5-8 minutes.

  4. Pour into a bowl and top with yoghurt, coriander and avocado.

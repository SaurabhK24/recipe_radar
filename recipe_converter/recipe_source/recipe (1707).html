Cheesy salmon and spaghetti bake

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Recommended by

2 people

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 200ml/7fl oz double cream
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 110g/4oz skinless salmon fillet, chopped
  • handful fresh flatleaf parsley, chopped
  • 55g/2oz parmesan cheese, grated plus a handful extra to sprinkle
  • 200g/7oz spaghetti, cooked according to packet instructions
  • fresh chives, chopped, to garnish

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.

  2. Heat the cream in a saucepan and season with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Once the cream has boiled, add the salmon, parsley and parmesan, simmer for two minutes.

  4. Add the cooked spaghetti, stir well and pour into a medium-sized gratin dish.

  5. Sprinkle with the extra Parmesan and bake for five to ten minutes, until the cheese is bubbling and golden.

  6. Serve garnished with chives.

Creamy shiitake broth with griddled sesame beef

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the creamy shiitake broth

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ onion, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • ½ Scotch Bonnet chilli, finely sliced
  • 115g/4oz shiitake mushrooms, thickly sliced
  • 100ml/3½fl oz white wine
  • 100ml/3½fl oz double cream

For the griddled sesame beef

  • 150g/5½oz fillet steak, sliced into three pieces
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ lime, juice only

Method

  1. For the creamy shiitake broth, heat the oil in a frying pan and gently fry the onion and garlic for 2-3 minutes.

  2. Add the sliced chilli and mushrooms and continue frying for a further 4-5 minutes. Add the wine and cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Simmer for 3-4 minutes.

  3. For the griddled sesame beef, rub the sesame oil into the beef and season well with freshly ground black pepper. Heat a griddle pan until hot, place the beef onto the griddle and sear on both sides, until cooked to your liking. Squeeze over the lime juice towards the end of cooking.

  4. To serve, ladle the broth into a shallow soup bowl and arrange the griddled beef on top.

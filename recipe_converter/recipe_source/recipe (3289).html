Egg Nog

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 4 free-range eggs, separated
  • 55g/2oz caster sugar
  • generous splash of whiskey, or to taste
  • 225ml/8fl oz milk
  • 100ml/3½fl oz double cream
  • nutmeg, to garnish

Method

  1. Place the egg whites into a large, clean bowl and whisk until stiff peaks form when the whisk is removed.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, sugar, whiskey, milk and cream, until pale and slightly thickened. Transfer to a heavy-bottomed pan and cook over a very low heat, stirring occasionally with a wooden spoon, until the mixture is thick and creamy.

  3. Remove from the heat and fold in half of the egg whites until well combined. Fold in the remaining egg whites.

  4. Divide the mixture among four heatproof glasses. Grate a little nutmeg on top of each and serve at once.

Gin and cranberry sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • ¼ clementine
  • 2 tsp cranberry jelly
  • 1 shot sloe gin
  • champagne
  • sprig redcurrants, dipped in sugar syrup (sold as 'gomme' syrup), then dipped in sugar

Method

  1. Squeeze the juice of the clementine quarter into a cocktail shaker, then add the quarter.

  2. Add the cranberry jelly and the sloe gin, top up with ice cubes and shake well.

  3. Pour the champagne into a flute glass until about two-thirds full.

  4. Strain the clementine, cranberry and sloe gin mixture into the flute glass.

  5. Garnish with the sprig of sugared redcurrants.

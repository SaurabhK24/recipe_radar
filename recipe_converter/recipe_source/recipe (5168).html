Preparation time

1-2 hours

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1 400g/14¼oz jar lemon curd
  • 4 pre bought meringue nests
  • 600g/1lb 5¼oz low-fat fromage frais
  • 150g/5¼oz fresh raspberries

Method

  1. Mix the lemon curd and fromage frais in a large bowl and crumble into it the meringue nests.

  2. Give a good mix and place in individual timbales or large ramekin dishes.

  3. Place in the freezer for an hour.

  4. Take out five minutes before serving so they just start to melt at the sides.

  5. Empty onto a place a garnish with fresh raspberries.

Title: Japanese-style carpaccio
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 1
Author: Ross Burden

Ingredients:
100g/3½oz sirloin steak
1 lemon, segmented
1 tsp sesame seeds
handful fresh coriander, chopped
1 tbsp soy sauce
½ leek, cut into thin strips and deep fried, to garnish

Instructions:
Slice the steak into really thin strips.
Lay on a plate and top with the lemon segments, sesame seeds, coriander, soy sauce and deep-fried leeks.
Serve raw.

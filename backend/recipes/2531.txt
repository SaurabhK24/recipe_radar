Title: Spring onion chilli prawns
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Paul Rankin

Ingredients:
20g/¾oz butter
2 spring onions, sliced
1 clove garlic, crushed
½ red chilli, seeds removed and finely chopped
4 king prawns,cooked and peeled, tails left on
small handful coriander leaves ½ lime, juice only

Instructions:
Melt the butter in a saucepan over a medium heat and add the spring onion, garlic and chilli and cook for one minute.
Add prawns and heat through.
Transfer the prawns, spring onion, garlic and chilli onto a serving dish, scatter over the coriander leaves and drizzle with the lime juice.

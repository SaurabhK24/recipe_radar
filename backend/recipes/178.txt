Title: Tomato and Parma ham spaghetti
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Paul Rankin

Ingredients:
100g/3Â½oz fresh spaghetti
2 tomatoes, blanched, peeled and chopped
3 slices Parma ham, roughly chopped
2 tbsp olive oil
black pepper, to taste
1 tbsp chopped fresh basil

Instructions:
Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water according to packet instructions.
Drain the spaghetti and stir in the chopped tomatoes, Parma ham, olive oil and black pepper.
To serve, spoon into a bowl and sprinkle with fresh basil.

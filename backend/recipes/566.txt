Title: Cheesy salmon and spaghetti bake
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 2
Author: Gino D'Acampo

Ingredients:
200ml/7fl oz double cream
sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
110g/4oz skinless salmon fillet, chopped
handful fresh flatleaf parsley, chopped
55g/2oz parmesan cheese, grated plus a handful extra to sprinkle
200g/7oz spaghetti, cooked according to packet instructions
fresh chives, chopped, to garnish

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.
Heat the cream in a saucepan and season with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper.
Once the cream has boiled, add the salmon, parsley and parmesan, simmer for two minutes.
Add the cooked spaghetti, stir well and pour into a medium-sized gratin dish.
Sprinkle with the extra Parmesan and bake for five to ten minutes, until the cheese is bubbling and golden.
Serve garnished with chives.

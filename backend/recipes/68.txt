Title: Bacon and mushroom salad
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 15 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Ross Burden

Ingredients:
1 mushroom
1/2 red onion
1 orange
1 slice bacon
85g Stilton
2 handfuls parsley
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
3 cloves garlic
1 slice bread

Instructions:
In a bowl mix together the mushrooms, red onion, orange zest, bacon, Stilton, parsley, olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
Heat a griddle pan and griddle the bread for two minutes on each side.
Serve the salad on top of the grilled bread, topped with the garlic.

Title: Drunken strawberries
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 5 mins
Serves: 2
Author: Lesley Waters

Ingredients:
8 strawberries
200ml sparkling wine
2 springs mint

Instructions:
Place four strawberries into two Martini glasses and top up with sparkling wine.
To serve, garnish with a sprig of mint.

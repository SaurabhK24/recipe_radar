Title: Lettuce cups with chilli beef stir-fry
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Garrey Dawson

Ingredients:
150g fillet steak
1 tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp chilli flakes
2 spring onions
salt
pepper
lettuce

Instructions:
Heat a griddle pan until hot. Drizzle the beef strips in the oil and chilli flakes and fry quickly in the hot griddle pan for 1-2 minutes. Add the spring onions, season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and cook for a further minute.
To serve, arrange the lettuce leaves on a serving plate and fill with the chilli beef stir-fry.

Title: Puff cinnamon twirls
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 4
Author: Ed Baines

Ingredients:
300g/10½oz ready-made puff pastry
1 tsp icing sugar
½ tsp cinnamon
drizzle honey

Instructions:
Heat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.
Roll out the pastry and cut into thin strips.
Lay onto a baking sheet in a twist shape. Bake in the oven for 8-10 minutes or until golden and risen.
Leave to cool, then sprinkle with icing sugar and cinnamon.
Drizzle with honey and serve.

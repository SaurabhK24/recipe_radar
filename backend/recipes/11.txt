Title: Tropical fruit smoothie
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 2 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Lesley Waters

Ingredients:
250g frozen fruit
1/2 lime
100ml Greek yoghurt
ice
85g raspberries
2 tsp honey

Instructions:
Place all of the ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth.
To serve, pour the smoothie into a tall glass.

Title: Apple and bacon chutney
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 2
Author: Ed Baines

Ingredients:
½ apple, cut into cubes
1 plum, stone removed and chopped
2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
3 tbsp white wine vinegar
½ tsp mixed spice
1 bay leaf
2 bacon rashers, fried until crisp and golden

Instructions:
Place all the ingredients, except the bacon, in a pan and cook over a low heat for 8-10 minutes, until thickened to a syrup. Place the chutney into a small bowl.
Cut up the bacon into small pieces and stir into the chutney just before serving.

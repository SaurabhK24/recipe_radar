Title: Sprouts, bacon and almonds
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 6
Author: Nigel Slater

Ingredients:
16 rashers smoked streaky bacon
40g/1Â½oz butter
1kg/2lb 4oz Brussels sprouts, sliced in half
200g/7oz almonds

Instructions:
Cut the bacon into pieces the shape of a postage stamp.
Melt the butter over a moderate heat in a large frying pan. Cook the bacon until it starts to crisp.
Add the sliced sprouts to the bacon and cook for six minutes so that the cut edge starts to brown
Add the almonds to the pan toast for a further two minutes. Serve.

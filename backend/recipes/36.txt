Title: Cheaty peach ice cream
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 5 mins
Serves: 5
Author: Hugh Fearnley-Whittingstall

Ingredients:
800g peaches
1/2 lemon
500ml double cream

Instructions:
Empty the contents of the can and the lemon juice into a liquidiser and blend until smooth.
Stir in the cream. Pour into an ice cream machine, churn and freeze.

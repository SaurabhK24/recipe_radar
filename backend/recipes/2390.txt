Title: Smoked salmon pâté
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 1
Author: Gino D'Acampo

Ingredients:
150g/5oz Greek yoghurt
squeeze of lime juice
110g/4oz smoked salmon, chopped
freshly ground black pepper
To serve

rye bread
lemon wedge
fresh dill

Instructions:
Place the yoghurt, lime juice, salmon and freshly ground black pepper in a food processor and blend until smooth. Transfer to a serving bowl.
Serve the rye bread alongside with the lemon wedges. Garnish with dill to serve.

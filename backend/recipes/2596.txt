Title: Sticky stir-fried beef
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Brian Turner

Ingredients:
1 tbsp sesame oil
2 spring onions, sliced
1 garlic clove, crushed to a paste in a mortar and pestle
3 baby sweetcorn, chopped
85g/3oz sirloin steak, sliced into strips
2 tbsp tomato ketchup
1 tbsp honey
1 tbsp sesame seeds

Instructions:
Heat the sesame oil in a pan and gently fry the spring onions and garlic for 2-3 minutes to soften, then add the sweetcorn and beef strips. Fry for a further 3-4 minutes, or until the beef is cooked to your liking.
Stir in the ketchup, honey and sesame seeds until the beef is evenly coated, then pile onto a serving plate.

Title: Roast pears wrapped in ham
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Phil Vickery

Ingredients:
1 pear, cored and cut into six
3 slices Black Forest ham
2 tbsp butter
25g/1oz walnuts, chopped
4 tbsp brandy

For the salad

1 handful rocket
2 tbsp fresh chives, chopped
2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped

Instructions:
Wrap half a slice of ham around each piece of pear.
Melt the butter in a pan and cook the wrapped pears for two minutes on each side.
Add the walnuts to the pan and heat through.
Add the brandy and carefully let it flame.
Mix the salad ingredients together.
Serve the pears on top of the salad.

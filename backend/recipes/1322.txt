Title: Lemon and orange tuna slices
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 1
Author: Brian Turner

Ingredients:
100g/3½oz good-quality tuna steak, sliced thinly
½ lemon, juice only
½ orange, juice only
salt and freshly ground black pepper
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
1 spring onion, chopped

Instructions:
Lay the tuna slices onto a serving plate.
Mix the orange juice and lemon juice together in a bowl and then pour over the tuna.
Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Drizzle with olive oil. Scatter over the sliced kalamata olives and spring onion and serve.

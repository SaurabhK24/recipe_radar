Title: Sticky sesame cod with Japanese-style broth
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Ross Burden

Ingredients:

For sticky sesame cod

100g/3½oz cod fillet
1 tsp sesame oil
2 tsp soy sauce
2 tsp honey
1 tsp sesame seeds

For the Japanese-style broth

½ pint chicken stock
1 tbsp soy sauce
½ tsp chilli flakes
50g/1¾oz peas (tinned or frozen, thawed)
3 tbsp coriander, chopped

Instructions:
In a bowl, mix the sesame oil, soy sauce, honey and cod and marinate for 15 minutes.
Place the cod into a steamer and cook for 12 minutes.
For the broth, place the chicken stock into a pan with the soy sauce and chilli flakes, and cook for five minutes to infuse the broth.
Add the peas and coriander and cook for one minute
Serve the cod on top of the broth in a large deep bowl, sprinkled with the sesame seeds.

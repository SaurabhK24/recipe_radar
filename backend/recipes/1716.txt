Title: Tuna steak with penne, lemon and rocket
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Paul Rankin

Ingredients:
125g/4½oz penne pasta, cooked according to packet instructions
1 tbsp olive oil, plus 1 tbsp
1 lemon, juice and zest
small handful rocket
55g/2oz fresh tuna steak
sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
½ tomato, chopped

Instructions:
Place the hot pasta, olive oil, lemon and rocket into a serving bowl, mixing the ingredients together.
Heat the extra oil in a frying pan on a high heat. Season the tuna, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper on each side. Place the tuna into the frying pan and fry for two minute on each side, or until cooked to your liking.
Remove the tuna from the frying pan and place on top of the pasta. Sprinkle the chopped tomato over the top to serve.

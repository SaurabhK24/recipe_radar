Title: Watercress and potato soup
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 15 mins
Serves: 1
Author: James Martin

Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1/4 onion
1 clove garlic
1/2 potato
150ml chicken stock
1/2 watercress
50ml double cream
salt
pepper

Instructions:
Heat the olive oil in a medium saucepan. Add the onion and garlic and saut√© gently until softened.
Add the remaining ingredients and cook gently for 8 minutes.
Blend with a hand blender until smooth and pour into a bowl to serve.

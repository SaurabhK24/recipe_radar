Title: Double chocolate mousse
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 5 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Gino D'Acampo

Ingredients:
100g dark chocolate
150ml double cream

Instructions:
Drizzle one tablespoon of the melted chocolate into a large dessert glass. Chill in the fridge until set.
Whisk the double cream until soft peaks form when the whisk is removed.
Fold the whipped double cream into the mascarpone.
Fold the remaining melted chocolate through the cream mixture until combined, and spoon into the chocolate drizzled glass.
Chill until required.

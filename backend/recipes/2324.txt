Title: Sesame roast pork with pan-fried courgettes
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Richard Phillips

Ingredients:

For the sesame roast pork

200g/7oz pork fillet
salt and freshly ground black pepper
1 tbsp olive oil
25g/1oz butter, melted
2 sprig fresh thyme, leaves only
2 tbsp sesame seeds, toasted

For pan-fried courgettes

1 tbsp olive oil
Â½ courgette, sliced
salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.
Rub the pork fillet with olive oil, salt and freshly ground black pepper. Place onto a baking tray and place into the oven to roast for 8-10 minutes, or until completely cooked through.
Remove the pork from the oven and carve into slices. Return to the roasting tray and add the melted butter, thyme and sesame seeds. Stir to combine with the pan juices.
For the pan-fried courgettes, heat the olive oil in a frying pan over a medium heat. Add the sliced courgettes, salt and freshly ground black pepper and pan-fry until the courgettes are golden-brown all over.
To serve, place the courgettes onto a serving plate. Top with the pork and pour over any juices from the pan.

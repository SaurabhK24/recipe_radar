Title: Spicy clams
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 2
Author: Gino D'Acampo

Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 shallot, finely chopped
½ tsp dried chilli flakes
2 tbsp tomato purée
75ml/3fl oz white wine
50ml/2fl oz hot water
250g/9oz fresh clams, washed
2 slices ciabatta, drizzled with olive oil and griddled, to serve
1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley, to garnish

Instructions:
Heat the olive oil in a medium saucepan, add the garlic and shallot and sauté gently for two minutes or until soft but not browned.
Add the chilli flakes, tomato purée, white wine and water, then stir well and bring to the boil.
Add the clams, cover with a lid and cook for three minutes, shaking the pan frequently. Check all the clams have opened and discard any that are still closed.
Pour into a bowl and serve with the griddled ciabatta on the side. Garnish with parsley.

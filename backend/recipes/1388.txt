Title: Mackerel pâté
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 1
Author: Gino D'Acampo

Ingredients:
1 can mackerel fillets, drained
30g/1oz butter
½ tsp chilli flakes
1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
½ lemon, juice only
salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:
Place all of the ingredients together in a food processor, pulse until smooth and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
To serve, transfer the mackerel mixture to a small serving bowl with toasted bread or crackers on the side.

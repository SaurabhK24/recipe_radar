Title: Tuna tartare
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 1
Author: Brian Turner

Ingredients:
110g/4oz fresh tuna steak, finely chopped
½ lemon, juice only
¼ onion, finely chopped
¼ red pepper, finely chopped
sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
1 thick slice ciabatta, toasted
olive oil, to drizzle

Instructions:
Place all of the ingredients except the ciabatta and oil into a bowl and mix together gently.
To serve, spoon the tuna mixture onto the ciabatta and drizzle with the olive oil.

Title: Stir-fried prawns and pak choi with lime, soy and coriander
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Phil Vickery

Ingredients:
1 tbsp sesame oil
1 tbsp vegetable oil
½ onion, finely sliced
1 red chilli, finely chopped
2 pak choi, finely sliced
½ lemon, juice and zest
½ orange, juice and zest
½ lime, juice and zest
2 tbsp soy sauce
3 raw king prawns, shells removed
3 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
To serve

1 lime, cut into quarters, one side of each dusted with paprika
1 small handful coriander leaves

Instructions:
Heat the sesame and vegetable oils in a wok and fry the onion and chilli for 2-3 minutes.
Add the pak choi, lemon, orange and lime juice and zest and soy sauce and cook for a further two minutes, or until the juice is starting to reduce.
Add the prawns and cook until they turn pink. Stir in the chopped coriander.
To serve, pile the stir-fry onto a serving plate and garnish with the paprika-dipped lime wedges and more coriander.

Title: Bloody Mary
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 1
Author: Sophie Dahl

Ingredients:
40ml/1½fl oz vodka
75ml/3fl oz tomato juice
dash lemon juice
½ tsp Worcestershire sauce
2-3 drops Tabasco sauce
salt and freshly ground black pepper
handful ice cubes
lime wedges, to serve

Instructions:
Pour all the ingredients, except for the lime wedges, into a cocktail shaker and shake well to mix.
Strain into a tall glass over ice cubes and garnish with lime wedges.

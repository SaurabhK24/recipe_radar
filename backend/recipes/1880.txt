Title: Spicy prawns and aubergines
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Gino D'Acampo

Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
½ onion, finely sliced
2 baby aubergines, chopped
1 tsp chilli flakes
1 tbsp tomato purée
2 king prawns, peeled, deveined and chopped
1 tsp chopped flatleaf parsley, to serve

Instructions:
Heat the oil in a frying pan and gently fry the onion until soft but not coloured.
Add the aubergine, chilli flakes and tomato purée and fry for 4-5 minutes, until the aubergine is soft.
Add the prawns and cook for a further 2-3 minutes, until completely cooked through.
To serve, sprinkle over the chopped parsley.

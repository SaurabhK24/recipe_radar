Title: Blue cheese Welsh rarebit
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Brian Turner

Ingredients:
1 free-range egg, yolk only
85g/3oz Danish blue cheese, crumbled
3 tbsp double cream
1 tsp Dijon mustard
dash Worcestershire sauce
salt and freshly ground black pepper
2 slices crusty white bread, griddled

Instructions:
Preheat the grill to medium.
Place the egg yolk, cheese, cream, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, salt and freshly ground black pepper into a mini food processor and blend until well combined. Top the griddled bread with the egg and cheese mixture and place onto a baking sheet. Grill for 3-4 minutes, or until golden-brown and bubbling on top.
Transfer to a serving plate and serve immediately.

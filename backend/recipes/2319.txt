Title: Sesame-crusted salmon with orange and soy sauce
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: James Tanner

Ingredients:
125g/4Â½oz salmon fillet, skin removed, sliced in half
1 tbsp sesame seeds
1 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander, leaves only
1 tbsp chopped fresh dill
1 orange, juice only
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 orange, peeled and segmented, to serve

Instructions:
Coat the salmon slices in the sesame seeds.
Heat the oil in a frying pan over a medium heat. Add the salmon and fry for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until cooked through and golden-brown on both sides.
Add the coriander, dill, orange juice and soy sauce and continue to cook for 2-3 minutes, until the sauce is bubbling.
To serve, place the sesame-crusted salmon pieces onto a serving plate, spoon the orange and soy sauce over the fish, and garnish with the orange segments.

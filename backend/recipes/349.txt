Title: Breaded plaice with herbed rice salad
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Tony Tobin

Ingredients:
2 plaice fillets
30g/1oz plain flour
1 egg, beaten
30g/1oz white breadcrumbs
1-2 tbsp olive oil

For the rice salad

110g/4oz basmati rice, cooked
Â½ red pepper, diced
1 orange, juice only
3 tbsp parsley, chopped
3 tbsp coriander, chopped

Instructions:
Coat the plaice fillets in flour, then in the beaten egg and finally in the breadcrumbs.
Heat the oil in a non-stick frying pan and fry the fillets for 2-3 minutes on each side, until golden brown.
For the salad, mix the rice, pepper, orange juice and fresh herbs into a large bowl.
Spoon the rice onto a serving plate and serve the breaded fillets alongside.

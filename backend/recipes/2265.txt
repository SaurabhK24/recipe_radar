Title: Seafood parcel
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Phil Vickery

Ingredients:
2 tbsp white wine
2 garlic cloves, peeled, chopped
2 shallots, peeled, chopped
1 tomato, chopped
a few fresh basil leaves, torn
1 tbsp lemon juice
6-8 mussels, cleaned, debearded (discard any that do not close tightly when tapped)

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.
Place two layers of tin foil in a frying pan over a high heat. Add the wine, garlic, shallots and tomato and heat until simmering.
Add the basil, lemon juice and mussels and scrunch the foil closed over the top to form a parcel.
Cook the parcel in the oven for 8-10 minutes. Open up the parcel and discard any mussels that haven't opened.
Serve the foil parcel on a plate.

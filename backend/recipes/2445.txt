Title: Spanish stew with chorizo and black olives
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Ross Burden

Ingredients:
100g/3Â½oz chorizo, sliced
1 garlic clove, chopped
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tin cherry tomatoes
50g/2oz small pitted black olives
25g/1oz butterbeans
5 tbsp red wine

Instructions:
In a saucepan, sweat the chorizo with the garlic and olive oil for four minutes.
Add the tomatoes, black olives, butterbeans and red wine. Cook for ten minutes.
Serve.

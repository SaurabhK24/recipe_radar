Title: Steak medallions with Guinness sauce
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Kevin Woodford

Ingredients:
250g/8¾oz rump steak, cut into medallions
1 bottle of Guinness, or any other stout
1 tsp brown sugar
1 tsp balsamic vinegar

Instructions:
Heat a griddle pan until smoking. Place the steak medallions on the pan and cook for 1 minute on each side, or until cooked to your liking. Remove from the pan, set aside and let rest in a warm place for five minutes.
For the sauce, place the Guinness in a small pan and bring to the boil. Let it reduce by ¾.
Add the sugar and vinegar and stir well.
Serve the steak with the sauce poured over it.

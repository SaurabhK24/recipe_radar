Title: Gnocchi with pesto
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Gino D'Acampo

Ingredients:
1/2 packet gnocchi
handful of rocket
1 lemon
5-6 tbsp olive oil
55g parmesan

Instructions:
Stir together all the pesto ingredients.
Add the gnocchi and stir to coat.
Sprinkle over more parmesan and serve.

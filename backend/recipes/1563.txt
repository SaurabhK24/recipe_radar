Title: Oysters with a coriander and passion fruit relish
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 1
Author: Lesley Waters

Ingredients:
3 rock oysters
1 lemon, juice only
1 lime, juice only
150ml/Â¼ pint olive oil
1 passion fruit, pulp scraped out
1 tsp chilli flakes
1 shallot, finely chopped
small handful fresh coriander, finely chopped
1 lime, cut into wedges, to serve

Instructions:
Carefully open the oysters using an oyster shucker and place on a serving plate.
Place all the remaining ingredients, except the lime wedges, into a small bowl and mix well.
To serve, pour the relish over the oysters and garnish with lime wedges.

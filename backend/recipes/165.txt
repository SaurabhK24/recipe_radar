Title: Toad in the hole
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 2
Author: Gino D'Acampo

Ingredients:
2 free-range eggs
110g/4oz self-raising flour
pinch baking powder
splash milk
salt and freshly ground black pepper
2 tsp fresh thyme leaves
1 tbsp vegetable oil
100g cooked cocktail sausages

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.
Beat the eggs in a large bowl. Add the flour and baking powder and whisk in enough milk to make a thick batter, the consistency of double cream.
Add the thyme leaves and stir again.
Heat a the oil in a small, ovenproof, non-stick frying pan. Pour the batter in and place the cocktail sausages on top. Transfer to the oven and cook for 10-12 minutes, until the batter is golden and the sausages are cooked.
Remove from the oven and serve.

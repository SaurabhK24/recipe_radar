Title: Steamed sea bass with ginger, sesame oil and chives
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Paul Rankin

Ingredients:
1 tsp sesame oil, plus extra for oiling
1 sea bass fillet
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp chopped fresh root ginger
Â½ red pepper, sliced into fine strips

Instructions:
Place the fish onto a small oiled plate, then place inside a bamboo steamer.
Drizzle over the soy sauce and sesame oil, scatter over the ginger and pepper and steam for 6-8 minutes, or until the fish is cooked through. Serve immediately.

Title: Smoky bacon and tomato soup
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Paul Rankin

Ingredients:
1 tsp olive oil
½ onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, chopped
4 slices bacon, chopped
½ pint chicken or vegetable stock
2 tomatoes, chopped
2 tbsp tomato purée

Instructions:
Heat the oil in a pan and gently cook the onion and garlic for 3-4 minutes or until softened.
Add the bacon and cook for a further three minutes.
Pour over the chicken or vegetable stock and add the tomatoes and purée. Cook for five minutes.
With a hand blender, blend the soup until it's smooth.
Serve warm.

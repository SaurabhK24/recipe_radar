Title: Sesame-crusted tuna with broccoli and sweet potato
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Gino D'Acampo

Ingredients:

For the blueberry sauce

50g/1½oz blueberries
2 tsp white wine

For the tuna

100g/3½oz tuna steak
1 tbsp sesame seeds
1 free-range egg yolk
salt and freshly ground black pepper
1 tbsp olive oil

For the broccoli and sweet potato

1 sweet potato, peeled, sliced into 2cm/¾in cubes and cooked until tender
½ broccoli head, blanched

Instructions:
For the sauce, place the blueberries and white wine into the bowl of a food processor and blend the ingredients until smooth.
For the tuna, place the tuna, sesame seeds and egg yolk into a clean bowl. Season the ingredients with salt and freshly ground black pepper and mix well.
Heat the olive oil in a frying pan and cook the tuna for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.
To serve, place the broccoli and sweet potato onto a clean plate. Place the tuna on top of the broccoli and sweet potato and pour the sauce over the top.

Title: Lemon and dill-marinated salmon with herb salad
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 1
Author: Brian Turner

Ingredients:

For the salmon

100g/3½oz salmon fillet, skin removed
1 tsp caster sugar
½ lemon, juice only
small handful fresh dill, finely chopped
drizzle olive oil

For the herb salad

small handful each fresh chives, parsley, basil, mint, chopped
squeeze lemon juice
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

Instructions:
Put all the salmon ingredients into a bowl and stir to coat to salmon well. Cover with cling film and leave to marinate for ten minutes. Slice thinly.
To make the herb salad, put the herbs into a large bowl and squeeze over the lemon juice and olive oil. Mix until the herbs are well coated.
To serve, put the herb salad on a plate and top with the slices of marinated salmon.

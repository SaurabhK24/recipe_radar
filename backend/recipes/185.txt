Title: Tomato rarebit
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Ed Baines

Ingredients:
1 free-range egg yolk
50g/1¾oz hard cheese (such as Cheddar), grated
dash Worcestershire sauce
salt and freshly ground black pepper
½ tomato, seeds scraped out

Instructions:
Heat oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.
Mix the egg, cheese, Lea & Perrins together. Season with the salt and freshly ground black pepper.
Fill the tomato with the mixture and bake in the oven for 20 minutes or until bubbling and golden.

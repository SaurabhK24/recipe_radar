Title: Grilled sausages with onion sauce
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Brian Turner

Ingredients:
dash olive oil
½ onion, finely sliced
85g/3oz butter
75ml/2½fl oz white wine
100ml/3½fl oz hot chicken stock
½ fresh red chilli, chopped
3 boerewors (South African) sausages, or other spiced sausages, grilled, to serve

Instructions:
Heat the olive oil in a frying pan, add the onion and fry until softened but not coloured.
Add the butter, white wine, stock and chilli and simmer for five minutes.
To serve, place the grilled sausages onto a serving plate and pour over the onion sauce.

Title: Hot prawn and smoked salmon salad
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Gino D'Acampo

Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil
150g/5oz shell on prawns
100g/3½oz smoked salmon
pinch dried chilli flakes
½ garlic clove, chopped
2 handfuls rocket
½ lemon juice only
1 tsp grated fresh horseradish
6 quails' eggs, fried

Instructions:
Heat the oil in a frying pan over a medium heat. Add the prawns, smoked salmon, chilli flakes and garlic and fry for three minutes.
Place the rocket, lemon juice and horseradish into a bowl and mix well.
Serve the rocket salad onto a plate, top with the smoked salmon and prawn mixture and top with the fried quails' eggs.

Title: Chickpea and cucumber salad
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 5 mins
Serves: 4
Author: Freddy Bird

Ingredients:
400g chickpeas
1 cucumber
1 bunch coriander
1 bunch parsley
1 green chilli
1 red onion
lemon juice
salt
pepper

Instructions:
Drain and rinse the chickpeas and place in a bowl. Add the cucumber, herbs, chilli and red onion.
Gently toss to combine. Season with lemon juice, salt and pepper, to taste.

Title: Tuna, tomato and spaghetti salad
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 2
Author: Phil Vickery

Ingredients:

For the salad

1 cos lettuce, roughly chopped
2 ripe tomatoes, chopped
100g/3½oz tinned tuna, drained
2 spring onions, chopped
handful fresh parsley, chopped
½ mozzarella ball, cut into cubes
110g/4oz spaghetti, cooked according to packet instructions, drained and allowed to cool

For the dressing

1 tbsp olive oil
2 tsp balsamic vinegar
salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:
For the salad, place all the salad ingredients into a bowl and mix well.
For the dressing, place the oil and vinegar into a bowl, season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and mix well.
To serve, pour the dressing over the salad, stir well and place into serving bowls.

Title: Spigola barcoletta (sea bass boat)
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Gino D'Acampo

Ingredients:

For the sea bass

1 sea bass, back-bone removed, belly left sealed
50m/2fl oz olive oil
Â½ lemon, juice and zest only
salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the rice salad

50g/2oz basmati rice, cooked according to packet instructions
1 asparagus spear, blanched, finely chopped
1 tbsp flaked almonds
1 tbsp pomegranate seeds
salt and freshly ground black pepper
To garnish

2 lime wedges
1 tsp chopped fresh chives

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.
For the sea bass, place the sea bass onto a baking sheet, and open it up like a boat shape.
In a bowl mix, together the oil, lemon juice and zest, then season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
Pour the lemon oil into the sea bass and place into the oven and roast for 15 minutes, or until completely cooked through.
For the rice salad, in a separate bowl, mix together the rice, asparagus, almonds and pomegranate seeds and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
To serve, place the sea bass on a large plate and fill the cavity with the rice salad. Garnish with the lime wedges and sprinkle with the chives.

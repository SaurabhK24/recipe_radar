Title: Snazzy smoked salmon pâté
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 4
Author: James Martin

Ingredients:
85g/3oz smoked salmon trimmings
85g/3oz smoked trout fillets
255g/9oz cream cheese
150ml/5fl oz double cream
1 tbsp chives
30g/1oz butter
½ tsp cayenne pepper
1 lemon, juice only
freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:
Place the salmon, trout and cream cheese and double cream into a blender.
Add the butter, cayenne pepper, lemon juice and seasoning.
Blend the mixture to a paste. Check the seasoning before removing from the blender. Put the paté in a dish and allow to set in the fridge.
Serve with salad leaves and toasted bread.

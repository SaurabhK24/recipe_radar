Title: Chocolate palmiers
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Nick Nairn

Ingredients:
55g puff pastry
icing sugar
cocoa powder
honey

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.
Roll the pastry out into a very thin rectangle and dust with the cocoa and icing sugar.
Roll up the pastry from both ends until they meet in the centre. Slice into 1cm/Â½in slices and place onto a baking sheet. Place into the oven and bake for 5-6 minutes, or until risen and cooked through.
To serve, place onto a plate, dust with cocoa and drizzle with honey.

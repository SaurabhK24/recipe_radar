Title: Stir-fried lamb with onion, cashews and redcurrants
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Brian Turner

Ingredients:
dash olive oil
100g/3½oz lamb chop, bone removed, sliced
½ onion, sliced
1 small red pepper, sliced
pinch chilli flakes
2 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbsp cashew nuts, toasted
1 pak choi, halved
2 tbsp redcurrants, stalks removed, to serve

Instructions:
Heat the olive oil in a pan, add the sliced lamb and fry for 2-3 minutes, or until browned.
Add the onion and red pepper and stir fry over a high heat for two minutes.
Add the chilli flakes, soy sauce, a splash of boiling water, cashew nuts and pak choi and stir fry for 1-2 minutes.
To serve, spoon into a bowl and garnish with redcurrants.

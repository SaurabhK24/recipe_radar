Title: Sesame prawn toasts
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: James Tanner

Ingredients:
2 slices French bread, cut on the diagonal
2 raw tiger prawns, peeled and de-veined
1 spring onion, finely chopped
½ red chilli, finely chopped
1 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
½ tsp toasted sesame oil

Instructions:
Heat a griddle pan until smoking. Add the slices of bread and grill on one side until golden.
Place the remaining ingredients into a mini food processor and blend until smooth.
Spread the un-toasted sides of the bread with the prawn mixture and then place prawn side down back onto the griddle pan to grill for three minutes or until cooked through.

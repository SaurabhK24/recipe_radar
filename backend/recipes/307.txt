Title: Virgin Mary
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 1
Author: Tony Tobin

Ingredients:
200ml/7oz passata
1 lime, juice only
½ tsp Tabasco sauce
½ tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp Dijon mustard
To serve

handful ice cubes
salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:
Place all the ingredients into a food processor and blend.
To serve, pour into a glass filled with ice cubes and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

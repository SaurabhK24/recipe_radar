Title: Basil and parsley-dressed pasta
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 5 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Gino D'Acampo

Ingredients:
55g basil leaves
30g parsley
2 tbsp olive oil
salt
pepper
150g pasta

Instructions:
Place the chopped herbs in a bowl with the olive oil and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Leave to infuse.
Cook the pasta according to the instructions on the packet. When the pasta is cooked, drain and stir in the oil and herbs. Serve immediately.

Title: Pan fried trout with new potatoes and green beans
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Paul Rankin

Ingredients:
110g/4oz new potatoes
110g/4oz French beans

For the trout

knob of butter
2 trout fillets
salt and freshly ground black pepper
To serve

55g/2oz unsalted butter, melted

Instructions:
Bring a pan of water to the boil and boil the new potatoes for 8-10 minutes, or until tender, and the beans for 3-4 minutes. Drain.
Gently heat the butter in a non-stick frying pan. Season the trout fillet and pan fry for 2-3 minutes on each side.
Transfer the trout to a serving plate. Spoon the new potatoes and beans alongside and drizzle with the melted butter to serve.

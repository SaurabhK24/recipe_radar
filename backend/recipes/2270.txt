Title: Seafood tagliatelle
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Garrey Dawson

Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
¼ onion, finely chopped
1 garlic clove, crushed
150ml/5fl oz double cream
85g/3oz cooked prawns, peeled
1 spring onion, finely sliced
100g/3½oz ready-cooked tagliatelle pasta
2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
1 free-range egg yolk
salt and freshly ground black pepper
½ lemon, juice only

Instructions:
Heat the olive oil in a pan and gently fry the onion and garlic for 3-4 minutes, or until softened, then stir the cream and reduce for 3-4 minutes.
Stir in the prawns and spring onion, then add the cooked pasta and stir well to coat. Heat through for 1-2 minutes, then add the parsley, egg yolk and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Stir in the lemon juice.
Place the pasta onto a serving dish and serve.

Title: Chicken satay
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 1
Author: Tony Tobin

Ingredients:
½ red chilli, finely chopped
2 tbsp crunchy peanut butter
splash water
2 tsp soy sauce
½ tsp toasted sesame oil
1 tsp sesame seeds
½ ready-cooked chicken breast, cut into chunks
drizzle of olive oil, to serve

Instructions:
Place the chilli, peanut butter, water, soy sauce, sesame oil and seeds into a food processor and blend until smooth.
Thread the chicken chunks onto two wooden skewers and drizzle with the oil.
To serve, place the satay sauce into a small bowl. Place the chicken skewers onto a plate with the sauce alongside.

Title: Smoked salmon bagel
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 1
Author: Emily Angle

Ingredients:
1 wholemeal or 50/50 thin bagel, sliced in half, toasted
15g/Â½oz light cream cheese
35g/1oz smoked salmon, torn into pieces
squeeze lemon juice
1-2 slices of tomato
1 thin slice of red onion (optional)
freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:
Spread the cream cheese onto the toasted bagel, cover with the smoked salmon strips and squeeze over the lemon juice. Add the slices of tomato and red onion, then season to taste with freshly ground black pepper.

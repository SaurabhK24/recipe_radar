Title: Marinated beef with honey, soy and sesame with coleslaw
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Brian Turner

Ingredients:

For the beef

100g/3½oz rump steak, sliced
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp honey
2 tsp sesame oil
1 tbsp vegetable oil
55g/2oz butter
handful each fresh parsley and chives, chopped

For the coleslaw

½ carrot, grated
½ courgette, grated
¼ onion, grated
3 tbsp Greek-style yoghurt
salt and freshly ground black pepper
handful of fresh coriander and parsley, chopped

Instructions:
For the beef, place the beef, soy sauce, honey and sesame oil into a bowl and leave to marinate for ten minutes.
Heat the vegetable oil in a frying pan, add the beef and stir fry for 2-3 minutes over a high heat.
Remove the meat from the pan and keep warm. Add the butter to the pan and mix with the beef juices. Stir in the herbs.
For the coleslaw, mix together all the coleslaw ingredients and then press into a chefs' ring on a serving plate.
To serve, slide the chefs' ring off the coleslaw, place the beef on top and pour over the herb sauce.

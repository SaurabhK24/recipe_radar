Title: Cranachan
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 5 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Nick Nairn

Ingredients:
50g oats
50ml double cream
150g Greek yoghurt
50g blueberries
2 tbsp honey
mint sprig

Instructions:
Place all the ingredients into a bowl and mix together.
To serve, transfer into a serving bowl and place the mint sprig on top to decorate.

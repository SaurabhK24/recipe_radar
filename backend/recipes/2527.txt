Title: Spinach salad with bacon and walnuts
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 1
Author: Gino D'Acampo

Ingredients:
50g/1¾oz spinach
50g/1¾oz walnuts, chopped
¼ onion, finely chopped
2 tbsp olive oil
salt and freshly ground black pepper
2 tsp red wine vinegar
1 slice streaky bacon, grilled until crisp, to serve

Instructions:
Place all the ingredients except the bacon into a bowl and stir well to coat the spinach leaves.
To serve, top with the bacon.

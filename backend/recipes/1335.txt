Title: Balsamic-glazed sesame salmon with spicy cherry tomatoes and spinach
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Gino D'Acampo

Ingredients:

For the salmon

175g/6oz salmon fillet
sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
1 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
1 tbsp honey
1 tbsp sesame seeds

For the tomatoes

1 tbsp olive oil, plus 1 tbsp extra
large handful of spinach
½ 400g/14oz can cherry tomatoes
½ tsp dried chilli flakes
fresh chives, chopped, to serve

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.
Season the salmon fillet with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper.
Heat the oil in an ovenproof frying pan add the salmon skin-side down and cook cook for two minutes, then turn and cook for one minute.
Pour over the vinegar and continue to cook for two minutes.
Add the honey and sprinkle with the sesame seeds, then place into the oven and cook for five minutes, or until cooked through.
Meanwhile, for the tomatoes, heat the oil in a large frying pan. Add the spinach and sauté for two minutes.
Add the tomatoes, chilli flakes and extra oil and allow to simmer and reduce for 2-3 minutes, until thickened.
To serve, place the spinach onto a warm plate and top with the salmon. Pour over the pan juices and garnish with chives.

Title: Buttered mint sugar snap peas
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Brian Turner

Ingredients:
75g sugar snap peas
25g unsalted butter
2 tsp fresh mint
salt
pepper

Instructions:
Boil the sugar snap peas in boiling salted water for three minutes.
Drain and mix in the butter, mint, salt and freshly ground black pepper. Serve in a bowl.

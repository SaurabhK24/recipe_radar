Title: Cheese and ham croque monsieur
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: James Tanner

Ingredients:
2 slices white crusty bread
100g/3½oz Brie, sliced
2 slices ham
2 free-range eggs, beaten
1 tbsp olive oil
15g/½oz butter
To serve

oakleaf and lamb's lettuce leaves
drizzle olive oil

Instructions:
Take one slice of bread, lay the slices of Brie on top and then cover with the ham. Place the second slice of bread on top, then dip the sandwich into the beaten egg, coating well.
Heat the oil and butter in a frying pan and fry the sandwich until golden-brown on both sides.
To serve, cut in half and arrange on a serving plate. Garnish with lettuce leaves and drizzle with olive oil.

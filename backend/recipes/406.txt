Title: Breakfast stack
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 2
Author: Michael Moore

Ingredients:
2 slices rye bread
1 tomato
50g/2oz cured ham
2 eggs
1 large pickled gherkin

Instructions:
Take the rye bread and place it on a plate.
Slice tomato, place it on the rye bread.
Take the ham and place it on top of the tomato.
Pre-heat pan with the oil - make sure pan is not too hot, break eggs into frying pan and fry until cooked.
Remove eggs, place on top of ham and serve with the gherkin.

Title: Fruit kebab skewers
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 10 mins
Serves: 4
Author: Maria Elia

Ingredients:
1/2 pineapple
2 mangoes
1 papaya
1 dragon fruit
2 kiwi fruit
12 lychees
1/4 watermelon
12 kaffir lime leaves
6 lemon grass sticks

Instructions:
Cut the fruits into rough shapes.
Thread the fruits through the lemongrass alternating every third fruit with a lime leaf.
Chill until required or place on a BBQ or grill and cook for 2 minutes on either side.
Serve hot or cold.

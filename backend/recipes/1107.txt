Title: Grilled scallops
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Phil Vickery

Ingredients:
3 scallops, cleaned, shells retained
cayenne pepper, to sprinkle
1 tbsp polenta
3 lime slices
1 tbsp chopped fresh mint
splash Tabasco sauce
squeeze lime, to serve

Instructions:
Preheat the grill to the highest setting.
Place the scallops in their shells. Sprinkle the scallops with the cayenne pepper and polenta and place a lime slice on top of each.
Place the scallops under the grill for 3-4 minutes, until cooked through.
Sprinkle with the mint and Tabasco sauce, to taste. Drizzle with a squeeze of lime and serve immediately.

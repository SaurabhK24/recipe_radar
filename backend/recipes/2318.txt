Title: Sesame coated tuna with tomato avocado salsa
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Paul Rankin

Ingredients:

For the tuna

140g/5oz fresh tuna steak
sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
25g/1oz sesame seeds
1 tbsp olive oil

For the salsa

¼ red onion, finely chopped
½ avocado, stone removed, cut into small chunks
½ tomato, chopped
1 tbsp olive oil
½ tsp balsamic vinegar
fresh flat-leaf parsley, to garnish

Instructions:
For the tuna, place the tuna on a plate, season, to taste, with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper. Sprinkle over half ofthe sesame seeds onto one side of the tuna, patting them onto the tuna steak, then repeat on the other side.
Heat the oil in a griddle pan until smoking. Add the tuna and cook over a high heat for two minutes on each side, or until cooked to your taste.
For the salsa, place all the ingredients in a bowl and stir to combine.
To serve, remove the tuna from the pan and place onto a serving plate. Spoon the salsa on the plate and garnish with the parsley.

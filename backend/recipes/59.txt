Title: Damson jam syllabub
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Lesley Waters

Ingredients:
250ml Greek yoghurt
3 tbsp double cream
2 tsp icing sugar
3 tbsp damson jam

Instructions:
Whisk the yoghurt, cream and icing sugar together.
Spoon the jam into the base of a glass serving dish and top with the yoghurt mixture. Refrigerate until serving.

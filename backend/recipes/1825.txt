Title: Pork skewer with chargrilled butternut squash
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Gino D'Acampo

Ingredients:

For the pork skewer

200g/7oz pork tenderloin, cubed
1 yellow pepper, chopped
1 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp clear honey

For the chargrilled butternut squash

2 large slices butternut squash
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp sesame seeds, to garnish

Instructions:
For the pork skewer, thread the pork and the yellow pepper pieces onto a skewer and drizzle with oil.
Heat a griddle pan and place the skewer into the pan. Drizzle with the soy sauce and honey and chargrill for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until golden-brown and completely cooked through.
For the chargrilled butternut squash, heat a griddle pan and place the butternut squash slices into the pan. Brush with oil and cook for 5-6 minutes on each side, or until tender and golden-brown.
To serve, place the butternut squash onto a plate, top with the pork skewer and sprinkle over the sesame seeds.

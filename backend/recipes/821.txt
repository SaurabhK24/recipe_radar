Title: Crisp prosciutto, tomato and rocket salad
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Brian Turner

Ingredients:
2 slices prosciutto
1 tomato, sliced
large handful fresh rocket
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp wholegrain mustard
1 tsp clear honey
salt and freshly ground black pepper
2 tsp white wine vinegar

Instructions:
Preheat the grill until hot. Place one slice of prosciutto onto a baking sheet and place under the grill for 2-3 minutes, or until crisp. Remove from the grill and set aside. Finely chop the remaining slice of prosciutto and set aside.
Arrange the tomato slices on a serving plate and top with the rocket.
Place the remaining ingredients and the chopped prosciutto into a small bowl and whisk until combined.
To serve, pour the dressing over the salad on the serving plate and top with the crisp prosciutto.

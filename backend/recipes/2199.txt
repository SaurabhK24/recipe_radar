Title: Sausage patties
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: James Martin

Ingredients:
3 sausages
1 lime, juice only
1 handful fresh coriander, chopped
50g/1¾oz, plain flour, to dust

For the fig jam

½ onion, finely chopped
2 figs, sliced
50g/1¾oz brown sugar
25ml/1 fl oz white wine vinegar

Instructions:
Mix the sausage meat, the lime juice and chopped coriander in a bowl.
Mould into three patties and dust in the flour.
Fry in a frying pan for 6-8 minutes or until cooked through.
For the jam, in a pan add the onions with the figs, brown sugar and the white wine vinegar.
Cook for eight minutes on a low heat until soft and syrupy.
To serve, place the patties on a plate with the fig jam on the side.

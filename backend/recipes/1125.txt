Title: Halibut ceviche
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 1
Author: James Tanner

Ingredients:
50g/1¾oz halibut, thinly sliced
½ lime, juice only
½ lemon, juice only
salt and freshly ground black pepper
½ lime, cut into wedges to garnish
fresh flatleaf parsley leaf, to garnish

Instructions:
Place the halibut slices in the centre of a plate and pour over the lemon and lime juice. Season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper, then leave to 'cure' for 15-20 minutes.
To serve, garnish the plate with lime wedges and the fresh parsley leaf.

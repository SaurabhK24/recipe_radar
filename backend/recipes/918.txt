Title: Baby scallops with a breadcrumb crust
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 2
Author: Max Beesley

Ingredients:
12 baby scallops, in their shells
55g/2oz breadcrumbs
1 garlic clove, peeled and crushed
handful flatleaf parsley, chopped
zest of 1 lemon
salt and freshly ground black pepper
dash olive oil

Instructions:
Preheat the grill to medium.
Place the baby scallops on a baking tray.
In a food processor, mix together the breadcrumbs, garlic, parsley and lemon zest.
Season the mix with salt and freshly ground black pepper, and sprinkle it over the scallops.
Drizzle a little oil over the scallops and cook under the grill for a couple of minutes until crisp on top and scallops are cooked through.

Title: Cucumber salad
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 2
Author: Brian Turner

Ingredients:
½ cucumber, diced
1 tbsp olive oil
25g/1oz anchovy fillets, chopped
1 tsp fresh chopped dill
1 tsp fresh chopped parsley
½ clove garlic, finely chopped

Instructions:
Mix together all the ingredients and serve on a small plate.

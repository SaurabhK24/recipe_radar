Title: Spiced beef salad
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Gino D'Acampo

Ingredients:
300g/12oz T-bone steak, bone removed
1 tsp Cajun spice seasoning
1 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
small handful each fresh chervil, basil and parsley
2 tsp balsamic vinegar
squeeze lime juice

Instructions:
Rub the steak with the Cajun spice and drizzle with oil. Heat a frying pan until smoking hot, add the steak and sear on all sides for 2-3 minutes.
Add the garlic and fry for one minute. Remove from the heat and leave to rest for five minutes. Slice thinly.
To serve, pile the herbs onto a serving plate. Top with the sliced steak and drizzle with balsamic vinegar, lime juice and a dash of olive oil.

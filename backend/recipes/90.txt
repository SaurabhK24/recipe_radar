Title: Thai beef patties
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Lesley Waters

Ingredients:
175g/6oz rump steak, finely chopped
1 garlic clove
¼ tsp dried chilli flakes
½ tsp sesame oil
2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
½ free-range egg, lightly beaten
sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
100g/3½ oz green beans, trimmed and thinly sliced
1 tsp plain flour
2 tbsp olive oil
fresh coriander to garnish
1 tsp toasted sesame seeds, to garnish

Instructions:
Place the steak, garlic, chilli, sesame oil, coriander and egg into a food processor. Blend to combine the ingredients.Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
Transfer into a bowl and stir through the green beans. Lightly flour your hands and form the meat mixture into four patties.
Heat the oil in a deep frying pan over a medium-high heat. Place the beef patties into the frying pan and fry for about three minutes on each side, until golden and cooked through.
To serve, place the patties on a bed of fresh coriander leaves and sprinkle over the toasted sesame seeds.

Title: Seared salmon fillet with sweet and sour peppers
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Brian Turner

Ingredients:

For the seared salmon

dash olive oil
85g/3oz salmon fillet
salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the sweet and sour peppers

25g/1oz butter
1 tbsp olive oil
100g/3Â½oz roasted peppers (from a jar)
2 tsp honey
2 tsp red wine vinegar
1 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbsp sun-dried tomatoes

Instructions:
For the seared salmon, heat the olive oil in a frying pan, season the salmon with salt and freshly ground black pepper and fry for 1-2 minutes on both sides, or until browned all over.
For the sweet and sour peppers, heat the butter and oil in a frying pan, add the rest of the pepper ingredients and simmer for five minutes, or until the peppers are tender.
To serve, spoon the peppers onto a serving plate and top with the salmon. Drizzle around some of the pan juices.

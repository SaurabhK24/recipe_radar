Title: Sashimi of halibut
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 1
Author: Brian Turner

Ingredients:
100g/3½ very fresh skinless halibut steak, thinly sliced
½ lemon, juice only
freshly ground black pepper
½ tomato, cut into wedges, to garnish

Instructions:
Arrange the sliced halibut on a serving plate.
Drizzle with lemon juice, season with freshly ground black pepper and garnish with tomato wedges. Serve chilled.

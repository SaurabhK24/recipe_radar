Title: Chinese-style stir-fried beef with spring onions and green peppers
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Tony Tobin

Ingredients:
1 tbsp vegetable oil
125g/4½oz beef steak, finely sliced
1 tsp Chinese five-spice powder
salt and freshly ground black pepper
½ green pepper, finely sliced
2 spring onions, finely sliced
5cm/2in piece fresh ginger, finely sliced
3 tbsp soy sauce
2 tsp sesame oil
splash boiling water
To serve

1 tbsp sesame seeds, toasted
small handful fresh coriander, chopped
1 tbsp chopped fresh chives

Instructions:
Heat the vegetable oil in a wok until very hot. Season the sliced beef with the Chinese five-spice powder, salt and freshly ground black pepper. Add the beef to the hot wok and fry over a very high heat for 30 seconds.
Add the sliced pepper, spring onions and ginger and fry for 1-2 minutes. Add the soy sauce and sesame oil and stir to coat. Add a splash of boiling water, stir well and spoon into a serving bowl.
To serve, garnish with toasted sesame seeds, coriander and chopped chives.

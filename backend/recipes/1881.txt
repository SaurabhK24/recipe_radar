Title: Prawn and black pudding pilaf
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 2
Author: Hugh Fearnley-Whittingstall

Ingredients:
125g/4oz best butchers black pudding, skin peeled and chopped roughly
2 tbsp olive oil
8 large spring onions, sliced
4 cups cooked basmati rice
125g/4oz peeled prawns
salt and black pepper

Instructions:
Heat the olive oil in a pan and fry the black pudding, stirring or tossing the pan occasionally for a few minutes until it starts to get crispy.
Add the spring onions and fry for another minute or so to soften without burning them.
Finally add the rice and prawns and stir-fry, tossing the pan until all the ingredients are well mixed and heated thoroughly. Season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

Title: Pork and sesame noodle soup
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: James Tanner

Ingredients:

For the soup

1 tbsp vegetable oil
100g/3½oz pork tenderloin, cut into cubes, seasoned with salt and freshly ground black pepper
1 garlic clove, finely chopped
½ green chilli, finely chopped
300ml/½ pint hot chicken stock
100g/3½oz rice noodles
½ savoy cabbage, finely sliced
To serve

2 tsp soy sauce
2 tsp sesame oil

Instructions:
Heat the oil in a saucepan over a medium heat, add the seasoned pork and fry, stirring frequently, for 2-3 minutes, or until beginning to brown on all sides. Add the garlic and chilli and cook for a further 1-2 minutes.
Add the chicken stock, noodles and cabbage and bring to the boil. Boil until the noodles have softened and the pork is completely cooked through.
To serve, pour the soup into a bowl and drizzle with soy sauce and sesame oil.

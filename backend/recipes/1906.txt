Title: Prawns with almond and parsley crust
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 2
Author: James Martin

Ingredients:
200g/7oz raw tiger prawns
2 tbsp Dijon mustard
100g/3Â½ oz toasted flaked almonds, crushed fine
2 tbsp chopped parsley

Instructions:
Pre-heat the oven 200C/400F/Gas 6
Mix together the almonds and parsley.
Rub the prawns with the mustard and roll in the mixture. Place on a baking tray in a single layer and cook for 5-6 minutes.
Serve warm or cold.

Title: Griddled smoked salmon on radicchio
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: James Martin

Ingredients:
1 piece smoked salmon
2 tsp vegetable oil
2 handfuls radicchio
lemon wedges

Instructions:
Heat a grill pan over a high heat.
Brush the smoked salmon with the oil and grill for 1-2 minutes on each side, or until nicely charred.
Serve on top of the radicchio with lemon wedges.

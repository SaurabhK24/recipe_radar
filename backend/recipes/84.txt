Title: Grilled peaches with honey and yoghurt
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 15 mins
Serves: 2
Author: Nick Nairn

Ingredients:
2 peaches
1 tbsp honey
1 tbsp Geek yoghurt

Instructions:
Heat a griddle pan and grill the peaches for 2-3 minutes on both sides.
Serve with a drizzle of honey and a dollop of Greek-style yoghurt.

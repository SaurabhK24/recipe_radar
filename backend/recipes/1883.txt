Title: Prawn and guacamole cocktail
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 2
Author: Liz Smith

Ingredients:
1 Little Gem lettuce, leaves only, shredded
1 avocado
½ lemon, juice only
salt, to taste
4 tbsp de-seeded and cubed cucumber
250g/9oz small cooked and peeled prawns
100ml/3½fl oz mayonnaise
paprika, for garnish
2 slices lemon, for garnish

Instructions:
Place the shredded lettuce into the bottom of two stemmed glasses.
In a bowl, mash the avocado with the lemon juice and season with salt, to taste. Spoon over the lettuce, then add the cubed cucumber and shelled prawns and drizzle over the mayonnaise.
To serve, dust with paprika and garnish with a slice of lemon.

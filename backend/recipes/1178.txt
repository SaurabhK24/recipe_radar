Title: Baked scallops with sunblush tomatoes and chilli
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: Less than 10 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Maria Elia

Ingredients:
2 scallops, cleaned
2 scallop shells, cleaned
3 sunblush tomatoes, chopped
2 spring onions, trimmed, chopped
½ lime, juice only
½ red chilli, seeds removed, chopped
1 tbsp soy sauce

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.
Return the scallop meat to the cleaned shells and scatter over the remaining ingredients.
Place the scallop shells onto a baking tray and bake in the preheated oven for 5-7 minutes, or until the scallops are cooked through. (NB: The scallops are cooked through when the flesh is opaque.) Serve immediately.

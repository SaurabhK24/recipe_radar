Title: Tomato rubbed toast
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 5 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Emily Angle

Ingredients:
1 tomato
1 tbsp olive oil
salt
pepper
bread

Instructions:
Cut the tomato in half and rub the flesh side into the toast. Season with salt and pepper and drizzle with olive oil.

Title: Mackerel rolls with rocket pesto
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: 10 to 30 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Phil Vickery

Ingredients:

For the mackerel rolls

1 mackerel fillet, cut in half
salt and freshly ground black pepper
1 tbsp olive oil
1 slice ciabatta, griddled

For the rocket pesto

1 bunch rocket
2 tsp sesame seeds
1 tbsp olive oil
2 tsp red wine vinegar
salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:
Roll up each piece of mackerel and secure with a cocktail stick.
Heat the olive oil in a small frying pan over a medium heat. Add the mackerel and fry for two minutes on each side, then remove from the pan and put onto a slice of griddled ciabatta.
To make the rocket pesto, place all the ingredients into a food processor and blend until smooth.
To serve, pour the rocket pesto over the mackerel rolls.

Title: Ginger apple fizz
Prep time: 45 mins
Cook time: 5 mins
Serves: 9
Author: The Vegetarian Society

Ingredients:
25g raisins
2L apple juice
2L ginger beer
2cm piece root ginger

Instructions:
Soak the raisins in the apple juice for 1 hour.
Add the ginger beer and root ginger juice. Serve at once.

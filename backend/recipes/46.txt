Title: Chocolate hazelnut pudding
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 20 mins
Serves: 1
Author: Ed Baines

Ingredients:
50g chocolate
100ml double cream
3 eggs
25g icing sugar
brandy
50g hazelnuts
1 orange

Instructions:
Melt the chocolate with the cream in a small pan then leave to cool for a few minutes.
Whisk together the egg yolks and icing sugar.
Pour this into the chocolate mixture and continue to whisk until light and fluffy.
Stir in the brandy and hazelnuts.
Serve in a bowl or glass and top with the orange segments, if you like.

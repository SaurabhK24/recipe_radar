Title: Vegetable broth
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 20 mins
Serves: 2
Author: Nick Nairn

Ingredients:
1/2 onion
2 carrots
1/4 potato
300ml chicken stock
salt
pepper
2 tbsp chives

Instructions:
Place the vegetables and stock into a saucepan and bring to the boil.
Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper and simmer for 10-15 minutes, or until all of the vegetables are tender.
To serve, ladle the broth into two soup bowls and sprinkle over the chives.
